SEE
Here are some common verbs that are often combined with other verbs:

I like to  v. ; I like  v. ing - I like to read; I like reading

I practice  v. ing - I practice singing; I practice speaking

Try to  v. ; Try  v. ing - Try to cook every day; Try making a To-do list

I recommend  v. ing - I recommend going there; I recommend trying it

I need to start  v. ing - I need to start making my presentation slides

I want to stop  v. ing - I want to stop smoking

Example

Bill:

Do you like listening to music, Ken?

Ken:

Yes, I do. I’m in a jazz band. I practice playing the trumpet on weekends.

Bill:

Wow, I didn’t know that.

Ken:

I need to start practicing every day. Our next concert is in April. I recommend coming to our concert.

レッスンのポイント

今回は動詞と動詞を　ingで繋げる文を学びます。

【動詞が２つ】

  • I like v. ing
  • I practice v. ing
  • I recommend v. ing / try v. ing

    • 【動詞が3つ】

  • I need to start v. ing
  • I want to stop v. ing

    • 動詞＋v. ing のみの動詞

    I practice v. ing I recommend v. ing

    動詞＋to v. のみの動詞

    I want to v. I plan to v.

    両方使える動詞

    I like v. ing / I like to v. I start v. ing / I start to v.

    SEE
    Here are some common verbs that are often combined with other verbs:

    ここでは、他の動詞と組み合わせて使われることが多い動詞をご紹介します:

    I like to  v. ; I like  v. ing I like to read; I like reading

    私はVが好きです；私はv.ingが好きです 読書が好き、読書するのが好き

    I practice  v. ing I practice singing; I practice speaking

    私は練習しています 歌の練習をする、話す練習をする

    Try to  v. ; Try  v. ing Try to cook every day; Try making a To-do list

    Vをしようとしています; v.ingを試しています 毎日料理に挑戦する；To-doリストを作ってみよう

    I recommend  v. ing I recommend going there; I recommend trying it

    v.ingをお勧めします。 行ってみることをお勧めします。試すことをお勧めします

    I need to start  v. ing I need to start making my presentation slides

    Ｖing を始める必要がある 私はプレゼンテーションのスライドを作り始める必要があります

    I want to stop  v. ing I want to stop smoking

    Ｖingをやめたい タバコをやめたい

    Bill:

    Do you like listening to music, Ken?

    ビル:

    Yes, I do. I’m in a jazz band. I practice playing the trumpet on weekends.

    Bill:

    Wow, I didn’t know that.

    ビル:

    Ken:

    ケン：

    毎日練習を始めないといけませんね。次のコンサートは4月です。コンサートに来ることをお勧めします。
