I WOULD LIKE...
SEE
You use “I would like n.“ to talk about something you want.

I would like a new phone.

 
You use “I would like to v.” to talk about an action you want to take.

I would like to buy a new phone.

 
You use “I would like someone to v.” to talk about another person’s action you want to see.

I would like my company to give me a new phone.

Example

Bill:

So, Ken, you are an engineer now. What would you like to do with your career?

Ken:

Well, I would like to continue my job for two or three more years. Then, I would like my company to send me overseas. And of course, I would like a raise.

wantの丁寧表現（I WOULD LIKE）

日本語サポートページ

レッスンの理解を深めるための予習用教材です。オンラインレッスンでは1ページの英語のみの教材を使用します。

レッスンのポイント

今回は　I would like　で始まるフレーズを3種類学びます。

I would like n. 「▼▼が欲しいです」

Coffee, a new computer , advice など、モノ(noun=名詞)が欲しい時に使います。

I would like to v. 「〇〇がしたいです」

Have coffee, buy a new computer, get advice など、自分が行動(verb=動詞)したい時に使います。

I would like someone to v. 「someoneに〇〇して欲しいです」

誰か(someone)に○○(verb=動詞)して欲しい時に使います。

SEE
You use “I would like n.“ to talk about something you want.

”I would like n. "を使って、自分の欲しいものについて話します。

I would like a new phone.

新しいスマホが欲しいです。

You use “I would like to v.” to talk about an action you want to take.

”I would like to v. "を使って、自分がしたい行動について話します。

I would like to buy a new phone.

新しいスマホを購入したいと思っています。

You use “I would like someone to v.” to talk about another person’s action you want to see.

I would like to someone to vを使って、自分が見たい他人の行動を語るときに使います。

I would like my company to give me a new phone.

会社に新しい携帯電話を支給してもらいたいです。

Example 例

Bill:

So, Ken, you are an engineer now. What would you like to do with your career?

ビル:

さて、ケンさんは今エンジニアですね。これからのキャリアはどうしたいですか。

Ken:

Well, I would like to continue my job for two or three more years. Then, I would like my company to send me overseas. And of course, I would like a raise.

ケン：

そうですね、あと2～3年は仕事を続けたいと思っています。そうしたら、会社から海外に派遣してもらいたいですね。もちろん昇給もしたいです。
