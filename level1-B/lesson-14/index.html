To compare things, you can use “-er” and “-est” with short words like these:

Drive Tech is smaller than Aichi Motors.

I think plan B is better than plan A.

June is the busiest month of the year.

N5000 is our newest product.



You can use “more,” “less,” “most,” and “least” with longer words like these:

This model is more expensive than that one.

E-mail has become less common than before.

This is the most exciting project ever.

Indonesia is the most important market for us.

Example

Bill:

How was your visit to the client’s?

Ken:

Oh, it was great. They had a very nice office!

Bill:

What was so nice about it?

Ken:

Well, their meeting room was cleaner and more spacious than ours, and they also had the most comfortable chairs ever. Their office is on the 50th floor, and I think they have the best view of Tokyo.

形容詞を変化させて、モノの程度を比べる「比較級」と「最上級」を学びます。

単語の長さにより変化の仕方が2種類あります ※最上級は the とセットで覚えましょう。

adj. + er (比較級）／ the + adj. + est (最上級）

Tall : taller / the tallest

Long : longer / the longest

Busy : busier / the busiest

SEE
To compare things, you can use “-er” and “-est” with short words like these:

物事を比較するときには、「-er」や「-est」などの短い単語を使って、「-er」と「-est」を使います。

Drive Tech is smaller than Aichi Motors.

ドライブテックは愛知自動車よりも小さいです。

I think plan B is better than plan A.

AプランよりもBプランの方がいいと思います。

June is the busiest month of the year.

6月は一年で最も忙しい月です。

N5000 is our newest product.

N5000は当社の新製品です。



You can use “more,” “less,” “most,” and “least” with longer words like these:

このような長い単語には、「より多く」「より少ない」「最も多い」「最も少ない」を使うことができます。

This model is more expensive than that one.

このモデルはそのモデルよりも高価です。

E-mail has become less common than before.

以前に比べてメールが少なくなってきました。

This is the most exciting project ever.

今までで一番ワクワクするプロジェクトです。

Indonesia is the most important market for us.

インドネシアは私たちにとって最も重要な市場です。

Example 例

Bill:

How was your visit to the client’s?

ビル：

クライアントの訪問はどうでしたか。

Ken:

Oh, it was great. They had a very nice office!

ケン：

ああ、最高でしたよ。とても素敵なオフィスでした。

What was so nice about it?

ビル：

何が良かったですか。

Well, their meeting room was cleaner and more spacious than ours, and they also had the most comfortable chairs ever. Their office is on the 50th floor, and I think they have the best view of Tokyo.

ケン：

そうですね、彼らの会議室は私たちの会議室よりも清潔で広々としていましたし、今までで一番座り心地の良い椅子もありました。彼らのオフィスは50階にありますが、東京の景色は最高だと思います。
