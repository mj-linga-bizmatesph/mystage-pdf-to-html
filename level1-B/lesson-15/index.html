SEE
To compare the number of countable things, use “more,” “the most,” “fewer,” “the fewest.”

There are more people in Yokohama than in Osaka. Tokyo has the most people in Japan.

Our company has fewer factories than Drive Tech. We have the fewest factories in this industry.

 
 
To compare the amount of uncountable things, use “more,” “the most,” “less,” “the least.”

My last department had the most trouble in our company. There is less trouble in my new department.

Meeting room C has the least space. Room B has more space than Room C.

Example

Ken:

At this year’s general meeting, our team will have more time to present than we did last year.

Bill:

How much time do we get?

Ken:

15 minutes. Other teams get less time than we do, so we are lucky. I have the fewest things to talk about. Mary has the most, so her part should be 10 to 12 minutes. I only need 3 minutes.

レッスンのポイント

形容詞を変化させて、モノの量や数を比べる「比較級」と「最上級」を学びます。

Countable nouns 数えられる名詞 : more, fewer, the most, the fewest

People, computers, problems, e-mails, meetings　など

例文：We need to have fewer meetings.

会議の数を減らす必要がある。

Uncountable nouns 数えられない名詞 : more, less, the most, the least

Money, work, time, space, trouble　など

例文： We have less free time this week than last week.

先週よりも今週の方が自由時間が少ないです。
SEE
To compare the number of countable things, use “more,” “the most,” “fewer,” “the fewest.”

数えることができるものの数を比較するには、"more" "the most" "the few" "the fewest "を使います。

There are more people in Yokohama than in Osaka. Tokyo has the most people in Japan.

大阪よりも横浜の方が人が多い。東京は日本で一番人が多い。

Our company has fewer factories than Drive Tech. We have the fewest factories in this industry.

当社はドライブテックよりも工場数が少ないです。この業界で最も工場数が少ないのが当社です。

 
 
To compare the amount of uncountable things, use “more,” “the most,” “less,” “the least.”

数えられないものの量を比較するには、"more"、"the most"、"less"、"the least "を使います。

My last department had the most trouble in our company. There is less trouble in my new department.

私の前の部署では、社内で一番トラブルが多かったです。新しい部署でのトラブルは少ないです。

Meeting room C has the least space. Room B has more space than Room C.

会議室Cは最も小さいスペースです。会議室Bは会議室Cよりも大きいスペースです。

Example 例

Ken:

At this year’s general meeting, our team will have more time to present than we did last year.

ケン:

今年の総会では、私たちのチームは昨年よりも発表する時間が増えます。

Bill:

How much time do we get?

ビル:

時間はどのくらいありますか。

Ken:

15 minutes. Other teams get less time than we do, so we are lucky. I have the fewest things to talk about. Mary has the most, so her part should be 10 to 12 minutes. I only need 3 minutes.

ケン:

15分です。他のチームは自分たちよりも時間が少ないので、運がいいですね。私は一番話すことが少ないです。メアリーが一番多いので、彼女のパートは10分から12分。私は3分でいいです。
