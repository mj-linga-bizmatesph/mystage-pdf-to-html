BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK CILESSON 1
TALKING ABOUT YOURSELF
1
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

My friends call me Jay. I was born in Shanghai. I lived there until I was 15 years old. Then I moved to Hong Kong. I’m married and I have two kids.

I have a lot of friends in Hong Kong, but my close friends are in Shanghai. I go back to Shanghai once a year to see them.

I majored in Engineering in university. I work for an electronics company now. I am pretty busy during the week, but on weekends I like to play tennis. I’m a really good tennis player.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • What is your name? What do your friends call you?
  • Where were you born? Where do you live now?
  • Can you tell me about your family?
  • Where do many of your friends live? How often do you see them?
  • What did you major back in university?
  • What kind of company do you work in?
  • Are you busy during the week? What do you do on weekends?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about yourself. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • Your name and nickname
  • Your birthplace
  • Where you live
  • Your family (siblings and relatives)
  • Your major in university
  • The kind of company you work in
  • Your usual weekly schedule
