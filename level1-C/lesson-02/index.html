TALKING ABOUT YOUR COMPANY
2
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

I work for TRITEC. TRITEC is a company that makes electronic goods. TRITEC’s head office is in Japan. It has over 2,000 employees.

TRITEC has 10 offices and 3 factories in China. The company has plans to open offices in other countries in Asia in the future.

Last year, TRITEC had a very good year. TRITEC’s sales increased by 10%. Our computer division got a very big contract with a Taiwanese company. This year, a new client purchased 5,000 computers from TRITEC.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • Where do you work? What kind of company is it?
  • Where is your head office located? How many employees does it have?
  • How many local offices does your company have?
  • Does your company have offices overseas?
  • Did your company perform well last year?
  • What were the company’s achievements last year?
  • Do you think it will be a good year this year?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about your company. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • Your company and the kind of company it is
  • Your head office and its employees
  • Your company’s local offices
  • Your company’s overseas facilities
  • The company’s performance last year
  • Highlights of the business last year
  • Your company’s forecast this year
