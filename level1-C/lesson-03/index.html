BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK CILESSON 3
TALKING ABOUT YOUR JOB
3
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

I work as a sales representative in the computer division at TRITEC. I’ve been working as a sales representative at TRITEC for 5 years now.

I usually get to the office at 8:30AM. I always check my e-mails from our clients in the morning. In the afternoon, I visit clients and make presentations to introduce our products.

My client visits are usually scheduled at the start of the week. So, I’m very busy every Monday. It’s not an easy job, but I really like it. In the future, I hope I can be promoted to manager.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • What is your job?
  • Where do you work? How long have you been working in the company?
  • What time do you usually get to the office?
  • What do you usually do in the morning? In the afternoon?
  • What is your weekly schedule like? When are you usually busy?
  • Is your job easy or difficult?
  • What position would you like to be promoted to?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about your job. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • Your job
  • Your company and your tenure
  • The time you arrive at the office
  • Your tasks in the morning and afternoon
  • Your weekly schedule
  • How you see your job (easy or difficult)
  • How you want to develop your career in the future
