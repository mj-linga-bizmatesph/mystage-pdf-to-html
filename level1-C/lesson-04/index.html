BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK CILESSON 4
TALKING ABOUT BUSINESS TRAVEL
4
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

I go on a business trip about once a month. I rarely go overseas, but I sometimes go to a few major cities in China. I hope I can travel to Canada in the future.

Last week, I visited a client in Shanghai. I made a presentation on our company’s new product. It was very successful because I closed the deal with the client.

Sometimes, I get really tired when I need to make day trips. But I really like traveling because I get to see many places and meet many people.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • How often do you have business trips? Where do you usually go?
  • What country would you like to travel to in the future?
  • When was your last business trip? What did you do?
  • Was it successful? How did it go?
  • Are business trips always enjoyable?
  • What do you like about traveling?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about business travel. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • Your business trips and usual destinations
  • The country you’d like to visit in the future
  • Your last business trip and the things you did
  • The outcome of your last business trip
  • The hardships of traveling
  • What you like about traveling
