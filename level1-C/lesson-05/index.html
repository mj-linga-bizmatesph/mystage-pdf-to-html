BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK CILESSON 5
TALKING ABOUT YOUR CUSTOMERS
5
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

At TRITEC, we mainly have two types of customers. These are electronic stores and corporations. Both are important customers because they make up most of our sales at TRITEC.

Electronic stores buy products from us and sell them to consumers at their stores. Corporations buy products from TRITEC for their employees to use for business.

I can’t tell you the name, but our biggest client is a discount electronics company. They have over 200 stores in China. They sell all kinds of electronics, such as computers, cameras, TVs, and so on. I hope we can reach out to other Asian countries soon, such as Malaysia and Singapore.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • How many types of customers does your company have?
  • What kind of customers are they? Why are they important?
  • How do they use your products or services?
  • What kind of business is your biggest customer in?
  • What other countries would you like to expand into?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about your customers. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • The types of customers in your company
  • What your products/services are used for by the customers
  • Your biggest customer
  • The products/services offered by your biggest customer
  • Other countries/markets you’d like your company to expand into
