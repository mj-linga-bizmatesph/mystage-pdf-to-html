BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK CILESSON 6
TALKING ABOUT YOUR PRODUCTS
6
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

At TRITEC, we sell all kinds of electronic goods. Our most popular products are tablets and computers. Customers like our products because they are low-priced and reliable.

Our best-selling product is the “TRITEC Light” laptop computer. It’s TRITEC’s thinnest and lightest computer. It is especially popular with salespeople who need to carry a computer around a lot.

I always recommend the TRITEC Light because it’s very portable and reliable. The only problem is that we cannot customize it. But, if you always have a stiff shoulder from carrying a heavy computer around, the TRITEC Light is the solution for you.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • What kind of products/services does your company have?
  • What are the most popular? Can you describe the product/service?
  • Who uses the product/service? Why do they buy it?
  • Why would you recommend this product/service?
  • Does it solve any problems your customers have?
  • What are the product’s/service’s weaknesses?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about your products. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • Your company’s product line
  • Your best-selling product/service
  • The people/companies who use the product/service and the reasons they buy it
  • The reasons you recommend your best-selling product/service
  • The product’s/service’s weaknesses
  • The problems the product/service solves
