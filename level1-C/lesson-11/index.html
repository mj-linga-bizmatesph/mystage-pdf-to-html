TALKING ABOUT YOUR COMPETITORS
11
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

TRITEC has two big competitors. These are Compu Asia and CE Corp. TRITEC is currently the third largest electronics company in China.

 

Compu Asia is the biggest company in the industry, while CE Corp is the oldest. They both specialize in making very high quality products for home and business use.

 

Compu Asia has a wider selection of products for consumers than TRITEC. However, its prices are higher. CE Corp’s computers have many features so its products are the most expensive. TRITEC’s low-priced, quality products give it a stronger brand image.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • Who are your biggest competitors?
  • Where does your company rank among your competitors?
  • Who’s the biggest company in the industry? The oldest?
  • What do your competitors specialize in?
  • What advantages do your competitors have over your company?
  • What are their weaknesses?
  • What are your advantages over your competitors?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about your company. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • Your biggest competitors
  • Your company’s rank in the industry
  • The biggest and oldest companies in the industry
  • Your competitors’ area of expertise
  • Your competitors’ edge over your company
  • Your competitors’ weaknesses
  • Your company’s advantages over your competitors’
