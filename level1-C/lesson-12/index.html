BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK CILESSON 12
TALKING ABOUT PROJECTS
12
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

I’m a member of a new project. The goal of the project is to open TRITEC’s first overseas office. We have a small budget and a tight deadline.

I’m working with two other colleagues on this project. We need to do market research in several countries and make a recommendation to our boss by next week.

We all have different opinions. I think we should open an office in Turkey because rent and labor costs are low. However, the other project members think the first office should be in Singapore. They thought it’d be easier for TRITEC to expand to other countries in Asia.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • What project are you working on? What’s the goal of the project?
  • What’s the budget and deadline like?
  • Who are you working with on this project?
  • What tasks do you need to do?
  • Do all the project members share the same opinions about the project?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about projects. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • An ongoing project and its objectives
  • The budget and schedule of the project
  • The people you’re working with
  • The tasks you need to accomplish
  • Agreements and disagreements between you and your project members
