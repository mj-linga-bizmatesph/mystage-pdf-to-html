BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK CILESSON 13
TALKING ABOUT MOTIVATION
13
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

Keeping employees motivated is very important at TRITEC. Everyone is happy because TRITEC offers a good salary and bonuses to employees who perform well.

TRITEC also holds a company dinner every year at a nice hotel for employees and their families. It’s a great opportunity for all of us to get to know each other as a person.

I have a lot of motivation at work. I have a good boss and work with friendly colleagues. I receive a good salary and sometimes earn bonuses. However, I get the most motivation from making my customers happy.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • Do you think everyone is motivated in your company?
  • What does your company do to keep its employees happy?
  • Does your company have special ways to motivate its employees?
  • How does everyone feel about it?
  • Do you have a lot of motivation at work?
  • What keeps you motivated at work?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about motivation. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • The morale of employees in your company
  • How your company motivates its employees
  • How employees feel about the company’s approach to motivating them
  • Your level of motivation at work
  • Motivators for you
