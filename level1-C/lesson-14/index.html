TALKING ABOUT DRESS CODE
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

A dress code helps employees dress comfortably while looking professional. I personally like wearing formal business attire because of this reason.

These days, many IT companies or start-up companies allow wearing casual clothing. People like me think it’s OK. However, others say that it’s unprofessional.

Factory workers at TRITEC wear uniforms and safety equipment. Those at the head office wear suits and ties except in summer. In summer, employees don’t need to wear a tie because it will be too hot if they do.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • What kind of dress code do you like?
  • Why do you like this kind of dress code?
  • What’s the dress code like in most companies in your country?
  • What is the dress code in your company?
  • Do some employees in your company dress differently from others?
  • Does your company have a flexible dress code?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about dress code. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • The kind of dress code you prefer
  • The reasons you like this kind of dress code
  • The dress codes in most companies in your country
  • The dress code in your company
  • Exceptions to the dress code
