TALKING ABOUT HIRING
15
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

TRITEC gets about 2,000 applicants each year. Most of them are new graduates. However, some already have impressive work experiences and skills. My company is always looking for global-minded people.

There are three steps in our hiring process. First, applicants must take a written test. Second is an interview with the HR manager. The final step is an interview with a division manager.

I applied to TRITEC about five years ago. The written test and the first interview were not so difficult. However, the interview with the division manager was very difficult. I’m really surprised I passed the interview and got the job.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • How many applicants does your company get each year?
  • What sort of background do most of them have?
  • What kind of businessperson is your company looking for?
  • How many steps are there in the hiring process and what are they?
  • When did you apply to your company?
  • Which steps were easy? Which steps were difficult?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about hiring. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • The number of applicants you get on average each year
  • Their typical background
  • The kind of talent your company seeks
  • An overview of your hiring process
  • Details of each step in your hiring process
  • The time you applied to your company
  • The steps you thought were easy and difficult
