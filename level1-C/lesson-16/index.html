TALKING ABOUT COMPANY RULES
16
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

TRITEC has several rules everyone must follow. The most important one is employees have to leave confidential documents at the office.

We have other strict rules such as working between 9 AM and 5 PM. However, some rules aren’t strict, like wearing name tags at the office.

In the sales department, the rule for work schedule is a bit flexible. Sales reps like myself often visit clients in the afternoon. Our boss allows us to go home after visiting a client. Not everyone has this kind of flexibility so I’m really happy about it.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • Does your company have many rules for its employees?
  • What’s the most important rule in your company?
  • What rules is your company strict about?
  • How do you feel about this kind of strictness?
  • What rules are flexible?
  • How do you feel about this kind of flexibility?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about company rules. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • An overview of the rules in your company
  • The most important rule in your company
  • The rules your company is very strict about
  • The rules your company is very lenient on
  • How you feel about your company’s rules
