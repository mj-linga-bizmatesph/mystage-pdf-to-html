TALKING ABOUT YOUR OFFICE
17
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

It takes 15 minutes on foot to the nearest train station from my house. Then, it takes about 20 minutes to get to the station nearest to my office. After I get off the train, it’s only a 5 minute walk to my office.

Our building is located in the financial district of Hong Kong. It’s such a convenient place because there are many shopping malls, restaurants and banks nearby.

My office is on the 10th floor of a 25-storey building. My desk is near the window so I can see Victoria Harbor. I always keep my desk clean by organizing my paperwork.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • How far is your house from the nearest train station?
  • How long does it take you to get to your office?
  • Where is your office building located?
  • What other businesses are there nearby?
  • On which floor is your office? How tall is the building?
  • What can you see outside the window from your desk?
  • How do you keep your desk organized?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about your office. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • The distance of your house to the nearest train station
  • Your commute
  • The location of your office building
  • Landmarks near your office building
  • The floor your office is on
  • The view from your desk
  • How you keep your desk organized
