BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK CILESSON 18
TALKING ABOUT YOUR GUESTS
18
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

Guests visit our sales office about twice a month. These guests are usually vendors. But sometimes people from the head office, like the sales director, come to visit us.

In his visit last week, our director presented the monthly sales figures. We also had an opportunity to bring up any problems we had been having in the sales department.

Also last week, a vendor came to the office to meet with me. The vendor works for a company that sells stationery. They offered a big discount but I’m still thinking about it. I’m expecting them to visit again next week.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • How often do guests visit your office?
  • Who are these guests, usually?
  • When was the last time someone visited you?
  • What did you discuss with this guest?
  • Are you expecting a guest soon?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about your guests. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • How often guests visit your office
  • Who your usual guests are
  • The last time you had a guest
  • The subject of discussion with this guest
  • Guests you are expecting in the near future
