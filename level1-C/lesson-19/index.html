BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK CILESSON 19
TALKING ABOUT E-MAILS
19
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

I get about 50 e-mails every day. I read the important ones first. These are usually e-mails from my customers and my boss.

I also get e-mails from my colleagues requesting something, and I sometimes get internal announcements. However, I don’t reply to all of them. I also get a lot of junk mail, which I always delete.

Yesterday, I received an e-mail from a new customer. The customer wanted information on TRITEC’s products. The customer works for a big company, so this can lead to a big contract. I replied to the customer right away to set up an appointment.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • How many e-mails do you usually get in a day?
  • Who are the important e-mails usually from?
  • Who else do you get e-mails from? Do you always reply to them?
  • Do you also get junk mail? What do you do with them?
  • What is an important e-mail you received recently?
  • What was the e-mail about?
  • How did you reply?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about your company. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • The number of e-mails you usually get in a day
  • The people you receive important e-mails from
  • How you handle other e-mails
  • An important e-mail you got recently
  • Your reply
