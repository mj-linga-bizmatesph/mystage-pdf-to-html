BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK CILESSON 8
TALKING ABOUT YOUR WEEKEND
8
SEE
Read the story aloud.

Jason:

I love to play golf on weekends. Last Saturday, I went to a golf course with my colleagues. We played a round of golf for a few hours.

 

After golf, we had lunch at a café. We talked about work for a couple of hours. Then, we all went home in the afternoon. We all had a good time.

 

I don’t have plans for this weekend yet. If it’s sunny, I think I’ll go jogging in the big park nearby. If it rains, I think I’ll just stay home and watch a movie. I like watching several movies when I’m at home during weekends.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • What do you love to do on weekends?
  • What did you do last weekend? Who did you spend it with?
  • What did you do after that? What time did you go home?
  • How did you feel about your last weekend?
  • What are your plans this weekend?
  • How do you spend your weekends when it’s sunny? When it rains?
  • What do you usually do when you spend your weekend at home?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about your company. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • Your favorite weekend activity
  • The people you spend your weekends with
  • Last weekend
  • How you felt about your last weekend
  • Your plans this weekend
  • The weekend activities when it’s sunny and rainy
  • How you entertain yourself at home
