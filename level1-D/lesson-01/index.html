BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK DILESSON 1
TALKING ABOUT MARKETING
1
SEE
Read the story aloud.

John:

I work in the marketing department at TC Com. I am responsible for finding out customers’ needs. I get information from customers by doing customer surveys.

TC Com’s marketing department wants to find out the types of services customers want. Furthermore, we also research our competitors’ services and their prices.

We try to communicate the strengths of our services in fun and interesting ways. To do that, it’s important to work closely with advertising agencies.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • What do people in the marketing department do?
  • Is it important to find out about customers’ needs?
  • What sort of information about your customers is important for your company?
  • What sort of information about your competitors is important for your company?
  • What does your company do to make your product more attractive?
  • Do you think working with advertising agencies is effective?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about marketing. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • The responsibilities of a marketing person
  • The importance of understanding customers’ needs
  • The information you want to know about your customers
  • The information you want to know about your competitors
  • How your company promotes its products/services
  • Your opinion on working with ad agencies
