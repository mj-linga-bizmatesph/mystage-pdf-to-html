BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK DILESSON 4
TALKING ABOUT PROMOTIONS
4
SEE
Read the story aloud.

John:

Every March, HR holds a promotion test for its employees. Employees recommended by their managers can take the test. The test is different depending on the position.

Many employees take the test to become a manager. The promotion test is really difficult. Only a few people pass the test each year.

I’ve been selected to take the promotion test this year. I am excited, but I’m not very confident. I need to have an interview with the HR manager and my boss. They will be asking about my work and performance, so I need to prepare for it.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • When does your company give promotions to its employees?
  • What kind of people are subject to promotion?
  • Is it hard to be promoted in your company?
  • Are you interested in getting a promotion? If yes, for which position?
  • What are the requirements to be promoted to that position?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about promotions. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • When your company gives promotions
  • The people qualified for a promotion in your company
  • The requirements for promotions
  • A position you are interested in taking up
  • The requirements for taking up that position
