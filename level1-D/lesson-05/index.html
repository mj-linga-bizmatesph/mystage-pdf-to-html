BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK DILESSON 5
TALKING ABOUT COMPANY FACILITIES
5
SEE
Read the story aloud.

John:

The facilities at TC Com are very good. On the first floor, we have a cafeteria and a lobby. The lobby is the most beautiful area because this is where we receive our guests.

On the second floor, we have a first aid room with a company nurse. We also have a smoking area there. There’s an executive lounge for the directors on the top floor.

I’m very happy about the facilities here at TC Com. However, I would like a better smoking area. It’s quite small and only holds up to ten people. I think TC Com should make it bigger or make another one.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • What facilities are there in your company? Which do you think is the most beautiful?
  • What are those facilities for?
  • Is there a special facility only some people can use in your company?
  • How do you feel about the facilities in your company?
  • Which facility do you think needs to be improved? What does it look like?
  • What can the company do to make it better?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about company facilities. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • The most beautiful facilities in your company
  • The function of those facilities
  • Exclusive facilities for some people in your company
  • How you feel about your company facilities
  • A facility with room for improvement
