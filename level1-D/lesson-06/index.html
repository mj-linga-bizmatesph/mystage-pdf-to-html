BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK DILESSON 6
TALKING ABOUT COMPANY SYSTEMS
6
SEE
Read the story aloud.

John:

Employees at TC Com use computers in their daily work. I use my computer to access my e-mail, write reports, record and analyze data, and so on.

TC Com has recently installed a new computer system. The new system helps us manage employee and customer information easily.

However, the new system is really expensive to maintain. Furthermore, many employees are having trouble using it and are complaining about it. I still like the old system because it was easier to use.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • Do employees in your company use the computer everyday?
  • What do you use computers for in your company?
  • Does your company rely on an internal computer system?
  • What does the system do?
  • What are its disadvantages?
  • Do some employees in your company complain about the system?
  • How do you feel about the computer system?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about company systems. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • How employees use computers in your company
  • Your company’s computer system
  • The features and functions of the computer system
  • The disadvantages of the system
  • How employees have reacted to the computer system
  • How you feel about the system
