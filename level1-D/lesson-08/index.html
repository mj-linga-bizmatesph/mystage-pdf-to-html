BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK DILESSON 8
TALKING ABOUT STRESS
8
SEE
Read the story aloud.

John:

Being an assistant manager can be stressful. I have had more work since I got promoted. Before, I could go home at 6PM. But now, I usually finish work at 10PM.

I sometimes work on Saturdays. It’s difficult, but I understand that I have to. I have more meetings to attend now and report to my boss on a weekly basis.

I always talk to my boss when I feel stressed out. She’s a good listener, so I feel much better afterwards. I sometimes go out drinking with my close colleagues to talk about my problems. I’m glad I have supportive people in our team.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • Is your job stressful?
  • What time do you usually finish work? Do you sometimes work overtime?
  • Do you sometimes work on weekends? How do you feel about it?
  • What else gives you stress at work?
  • Who do you talk to when you feel stressed out?
  • What else do you do when you feel stressed out?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about stress. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • The level of stress you experience at work
  • Working overtime
  • How you feel about working on weekends
  • The other sources of stress in your job
  • The people you talk to when you are stressed out
  • The things you do to relieve stress
