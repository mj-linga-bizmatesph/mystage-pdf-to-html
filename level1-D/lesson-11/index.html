BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK DILESSON 11
TALKING ABOUT COMPANY EVENTS
11
SEE
Read the story aloud.

John:

TC Com holds an annual employee dinner party. It’s a chance for employees’ families to visit the company. About 1,000 people visit TC Com for the event each year.

There are always lots of food and drinks, and live music. They also hold raffle draws and give out prizes such as cameras and music players.

Last year, my wife and I attended the annual company dinner. I introduced her to my boss and colleagues. My wife enjoyed talking with my female colleagues. She had a lot of fun, so she might go with me again this year.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • What are some of the events in your company?
  • What is the event for? How many people usually attend?
  • What’s it like to attend that kind of event?
  • Are there any fun activities during the event?
  • Did you attend the last company event with a family member or friend?
  • What have other people said about the event?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about company events. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • The events in your company
  • The purpose for having these events
  • What it is like to attend your company events
  • The activities held during the events
  • Others’ opinions on the event
