BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK DILESSON 13
TALKING ABOUT CUSTOMER SERVICE
13
SEE
Read the story aloud.

John:

Excellent customer service is very important at TC Com. So, employees are trained to sound polite, look professional and be very helpful to customers.

We have a 24-hour hotline where customers can call and ask any questions about our services. Our customers are very satisfied with the service we provide.

I’m glad our customers are happy with our service. However, I think it’s also important to do something special for customers. My idea is to offer some gifts to longtime users. I will be presenting this idea to my boss tomorrow.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • How important is customer service in your company?
  • What does your company do to provide excellent customer service?
  • How do your customers contact your company?
  • How do they feel about your customer service/support?
  • Are there areas that can be improved?
  • How can your company improve these areas?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about customer service. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • The value of customer service in your company
  • What your company does to provide good customer service
  • Ways that customers contact your company (24/7 hotline, live chat, etc.)
  • How customers feel about the service/support you give them
  • Your opinion on your company’s customer service
  • Areas for improvement in your company’s customer service
  • Your ideas to improve customer service in your company
