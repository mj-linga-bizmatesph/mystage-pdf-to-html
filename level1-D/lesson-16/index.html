TALKING ABOUT OFFICE PROBLEMS
16
SEE
Read the story aloud.

John:

One common complaint is noise here at TC Com. Many employees complain that some workers are too loud. They’re complaining that they lose their focus.

The most serious office problem we have at TC Com is harassment. Some of the new hires complain that they are being bullied by some of their senior colleagues.

Yesterday, I complained to the HR department about a colleague. My colleague likes garlic and spicy foods. He often brings a smelly lunch. I can’t stand strong smells, so I requested to move to a different desk.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • What’s a common complaint in your company?
  • Why do some employees complain about it?
  • What’s a serious office problem in your company?
  • How serious is the problem?
  • Have you ever complained about something at work?
  • Why did you complain about it?
  • Was your problem resolved? If not, how can it be resolved?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about office problems. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • Common office problems in your company
  • Why people make these complaints
  • Serious office problems in your company
  • The severity of the problem
  • Something you complained about in your company
  • A resolution for your problems
