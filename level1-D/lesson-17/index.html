TALKING ABOUT THE INTERNET
SEE
Read the story aloud.

John:

The marketing department at TC Com needs the Internet to function effectively. With the Internet, marketers can see consumer trends.

Marketers analyze data on how users behave on our website and how they respond to online advertisement. This kind of data helps us make quick decisions for the company.

However, some employees use the Internet for personal use. Some check their personal e-mail, while others shop online. I’m very busy during the week, so I shop online at the office for a few minutes. I think it’s no big deal.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • Is the Internet an important part of the job in your department?
  • How does the Internet help your department function effectively?
  • What sort of work-related information do you get from the Internet?
  • How does this information help you in your job?
  • Do some people use the Internet for personal use in your company?
  • How do they use the Internet for personal use?
  • How do you feel about using the company’s Internet for personal use?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about the Internet. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • The necessity for the Internet in your department
  • How the Internet benefits your department
  • The information you search for on the web
  • How this information helps you in your job
  • Personal usage of the Internet in your company
  • How you feel about the personal use of your company’s Internet
