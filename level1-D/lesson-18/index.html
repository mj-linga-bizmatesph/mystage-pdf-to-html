BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK DILESSON 18
TALKING ABOUT OUTSOURCING
18
SEE
Read the story aloud.

John:

At TC Com, outsourcing has become a common part of our business. I think it has helped the business a lot in terms of cost and productivity.

We only had 15 people in IT three years ago, so TC Com started to outsource its IT work to India. Now, IT work is being handled by 100 IT experts.

Last year, TC Com also outsourced its call center to a small European company. However, many customers have complained that the call center operators lack information about TC Com’s services. I think more training should be given.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • Do external organizations play a big role in your company’s business?
  • What are the advantages of outsourcing?
  • What is a type of work that your company outsources? Where was it outsourced to?
  • How has this outsourcing helped?
  • Has your company had any problems regarding outsourcing?
  • What do you think should be done to improve the situation?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about outsourcing. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • How essential outsourcing is to your company’s business
  • The advantages of outsourcing
  • A type of work in your company outsourced to other countries
  • The result of outsourcing this type of work
  • Room for improvement regarding outsourcing
  • Ways to improve an outsourced work in your company
