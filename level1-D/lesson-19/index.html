BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK DILESSON 19
TALKING ABOUT NETWORKING
19
SEE
Read the story aloud.

John:

I only have a small network of contacts. I only know a couple of vendors and a few people outside my company. In the future, I would like to know more people in different industries.

Until recently, I had only interacted with other businesses through e-mail and company visits. My boss told me that one good way to network is to attend trade shows.

So, I attended a telecommunications trade show last week. I visited many booths and exchanged business cards with different people in the same industry. It was nice meeting them all, so I sent a thank you e-mail to all of them. I’ll try to keep in touch with them.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • Do you have a wide network of contacts? What kind of people do you know?
  • What kind of people would you like to meet?
  • How did you meet most of your contacts?
  • How could you meet more people to increase your network?
  • Have you ever attended a tradeshow?
  • How do you feel about meeting new people?
  • Are you good at keeping in touch?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about networking. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • Your current network of contacts
  • The people you’d like to meet and do business with
  • How you met most of your contacts
  • Different ways to expand your network
  • The tradeshows/events you’ve attended
  • How you feel about meeting new people
  • Your ability to keep in touch
