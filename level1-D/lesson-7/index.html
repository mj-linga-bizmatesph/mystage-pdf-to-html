TALKING ABOUT PRESENTATIONS
7
SEE
Read the story aloud.

John:

I like making presentations. I make sure I’m always prepared. First, I think about who the presentation is for. Then, I think about the main message of my presentation.

After that, I think of a few subtopics to support my main message. Finally, I create an outline and think of the details. I sometimes practice my presentation with my colleagues.

I presented a new marketing idea to the executives last week. The presentation lasted 15 minutes. However, the Q&A took over 30 minutes. I felt the presentation went well because the executives were interested in my new idea.

▼▼▼
TRY
To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.
  • How do you feel about making presentations?
  • How do you prepare?
  • Do you sometimes practice your presentation with someone?
  • When was the last time you made a presentation? Who did you make a presentation to?
  • How long did the presentation last? What about the Q&A?
  • Did your presentation go well?
▼▼▼
ACT
Make a short presentation about presentations. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.
  • How you feel about making presentations
  • How you create your presentations
  • How you prepare for presentations
  • The last time you delivered a presentation
  • The result of your last presentation
