TALKING ABOUT FINANCE
4
SEE

Read the story aloud.

Sandy:

I work in the finance department, so my team needs to create the monthly income statement for P-Chem Corp. It’s also called the profit and loss statement or P&L statement.

The P&L statement explains the company’s revenues, costs, and profit or loss. All the managers look at the P&L statement to see the company’s performance.

Last year, our revenues were very good, but costs were very high. So, our profits were small. Two years ago, P-Chem had a loss, but things are getting better. This year, we are trying to cut costs, so I think the company will perform well financially.

▼▼▼
TRY

To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.

  • What does the finance department do in your company?
  • How do you find out about your company’s performance?
  • How was your company’s financial performance last year?
  • Are things getting better at your company financially?
  • How do you think the company will perform financially this year?
  • Why do you think so?
▼▼▼
ACT

Make a short presentation about finance. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.

  • The duties of the finance department in your company
  • Ways you find out about your company’s performance
  • Your company’s financial performance last year
  • Recent trends in financial performance
  • Your forecast for this year
