TALKING ABOUT CHANGE
5
SEE

Read the story aloud.

Sandy:

Recently, there have been many changes in the plastic products industry. There are now many new Chinese competitors. They sell their products at much lower prices than P-Chem, so we need to communicate the value of our products to our customers better.

Another big change is customer needs. Customers have begun to take more notice of design and not just quality. The board of directors are now discussing how P-Chem should adapt to this change.

We will use a new computer system starting next year in the finance department. However, some people are not looking forward to the change. They like the system they are using now. They are afraid that the new system will slow down their work.

▼▼▼
TRY

To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.

  • Are there any recent changes in your industry?
  • What kind of impact will it have on your company’s business?
  • How is your company going to deal with these changes?
  • What big change has happened in your department?
  • Is everyone in your department open to those changes?
  • Why aren’t some of them open to such changes?
▼▼▼
ACT

Make a short presentation about change. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.

  • The recent changes in your industry
  • The impact the change has on your company
  • How your company will adapt to these changes
  • A major change in your department
  • The staff’s reaction toward the big change
  • Reasons why some aren’t receptive to such a change
