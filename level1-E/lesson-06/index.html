BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK EILESSON 6
TALKING ABOUT JOB SKILLS
6
SEE

Read the story aloud.

Sandy:

I would like to hire a new person in the marketing department. The employee should have good computer skills, communication skills, and of course, web marketing skills. By good communication skills, I mean being able to share ideas with the team, understand our product’s value well and communicate it to our customers effectively.

From my experience, good marketers have good people skills. They are interested in people, and like to meet and listen to others. It’s important because they need to know how people think and behave on the Internet. I don’t have great people skills, but I think I’ve become better with people over the past few years.

▼▼▼
TRY

To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.

  • What kind of skills or experience are useful in doing your job?
  • If your team was to hire a new person, what kind of person would you like?
  • What do you think “good computer skills” means?
  • What do you think “good communication skills” means?
  • Do you think people skills are important?
  • Do you have good people skills?
▼▼▼
ACT

Make a short presentation about job skills. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.

  • The qualifications that matter in your job
  • The characteristics you would like to see in a new hire
  • Your idea of “good computer skills”
  • Your idea of “good communication skills”
  • The importance of people skills
  • Your people skills
