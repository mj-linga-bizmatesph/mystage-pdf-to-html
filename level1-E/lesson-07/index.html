BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK EILESSON 7
TALKING ABOUT TRAINING
7
SEE

Read the story aloud.

Sandy:

A new employee started last week. Her name is Jenny. She’s young and doesn’t have much experience in marketing. There are many things I would like to train Jenny on.

First, Jenny will learn the department rules. Then, little by little, I will teach her the basics of marketing. Then, I will ask one of the senior staff members to train her.

In addition, I would like to give training on liberal arts to all of my staff in the marketing department. So, I contacted the HR manager to ask about workshops available for them. My staff members are very excited and signed up for some workshops.

▼▼▼
TRY

To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.

  • When you joined your company, did you already have a lot of experience?
  • What kind of training did you go through when you first started?
  • Who trained you?
  • Do you think your team needs more training?
  • What kind of training do you think your team needs?
  • Is everyone in your team excited about having more training?
▼▼▼
ACT

Make a short presentation about training. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.

  • Your experience at the time of recruitment
  • The training you received at the start of your career
  • Your trainers or mentors
  • The necessity of training in your team
  • How your team feels about receiving more training
