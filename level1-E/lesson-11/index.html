BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK EILESSON 11
TALKING ABOUT BRANDS
11
SEE

Read the story aloud.

Sandy:

P-Chem has a kitchenware brand called P-WARE. P-WARE products include silverware, pans, espresso machines and more.

The brand is known for having a simple and elegant design. Its main target is young families, but it is also popular among young single women in their 30’s.

I think many people like the brand because its products are so durable. P-WARE products can be used for a long time, so they easily become part of people’s lives. I think that’s why P-WARE is so popular.

▼▼▼
TRY

To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.

  • Does your company have a brand? If not, what brand do you like?
  • What do people think of the brand?
  • What kind of market does the brand target?
  • In what markets is it popular?
  • Why do you think people like the brand?
▼▼▼
ACT

Make a short presentation about brands. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.

  • Your company’s brand or your favorite brand
  • How the brand is recognized
  • The brand’s target market
  • The markets the brand appeals to
  • The reason the brand is popular
