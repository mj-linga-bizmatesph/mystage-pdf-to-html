TALKING ABOUT CORPORATE SCANDALS
SEE

Read the story aloud.

Sandy:

There are many kinds of corporate scandals. These can have a negative impact on a company’s brand. Companies must show that they’re doing business legally and ethically.

Many companies have a public relations department. The PR department communicates to the public in a positive way to build the company image.

Our PR department received complaints from a human rights organization. According to them, many workers in the Shenzhen factory do dangerous jobs without safety equipment. The factory manager says it’s not true because everyone is given safety equipment.

▼▼▼
TRY

To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.

  • What sort of corporate scandals are there in your industry?
  • How do corporate scandals affect a company’s image?
  • Does your company have a PR department?
  • What does the PR department do in your company?
  • What corporate scandal have you heard lately?
  • What was the issue about?
  • How did the company react?
▼▼▼
ACT

Make a short presentation about corporate scandals. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.

  • Examples of corporate scandals in your industry
  • The impact of corporate scandals on a company
  • Your company’s PR department
  • A corporate scandal you have heard of recently
  • The issues behind the corporate scandal
  • The company’s reaction to the scandal
