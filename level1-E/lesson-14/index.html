TALKING ABOUT CORPORATE VISION
14
SEE

Read the story aloud.

Sandy:

P-Chem’s corporate vision is simple. We want to give convenience to our customers through plastic products in an eco-friendly society.

Our products can be used over and over, so they are environmentally friendly. P-Chem’s products, such as kitchenware and stationery, make daily life easier for our customers.

Our president often makes announcements to remind employees of the corporate vision. Managers and their staff also need to set goals that support our vision. So I always tell my team that it’s very important to think about the vision.

▼▼▼
TRY

To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.

  • What’s your company’s corporate vision like?
  • Are your products and services in line with this vision?
  • How often are you reminded of the corporate vision?
  • How often do you think about the corporate vision?
  • How do your team goals support the company’s vision?
  • Does your team have enough chances to think about the vision?
▼▼▼
ACT

Make a short presentation about corporate vision. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.

  • The corporate vision of your company
  • How your products and services align with the company’s vision
  • Ways you are reminded of the corporate vision
  • How often you think about the corporate vision
  • How your team’s goals support the corporate vision
  • Opportunities to think about the company’s vision
