BIZMATES PROGRAM LEVEL 1IRANK EILESSON 15
TALKING ABOUT ADVERTISING
15
SEE

Read the story aloud.

Sandy:

P-Chem spends a lot of money on advertising each year. P-Chem uses advertising to build its brand and to attract new customers.

P-Chem’s products are mostly purchased by women, so the company advertises in women’s magazines, on the Internet, and by direct mail.

One of our most effective advertising methods is direct mail. We send pamphlets with coupons to customers. The pamphlet explains the kinds of products P-Chem has. Customers can get a small discount when they use the coupon to buy P-Chem’s products.

▼▼▼
TRY

To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.

  • Does your company spend a lot on advertising?
  • What are the goals of advertising for your company?
  • Who uses your products the most?
  • Where do you advertise to reach these people?
  • What’s your most effective advertising method?
  • Why is it effective?
▼▼▼
ACT

Make a short presentation about advertising. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.

  • The importance of advertising for your company
  • What you use advertising for
  • Your company’s target customers
  • Your company’s advertising channels
  • Your most effective advertising medium
  • The reasons it is effective
