TALKING ABOUT WORK-LIFE BALANCE
19
SEE

Read the story aloud.

Sandy:

P-Chem Corp wants its employees to be happy. The company believes that happy workers will work harder, take fewer sick days, and stay longer with the company.

P-Chem encourages its employees to avoid overtime work unless it’s necessary. This way, employees would have more time for themselves, their families and their friends.

I work hard during the day so that I can finish my work early. I usually go to the gym on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays. On weekends, I usually meet with my friends or just take it easy at home. I really like how P-Chem promotes work-life balance.

▼▼▼
TRY

To prepare for the ACT Part, practice these questions with your trainer.

  • How important is work-life balance in your company?
  • What are the benefits of having happy workers?
  • How does your company promote work-life balance?
  • How do you balance your work life and personal life?
  • What sort of activities do you do after work or on weekends?
  • How do you feel about the work-life balance in your company?
▼▼▼
ACT

Make a short presentation about work-life balance. Keep these points in mind. Then, answer questions from your trainer.

  • The importance of work-life balance in your company
  • The benefits of having happy workers
  • Ways your company promotes work-life balance
  • Your work-life balance
  • The activities you do after work and on weekends
  • How you feel about the work-life balance in your company
