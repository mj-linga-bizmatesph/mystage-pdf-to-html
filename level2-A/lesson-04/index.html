Level 2 - Rank A - Lesson 4: Explaining what your company does

Biz Tip

Don’t just give the name of your company. This is your chance to let them know about your company and to learn about theirs.

Biz Tip

When you explain your company, say:

  • the name of your company
  • the kind of company it is
  • some other details.
See

Example

Wei:

So, where do you work?

Taro:

I work for MMB Corp.

Wei:

Oh, what kind of company is it?

Taro:

It’s a maker.

Wei:

I see.

Bizmates Style

Wei:

So, where do you work?

Taro:

I work for MMB Corp. It’s a manufacturer of electrical wiring. We make electrical wires for major companies. What about you?

Wei:

I work for EnergyTec. It’s an IT service provider. We provide IT solutions to medium-sized companies.

Taro:

That’s interesting.

Try

Explain the following companies by saying the name, kind of company, and some other details.
