Level 2 - Rank A - Lesson 4: Explaining what your company does

Biz Tip

Don’t just give the name of your company. This is your chance to let them know about your company and to learn about theirs.

Biz Tip

When you explain your company, say:

  • the name of your company
  • the kind of company it is
  • some other details.
See

Example

Taro:

Hi. My name’s Sato.

Wei:

Is that your first name?

Taro:

No, it’s my last name.

Wei:

I see. It’s a pleasure to meet you, Mr. Sato.
I’m Wei Chan. Please call me Wei.

Taro:

Nice to meet you. By the way, this is Tanaka.

Wei:

Hi, I’m Wei. It’s nice to meet you, Mr…?

Bizmates Style

Taro:

Hi. My name’s Taro Sato.

Wei:

It’s a pleasure to meet you, Mr. Sato.

Taro:

Please call me Taro.

Wei:

OK, Taro. I’m Wei Chan. You can call me Wei.

Taro:

It’s nice to meet you too, Wei. By the way, I’d like you to meet my boss, Mr. Yoshi Tanaka. He’s our department manager.

Wei:

Hi, I’m Wei. It’s nice to meet you, Mr. Tanaka.

Try

Practice introducing yourself and the following people.

  • Jim Garfield, Sales Manager
  • Sarah Chow, a vendor from Malaysia
  • Kumar Patel, IT Engineer
  • Bob Patterson, Head of legal department
  • Stacey Park, Ph.D.
Act

Introduce yourself and someone important in your office.
