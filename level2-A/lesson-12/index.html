Level 2 - Rank A - Lesson 12: Explaining your company’s products

Biz Tip

When explaining your company’s products, avoid using technical words or give clear explanations when you do.

Biz Tip

Use necessary background information to help explain your product.

See

Example

Wei:

What kinds of products does your company make?

Taro:

Basically, we make connectors and wires for automobiles. Connectors can be grouped into sealed, unsealed, and high frequency connectors. Our wires have different models based on heat resistance.

Wei:

Sorry, I have no idea what you just said.

Taro:

I’m sorry. Our products are very technical..

Bizmates Style

Wei:

What kinds of products does your company make?

Taro:

Basically, we make wires and connectors for automobiles. Do you have a car?

Wei:

Yes, why?

Taro:

Well, your car has many electric wires for the engine, brakes, stereo, navigation system, and so on. All those wires have different functions and specifications. Some need to be thin or strong or flexible. We make all those wires.

Wei:

Wow, I didn’t know that.

Taro:

Also, those wires need to be connected to other electrical parts. So, we make those connectors.

Wei:

So, my car wouldn’t run without your company’s products.

Taro:

That’s right. Many people don’t know about our products, but they are everywhere in our daily lives.

Try

Before you explain your company’s products to a business partner, answer these questions.

  • What background information can you give to help understand your product?
  • Who uses your products/services?
  • What benefits do consumers get from using your products/services?
Act

Explain your company’s products/services to a business partner.
