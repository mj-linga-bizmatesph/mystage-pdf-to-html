Level 2 - Rank A - Lesson 14: Describing your competitors

Biz Tip

When describing your competitors, divide them into understandable groups.

Biz Tip

Explain your company’s competitive advantage by focusing on its strengths.

See

Example

Wei:

Do you have a lot of competitors in the electrical wiring industry?

Taro:

Yes, we have many small and big competitors.

Wei:

So, it’s a tough market, I guess?

Taro:

Yes, we are just a medium-sized company, so it’s very tough.

Wei:

I see.

Bizmates Style

Wei:

Do you have a lot of competitors in the electrical wiring industry?

Taro:

Yes, we have two major types of competitors, major companies that offer total wiring solutions and smaller companies that offer specialized electrical wiring.

Wei:

I see.

Taro:

We’re a medium-sized company, so we compete with both types of competitors. However, we make highly customized wires that major competitors can’t design. Also, the quality of our wires is much higher than our smaller competitors.

Wei:

So, your company is in a strong position.

Taro:

Yes, that’s right.
