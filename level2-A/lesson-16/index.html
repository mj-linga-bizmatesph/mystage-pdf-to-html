Level 2 - Rank A - Lesson 16: Describing your corporate structure

Biz Tip

When explaining corporate structure, start with the objective of the structure.

Biz Tip

Try to focus on the positive features of your company rather than negative ones.

See

Example

Wei:

What type of organizational structure does MMB have?

Taro:

We have a traditional structure for a manufacturing company.

Wei:

And what’s a traditional structure?

Taro:

We have many levels of management.

Wei:

So, does it take time for decisions to be made?

Taro:

Yes, it’s a slow process.

Bizmates Style

Wei:

What type of organizational structure does MMB have?

Taro:

Well, in our business, quality and consistency are most important. So, we have many levels of management under the CEO and COO. But the Head of Production has a lot of authority too.

Wei:

So, decisions on quality control can be made faster?

Taro:

That’s right. So, our factory managers can speak directly to the Head of Production.

Wei:

I see. That’s a very efficient corporate structure.

Taro:

Thank you.

Try

Before you explain your corporate structure to a business partner, answer these questions.

  • What are some important factors for your company’s business?
  • What are the main objectives of your corporate structure?
  • Does your company have a flat structure or a hierarchical structure?
  • How are decisions made in your company?
Act

Explain your company’s corporate structure to a business partner.
