Level 2 - Rank A - Lesson 18: Describing your CSR activities

Biz Tip

Being environmentally friendly is something every company says. Be specific to show your company really cares.

Biz Tip

CSR covers a wide range of areas including, the environment, human and animal rights, diversity, education, health, developing countries, etc.

See

Example

Wei:

What sort of corporate social responsibility activities does MMB do?

Taro:

We try to be environmentally friendly.

Wei:

That’s good.

Taro:

I think we also offer scholarships to support engineering students in rural areas.

Wei:

So, your company cares about education too.

Taro:

Yes, we do.

Bizmates Style

Wei:

What sort of corporate social responsibility activities does MMB do?

Taro:

Well, we are a manufacturing company, so we do everything we can in our production process to be environmentally friendly. So, we take great care from the selection of vendors, the materials, to our production process, packaging, and even delivery.

Wei:

Wow, MMB takes environmental conservation very seriously.

Taro:

Yes, we do. We also believe in supporting the engineering industry. So, we offer scholarships to talented engineering students who can’t afford to go to university.

Wei:

I’m really impressed with your company’s efforts to give back to society.

Try

Before you explain your company’s CSR activities to a business partner answer these questions.

  • What does your company do to protect the environment?
  • What does your company do to support diversity in the workplace?
  • What does your company do to support education?
  • What events/organizations does your company sponsor?
Act

Explain your company’s CSR activities to a business partner.
