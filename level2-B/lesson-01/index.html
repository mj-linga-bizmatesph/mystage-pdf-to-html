Level 2 - Rank B - Lesson 1: Greeting your colleagues
See

Biz Tip

Rather than the standard, “How are you?” greeting, it’s more natural to call the person by their first name.

Biz Tip

It’s natural to make small talk after greeting someone.

Example

Taro:

How are you?

Li:

I’m fine, thank you. And you?

Taro:

I’m fine, thanks.

Li:

So how was your weekend, Taro?

Taro:

I just stayed at home.

Bizmates Style

Taro:

Hi Li. How’s it going?

Li:

Pretty good, thanks. How about you, Taro?

Taro:

Not too bad. Did you have a nice weekend?

Li:

I did, thanks. We had nice weather on Saturday, so I just read a book at a park. What about you?

Taro:

Nothing special. Just stayed home and watched the big game on TV. Did you see the game?

Li:

Goodness, I totally forgot about it! So, what happened?

Taro:

The home team won by a landslide! The other team didn’t stand a chance. At first, it looked as if…

Try

Practice greeting your colleague and start small talk using some of the following topics:

  • Weather
  • A sports game
  • Recent news
  • A new movie
  • Holiday
Act

Greet your colleague and think of other topics to start small talk.
