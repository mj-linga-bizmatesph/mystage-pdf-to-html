Level 2 - Rank B - Lesson 4: Describing your co-workers

Biz Tip

To ask about someone’s physical appearance, we say “ What does he/she look like?”. To ask about someone’s personality, we say, “ What’s he/she like?”.

Biz Tip

Give clear descriptions with several pieces of information:

  • location of desk
  • physical appearance
  • clothing
  • personality

Example

Li:

Who should I speak to about getting a new PC?

Taro:

You can talk to Mr. Tanaka.

Li:

Where is he?

Taro:

He’s in IT.

Li:

What’s he like?

Taro:

He’s tall with short black hair.

Li:

No, I mean, is he a nice guy?

Taro:

Oh, yeah. He’s very helpful.

Li:

OK, I’ll try to find him.

Bizmates Style

Li:

Who should I speak to about getting a new PC?

Taro:

You can talk to Mr. Tanaka in IT.

Li:

Where is he?

Taro:

He sits in the back. His desk is the third one from the left. He’s the tall guy with short black hair with the blue tie.

Li:

I see him. What’s he like?

Taro:

He’s a really nice guy, but his English isn’t very good. So, just make sure you speak slowly.

Try

Practice describing the following people.

2. Ken Cheng tall with little hair, black suit in this row, fifth desk from the left easy to speak to
