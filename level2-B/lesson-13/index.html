Level 2 - Rank B - Lesson 13: Recommending an idea

Biz Tip

To get your idea accepted by others, how you say it can be just as important as the idea.

Biz Tip

When you recommend an idea, follow three steps:

  • Clearly state the root cause of the problem.
  • Recommend your idea.
  • And explain the possible results.

See

Example

Li:

This sales campaign is a big problem. What are we going to do?

Taro:

We should change the message of our ads.

Li:

Why do you say that?

Taro:

The message is too general.

Bizmates Style

Li:

This sales campaign is a big problem. What are we going to do?

Taro:

Well, the problem is that our ads are not reaching our target customers.

Li:

So, we need to change our advertising medium?

Taro:

I don’t think that’s the problem. I see the root cause in the message of our ads. It’s too general. It needs to be more specific to the industry of our target.

Li:

I see.

Taro:

My suggestion is to create three industry-specific ads which target our main customers, I’m confident we can see at least a 10% increase in inquiries from each sector. This would result in achieving our target of 100 new inquiries.

Li:

That’s a great idea, Taro.

Try

Practice stating the problem, recommending your idea, and explaining the possible results with the following problems.

  • You have too much work to do.
  • Sales volume is down.
  • Think of a problem at your office
Act

State a problem, recommend an idea, and explain the possible results to your colleague.
