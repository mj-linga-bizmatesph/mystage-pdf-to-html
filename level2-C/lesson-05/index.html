Level 2 - Rank C - Lesson 5: Going over the agenda

Biz Tip

At the start of a meeting, go over the topics you’d like to talk about and how much time you have.

Biz Tip

After going over the agenda, it’s polite to ask the guest if the agenda is OK.

See

Example

Taro:

OK, so please tell me about your products.

Luke:

Oh, OK. We have over 30 different models. Is there a particular one you are interested in?

Taro:

Ah, sorry. I don’t know any of the models.

Luke:

OK, ah…, how much time do we have?

Taro:

I only booked this room for 30 minutes.

Luke:

Oh OK, I’ll talk fast.

Bizmates Style

Taro:

First of all, thank you for coming to our office today. We only have this room for 30 minutes, so could I start by going over our specific requests? And then you can recommend a model, and then we can discuss pricing. Is that OK?

Luke:

Yes, that sounds good.

Taro:

Is there anything you’d like to add?

Luke:

Ah, no I think you covered everything.

Taro:

Great

Try

Before going over the agenda with your business partner, practice going over the agenda with these topics.

  • Explain background information, come up with ideas, make a decision.
  • Bring up issues, discuss challenges, set action plans.
  • Talk about last month’s sales, talk about this month’s forecasts, create a new sales strategy.
Act

Go over a typical agenda in your meeting with your business partner.
