Level 2 - Rank D - Lesson 16: Setting goals

Biz Tip

Always be prepared for your meetings with your boss.

Biz Tip

When setting goals, remember the word “SMART”

  • Specific
  • Measurable
  • Attainable
  • Relevant
  • Time-bound
See

Example

(Taro is having his mid-year evaluation with his boss.)

Don:

OK, so let’s create some goals for the 2nd half of this year.

Taro:

OK, I will continue working hard to increase sales.

Don:

Good, but you need to be a little more specific.

Taro:

OK, I’ll increase it by 10%.

Don:

Why 10%?

Taro:

I think it’s reasonable.

Don:

OK, how are you going to do that?

Taro:

Ah …, I’ll visit more customers and …

Bizmates Style

(Taro is having his mid-year evaluation with his boss.)

Don:

OK, so let’s create some goals for the 2nd half of this year.

Taro:

OK, I’ve given this a lot of thought. I know I was concerned about my targets two months ago, but thanks to the HT Motors contract, I’m only 9% behind. I’d like to keep my sales target the same.

Don:

Are you sure?

Taro:

Yes. But I need to increase new contracts by 15%. And I plan to do this by getting leads from Taka and Rio. They said they were too busy to visit some prospective clients, so I will get those from them. I also have a list of potential clients I can approach.

Don:

Sounds good.

Try

Practice using the “SMART” approach on these goals:

  • To lose weight.
  • To save money.
  • To maintain a work-life balance.
Act

Set some goals with your boss.
