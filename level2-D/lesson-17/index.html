Level 2 - Rank D - Lesson 17: Showing commitment

Biz Tip

Impress your boss by acknowledging your areas of weakness and committing to improving them.

Biz Tip

Show commitment by reassuring your boss that your goals are in line with the departmental goals.

See

Example

Don:

OK, so we have your goals for the year. Are there any areas you’d like to improve?

Taro:

No, I’m OK.

Don:

Do you have any other comments?

Taro:

No, that’s all.

Bizmates Style

Don:

OK, so we have your goals for the year. Are there any areas you’d like to improve?

Taro:

Yes. I know that I need to work on my skills to create better proposals. So, I’ve asked Hiro to help me with that. I’ll do my best to improve in this area.

Don:

I’m glad to see that you are taking the initiative. Do you have any other comments?

Taro:

I just want to take this opportunity to say that I will do the best I can to achieve our team’s targets.

Don:

Thank you for your commitment, Taro.

Try

Before showing commitment to your boss, answer these questions.

  • Are there any particular skills that you’d like to improve for your job?
  • What can you do to improve in those areas?
  • What are your departmental goals?
Act

Show commitment to your boss by acknowledging your areas of weaknesses and committing to achieving your departmental goals.
