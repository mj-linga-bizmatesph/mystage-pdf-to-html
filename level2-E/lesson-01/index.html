Level 2 - Rank E - Lesson 1: Introducing an employee

Biz Tip

When introducing someone to your team, it’s courteous to give some background information about the person.

Biz Tip

Help the new person get acquainted by being clear about the person’s goals and motives to your team.

See

Example

Taro:

Hi, everyone. Please be quiet. We have a new sales rep joining us from our Shanghai office, David Chang.

(Everyone applauds.)

David:

Thank you, Taro. I’m very excited about this opportunity to work with you. Let me briefly introduce myself…

Bizmates Style

Taro:

Hi, everyone. I have an announcement to make. We have a sales rep joining us from our Shanghai office, David Chang. David has been with MMB China for 3 years as a sales rep. He will be working here in Tokyo on a 1-year assignment to better understand our company culture and operations so that he can share these with his team in Shanghai. So, please give David a warm welcome and help him learn our processes and procedures.

(Everyone applauds.)

David:

Thank you, Taro. I’m very excited about this opportunity to work with you. Let me briefly introduce myself…

Try

Before introducing an employee to your staff, answer these questions.

  • Have you ever had a guest transfer to your department?
  • Who might be transferred to your department in the future?
  • What would you say about that person when introducing him/her?
Act

Make a short announcement to introduce a transfer employee to your department.
