Level 2 - Rank E - Lesson 2: Going over office rules
See

Biz Tip

Try to avoid saying, “You can’t …” “You have to …” “You mustn’t …”, instead, you can say, “… is not permitted.” “We’d like all employees to …”

Biz Tip

Although all rules are important, it’s helpful to highlight the rules that are particularly important in your company.

Example

Taro:

So, David, you have to understand some office rules.

David:

OK.

Taro:

First, you have to be at the office from 10AM to 4PM.

David:

OK, I understand.

Taro:

We have cool biz now, so you don’t have to wear a tie, but starting next month, you must wear a tie.

David:

Sure. By the way, where is the smoking area?

Taro:

Oh, you can’t smoke in the office. There is a smoking area outside.

David:

Sure, that’s no problem.

Bizmates Style

Taro:

So, David, let’s go over some office rules first.

David:

Yes, that would be great.

Taro:

Our core working time is from 10AM to 4PM. This rule is strongly enforced, so please be very careful with this one.

David:

OK, I understand.

Taro:

We have cool biz now, so there’s no need to wear a tie, but from next month, a tie will be required.

David:

By the way, where is the smoking area?

Taro:

There is a smoking area outside. I’ll take you there after our meeting.

David:

Great.

Try

Before going over office rules with your trainee, answer these questions.

  • What are your company’s working hours?
  • Where is the smoking area in your company?
  • What is the dress code in your office?
  • What other general rules does your office have?
Act

Go over the office rules with your trainee.
