Level 2 - Rank E - Lesson 9: Congratulating someone
See

Biz Tip

Congratulate your staff in front of others rather than in private as it will be more meaningful to them.

Biz Tip

When congratulating someone, show that you understand the effort that he/she put into the work.

Example

Taro:

David, could I talk to you in private for a minute?

David:

Sure.

Taro:

Good job on the survey.

David:

Thanks.

Taro:

I saw the results of the survey and your recommendations. Keep up the good work.

David:

Thanks.

Try

Before congratulating your trainee, answer these questions.

  • Have you ever been openly congratulated by your boss?
  • What sort of work in your job receives congratulations?
Act

Congratulate your trainee for successfully completing an assignment.
