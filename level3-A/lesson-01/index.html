Biz Tip

The first step to developing time management skills is to understand how effectively you manage your time.

Yasu :

Frank, I’ve just been assigned as Project Manager. However, we have a really tight schedule. Do you have any tools I can use to manage my time effectively?

Frank :

Sure, but before you use any tools, you need to develop your time management skills.

Yasu :

What do you mean?

Frank :

Well, time management is a skill. It’s how effectively you can manage your time to get work done. It means focusing on getting results rather than just managing tasks.

Yasu :

And how can I develop this skill?

Frank :

Well, I suggest you keep an activity log to understand how you manage your time. It’s like a record of how much time you spend on each task.

Yasu :

You mean like a timesheet?

Frank :

A timesheet is for the company. An activity log is for yourself. Keep a record of what you do, when you do it, how much time you spend on it, how you feel when you do it, and the value of the task. That’ll really show you how you manage your time.

Yasu :

OK, I’ll try that. Thanks.

Try

Answer these questions.

  • How would you rate your time management skill?
  • What do you think is the benefit of focusing more on getting results?
  • Do you always keep an activity log?
  • How else do you maximize your time at work?
