Level 3 - Rank A - Lesson 15: Respecting your elders

Biz Tip

Some people would have difficulty understanding how being open and honest could be seen as disrespectful. So, show patience when explaining this.

Biz Tip

It also helps to let the other person know that there is a separate time and place to express their opinions.

See

Example

Makoto:

Frank, before our meeting with the managers today, let me go over some meeting customs in Japan.

Frank:

OK, I was just going to be open and honest.

Makoto:

Ah, no, please don’t be open and honest. You shouldn’t disagree with people who are older or of higher authority than you.

Frank:

What? Then, what’s the purpose of this meeting?

Makoto:

Well, that’s the Japanese way.

Bizmates style

Makoto:

Frank, before our meeting with the managers today, let me go over some meeting customs in Japan.

Frank:

OK, I was just going to be open and honest.

Makoto:

Well, in Japan we place high importance on respect for our elders. And in this meeting, we’re going to have several senior managers. They will most likely refrain from giving their opinions, but they may make some comments on our ideas. To save face, it’s polite to show agreement, even if we may think that their comments are unjust. We can always express our grievances in private.

Frank:

OK, I’ll show them respect.

Makoto:

Yes, so the purpose of this meeting is to present our ideas, not to convince them. There’s a separate time for that.

Frank:

I’m glad you’re telling me this.

Try

Answer these questions.

  • What was it that Frank could not understand in the first dialogue?
  • In the second dialogue, what did Makoto say about the meeting before explaining what to do and what not to do?
  • In the second dialogue, what did Makoto say that they could do in private?
  • Is this the business custom in your company as well?
Act

Role play

Explain the importance of respecting your elders in meetings. Then, explain other customs that show respect to elders such as how to bow and how to address your elders in the business.
