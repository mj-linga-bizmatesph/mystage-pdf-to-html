Level 3 - Rank B - Lesson 1: Understanding problem solving

Biz Tip

Solving a problem isn’t just about finding a way out of it, but looking for a permanent solution.

Biz Tip

A problem-solving tool called Simplex Process, developed by Min Basadur, uses an 8-stage cycle, which is more effective than the usual linear process.

Example

Jerry:

So Tomo, how’s your new position as manager?

Tomo:

Good, I guess. It’s been a month since I got promoted to the position, but our team performance hasn’t improved.

Jerry:

What’s the problem?

Tomo:

The problem is that there are no problems. Everything is going smoothly but we’re not getting the results we want.

Jerry:

Well, I want to teach something that’ll change your perception on what problem solving really is.

Tomo:

What do you mean?

Jerry:

Tomo:

I see. So it’s an 8-stage cycle.

Jerry:

Tomo:

I understand. So this approach opens more room for fine-tuning any implemented solution for problems in the future, correct?

Jerry:
