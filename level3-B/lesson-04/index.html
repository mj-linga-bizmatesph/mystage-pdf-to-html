Level 3 - Rank B - Lesson 4: Defining the problem

Biz Tip

When dealing with complex issues, use the Drill Down technique in order to break the problem down into smaller parts.

Biz Tip

Another effective method is to use 5 Whys. Asking several “why?” questions one after the other can help you find the root cause of the problem.
