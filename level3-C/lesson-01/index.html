Level 3 - Rank C - Lesson 1: Reading numbers

Biz Tip

Numbers are read differently depending on what they are used for.
  • Big numbers: million, billion, trillion, etc.
  • Ordinals: 1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.
  • Fractions: ½ = one half,
    ¼ = a quarter or one fourth
See

Frank:

I need to get some information about MMB for my presentation. Do you happen to know in what year MMB was founded?

Makoto:

Yeah, in one thousand nine hundred and fifty two.

Frank:

Do you mean in nineteen fifty-two (1952)?

Makoto:

Yes, that’s right.

Frank:

Do you know the breakdown of male and female employees?

Makoto:

I think our female employees are two parts of five.

Frank:

I think you mean two fifths (2/5). You mean 40% right?

Makoto:

Yes, 40%.

Frank:

And what was our sales amount last year?

Makoto:

Ah… It’s a very big number. I’m sorry but I can’t read it.

Frank:

Maybe we need to practice reading numbers.
