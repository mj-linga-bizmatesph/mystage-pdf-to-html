Biz Tip

Keep your charts and graphs simple with just the key information in it. By selecting the most appropriate graph or chart, you can highlight what’s important.

Makoto:

Frank, I’m having trouble finding the right graph to present these figures.

Frank:

What graph are you thinking of using?

Makoto:

I’m thinking of using a round chart.

Frank:

Do you mean a pie chart?

Makoto:

That’s right, a pie chart.

Frank:

Well, I would use a bar graph.

Makoto:

A bar graph is too simple. I wouldn’t be able to put all the information in it.

Frank:

A horizontal bar graph would give you more space.

Makoto:

What’s that?

Frank:

It’s a bar graph with the bars going horizontally instead of vertically. Maybe we need to learn the different names of graphs and charts.
