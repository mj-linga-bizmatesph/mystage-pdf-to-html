Biz Tip

As some people may be unfamiliar reading charts and graphs, give a simple introduction of the chart before you go into the figures.

Here, you can see a comparison of sales volumes between products A and B.

The horizontal axis shows the first quarter to the fourth quarter.

The vertical axis shows sales in millions of dollars.

The blue line represents product A and the green line represents product B.
Try

Use these expressions to help you introduce the charts and graphs below.

  • Let me draw your attention to …
  • This graph describes …
  • The ____ is shown …
  • The ____ can be seen …
  • The solid line represents …
  • The broken line shows …
  • The dotted line is …
