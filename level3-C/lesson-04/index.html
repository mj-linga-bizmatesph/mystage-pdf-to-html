Level 3 - Rank C - Lesson 4: Giving a brief overview

Biz Tip

Rather than going right into the details, start with an overview of what the chart is telling us. Then, go into the finer details.

See

Last year we achieved fairly good results with product A, peaking in the third quarter but falling in the fourth.

Product B started slow but moved steadily hitting its highest point in the third quarter, but losing ground in the fourth.

Try

Use these expressions to help you give an overview of the charts and graphs below.

  • Over the last three years, …
  • In these three years …
  • I’m pleased to say that …
  • I’m proud to say that …
  • An obvious trend that …
  • As you all know, we’ve seen …
