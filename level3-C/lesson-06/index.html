Biz Tip

Simply explaining movements in a chart has little value. Give reasons for or make comments on these movements.

The steep rise of Product A in Q3 is due to the success of our sales campaign.

On the other hand, the drop in Q4 was the result of production problems at the Thai factory.

The fall of Product B in Q4 can be attributed to the sudden rise of the yen.
