Level 3 - Rank C - Lesson 7: Giving meaning to numbers

Biz Tip

Just the number alone may not mean much to people. Try to give meaning to the numbers by explaining it from a different perspective.

See

Product A and B peaked at a total of 70 million dollars in Q3.

With 2000 employees at MMB, that means each of us brought in roughly 35,000 dollars in sales in the third quarter. Now that’s an achievement!

So, we sold 68 billion sets of wires last year. That’s enough to give 10 sets of wires to every person on this planet.

Try

Use the expressions below to give meaning to the following figures.

  • That means …
  • That’s equivalent to …
  • That’s like saying …
  • That’s the same as ….
    •
  • We sold 6.5 million cars last year.
  • The yen depreciated from 76 to 83 to the US dollar in three months.
  • We will transfer 200 employees to offices overseas this year.
  • 28.9 million dollars in sales came from China.
