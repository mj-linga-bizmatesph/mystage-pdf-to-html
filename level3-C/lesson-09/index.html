Level 3 - Rank C - Lesson 9: Drawing conclusions

Biz Tip

The most important thing about a presentation on figures is your recommendation or conclusion. What is your point?

See

So, these are the sales figures for product A and B.

We must focus our attention on regaining the trust of our clients.

What I suggest is that each of you visit your clients and carefully explain the details of the production problems we suffered, highlight the actions we’ve taken to resolve the issue, and show commitment to exceeding their expectations.

Try

Analyze the charts below. Look for trends and changes in the charts and try to come up with reasons that might have affected the figures. Then, come up with concluding statements and recommendations.

  • We must focus our attention on ...
  • The only choice we have is to …
  • What we need to do now is …

  • My suggestion is …
  • I strongly urge you to …
  • I recommend that we …
Act

Role play
