Level 3 - Rank E - Lesson 5: Making analogies

Biz Tip

Analogies can be an effective tool to explain complex ideas or to help the listener visualize your idea.

Biz Tip

When you make your analogy,

  • Present your idea
  • Make an analogy to explain the basic concept
  • Give details of your idea
See

Example

Yasu:

My proposal is a systematic customization model.

Our clients can select the features they want in their wiring products over the internet.

Let me now explain each part of the model…

Bizmates style

Yasu:

Imagine a systematic customization model which allows our clients to pick and choose the features they want in every one of their wiring products over the internet.

It’s like being able to create your own pizza instead of choosing from a set menu.

The client can select the materials, the color, and a number of other features without having to make an appointment with a sales representative days in advance.
