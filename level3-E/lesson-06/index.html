Level 3 - Rank E - Lesson 6: Supporting with data and examples
See

Biz Tip

When using data to support your idea, don’t just show the figures – give meaning to the figures.

Biz Tip

To support your statement, you can use:

  • Examples
  • Figures
  • Opinion from an expert

Example

Yasu:

Customization is a trend that we see everywhere in the consumer business. As our clients offer more flexibility in their products, they need their suppliers to do the same. We already receive many requests for customized products from our clients. And each year, that figure is growing. However, our production team cannot handle this new demand. We need to increase our resources and implement new processes.

Bizmates style

Yasu:

Customization is a trend that we see everywhere in the consumer business. For example: customized travel plans, customized car interiors, customized internet services, etc. Here, I have a chart that shows the number of requests for customized products from our top three clients over the last three years. We received 300 requests in 2010, 450 requests in 2011, and over 850 in 2012. What do these figures tell us? They show that customization is growing and it’s growing fast.

Try

Answer these questions.

  • What examples did Yasu use to support his ideas in the second speech?
  • What data did Yasu use to support his ideas in the second speech?
  • What kind of data do you use in your job?
Act

Role play

If you were making a proposal to a colleague, your boss, or an executive, what data or examples would you use to support your ideas?
