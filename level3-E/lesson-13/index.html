Level 3 - Rank E - Lesson 13: Showing dedication

Biz Tip

For followers to see that their manager is a true leader, the leader needs to show complete dedication to the vision he or she has committed to.

Biz Tip

To show dedication, the leader needs to be willing to spend necessary resources to progress towards the vision.

See

Example

Lin:

Could you tell me how much we have in our team budget?

Yasu:

Why do you need to know?

Lin:

I was considering purchasing some items.

Yasu:

Well, the budget is confidential information.
It’s only for managers.

Lin:

OK, how much can I spend?

Yasu:

Well, I need to know what you want to purchase, why you want to purchase it, and how much it’s going to cost.

Lin:

Well, I’d like to get a whiteboard to be used as a message board to improve communication within our team.

Yasu:

Could you give me a report with that information, and I will think about it?

Bizmates style

Lin:

Could you tell me how much we have in our team budget?

Yasu:

I’ve set aside a budget for miscellaneous purchases. Are you planning on purchasing something?

Lin:

I was thinking of getting a whiteboard to be used as a message board to improve communication within our team.

Yasu:

That’s an excellent idea. That’s in line with our vision to willingly share information. Good job.

Lin:

Thanks.

Yasu:

But before you make the purchase, could you share this idea with the team to get their input? And once you’ve decided what to purchase, could you do a simple price analysis first? Let’s try to find the best deal so that we can use the budget for other ideas, too.

Lin:

No problem. I’ll get started right away.

Try

Answer these questions.

  • If you were Lin, how would you feel in the first dialogue with Yasu? How about in the second dialogue?
  • How did Yasu fail to show dedication to the vision in the first dialogue?
  • How did Yasu show dedication to the vision in the second dialogue?
  • How would your boss have reacted to Lin?
Act

Role play

Respond to a request for a purchase from your staff.
