Level 3 - Rank E - Lesson 14: Showing humility

Biz Tip

Leaders show humility by giving credit where credit is due.

Biz Tip

Leaders get joy from seeing their members succeed and develop.

See

Example

Yasu:

Lin, could I speak to you for a minute?

Lin:

Sure, is everything OK?

Yasu:

Oh, yes. One of the senior managers asked me about our new message board. He is thinking of doing something similar in his department.

Lin:

Oh, great. Do you want me to explain the system to him?

Yasu:

No, that’s not necessary. I’ll do that. Could you just make a simple report on how it’s used, the merits of the system, and how much it costs?

Lin:

OK.

Bizmates style

Yasu:

Lin, I have some great news.

Lin:

Really? What is it?

Yasu:

How would you like to meet with one of the senior managers?

Lin:

It depends on the reason.

Yasu:

Don’t worry, it’s good news. One of the senior managers asked me about your new message board. He is thinking of doing something similar in his department.

Lin:

Oh, great. Do you want me to explain the system to him?

Yasu:

Yes, I do. It was your idea. You know the system better than anyone.

Lin:

OK, I’ll put something together right away.

Yasu:

I think he’ll want to know how it’s used, the merits of the system, and the budget, too.

Lin:

OK, I’ll put those in.

Try

Answer these questions.

  • If you were Lin, how would you feel in the first dialogue with Yasu? How about in the second dialogue?
  • How did Yasu fail to show humility in the first dialogue?
  • How did Yasu show humility in the second dialogue?
  • Have you experienced a situation like this?
Act

Role play

Imagine that you were in a similar situation as the manager. How would you handle it?
