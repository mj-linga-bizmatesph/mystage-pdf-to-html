Level 3 - Rank E - Lesson 17: Communicating

Biz Tip

Leaders know that what and how they communicate can strengthen or weaken their relationship with their staff.

Biz Tip

People remember almost nothing from an e-mail, very little from someone else’s presentation, and almost everything from their own presentation. So, give your people a chance to present.
See

Example

Yasu:

All right, everyone. OK let’s start our weekly meeting. I hope you all remember our vision. Please keep that in mind at all times.

As part of our vision is to share information, let me share some news. AutoTech merged with SPT Company so they may change suppliers in the near future.

As for sales, we are in third place now. We are behind by 2.3% from first place.

Bizmates style

Yasu:

All right, everyone. Let’s have our weekly meeting.
Jack, could you tell me what our vision is?

Jack:

To create a tight-knit team that willingly shares information and willingly offers support to our members so that we can all surpass our targets and be recognized and rewarded for our performance.

Yasu:

Excellent. So, in terms of information sharing, how is that going, Lin?

Lin:

It’s going great. Here’s some key information that we’ve gathered over the past two weeks. One interesting news is the merger between AutoTech and SPT Company. They may be looking to change suppliers in the near future. You can review the rest in your own time.

Yasu:

In terms of sales performance, how are we doing, Sang?

Sang:

According to the ranking, we are in third place. We are behind by only 1.4% from second place and 2.3% from the top.

Yasu:

OK, everyone. I want you to think about what you can do as individuals to improve sales. Then, we’ll look at what we can do as a team.

Try

Answer these questions.

  • If you were a member of Yasu’s team, how would you feel in the first meeting? How about in the second meeting?
  • How did Yasu’s communication style differ in the two meetings?
  • What type of communication style does your boss use?
Act

Role play

Imagine that you were Yasu holding a vision meeting. What communication style would you use?
