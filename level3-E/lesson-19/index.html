Level 3 - Rank E - Lesson 19: Giving advice on leadership

Biz Tip

Your leadership skills are measured by the number of followers you have, not by the number of staff reporting to you.

Biz Tip

Leadership is a skill that is not only reserved for managers. It’s essential for people in all levels of an organization.

See

Willy:

Yasu, congratulations on your team winning the top prize on the sales ranking.

Yasu:

Thanks Willy, but it wasn’t me. It was due to the hard work and collaboration of my team. I was really lucky to have such dedicated people.

Willy:

I wish I could have more leadership skills like you.

Yasu:

Well, I’m not an expert yet. But you can become a great leader. Leadership is a skill that you need to learn and practice, just like any other communication skill.

Willy:

Yeah, but I’m not charismatic like you.

Yasu:

I used to think that leadership is about charisma and confidence. But I’ve realized there’s a lot more to it than that. It’s about integrity, taking responsibility, showing dedication to your vision, and many more qualities.

Willy:

Well, I’m not going to be a manager for at least a few years, so I guess I won’t have to worry about that.

Yasu:

The most important thing that I’ve learned is that leadership isn’t just for managers. It’s something that every employee should have.

Willy:

In that case, tell me more.

Try

Answer these questions.

  • What was Willy’s initial understanding of leadership?
  • What was Yasu’s definition of leadership?
  • What was the most important thing that Yasu learned about leadership?
  • What are some other tips that Yasu could give Willy about leadership?
Act

Role play

Imagine that a colleague asks you about how to be a good leader. Give your colleague some advice.
