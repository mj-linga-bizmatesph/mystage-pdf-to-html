Working in a dynamic environment with diverse cultures can be an enriching experience as perspectives different from our own make for countless “I never thought of that” moments. However, cultural differences can also cause frustration, confusion, and stress over the simplest of matters.
Imagine working in a German company and reporting to an American boss while negotiating with Chinese clients.In an ideal setting , you would enjoy the clarity of the rules in your German company, the openness of your American boss, and the respectfulness of your Chinese clients. However, a lack of cultural awareness and adaptability may result in you unknowingly violating company policies, disappointing your boss, or offending your customers.
Though there is no one best way to approach the matter, understanding the values of different cultures as well as your own will ultimately put you in a better position to manage these differences, handle any disparities, and become truly successful in global business.
