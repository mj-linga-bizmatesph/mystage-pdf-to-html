THE NEW BOSS
16
WORDS & PHRASES
floundering – in serious difficulty, struggling
beat around the bush – to avoid coming to the point or being direct
draconian – excessively strict
at a loss – confused about what to say, think, or do
SEE

Read and analyze the story.

Hiroshi had been headhunted by a medium-sized real estate company to turn their floundering sales department around. The previous sales director had been way too easy on the team and Hiroshi felt a total change was needed. Although performance was improving, his tactics made many uncomfortable.

Hiroshi

In order to make big improvements, we need to make big changes. We can no longer beat around the bush. If you’re not helping or improving, you’re hurting the team. Yes, my style has forced some people to leave, but that’s the point. We need strong and capable people. For the first time in many months, numbers are going up. This is what I do.

Sarah

As head of HR, I’m responsible for the welfare of our employees. Many have come to me complaining about Hiroshi’s draconian policies and some have even gone so far as to call it power harassment. I don’t care what his sales targets are; we don’t support yelling, banging on tables, and embarrassing and ranking people in front of others. This must stop.

Sarah confronted Hiroshi about his management style and warned him to change his methods or risk severe consequences. Hiroshi was shocked and couldn’t believe what he was hearing. He had been hired to realign the Sales Department, not to maintain it. Now Hiroshi is at a loss for what to do.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • How would you define Hiroshi’s management style? (Goal-/ Process-oriented)
  • Would you consider Hiroshi’s approach successful? Why or why not?
  • What do you think is the right amount of pressure to give staff?
  • How do people typically respond to pressure in your company or department?
▼▼▼
ACT

Role play.

Imagine you are Hiroshi’s boss. What would you say? Would you support his methods or would you advise him to adjust his style?
