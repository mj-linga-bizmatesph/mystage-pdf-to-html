WORDS &
PHRASES
rub (someone) the wrong way – to anger, frustrate, or bother (someone)
grant – to allow or permit
have (someone’s) ear – to have access to (someone) and be able to influence them
rock the boat – to cause trouble by changing something that other people don’t want to change
SEE

Read and analyze the story.

Carla is the new office manager at DataSys. Her experience, charisma, and close relationship with the founder landed her the position as well as the support of the president himself. Upon starting, Carla began proposing and implementing bold new office policies, resulting in a jarring transition that rubbed many staff the wrong way.

Carla

My experience in call centers has granted me unique insights into things like privacy and efficiency. So, as manager, I decided to make changes. All personal items, including phones and bags, are to be kept on a separate floor to prevent information leakage. I’ll also start recording how much time everyone spends on their PCs and what they’re doing.

Ross

I’ve been at DataSys for decades and this is the first time that anyone has said privacy and efficiency is a problem. I know Carla is eager to impress and she has the ear of the president, but she should be more respectful to her seniors. We’re proud of our work. Carla might be our “boss,” but she needs to prove herself before we can trust her.

Although the staff were initially eager to have a fresh face in the office, their excitement soon turned to frustration as Carla began imposing more and more of her ideas on the staff. Unaware of the situation and emboldened by her title and the president’s favor, Carla continued to rock the boat.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • Based on how Carla landed the job, how would you describe the position? (Closed/Open)
  • What kind of knowledge does Carla bring to DataSys? (Esoteric/general?) How does this impact the way she approached her new position?
  • If you were one of Carla’s staff, how would you react to her style and these new policies? Why?
  • What has your experience with new managers been?

ACT

Role play.

You are the President of DataSys. Although you really like Carla and believe she has good potential, her feedback from the staff hasn’t been good. Talk to Carla and give her some advice.
