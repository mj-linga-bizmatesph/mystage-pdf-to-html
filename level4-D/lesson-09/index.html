When it comes to leadership, men and women have been known to take different approaches. Men tend to take a more aggressive, top-down approach, whereas women are more likely to focus on relationship building. Whatever the situation, all good leaders must know how to inspire greatness.
Joanne (40) - Australian – School Principal: What is your approach to effective leadership?
 I lead first by listening. To me, effective leadership means making the best decisions given the circumstances. The only way to know what is happening is by getting the full picture from the people around you. You have to trust them in order for them to trust you. Leadership is a team game.
Steven (51) – Dutch – Small Business Owner: What is your approach to effective leadership?:
 Leadership is about flexibility. Business is always morphing and evolving. As a leader, you need to know when to change with the times. It’s about being someone that people want to follow. That means creating a culture where people feel challenged, motivated, and above all else – respected.
Though there are many different schools of thought regarding effective leadership, gender is but a small cog of a large, complex machine. In the end, the important thing to remember is that good leadership is less determined by gender as it is by expertise, communication, and vision.
