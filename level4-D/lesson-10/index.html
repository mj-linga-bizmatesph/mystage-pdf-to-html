ACTIVITY 1

Review the following content, ideas, and perspectives and answer the questions.

  • Lesson 1: Gender Culture

    How would you define gender culture?

  • Lesson 2: Gender Roles & Values

    How are modern men and women viewed and what roles are they expected to play?

    Who are you and what do you value?

  • Lesson 3: Gender Perspectives & Worldview

    How do modern men and women view the world based on their experiences?

    How has your gender impacted your worldview?

  • Lesson 4: Physicality

    How has physicality defined gender differences in the past compared to now?

    How important is physicality for men and women in the modern world?

  • Lesson 5: Psychology

    Do men and women really think differently?

    Would you say your way of thinking is typically male/female?

  • Lesson 6: Emotions

    How are men and women emotionally similar and different?

    How emotional are you?

  • Lesson 7: Office Relationships

    How are relationships determined by gender, If it does at all?

    Do you find it easier to work with men or women?

  • Lesson 8: Communication Styles

    How do men and women communicate?

    Describe typical male/female communication styles versus your own.

  • Lesson 9: Leadership

    What is considered effective leadership and does gender play a role?

    What is your approach to effective leadership?

ACTIVITY 2

Using the content covered above, talk about your experiences as a man or woman and how they’ve shaped your current circumstances in your professional life.
