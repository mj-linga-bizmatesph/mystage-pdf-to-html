SEE

Read and analyze the story.

Ark Manufacturing is located in the north end of the city. Recently, muggings have become more and more of a problem in the area. Tim, the HR manager, wanting to ensure the safety of his staff, posts a memo asking all male staff to escort their female colleagues to their vehicles at night.

Karen

I understand where Tim is coming from – but I can’t help but feel a bit offended by the memo. I don’t need to be escorted to my car like a helpless child. I would much rather Tim organize some self-defense classes or approach the problem differently instead of this antiquated “guys should protect girls” nonsense.

Tim

I’m not sure why Karen is giving me such a hard time about this. This has nothing to do with gender equality or inequality. It’s about making sure my staff get home safely. I appreciate Karen’s suggestion about the classes, but something needs to be done right away, and this is our best short-term solution.

What started off as a safety problem suddenly became a battle about gender differences and equality. Although Tim and Karen are on the same side, their ideas about gender equality and approach to the problem put the two at odds and complicated an already serious matter.
