A QUESTION OF VALUES
14
WORDS & PHRASES
pedigree - history or background
a safe bet - something that is certain to succeed
credible - convincing, believable
to be rooted in (something) - to be formed from or developed by (something)
SEE

Read and analyze the story.

Erik and Liz have been going back and forth for hours trying to decide on whom to promote to department head. On one hand they had Hiro – a young, up-and-coming talent with great pedigree and potential; on the other hand, they had Mai – a highly experienced woman in her early thirties.

Erik

I like Mai; it’s just that she just got married and is planning on having children soon. Right now, we’re looking for someone who can make this post a priority. I don’t want to make this decision an issue about gender, but based on my experience, she’s not the right choice given her current circumstances. Hiro is the safe bet.

Liz

I had my son when I was in my early thirties and I’m still here and playing an important role in the company. It seems like you’re basing your decision on speculation. I’m not blaming you for having a male’s perspective, but I think you may be lacking credible insight into the issue. As far as I’m concerned, Mai is the best and only choice.

As the two continued to debate, it was clear that Erik and Liz would not see eye to eye. Erik’s values were rooted in a more traditional corporate upbringing where men were seen as the driving force of the business. Liz, on the other hand, came from a new breed of women with more modern sensibilities.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • Have you encountered or heard of a similar situation?
  • Would you say that Erik’s views on the matter are logical and fair?
  • Do you think Liz’s position on the issue comes from a feminist perspective?
  • If you were to promote someone in your section to a management position, who would it be and why?

ACT

Role play

You are Erik and Liz’s boss. Both of them have presented their opinions and you must decide who to select for the position. Gather any additional information you need and make your decision.
