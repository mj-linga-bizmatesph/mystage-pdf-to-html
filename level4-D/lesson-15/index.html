A MATTER OF PERSPECTIVE
15
WORDS &
PHRASES
two cents – opinion or point of view
to lounge around - to rest or relax
to perplex - to confuse
to pull one’s weight – to do one’s fair share of work
SEE

Read and analyze the story.

George had been working at a new but fairly traditional company for a few months but still hasn’t quite adjusted to the way certain office responsibilities were delegated to the women. In order to see if there was anything he could do, George asks Joe, his manager, for his two cents.

George

I’m not sure I understand why the ladies - and only the ladies - in the office are cleaning the public spaces every morning. They even wipe my desk for me. Also, when someone buzzes in on the intercom, all the ladies rush to answer the call while most of the men lounge around – even if they are closer. As a grown man, I feel a bit embarrassed by this.

Joe

It’s always been like that. They’re not asked to do it; it’s an unspoken duty they’ve taken on for themselves. If you feel guilty and want to take on some of these tasks, I’d say don’t worry about it. It might perplex some of the staff and our boss might not feel comfortable with you upending the office culture and the way things are done.

Although George understood the situation, he still couldn’t shake the feeling that as a man, he should be doing more to pull his weight. He didn’t understand why this might confuse some of the staff and so decided to act on his beliefs, even if it meant going against Joe’s advice.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • How do you think the other men and women will react to George’s opinions and actions?
  • What are the pros and cons of George acting on his beliefs?
  • Are there certain roles that only women or men take on in your office?
  • Do you see these gender-based roles changing in the future?
