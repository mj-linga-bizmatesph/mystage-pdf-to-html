OFFICE JOKES

WORDS &
PHRASES
to rub (someone) the wrong way - to anger, frustrate, or bother (someone)
to lighten up – to become less serious, dark, and gloomy
off-putting – unpleasant and offensive
to put up with (something) - to withstand an unpleasant situation
SEE

Read and analyze the story.

Tim has always been the office clown and loves to make everyone laugh. Although his humour has always been in good fun, sometimes his comments would rub people the wrong way - especially the women in the office. Marcia, in particular, has just about reached her breaking point.

Tim

Why is everyone so serious all the time? Some might think my humor is a bit crude, but the way I see it, the best way to make a hard situation easier is to laugh at it. Men and women have been misunderstanding each other for centuries. We’re not going to find the answer any time soon, so might as well have a bit of fun with it, right? Lighten up!

Marcia

Tim wouldn’t be joking about it if he really understood how tough it is for people who are struggling with these issues. It’s only funny if you haven’t been personally affected by the problem. Tim simply lacks the maturity, insight, and values to see that his jokes about women are in fact not funny at all – they’re really quite off-putting.

Marcia had been putting up with Tim’s insensitivity for as long as she could. But one day, when Tim casually joked about Marcia being unable to control her emotions due to her gender, she exploded in rage. Their working relationship dived and the office atmosphere has been tense ever since.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • Do you think humor is a good way of handling difficult or sensitive situations?
  • What kind of jokes do you think Tim made that upset Marcia?
  • Do you think men tend to joke more about sensitive topics than women? Why or why not?
  • Is there anything that Marcia and Tim could’ve done to prevent the issue from escalating?

ACT

Role play

You are Tim and Marcia’s mutual friend. You heard about their fallout at the office and want to help patch things up. Speak to Tim and Marcia separately and give them advice.
