substantial – a large or great amount of something

a facelift – a renovation to give a more attractive or modern appearance
consensus - an agreement by the majority of a group
SEE

Read and analyze the story.

Firefly, a small but growing software engineering company, has decided to invest a substantial amount of money to make working at their company more appealing to applicants. Unsure about the best way to use the funds, management has asked the staff for their opinions.

Marcus

I say we use the money as a sign-on bonus. Or, we could use it as an incentive to reward our best performers. Nothing is more attractive than a company that properly awards its best employees for their hard work. Aside from that, I guess we could use the money to buy better, more advanced laptops, computers, and gear.

Karla

Our office has been needing a facelift for some time. Especially the public spaces, like the cafeteria and the restrooms. We spend so much of our lives at work it’d be best to invest some of that money back into the office. I think it would really boost our company’s appeal and improve the morale of our existing staff

Both suggestions were taken into account, but the management team was having a difficult time coming to a consensus. More men tended to agree with Marcus’ proposal, while more women sided with Karla. Ultimately, the decision would come down to what the President thinks is best.
