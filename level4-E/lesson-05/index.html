SEE

Read the article and take note of the highlighted phrases.

The first generation to be fully raised on mobile technology, social media, and a progressive society comprised of mixed-race children and single-sex parents, the iGeneration believes that traditional choices don’t guarantee success and are out to make their own way.

Characteristics:

Progressive and tech-innate, iGeners are expected to be more entrepreneurial than previous generations. They seek fulfillment, excitement, and flexibility. However, they are more self-conscious, lack situational awareness, and have the shortest attention span of any generation.

Landmark Events:

Indian Ocean Tsunami (2004): Sparking global relief efforts, the tsunami brought the world together.
Great Recession (2007): Career and financial instability profoundly impacted iGen families.
Donald Trump (2017): An unprecedented political event that redefined politics and changed the world.

Defining Technology:

AR/VR: Although it may be too soon to truly determine what technological advancements will shape the iGeneration, some believe that more seamless integration of digital and real-world spaces through augmented and/or virtual reality will have far-reaching effects on both business and society.
Driven by social causes, the desire to make a difference, and a level of maturity that exceeds the youngsters of previous generations, the iGeneration’s “togetherness” mentality has many hopeful for a brighter, more socially responsible, and mutually beneficial future.
