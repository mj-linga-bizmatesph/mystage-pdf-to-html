MY WAY OR THE HIGHWAY
15
WORDS & PHRASES
the old guard – original or long-standing members of a group or organisation
Can you believe the nerve? – an expression used to express disbelief at a person’s selfishness or shameless boldness
nonsense – words or writing that have no meaning or make little sense
deliberate – to discuss or consider carefully
SEE

Read and analyze the story.

Ken‘s company has always been very traditional. It was important to know your position and everyone was careful not to step outside their bounds. However, with many of the old guard retiring, more and more young people began entering the company — putting a strain on the culture.

Ken

I told my subordinate to do some research on a potential market and do you know what she said? She looked at me and said, “Why?” Can you believe the nerve? There is no why. Just do it. I don’t need to explain my reasoning. If you can’t follow simple orders without questioning everything, you have no right to call yourself an employee.

Mei

I don’t get why Ken blew up like that! I only asked why because I wanted some background information. If Ken wants me to be at my best, I need to believe in my work. That means being included in discussions and decision-making processes. This “know your place” nonsense is getting in the way of our work and it’s making it very hard for me to care.

Ken would not budge. For the sake of efficiency, Ken believed that a clear top-down approach was the best way to get things done; hours spent deliberating and explaining every detail was a waste of time. Mei, on the other hand, kept on insisting that she be more involved — leading to a breaking point.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • Are older generations easily offended by younger generations’ lack of traditional values?
  • Is Mei out of line for questioning Ken’s orders and wanting to be more involved in discussions?
  • Would Gen Xers and baby boomers react similarly or differently to this situation? How so?
  • What do you think can be done to keep this problem from getting worse for Mei and Ken?
