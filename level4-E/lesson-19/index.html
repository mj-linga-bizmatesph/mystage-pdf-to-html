FREEDOM OF SPEECH
19
WORDS & PHRASES
stooge – a disrespectful way of describing one who is paid to do unethical work by an authority
cross the line – to do something beyond what is acceptable
bug (someone) – to bother, worry, or annoy someone
resolve – determination to do something
SEE

Read and analyze the story.

Liana grew up around social media and was accustomed to posting her life online. This was part of her daily routine and no one had ever expressed any concerns about it before. One day, after posting a comment about “slaving away for an unethical, life-draining corporate stooge”, she was pulled aside.

Peter

I know that social media is a big part of life these days, but your recent post has crossed the line. Please keep those kinds of personal comments to yourself and off social media. We don’t appreciate those kind of insults directed towards management and we demand that you remove your post or risk facing potentially severe consequences.

Liana

This issue is bigger than what I wrote in my post; it’s about my right as an individual to express my thoughts. If Peter wanted me to take it down, maybe he should’ve asked what was bugging me and what the company could do to fix it. Ordering me to delete it or risk being punished is everything that’s wrong with the corporate world today.

Liana’s frustration at how the situation was being handled doubled her resolve. Peter, on the other hand, was caught in an incredibly tough situation. He understood the concept of free speech, but it was also his job to protect the company’s interests and to make sure Liana acts appropriately.
