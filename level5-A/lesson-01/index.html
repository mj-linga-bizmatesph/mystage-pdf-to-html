If you’ve worked in the same organization for quite some time, you probably already know your company’s core values like the back of your hand. These core values have influenced your organization’s internal processes, its vision, and its culture. You might have realized by now how some of your values, if not all, perfectly align with your company’s. But, have you ever stopped to think what YOUR core values are as an individual?
Our core values aren’t purely inherent in nature but are mostly shaped and influenced by different morals and principles around us. There are values that define our character (e.g. integrity, ambition, etc.). There are values that dictate how we want to live our lives (e.g. to be free, to be happy, etc.). And, there are values we share with other people (e.g. religion, political views, etc.).
Whatever they are, these fundamental beliefs deeply impact our behavior, decisions, or actions, which in turn can make or break anything we do. This rank aims to present different core values from various businesspeople and how these values have greatly influenced their business success.
