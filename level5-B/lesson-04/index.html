The wheel is arguably one of man’s greatest inventions. We’ve come a long way from using crude wooden rollers and spoked metal wheels. While the earliest wheels were effective in getting us from point A to point B, they offered little to no comfort during travel and easily broke. One day, a Scottish veterinarian by the name of John Boyd Dunlop, found the solution that would change the world of travel forever: inflatable rubber tires.
Dunlop’s profession required him to do a lot of traveling and his travelling along cobbled streets was always bumpy and uncomfortable. However, it was his instincts as a parent that ultimately led to the invention. Although fitted with rubber, Dunlop noticed how his son was having a hard and uncomfortable time riding his tricycle. So, he stitched strips of rubber together to make a tube, wrapped it with another layer of rubber, filled it with air and slapped it on his son’s tricycle wheels.
Dunlop’s solution to a trivial problem turned out to be a multi million-dollar product that served as a template for tire manufacturers. From everyday transportation to motorsports, the pneumatic tire has proven to be indispensable. Who would have thought a humble veterinarian could come up with such a brilliant idea that would forever change the way we travel? It only goes to show that anyone, regardless of his/her background or profession, can make an impact and leave a lasting legacy.
