We’ve come to understand how core values form our goals. Any business person would tell you that his/her goal is to become successful. But then, despite having such a clear vision, you might wonder why some people utterly fail at what they want to achieve. The answer is passion. Passion is arguably the strongest motivation that drives us in anything we do. It’s that burning desire that makes us want to do more and push the envelope even further.
The path to business success isn’t about how passionate you are, it’s about where you place your passion. A man who is passionate about climbing the ranks would likely be more successful than a man who is just passionate about his tasks. So you see, it’s not just being passionate about what you do, it’s more on being passionate about what you want to achieve.
In this rank, we will clearly see how successful business leaders went out of their way to achieve their goals. We will have a glimpse of the struggles they had to go through and the challenges they had to put up with, all in the name of success.
