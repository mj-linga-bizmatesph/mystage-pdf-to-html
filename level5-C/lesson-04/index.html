SEE

Read the article.

As the former US President Bill Clinton went through a scandal that shook the entire country and his marriage, he called Anthony “Tony” Robbins to help him weigh his options and redirect his life. His wife, Mrs. Hillary Clinton, who never left him despite the controversy, was a major support system for him, but Tony Robbins' presence made a huge difference as well. After all, Tony Robbins is one of the world’s most dynamic life coaches.
Bold and charismatic, Tony created an empire out of inspiring people towards success. His great desire to help others achieve their true potential came from his personal experience with family troubles and depression. He grew up being poor and had a mother who never supported his interests. On one occasion, while the family had nothing to eat, a man knocked on their door and brought them food. That moment had a positive impact on Tony and he vowed that he would help people someday.
After being chased out of the house, Tony never went back home. Instead, he turned his life around with his burning desire to make a difference and did what he does best—motivating people. He learned from the best motivational speakers, and is now the most famous and well-respected performance coach in the world. Tony Robbins' life is a testimony of conquering fears—something he himself declares everybody is capable of doing.
