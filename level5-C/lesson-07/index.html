SEE

Read the article.

Born with a silver spoon in her mouth, Cher Wang was meant to be an entrepreneur. Her father, the late Y.C. Wang, was a legendary tycoon who wanted his children to experience many different things, so Cher was sent to study in the US. After high school, she pursued music at Berkeley, but she found her true calling in economics. She switched courses, and after finishing her degree, she followed her passion and sold computers at First International Computer.
Later on, as Cher envisioned creating a device that could act as a computer, phone, and calculator, but things did not go smoothly as planned as she started her company. HTC began launching laptops, but it failed in the industry. The company redirected itself and became a contract manufacturer of PDAs and mobile phones. During this time, Cher designed computer chips in her living room, spent nights at work, and even encouraged her R&D staff to work late in the office.
Then, the long nights finally paid off. The company had a big break when HTC won its first major project to design and produce Compaq Aero PDA in 2000. Since then, HTC continued to rise to the top ranks and this led to Cher being named as one of the most powerful women by Forbes Magazine. Still to this day, “ the pride of Taiwan” continues to lead one of the largest mobile phone makers in the market.
