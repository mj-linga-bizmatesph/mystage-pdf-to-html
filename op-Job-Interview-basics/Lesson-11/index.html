QUESTIONS ABOUT YOUR PERSONALITY
11
BIZ TIP
When answering questions about your personality, be sure to include examples and goals to make your response more complete:
I am…
For example,… / because /
I’d like to…
SEE

Angela Ortiz, the HR Director, would like to know more about Ken’s personality…

Angela: Can you give me 3 words that best describe you?
Ken: I am incredibly ambitious because I always want to improve myself. I am friendly. For example, I always have great working relationships with my coworkers and clients. Finally, I’d say I am creative because I always look for new approaches and strategies. I’d like to utilize these strengths in your company.
Angela: Excellent. Can you give me more details about…
