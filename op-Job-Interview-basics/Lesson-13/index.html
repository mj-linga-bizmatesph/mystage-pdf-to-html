BIZ TIP
When answering tricky questions, take some time to think about your answer and find a way to highlight your qualities or skills. Use the phrases below to help you:
That’s an interesting question.
Give me a moment to think about the answer.
I would say…because…
I am…
SEE

Angela is asking Ken curveball questions to see how he handles pressure…

Angela: If you were an animal, what animal would you be?
Ken: That’s an interesting question. Give me a moment to think about the answer… I would say an eagle because I can see both the big picture and the small details. I am able to see things from different perspectives.
Angela: Very interesting answer. Thank you very much.
