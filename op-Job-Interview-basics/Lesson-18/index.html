ENDING THE INTERVIEW
18
BIZ TIP
Before the interview finishes, thank your interviewer, ask about the next steps, and close with impact. Use the following expressions to help you:
Thank you very much for your time. I really enjoyed…
If you don’t mind, can I ask about the next steps?
I’m incredibly excited about the position and hope to have the chance to prove myself.
SEE

The interview is coming to a close. Angela is thanking Ken for coming to the interview…

Angela: That’s all. Thank you for coming to the office today.
Ken: Thank you very much for your time. I really enjoyed speaking with you today. If you don’t mind, can I ask about the next steps?
Angela: We will let you know the results within one week. If you pass, we will send you the details of the position, remuneration, and signing procedures by e-mail. From there, we will arrange a date for you to come back to the office and sign the contract.
Ken: I understand. I’m incredibly excited about the position and hope to have the chance to prove myself. Thank you once again. It was a pleasure meeting you.
Angela: The pleasure was mine.
