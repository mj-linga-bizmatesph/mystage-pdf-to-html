19
BIZ TIP
No matter the result of your interview, it’s important to respond politely and professionally. Use the following to help you write an effective reply:
Thank you very much for the wonderful news
I am incredibly honored to accept the position of…
I will review the contract and…
Thank you very much for giving me this amazing opportunity.
SEE

Ken received an official invitation email and is writing his response...

Dear Mr. Sato,

Thank you for applying at ARK Co. We are very pleased to inform you that you’ve passed the interview and we would like to formally invite you to join the Ark team as a Pharmaceutical Sales Associate.

Please find the contract details attached below.

We would like to invite you to the office to sign the contract officially next Friday, the 23rd, between 4:00pm and 5:00pm.

Congratulations.

Best regards,

Angela Ortiz
HR Director

Dear Ms. Ortiz,

Thank you very much for the wonderful news. I’m incredibly honored to accept the position of Pharmaceutical Sales Associate.

I will review the contract and stop by the office on the 23rd to sign it.

Thank you very much for giving me this amazing opportunity.

Best regards,

Ken Sato
