REVIEW 復習
In this lesson, you will review the phrases you have learned in Lessons 6 through 9. このレッスンでは、レッスン 6 〜 9 までに学んだフレーズを復習します。

学んだフレーズをつかって空欄を埋めましょう。
1

Practice asking to speak more slowly

ゆっくり話してもらう練習

“We need to prepare.”

「準備しなければなりません。」

(ask to slow down)

Oh, sorry about that.
We need to prepare. Prepare.

失礼しました。
“We need to prepare. Prepare.”

(ask to repeat)

We need to prepare.
We need to prepare.

“We need to prepare.
We need to prepare.”

(ask to use the chat box)

Sure.

いいですよ。

2

Practice asking for meaning

単語の意味を聞く練習

“We are colleagues.”

“We are colleagues.”

(ask for meaning)

“Colleague” means a person you work with at the office.

“Colleague” はオフィスで
一緒に働く人のことです。

(ask to rephrase)

Sure. You can use “co-worker” or “workmate.”

いいですよ。“co-worker” や “workmate” とも言います。

(ask for the difference)

They are the same.

意味は同じですね。

(ask when to use)

I think we use “workmate” with friends.

“workmate” は友人との
会話で使うと思います。
