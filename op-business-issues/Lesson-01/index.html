WORDS & PHRASES
component – a part of a whole

vibe – a feeling or an atmosphere

SEE

Read the following and take note of the highlighted phrases

People are the most important component of any business. The right group of employees can take a company to heights of great success, whereas the wrong group of people can bring a company to its knees. Though many agree with this, not all agree on what is considered “right” for the company.
Manny:
Although he’s really a great person with a wonderful attitude and good potential, he just doesn’t have the skills and flexibility we are looking for. Given time, I’m sure he could be a great asset to the company, but as of now, he just doesn’t clear our current benchmarks. I say we cut him loose.
Joe:
Skills can be learned and experience can be gained. What we need are excellent people. Loyal, committed, decent people with the right attitude and the right vibe. There are some things that no amount of training and education can provide. If someone has that, they have my vote.
Corporate culture, quality, innovation, efficiency, and everything a company values stem from its employees. Though many factors will ultimately determine the impact an employee will have on the business, it is undeniable that people are any company’s greatest resource.
