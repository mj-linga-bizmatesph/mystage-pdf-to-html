Though many companies strive for a more diverse workforce, it is important to note that diversity is more than just a mix of different cultures and visible minorities. It is a combination of many different elements that may impact everything from corporate culture to earning potential and more.
Al:
The more the merrier. That’s what I say. Being in an environment that’s too similar isn’t conducive to growth. I’m sure there are many challenges, but working with a more diverse team will not only help us grow as a business, but as people. Let’s embrace change and see where it takes us.
Kai:
We have a corporate culture to protect. I’m worried that an influx of too many people with too many different values is going to pull our company apart. I understand the need for diversity, but I think we need to be very careful about the kind of culture we want to create and the impact it’s going to have.
Though there are arguments for both sides, any business operating globally requires more than a standard workforce to succeed. By leveraging the skills and perspectives of a truly diverse workforce, companies may be able to secure stronger footholds in an unstable global business scene.
