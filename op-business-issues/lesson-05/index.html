PHRASES
be all it’s cracked up to be – be as wonderful as people say
scrounge for – to look for or obtain something with difficulty
prioritize – to determine something or someone’s order of importance
take the plunge – to commit to something that one is unsure of or nervous about
SEE

Read the following and take note of the highlighted phrases

The decision to go public has enormous impacts on any company. Though large cash injections seem appealing, resulting shifts in corporate culture, changes in reporting procedures, and the need to meet investor demands make some wonder if being publicly traded is all it’s cracked up to be.
Don:
This is why we started our business, right? To get to where we are now. Not everyone can make it to the point where an IPO is within reach. Yes, things are going to change, but imagine what we can do with the money! No more scrounging for capital and swallowing half-realized dreams because of financial limitations. This is our big break!
Anna:
I don’t want us to lose control. I’m afraid that if we go public, we’re going to lose the soul of our company. As soon as we start prioritizing money and investors’ needs above our own, we’re going to start looking for short-term solutions to meet targets and lose sight of our vision. Everything is going to be about money…and that’s not me.
Though there are success stories on both sides, there are dangers to both courses of action. Does the money and glamour of a successful IPO outweigh the added pressure, cost, and confines of being a public company? All are important points to consider when taking the plunge.
