INTERNAL & EXTERNAL EXECUTIVE PLACEMENTS
14
WORDS &
PHRASES
fresh eyes – an unbiased, new view or opinion of something
expertise – high-level skill or knowledge in a particular field, subject, or topic
a fresh take – a new perspective
name (someone) to a position – to appoint or assign someone to a particular job title
SEE

Read the following and take note of the highlighted phrases.

There may be a time when a company must decide how best to replace a retiring executive. Though internal promotions provide most with a sense of ease, certain aspects of a business can only be improved through a pair of fresh eyes.
Peter:
This is a family business. It has always been a family business, and it will always remain a family business. No one knows this business like we do, and if we start relying on the “expertise” of others, it won’t be long before we lose control of everything. It takes more than just a good resume to be an executive; it takes trust. Years of it.
Mari:
We need a fresh take on things. We’ve been okay so far, but I fear we lack something very important: innovation. Now is the chance to reinvent ourselves, to take the business in a bold new direction. It won’t be easy and there will be changes, but we need to stop thinking of change as a bad thing.
Naming anyone to a position of power can have significant impact. Though there are many things that determine if one is suited to the role or not, it is an irrefutable fact that support and trust from other executives and staff are just as important as one’s skills, abilities, and experience.
