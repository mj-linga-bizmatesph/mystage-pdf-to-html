SETTING UP A VISIT THROUGH E-MAIL
1
BIZ TIP
When setting up a visit with your business contact, clearly explain your purpose for the visit. Use these phrases to help you:
I'm planning to visit [place] [date]
The purpose of my visit is to…
Will you be free on ____ or ____?
SEE

Taka is e-mailing his contact about his upcoming visit.

Dear Mr. Tran,

I hope all is well.

I’m planning to visit Hanoi in two weeks. The purpose of my visit is to discuss some business opportunities and to see your factory and its facilities. Will you be free on March 15 or 16?

I look forward to meeting you soon.

Best regards,
Taka Mitsui
