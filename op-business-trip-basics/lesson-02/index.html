CALLING TO CONFIRM THE VISIT
2
BIZ TIP
When calling your business contact, make sure you give the details of your arrival. Use the following phrases to help you:
I’m calling to…
I should arrive at... Will this be OK?
See you on [date] at [time].
SEE

Taka is calling Jake Tran.

Taka
Hello, Mr. Tran. This is Taka Mitsui with EON Devices.
Jake
Hello, Mr. Mitsui.
Taka
I’m calling to confirm our appointment on the 16th.
Jake
Oh yes. Thank you for your e-mail.
Taka
Not at all. I should arrive at around 10AM. Will this be OK?
Jake
Yes, that’s fine.
Taka
Great. Thank you, Mr. Tran. See you on the 16th at 10AM.
