MEETING THE CONTACT PERSON
3
BIZ TIP
When meeting your business contact for the first time, make sure you greet him/her appropriately. Use the phrases below to help you:
Hi, you must be...
It's nice to finally meet you.
Thanks for _____. I really appreciate it.
SEE

Taka has just arrived in Hanoi.

Taka:
Hi, you must be Jake Tran.
Jake:
Mr. Taka Mitsui? Yes, I’m Jake. Good to see you.
Taka:
It’s nice to finally meet you.
Jake:
So how was your flight, Taka?
Taka:
It was great. The flight was smooth.
Jake:
That’s good to hear.
Taka:
By the way, thanks for picking me up. I really appreciate it.
Jake:
You’re welcome. So, shall we?
