OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIP BASICSILESSON 4
MAKING SMALL TALK ON THE WAY
4
BIZ TIP
In order to avoid silence, make small talk with your business contact by asking questions. Here are some examples to help you:
So how's the weather around here?
Do you know a place where I can/could...?
What about…?
SEE

Jake and Taka are on their way to the factory.

Taka:
So how’s the weather around here?
Jake:
It’s summer, so it can get really hot and humid. Is this your first time to visit Vietnam?
Taka:
Yes it is. Do you know a place where I can buy some souvenirs?
Jake:
Sure, there are lots of shops nearby. I can go with you tomorrow if you’d like.
Taka:
That’d be great. Thank you, Jake. What about popular tourist spots?
Jake:
Hanoi is famous for old buildings. See that building over there?
Taka:
You mean that one?
Jake:
Yes. It’s a popular tourist spot. It’s called…
