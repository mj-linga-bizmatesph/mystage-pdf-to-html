PART 1

Review the Biz tips with your trainer and make sure you are comfortable with the expressions and concepts.

When setting up a visit with your business contact, clearly explain your purpose for the visit.
Use these phrases to help you:
I'm planning to visit [place] [date]
The purpose of my visit is to…
Will you be free on ____ or ____?
When calling your business contact, make sure you give the details of your arrival. Use the following phrases to help you:
I’m calling to…
I should arrive at... Will this be OK?
See you on [date] at [time].
When meeting your business contact for the first time, make sure you greet him/her appropriately. Use the phrases below to help you:
Hi, you must be...
It's nice to finally meet you.
Thanks for _____. I really appreciate it.
In order to avoid silence, make small talk with your business contact by asking questions. Here are some examples to help you:
So how's the weather around here?
Do you know a place where I can...?
What about…?

PART 2

Make a connection with your business partner by doing the tasks below. Apply the expressions you learned in Lessons 1 to 4.

  1. E-mail your business partner to set up a visit.
  2. Call your business partner to confirm the schedule of your visit.
  3. Make small talk with your business partner by asking questions.
