OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIP BASICSILESSON 6
STARTING THE TOUR OF THE FACTORY
6
BIZ TIP
When touring the factory, comment on the facilities to show interest. Use these examples to help you:
This is a very nice building. How big is it?
It looks well-maintained. How old is it?
I don’t see too many workers though. Is everything automated?
SEE

Jake is taking Taka on a tour of the factory.

Jake:
So here we are. Welcome to our factory.
Taka:
This is a very nice building. How big is it?
Jake:
It’s about 6,000 square meters.
Taka:
I see. And it looks well-maintained, too. How old is it?
Jake:
It’s about 10 years old.
Taka:
I don’t see too many workers though. Is everything automated?
Jake:
That’s right. Most of the processes are automated.
Taka:
That’s great.
Jake:
Come, let me introduce you to the plant manager.
