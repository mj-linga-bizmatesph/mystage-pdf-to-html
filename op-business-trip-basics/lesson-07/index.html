MEETING THE PLANT MANAGER
7
BIZ TIP
When meeting someone from a different culture, make sure you introduce yourself properly. Use the following phrases to help you:
Nice to meet you,…
I’m [complete name].
And I’m [position] at [company].
SEE

Taka is being introduced to the plant manager.

Jake
Taka, this is Van Nguyen, our plant manager.
Taka
Nice to meet you, Mr. Nguyen. I’m Taka Mitsui. I’m the head engineer at Eon Devices. Please call me Taka.
Van
Hello, Taka. Please call me Van.
Taka
Thank you for making time to meet me.
Van
My pleasure.
