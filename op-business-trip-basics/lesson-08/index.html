OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIP BASICSILESSON 8
ASKING ABOUT QUALITY AND EFFICIENCY
8
BIZ TIP
When asking about important information, make sure you confirm your understanding and follow up with a question. Here are some examples to help you:
How do you ensure the quality of...?
So, you mean...?
And what steps do you take when...?
SEE

Taka and Jake are now accompanied by the plant manager.

Van:
Do you have any questions so far about the plant, Taka?
Taka:
Yes, how do you ensure the quality of each battery?
Van:
Every battery we produce goes through several tests like voltage testing, electrical interference, and even mechanical testing.
Taka:
I see. So you mean you have your own testing facility?
Van:
Yes, that’s right.
Taka:
And what steps do you take when one fails any of these tests?
Van:
We check the serial number and re-test other batteries that belong to the same batch.
Taka:
That’s good. So what are the causes of…?
