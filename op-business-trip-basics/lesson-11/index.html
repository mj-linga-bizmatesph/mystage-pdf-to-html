STARTING THE MEETING
11
BIZ TIP
When starting a meeting, clearly explain the purpose, the agenda, and the duration of the meeting. Here are some phrases to help you:
The purpose of this short meeting is to...
I'd like to talk about…
May I ask for [duration] to discuss these?
SEE
Taka is about to have a meeting with Jake and Van.
Taka Thanks for giving me a tour of the factory.
Jake You’re welcome, Taka.
Jake Okay, so the purpose of this short meeting is to discuss a few issues.
Jake Sure, Taka.
Taka I’d like to talk about three things: First is quality assurance, second is safety, and third is our next steps.
Van Okay.
Taka May I ask for 30 minutes to discuss these?
Van Absolutely, Taka.
Taka Thank you.
