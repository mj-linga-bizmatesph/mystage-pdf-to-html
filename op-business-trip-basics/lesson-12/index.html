OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIP BASICSILESSON 12
BRINGING UP YOUR CONCERNS
12
BIZ TIP
When bringing up a concern, show the other person you understand the situation first. Take a look at the following phrases:
I’m glad to know that…
However, I noticed that...
If possible, I’d like to...
SEE

Taka is bringing up some concerns.

Taka:
I’m glad to know that each battery has a number of tests to ensure quality. However, I noticed that there isn’t a test to measure actual performance.
Jake:
Sorry, what do you mean?
Taka:
I mean the batteries aren’t tested with an actual device they are made for.
Van:
I see. Something like an abuse test.
Taka:
Exactly. If possible, I’d like to have sample batteries tested with several devices and push them to their limits.
Van:
I agree. However, we don’t have access to these devices which we can test the batteries with.
Taka:
I think I can arrange that.

TRY

Bring up the following concerns.

SITUATION POSITIVE COMMENT CONCERN YOUR WANTS
Workers aren’t wearing protective clothing. I’m glad to know how committed the workers are. However, safety is really a big concern for me. So, I want to implement a strict policy on safety.
Low production rate I understand… But… What I want to do is…
