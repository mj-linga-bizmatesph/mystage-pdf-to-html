CONFIRMING THE ACTION PLANS
14
BIZ TIP
When confirming action plans, make sure everyone is clear about his/her tasks. Use the phrases below to guide you:
So, we decided to...
[Person] will...
Are we all clear?
SEE
Taka is wrapping up the meeting.
Taka Okay, so we decided to add two more steps to the quality control process. I will send Jake the test devices for your engineers.
Jake Sure.
Taka Then, Van will coordinate with the engineers to test the prototypes and samples from the final product.
Van Okay.
Taka Are we all clear?
All [Yes]
Taka Great. Thank you for your time.
