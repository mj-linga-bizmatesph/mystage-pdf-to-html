PART 1

Review the Biz tips with your trainer and make sure you are comfortable with the expressions and concepts

When starting a meeting, clearly explain the purpose, the agenda, and the duration of the meeting. Here are some phrases to help you:
The purpose of this short meeting is to...
I'd like to talk about…
May I ask for [duration] to discuss these?
When bringing up a concern, show the other person you understand the situation first. Take a look at the following phrases:
I’m glad to know that…
However, I noticed that...
If possible, I’d like to...
When proposing a solution, make sure you identify the problem and explain the possible results of your suggestion. Here are some helpful phrases:
So one issue we're facing is...
I suggest we...
If we do this, we can be sure that…
When confirming action plans, make sure everyone is clear about his/her tasks. Use the phrases below to guide you:
So, we decided to...
[Person] will...
Are we all clear?
