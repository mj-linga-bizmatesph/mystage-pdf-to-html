RESPONDING TO AN INVITATION
16
BIZ TIP
When responding to an invitation, vary your answer instead of the usual “sure” or “sorry, I can’t.” Here are some phrases to help you:
Saying YES: Sure, that sounds good.
Saying NO: I'd love to, but...
Saying MAYBE: Can I get back to you? I might…
SEE
Situation 1: Saying YES
Jake:
Hey, Taka, if you’re not doing anything tonight, Van and I would like to invite you for a few rounds of drink.
Taka:
Sure, that sounds good. Thank you.
Situation 2: Saying NO
Jake:
Hey, Taka, if you’re not doing anything tonight, Van and I would like to invite you for a few rounds of drink.
Taka:
I’d love to, but I have a report to finish. Sorry, Jake.
Situation 3: Saying MAYBE
Jake:
Hey, Taka, if you’re not doing anything tonight, Van and I would like to invite you for a few rounds of drink.
Taka:
Can I get back to you? I might need to work late.
