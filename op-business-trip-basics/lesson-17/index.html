TALKING ABOUT THE MENU
17
BIZ TIP
To start a conversation at the dinner table, show interest in the local dishes by talking about the menu. Here are some examples to help you:
What do you recommend?
What’s in it?
What does it taste like?
SEE
Taka is having dinner with Van and Jake at a restaurant.
Jake Have you tried any Vietnamese dishes, Taka?
Taka No, I haven’t… What do you recommend?
Jake A very common dish is Bun Bo Hue.
Taka What’s in it?
-- Jake explains the dish --
Taka What does it taste like?
Van It’s a bit spicy. Are you OK with spicy foods?
Taka Oh, yes, I love spicy foods. And how is it prepared?
Van Well, first you prepare the broth by…
▼▼▼
TRY

Talk about the menu by asking your trainer about his/her favorite local dishes.

TRAINER STUDENT
List 3 of your favorite local dishes.
Ask questions about the dishes your trainer has listed down.
▼▼▼
ACT

Start a conversation by talking about the menu.
