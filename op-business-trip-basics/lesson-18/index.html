BIZ TIP
When you want to get to know the other person, make small talk and try to find something in common. Use the examples to help you:
So, what do you do on weekends?
Same here.
If you don’t mind my asking…
SEE
Taka, Jake and Van are now having dinner.
Taka So, what do you do on weekends, Jake?
Jake I usually just stay at home and play video games with my son.
Taka Same here. My son and I also enjoy playing video games together.
Jake Kids nowadays are different compared to our generation.
Taka I agree. If you don’t mind my asking, how old is your son?
Jake Not at all. He’s already…
