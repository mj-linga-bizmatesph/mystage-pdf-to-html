OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIP BASICSILESSON 19
FOLLOWING UP BY E-MAIL
19
BIZ TIP
When sending a follow-up e-mail, add a personal touch and confirm the action plan you agreed to. Here are some phrases to help you:
Thank you for…
Based on our meeting, we agreed to…
Please let me know if you have any questions.
SEE
Taka is making a follow-up e-mail.
Hello, Jake.

Thank you for giving me a tour of your factory. I really had a wonderful time in Hanoi with you and Van.

So based on our meeting, we agreed to add two more steps to the quality control process:
  • Prototype testing
  • Final product testing


I will send you the devices you need to run these tests and Van will coordinate with your engineers to set up the new processes.

Please let me know if you have any questions. I look forward to seeing you and Van again soon.

Cheers,
Taka

TRY

Send a follow-up e-mail to the following people.

PERSON SITUATION ACTION PLAN
A new supplier Visited their factory Manufacture new OEM products
A new contact Attended a tradeshow Exchange information about new business opportunities
Global team Had teleconference to talk about a new project Present different concepts by next week

ACT

Send a follow-up e-mail to your business partner. Add a personal touch and confirm details.
