OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIPILESSON 1
RESPONDING TO AN INQUIRY
1
BIZ TIP
Remember these key points when responding to an e-mail inquiry:
  • Give appropriate background information about your company
  • Provide additional relevant information
  • Provide requested materials or answers
SEE

Example:

(Ryo responds to an inquiry from a foreign distributor)

To:
Subject:
Bob_Cornell@btdistrbutors.com
RE: Product inquiry
Hi Bob,

We are a small-medium sized semiconductor manufacturer here in Japan. You may visit our website to view our products further.

I hope to hear from you again soon.

Regards,
Ryo

Bizmates style:

(Ryo responds to an inquiry from a foreign distributor)

To:
Subject:
Bob_Cornell@btdistributors.com
RE: Product inquiry
Dear Mr. Cornell,

Thank you for your interest in ElectroCon Japan.

We specialize in optical semiconductor devices ranging from White LEDs to photocouplers and photo relays. During our 30 plus years in the industry, we have supplied our products to a long list of satisfied clients in various industries including yours. I have attached our brochure to give you an overview of our products.

Should you have any questions, please feel free to contact me. I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Regards,
Ryo Watanabe
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions:

  1. Who do you get e-mail inquiries from in English?
  2. What do they usually inquire about?
  3. What background information would you need to give?
  4. What additional relevant information could you give?
  5. What materials or answers do you usually provide?
