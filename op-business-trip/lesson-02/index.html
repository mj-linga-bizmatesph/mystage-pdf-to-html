OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIPILESSON 2
KNOWING THEIR BUSINESS CULTURE
2
BIZ TIP
It’s always a good idea to do some research on the business culture of the place you are visiting. By learning their way of business, you are less likely to encounter misunderstandings.
SEE
Sam:
Ryo, I’ve heard you’re going to visit a distributor in the U.S. Is it your first time there?
Ryo:
Yes, it is. I’ve been to several Asian countries on business but never to the U.S. I think Americans are very informal and friendly, so I plan to dress casually.
Sam:
I would be careful of that. That really depends on the industry and the area within the US. You’re going to the East Coast and this is your first meeting with them, so I would wear a suit. Americans are usually very friendly, but they are also very professional and business-like.
Ryo:
I see. What do you think is a good gift for them? I usually give Japanese wine to my Asian clients.
Sam:
The practice of gift-giving is not common in the U.S. and could be misinterpreted as trying to bribe them. So, I would avoid any gifts.
Ryo:
Oh really? But if we go drinking after our meeting, the client will probably pay for me, so shouldn’t I have something prepared for them?
Sam:
First of all, it’s not likely that they will ask you out. And if they do, they’ll probably expect to go Dutch, which means to split the bill.
Ryo
I see. Thank you for filling me in.

TRY

Answer these questions about your destination:

  1. What country will you be visiting on business in the future?
  2. What’s the proper way to greet someone in this culture?
  3. What are some words in the local language?
  4. What’s the proper dress code for business in this culture?
  5. What are some common gestures used in this culture?
  6. What are some common gestures that should be avoided in this culture?
  7. How punctual are people in this culture?
  8. Is the practice of gift-giving common in this culture?
  9. Do you think you will have the opportunity to interact socially with people from this culture?
  10. What can you talk about during a social event with people from this culture?
▼▼▼
ACT

Final Activity:

Imagine that you are taking a business trip overseas. Describe what you know about this particular culture and what you still need to find out.
