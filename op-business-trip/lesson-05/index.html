OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIPILESSON 5
ANSWERING A CALL FROM THE CONTACT PERSON
5
BIZ TIP
Your contact person might call you up and even offer to pick you up at the airport. In this case, you should provide the following details:
  • Date and time of your arrival (using their local time zone)
  • Other flight details
  • What you’ll be wearing
(Remember to pause every now and then as your contact might be taking down notes)
SEE

(Ryo answers a call from Bob)

Bob:
Hello, Mr. Watanabe? This is Bob Cornell of BT Distributors. How are you?
Ryo:
Hi, Mr. Cornell! Yes, this is Ryo Watanabe.
Bob:
I am calling to confirm our appointment on the 27th at 10 a.m. We’d be happy to listen to your presentation.
Ryo:
That’s great! Thank you for your confirmation.
Bob:
Also, may I offer to pick you up at the airport?
Ryo:
Thanks Mr. Cornell, but I don’t think that will be necessary. I don’t want to trouble you.
Bob:
Oh, no trouble at all. In fact, I would like to meet you in person before we hold the meeting on the 27th.
Ryo:
That’s very kind of you. I really appreciate what you’re doing.
Bob:
Not at all. It’s my pleasure. So when are you arriving?
Ryo:
I’ll be arriving on the 26th at 11 a.m. NY time.
Bob:
OK. (Bob takes note of the details)
Ryo:
And I’ll be on Tokyo Air, flight number 4454.
Bob:
Right. Thank you, Mr. Watanabe. What will you be wearing by the way?
Ryo:
I’ll be wearing a white shirt with a red-striped tie and a dark gray suit.
Bob:
Great! I’ll be wearing a light gray shirt with a blue tie and a black suit.
Ryo:
All right! So that’s April 26th at 11 am NY time. Tokyo Air, flight 4454. See you soon, Mr. Cornell. Thanks again.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions:

  1. Has any of your contacts offered to pick you up at the airport?
  2. Would you grab such an offer right away?
  3. What is the time difference between Japan and your destination?
  4. Is Japan ahead or behind their local time zone?
  5. What other flight details would you give?
  6. What is your usual attire during your business trip?
  7. Would it be much easier for your contact to spot you if they knew exactly what you were wearing?
  8. Do you also ask your contact what he/she will be wearing?
  9. Do you think it’s still necessary to detail your attire even if you’ve seen each other in a video call?
  10. Do you do a recap on all the details before ending the conversation?
▼▼▼
ACT

Final Activity:

Imagine your contact called you to confirm the appointment and offered to pick you up at the airport. Provide all the necessary details of your flight and your attire.
