OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIPILESSON 6
MEETING AT THE AIRPORT
6
BIZ TIP
This is the part where you can practice what you learned about their business culture. When meeting a contact at the airport, be sure to:
  • Greet them professionally according to their business culture
  • Engage in small talk and exchange pleasantries
  • Avoid starting a conversation about business or other sensitive topics
SEE

Example:

Bob:
Mr. Watanabe?
Ryo:
Hi, you must be Bob. It’s nice to finally meet you.
Bob:
Yes, same here. So, how was your flight Mr. Watanabe?
Ryo:
Oh it was great! By the way, let me give you a printout of our product specs so you can review them tonight. It’s in my bag here somewhere.
Bob:
Oh, OK.
Ryo:
By the way, you look so young. How old are you? Are you married?
Bob:
Well, ah... Come, let me help you with your luggage.

TRY

Answer these questions about your destination:

  1. What is the proper way of greeting in their culture?
  2. What are some of the safe topics for a short conversation in their culture?
  3. What are some topics you need to avoid in a conversation?
  4. What topics do you usually talk about when making small talk?
  5. What other things have you learned about their culture that you are able to apply on your first meeting?
▼▼▼
ACT

Final Activity:

Engage in small talk with a contact you’ve just met for the first time.
