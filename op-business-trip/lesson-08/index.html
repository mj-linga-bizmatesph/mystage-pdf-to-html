OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIPILESSON 8
ATTENDING A TRADESHOW
8
BIZ TIP
Tradeshows, conventions, seminars, etc. are good opportunities to expand your network and introduce your company. When attending events like these:
  • Have a good reason for making connections
  • Avoid introducing yourself and company right away
  • Be sensitive to opportunities where you can offer help and gain something
SEE

Example:

(Ryo is attending an electronics tradeshow)

Ryo:
Hi there! I’m Ryo Watanabe. Nice to meet you.
Alex:
Ah... Hi there, Mr. Watanabe. Nice to meet you too.
Ryo:
Sorry I didn’t catch your name.
Alex:
Well, ah... It’s Alex. Alex Robbins.
Ryo:
Hello, Mr. Robbins. By the way, I’m with ElectroCon. It’s a company based in Japan, and we specialize in manufacturing semiconductors.
Alex:
I see. That’s good to hear.
Ryo:
What about you? What kind of company do you work for?
Alex:
Well ah.... I’m just trying to have a look around. Oh, please excuse me. I just remembered I need to make an important phone call.

Bizmates style:

Ryo
The place looks very busy, doesn’t it?
Alex:
Yes, I think there are more exhibitors this year.
Ryo
Really? Have you seen anything new or interesting so far?
Alex:
A lot, actually. But the problem is, most of the exhibitors here are major companies.
Ryo
What exactly are you looking for?
Alex:
I was hoping we could partner up with semiconductor manufacturers.
Ryo
Actually, I work for a semiconductor manufacturer based in Japan.
Alex:
Wow, really?
Ryo
I’m Ryo, by the way. Ryo Watanabe.
Alex:
I’m Alex Robbins. Pleased to meet you, Mr. Watanabe.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions about your destination:

  1. What kind of networking event overseas would you like to attend next?
  2. What kind of people would you like to meet and become part of your connection?
  3. What could be your purpose for making a connection?
  4. What topic would you start your conversation with?
  5. How would you continue a friendly conversation?
  6. What could you offer to help your new connection?
  7. What could you gain from your new connection?
  8. How would you introduce yourself and your company?
▼▼▼
ACT

Final Activity:

Imagine you are attending a tradeshow relevant to your situation at work. Make a new connection by making small talk until you have introduced yourself and your company.
