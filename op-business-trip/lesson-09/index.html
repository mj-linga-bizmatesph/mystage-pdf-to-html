OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIPILESSON 9
CALLING THE CONTACT PERSON
9
BIZ TIP
Don’t just confirm the appointment when calling your contact person. Use it as an opportunity to:
  • Ask if he/she has received the agenda via email
  • Ask how many people are attending so you can prepare enough copies
  • Ask for directions and exact address of their office
SEE

Example:

(Ryo calls Bob a day before the meeting.)

Bob:
Hello, this is Bob Cornell.
Ryo:
Hi Bob, good morning. This is Ryo.
Bob:
Good morning, Ryo. Nice to hear from you again.
Ryo:
I just wanted to confirm our meeting tomorrow at 10 a.m.
Bob:
Ah, yes. We’re all looking forward to hearing your presentation.
Ryo:
Great. Thank you, Bob. See you tomorrow.

Bizmates style:

Bob:
Hello, this is Bob Cornell.
Ryo:
Hi Bob, good morning. This is Ryo. I’m just calling to confirm our meeting scheduled at 10 a.m. tomorrow.
Bob:
Ah, yes. We’re all looking forward to hearing your presentation.
Ryo:
Great! By the way, did you get the agenda I emailed you?
Bob:
Yes. We’re all good with that.
Ryo:
OK. Also, may I ask who else is attending so I can prepare enough copies of the handouts?
Bob:
Sure. Let’s see. There’ll be 5 attendees including myself.
Ryo:
I’ll be taking a taxi to your office. Would the driver know if I just tell him to go to the High Tower Building?
Bob:
Yes, the driver should know. It’s the tallest building on 53rd Street, so you can’t miss it. I can meet you downstairs. Just give me a call when you get there.
Ryo:
Sounds good. Thank you, Bob. See you tomorrow.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions about your destination:

  1. What would you ask your contact person prior to your appointment?
  2. How many attendees would be there in your presentation?
  3. What was the most number of attendees you’ve had in a single presentation?
  4. What was the least number of attendees?
  5. How much time do you allow yourself to confirm and prepare for an appointment?
  6. How far is their office from the hotel you’re staying?
  7. What other information could you ask during the phone call?
▼▼▼
ACT

Final Activity:

Imagine you’re calling your contact person to confirm a meeting. Use the opportunity to ask all the necessary information you need.
