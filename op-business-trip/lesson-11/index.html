OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIPILESSON 11
TAKING A TOUR OF THE FACILITIES
11
BIZ TIP
Upon meeting your contact person, he/she may take you on a short tour of their facilities. Try to carry a conversation by following these steps:
  • Introduce yourself and greet them according to their business custom
  • Exchange pleasantries
  • Show interest by asking meaningful questions
SEE

Example:

Bob:
Good morning, Ryo. Good to see you again.
Ryo:
Good morning Bob. Yes, same here.
Bob:
Well, since you’re early, let me take you on a short tour of our office.
Ryo:
Sure.
Bob:
OK, here we are. Welcome to our office.
Ryo:
Thank you.
Bob:
Would you like to see our employee cafeteria?
Ryo:
No, that’s OK.

Bizmates style:

Bob:
Good morning, Ryo. Good to see you again.
Ryo
Good morning Bob. It’s nice to see you again too.
Bob:
Well, since you’re early, let me take you on a short tour of our office.
Ryo
Sure, that would be great. By the way, this is a really nice office. How long have you been staying in this building?
Bob:
We moved here about 3 years ago. OK, here we are. Welcome to our office.
Ryo
Wow, the place looks very nice! You must have a wonderful view of the city.
Bob:
Yes actually. The conference room has the best view because of its large glass windows.
Ryo
So, how many people are based in this office?
Bob:
We currently have just over a hundred. We have another office on the other side of the city which…
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions about your destination:

  1. Do your clients often take you on a tour of their facilities overseas when you visit?
  2. What facility would you likely be taking a tour in on your next trip overseas?
  3. What would you and your contact talk about while taking the short tour?
  4. What meaningful questions could you ask?
  5. How else could you show interest in being in their facility?
▼▼▼
ACT

Final Activity:

Imagine you are taken on a short tour by your contact in their new facility. Carry a conversation and ask meaningful questions.
