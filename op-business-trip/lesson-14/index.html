OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIPILESSON 14
ANSWERING QUESTIONS
14
BIZ TIP
Answering questions after a presentation could be a daunting task. Follow these steps to sound professional when answering:
  • Check your understanding of the question and if they’re satisfied with your answer
  • Give your answer before any background information (not the other way around)
  • Avoid phrases like “I think…” or “I believe”
SEE

Example:

Tom:
So, what’s the secret behind the high quality of ElectroCon’s products?
Ryo:
Yes, that’s right.
Tom:
No, I mean how does your company produce such high quality products?
Ryo:
Oh I’m sorry. I think our design is very good.

Bizmates style:

Tom:
So, what’s the secret behind the high quality of ElectroCon’s semiconductors?
Ryo
Do you mean how we create such high quality products?
Tom:
Yes, that’s right.
Ryo
That’s a good question. Our secret is in our design process. We have a system that collects all good design practices into a database that covers everything from coding guidelines to testability. This database is accessible by all of our design engineers and is constantly upgraded as new developments are found. So, we have a system that continuously creates high quality products. Does that answer your question?
Tom:
Yes, it does. Thank you.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions:

  1. What questions do you think your audience would ask you after your presentation?
  2. What are some difficult questions they might ask you?
  3. How would you rephrase those questions to check your understanding?
  4. What background information would you use to answer the question?
  5. What would you say to confirm if you’ve answered the question correctly?
