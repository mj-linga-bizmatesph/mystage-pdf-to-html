OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIPILESSON 17
HAVING DINNER WITH YOUR CLIENTS
17
BIZ TIP
This is another situation where your knowledge of their culture pays off. Watch out for the following key points when dining out with clients:
  • Their table manners
  • Their popular local dish
  • Topics to talk about at the dinner table
Table Manners: http://www.businessinsider.com/rules-of-dining-etiquette-2012-4?op=1
SEE

Example:

Bob:
What are you going to have?
Ryo:
I’m not so hungry. I’ll just have an appetizer.
Bob:
Oh OK. I’m going to have some wine. What would you like to drink?
Ryo:
I’ll just have coke.
Bob:
Coke? OK.
Ryo:
I forgot to ask you something in our meeting. What are some of the big manufacturers on your client list?
Bob:
Well ah. I would have to go back to the office to find that out. Can I get back to you later on that?

Bizmates style:

Bob:
What are you going to have?
Ryo
I’m not sure. Everything looks so good. What about you?
Bob:
For starters, I was going to get the stuffed mushrooms and clam chowder. For my entrée, I’m trying to decide between the Swordfish and Fried Oysters.
Ryo
OK, how about you order the Swordfish and I’ll get the Fried Oysters? And I’ll give you a piece.
Bob:
Yeah, sure. What about your appetizer?
Ryo
I’ll try the cream of broccoli and a garden salad.
Bob:
I’m going to have some wine. What would you like to drink?
Ryo
I’ll have a bit of wine to start and then switch to iced tea later on.
Bob:
Great.
Ryo
So, what do you do in your free time?
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions about your destination:

  1. What is considered taboo at the dinner table in your country?
  2. What do you think is taboo in their culture while eating?
  3. How else are their table manners similar to your culture’s?
  4. How else are they different?
  5. What are the most popular dishes in that culture?
  6. What are some good topics you can start with as small talk?
▼▼▼
ACT

Final Activity:

Imagine you are at a fine dining restaurant with your clients overseas. Carry a conversation appropriate at the dinner table.
