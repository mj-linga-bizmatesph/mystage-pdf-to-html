OTHER PROGRAMS BUSINESS TRIPILESSON 18
SIGHTSEEING
18
BIZ TIP
Be careful when sightseeing or buying souvenirs as locals can easily spot a tourist. Beware of muggers, pickpockets, and swindlers. Keep these things in mind when sightseeing to avoid getting into trouble:
  • Avoid using a map in public. Ask shop clerks for directions instead
  • Leave unnecessary luggage back at the hotel
  • Be observant. Don’t allow yourself to be caught off guard
SEE

Example:

Ryo:
Excuse me, sir! I think I’m lost.
Rob:
Oh! Sorry to hear that. Are you new around here?
Ryo:
Yes, I’m here on an important business trip. Can you tell me how to get to the museum using this map?
Rob:
I see. Keep the map, follow me and I’ll show you. By the way, my name is Rob. Let me help you with that luggage.
(After several minutes of walking)
Ryo:
Ah… Rob, this place doesn’t look like anywhere near the museum on my map.
Rob:
Be silent and give me your cash, your phone and that camera.

Bizmates style:

(At a souvenir shop)

Ryo
HI! How much are those?
Clerk:
They’re $2.00 each. These are actually good souvenirs.
Ryo
Yes, I think so. Anyway, could you tell me how to get to the museum?
Clerk:
Oh, it’s about 20 minutes away by foot.
Ryo
I see.
Clerk:
If you plan on walking, you may want to keep an eye on your luggage. You really shouldn’t be hauling any of those around you know.
Ryo
Right. I’ll go back to the hotel and leave it then.
Clerk:
I suggest that you do. There are a lot of friendly faces out there, and it’s almost impossible to distinguish the crooks from the good people.
Ryo
I really appreciate you telling me this. Sorry, here’s $10.00. I’ll take five of those.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions about your destination:

  1. Who are the people considered safe to ask directions from?
  2. Would you know how high the crime rate is when it comes to robbery?
  3. Do you know how foreigners are being scammed in those areas?
  4. What can you do to protect yourself from these people?
  5. Are the police trustworthy in that country?
  6. Do they have a hotline you can call in case you get yourself into trouble?
▼▼▼
ACT

Final Activity:

Discuss what you can do to avoid being scammed, robbed or swindled during your business trips and the things you need to do if you get into trouble with offenders.
