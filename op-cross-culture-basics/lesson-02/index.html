BIZ TIP
Language is a verbal expression of its culture. Here are some phrases to talk about the language(s) of your country:
… is the most widely spoken language in…
the language is similar to…
the real beauty of the language is…
SEE

Ken is asking Ivan about the Russian language.

Ken: I’m taking a business trip to Moscow in three months. Do you think I should learn Russian before I go, Ivan?
Ivan: Yes, it will help. There’re many languages that are spoken in Russia, but Russian is the most widely spoken language.
Ken: I see. Is it hard to learn?
Ivan: Many people say it’s hard, especially its pronunciation and grammar. Russian is similar to Ukrainian and Belarusian.
Ken: I don’t know those languages.
Ivan: Then, I recommend learning Russian. The real beauty of the language is its creativity and flexibility. You can create so many words that are not in the dictionary. Also, if you want to be an astronaut, you need to be able to speak Russian. Russian is the international language of space.

TRY

Answer the following questions about your country:

  • What language is spoken in your country?
  • Do you think the language of your country is a beautiful language? Why?
  • Are there any words or ideas in your country’s language that are hard to translate?
  • What is a word in your country‘s language that you often see or hear on TV, the news or the Internet? What does it mean?
  • How has the culture of your country affected its language(s)?
  • Ask your trainer questions about the language(s) of his or her country. Check your understanding by asking follow-up questions.
▼▼▼
ACT

Explain the characteristics of the language of your country and how it has affected its culture.
