PART 1

Answer three of the questions below and any follow-up questions from your trainer. Make sure to give examples to support your point.

  • Where is your country located, and how is the location connected with culture?
  • What is the most widely spoken language in your country, and how is it connected with culture?
  • What is family life typically like in your country, and how is it connected with culture?
  • What is the food culture like in your country, and how is it connected with the overall culture?
  • What is work life typically like in your country, and how is it connected with culture?
  • How are sports enjoyed in your country, and how are they connected with culture?
  • What is marriage typically like in your country, and how is it connected with culture?
  • What forms of entertainment are enjoyed in your country, and how are they connected with culture?
  • What is a celebration that may help foreigners understand some parts of your country’s culture?
