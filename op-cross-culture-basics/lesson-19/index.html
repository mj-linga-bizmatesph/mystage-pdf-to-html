OTHER PROGRAMS CROSS CULTURE BASICSILESSON 19
THE NEXT GENERATION
19
BIZ TIP
The cultures our next generation grows up in and the ones we grew up in can be very
different. Use these phrases to talk about ways both sides can work together:
… is/are far more… than…
… as we grow older
… need to make an effort to…
SEE

Ken is talking with his Saudi Arabian colleague, Omar, about the next generation.

Omar: How was your vacation? You visited your relatives, right?
Ken: Yes, I did. It was nice to see everyone again. But I had trouble communicating with my thirteen-year-old niece. I had no idea about any of the pop culture she mentioned.
Omar: I hear you. My teenage daughter’s life is so different from mine. She watches foreign dramas and plays online video games. I didn’t have any access to those when I was her age. She is far more globalized than I am.
Ken: But it’s hard to understand the world young people see.
Omar: I agree. She doesn’t feel comfortable talking about her interests. Maybe she is afraid that I wouldn’t like her ideas.
Ken: You‘re probably right. We fear new things as we grow older. I think there are things we can learn from the next generation. Of course, we know several things they should learn, too.
Omar: We need to make an effort to communicate with young people more.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer the following questions about the next generation in your country:

  • How is life different between your generation and the next?
  • What are the advantages the next generation has that you don’t? Disadvantages?
  • What are some values of your country’s culture that can be helpful for the next generation?
  • What are some values of your country’s culture that can be harmful for the next generation?
  • How can you work in partnership with the next generation and help them?
  • Ask your trainer questions about the next generation of his or her country.
    Check your understanding by asking follow-up questions.
▼▼▼
ACT

Explain the culture of the next generation in your country and how your generation can help them to succeed in life.
