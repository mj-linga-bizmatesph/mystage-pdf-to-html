20
PART 1

Answer three of the questions below and any follow-up questions from your trainer. Make sure to give examples to support your point.

  • What is a key event in your country’s history, and how is it connected with culture?
  • What is a unique law of your country, and how is it connected with culture?
  • Which industry represents the uniqueness of your country, and how is it connected with culture?
  • How do people generally socialize in your country, and how is it connected with culture?
  • What is your country’s idea of happiness, and how is it connected with culture?
  • Which neighboring country has influenced your country’s culture and how?
  • What was schooling like for you, and how did it affect your individual culture?
  • What was your childhood like, and how did it affect your individual culture?
  • What are some generation gaps in your country, and how can different generations help each other?
▼▼▼
PART 2

Select three of the above topics and ask your trainer. Be sure to follow up with questions to really understand your trainer.

PART 3

Now that you have completed this program, what do you think are the benefits of being interested in other cultures? Explain your idea to your trainer.
