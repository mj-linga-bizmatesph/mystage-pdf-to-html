UNDERSTANDING THE BASIC E-MAIL STRUCTURE
BIZ TIP

A good business e-mail should follow a standard structure. Below is the basic format:

  • Subject line
  • Greeting
  • Opening
  • Main message
  • Closing
SEE

The basic e-mail structure:

Welcome to the E-mail Writing Basics program
Subject line

Dear Mr. Suzuki,
Greeting


Thank you for taking the E-mail Writing Basics course.
Opening


I am pleased to say that you will learn a lot in these lessons.
Attached is the list of topics we have in this program.
Main message


I hope you enjoy studying the entire course.
Closing


Best regards,
Taro Sato
