4
CLOSING YOUR E-MAIL
BIZ TIP

The tone should be consistent throughout the e-mail. Here are some expressions to help you close your e-mail:

Formal:
  • Thank you for your patience.
  • I look forward to doing business with you.
  • I hope we can meet again soon.
Casual:
  • Thanks.
  • Can't wait to start our new business venture.
  • See you again soon.
SEE
Formal
Dear Mr. Silverton,

I am writing to let you know that the issue with your online account has been resolved.

Please contact us if you encounter other issues. Thank you for your patience.

Best regards,
Casual
Hi, Bob.

Just wanted to let you know that I've already fixed the problems you reported.

Let me know if you have any other
problems. Thanks.

Cheers,
TRY

Practice closing your e-mail for these situations:

FORMAL CASUAL
A prospective client you met at a convention A former colleague you haven't seen in years
ACT

Write a short e-mail with a good closing for the following situations:

  1. A former colleague wanting to meet with you and catch up.
  2. If time permits, write a similar work-related e-mail.
