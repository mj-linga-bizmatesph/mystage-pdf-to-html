11
GIVING AN UPDATE
BIZ TIP

A good update should be clear and concise. Go straight to the point by using these sample expressions:

Formal:
  • I'd like to inform everyone that...
  • I’d like to give you some updates on…
  • I'm pleased to say that...
Casual:
  • Just an update.
  • Just a quick heads up.
  • I’m glad to tell you that…
SEE
Formal
Dear project team,

I’d like to inform everyone that the directors have already approved our request for…

Best regards,
Casual
Hi, everyone.

Just an update. The directors have already given us the OK to…

Cheers,
TRY

Write a short update about the following:

SITUATION FORMAL CASUAL
A bonus to all employees for a job well done last year
New staff members transferring from the HK office
Upcoming projects for next year
ACT

Write a short update for the following situations:

  1. A huge increase in sales figures last quarter.
  2. If time permits, write a similar work-related e-mail.
