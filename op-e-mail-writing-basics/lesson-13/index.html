13
ASKING FOR FEEDBACK
BIZ TIP

It’s always a good idea to get other people’s opinions. Ask for feedback and suggestions by using these phrases:

Formal:
  • I'd like to get your opinion on...
  • I look forward to hearing your feedback on…
  • Your feedback is highly appreciated.
Casual:
  • What are your thoughts on...?
  • Please let me know what you think.
  • Any thoughts or suggestions?
SEE
Formal
Dear Ms. Nakamura,

I’d like to submit a proposal for our next project. Please see the attached file.

I look forward to hearing your feedback on the new concept.

Best regards,
Casual
Hi, Yuki.

Attached is a new concept for our next project.
 
Please let me know what you think.

Cheers,
TRY

Practice asking for feedback for the following situations:

READER ASK FOR FEEDBACK ON
Your boss Your first client presentation yesterday
Your close colleague The report you prepared for your foreign boss
Your teammates The proposed venue for the next company party
ACT

Write a short e-mail and ask for feedback for the following situations:

  1. Ask your team about the possible activities for your next get-together.
  2. If time permits, write a similar work-related e-mail.
