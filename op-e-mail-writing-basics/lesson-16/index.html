16
WRITING WITH FEELING
BIZ TIP

One way to connect with your reader is to write with feeling. Here are some expressions you can use:

Formal:
  • It was an honor to have met you.
  • I certainly had a wonderful time during...
  • I couldn’t help but feel sad when…
Casual:
  • It was a pleasure meeting you.
  • I really had a great time during…
  • I felt really blue when…
SEE
Formal
Dear Ms. Ellis,

I certainly had a wonderful time during my visit to Jakarta. I greatly appreciate your team’s hospitality.

I hope to see everyone again soon.

Best regards,
Casual
Hi, Myra.

I really had a great time during my visit to Jakarta. You guys are the best.

See you again next time.

Cheers,
TRY

Practice confirming details for the following situations:

SITUATION TONE/EMOTION FORMAL CASUAL
Your boss introduced you to the President of the company. appreciation
A close colleague is about to leave the team. sadness
You were assigned to lead a big project. excitement
ACT

Write a short e-mail and express how you feel for the following situations:

  1. You’re back home after visiting an overseas office.
  2. If time permits, write a similar work-related e-mail.
