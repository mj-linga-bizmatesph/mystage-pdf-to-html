18
APOLOGIZING THROUGH E-MAIL
BIZ TIP

Sometimes it’s not enough to just apologize. We also need to explain what happened and take some action. Follow the steps below:

Formal:
  • I would like to apologize for…
  • Allow me to explain what happened.
  • Rest assured that…
Casual:
  • I’m so sorry about…
  • What happened was…
  • You know that…
SEE
Formal
Dear Yoshi,

I would like to apologize for my actions yesterday.

Allow me to explain what happened. I completely misunderstood what you said about…

Rest assured that I meant no disrespect and that it won’t happen again.
Casual
Hi, Yoshi.

I’m so sorry about my actions yesterday.

What happened was I completely misunderstood what you said about…

You know that I didn’t mean it and that it won’t happen again.
TRY

Practice confirming details for the following situations:

SITUATION FORMAL CASUAL
Your client You cancelled an appointment. Emergency at home
Your boss You were caught sleeping on the job. Stayed up late because friends dropped by
Your senior colleague You had a disagreement. You strongly opposed his opinion.
ACT

Write a short apology e-mail in the following situations:

  1. A VIP filed a complaint because of poor service from a new employee.
  2. If time permits, write a similar work-related e-mail.
