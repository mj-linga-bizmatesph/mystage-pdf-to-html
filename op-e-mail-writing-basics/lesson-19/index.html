EXPRESSING SYMPATHY
BIZ TIP

Expressing sympathy in writing can be very tricky. Make sure you communicate the right tone by using these sample expressions:

Formal:
  • My heart goes out to…
  • I can only imagine what you're going through.
  • I just want you to know that...
Casual:
  • I'm sorry to hear about…
  • I know you’re going through a lot…
  • Hang in there.
SEE
Formal
Dear Sarah,

My heart goes out to you and your family.

I can only imagine what you’re going through. I just want you to know that the whole team is here for you.

Best regards,
Casual
Hi, Sarah.

I’m sorry to hear about your grandfather.

I know you’re going through a lot, so hang in there. Everyone is here for you.

Cheers,
