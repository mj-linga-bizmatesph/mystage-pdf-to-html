Using proper mechanics
8
BIZ TIP
The last thing you should do before sending is to read over your e-mail for proper mechanics. To check the mechanics:

  • Use your spell check feature
  • Double-check your grammar
  • Check for correct punctuation
  • Check attachments and e-mail addresses
SEE

Find the grammar, punctuation, and spelling mistakes. There is one mistake in each sentence.

I am writing in respond to your request for a transfer.
