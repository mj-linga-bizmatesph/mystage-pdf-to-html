WRITING SHORT REQUESTS/INQUIRIES
12
BIZ TIP
When writing requests or inquiries, the recipient should have a good idea of what you wish to accomplish. To do this, you should:
  • Begin with background information
  • Be clear and specific about your request
  • Set a deadline
SEE

Example:

  • Beginning with background information
    I would like to inform you that…
    It has been brought to my attention that…
    Lately, we’ve been having some issues on…

  • Being clear and specific
    Could you please…
    I’d like to ask you to…
    May we request…

  • Setting a deadline
    It would be great if you could let me know by…
    I look forward to hearing from you ASAP, so I could…
    We would greatly appreciate it if you could send it this weekend.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  1. How often do you send requests or inquiries to your main office?
  2. What are your requests/inquiries usually about?
  3. What background information do you tell your recipients?
  4. How soon do they take action upon receiving your request?
▼▼▼
ACT
Part 1:
Write an e-mail to your main office requesting to give a new staff member access to the system.

Part 2:
For further practice, write a similar, work-related e-mail. Do it for homework if you run out of time.
