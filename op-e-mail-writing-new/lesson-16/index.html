WRITING AN APOLOGY
16
BIZ TIP
A good apology means we care and that we value people. Before asking the reader to accept your apology, you should:
  • Stating your regret
  • Summarizing what happened
  • Acknowledging the inconvenience
  • Offering the remedies
SEE

Example:

  • Stating your regret
    I’m sorry for…
    I would like to apologize for…
    We are terribly sorry…

  • Summarizing what happened
    Please allow me to explain what happened.
    After fact-finding, here’s what I discovered.
    As you may know, we are…

  • Acknowledging the inconvenience
    I totally understand how you feel about…
    I understand how much trouble this may have caused you.
    We know how inconvenient it was for you to…

  • Offering remedies
    Rest assured that I will…
    I would like to make it up to you by…
    We want to rectify the situation by…
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  1. Has any client complained about a delay in your product/service?
  2. How did you explain what happened?
  3. How much inconvenience do you think the incident might have caused?
  4. What sort of remedy did you offer?
▼▼▼
ACT
Part 1:
Write an apology to a client for the delay in fixing some issues on a website you are developing for them.

Part 2:
For further practice, write a similar, work-related e-mail. Do it for homework if you run out of time.
