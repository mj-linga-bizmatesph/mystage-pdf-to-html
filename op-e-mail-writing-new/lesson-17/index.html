OTHER PROGRAMS E-MAIL WRITINGILESSON 17
GIVING INSTRUCTIONS
17
BIZ TIP
Giving instructions is like making a request, except that you are telling your recipient EXACTLY what you need them to do.
  • State your purpose
  • Make your instructions short and very clear
  • Include attachments, if necessary
  • Offer to answer questions
SEE

Example:

  • Stating the purpose
    I’m writing to explain the necessary steps to…
    Here is the process we need to follow to…
    I’ve outlined the steps to…

  • Making your instructions short and clear
    There are four steps in the process.
    First…; next,…; and then,…
    It would be great if you could send it by….

  • Including attachments
    Below are the details of…
    Please see the attached file.
    Here’s the data you’ll be needing.

  • Offering to answer questions
    Please don’t hesitate to contact me for clarification.
    If you have any questions, please let me know.
    Please let me know if you have any questions.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  1. In what situations do you give your vendor some instructions?
  2. Do they always do exactly what you ask them to?
  3. What sort of attachments do you include to give them a better idea of what they need to do?
