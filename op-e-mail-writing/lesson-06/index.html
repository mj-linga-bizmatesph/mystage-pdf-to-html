OTHER PROGRAMS E-MAIL WRITINGILESSON 6
Adding a personal touch
6
BIZ TIP
By adding a personal touch, you can show the reader that you truly value them. To add a personal touch:

  • Use common everyday words
  • Use names
  • Write the way you speak
  • Express how you feel or felt
SEE

Impersonal Example:

Hi, Jon.

It was a pleasure for us to meet your colleague when we visited your office on Dec. 13.

We will review your requests and revise our proposal accordingly. At MMB, we strive to deliver the best possible solution to meet every one of our customers’ needs.

We hope to develop a long lasting relationship with your company.

Personal Example:

Hi, Jon.

Thanks so much for introducing me and Taka to Susan. She was so polite and made us feel right at home.

Please give us a few days to include your requests in our revised proposal. I’m confident that we can find exactly what you are looking for.

I look forward to working with you and Susan.
▼▼▼
TRY

Add a personal touch to the following sentences.

Impersonal Personal touch
This is to confirm receipt of your e-mail.
We are pleased to inform you that…
I hope this e-mail finds you well.
As per your request,…
We express our utmost gratitude for everything you have done.
▼▼▼
ACT
Part 1:
Come up with a reason to reply to the personal e-mail above. Then, write the e-mail by adding a personal touch.

Part 2:
For further practice, write a work-related e-mail. Do it for homework if you run out of time.
