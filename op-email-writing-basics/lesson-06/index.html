6
WRITING A GOOD OPENING
BIZ TIP

Your main message should start with a good opening. Connect with your reader right away by using the examples below:

Formal:
  • I hope all is well with you.
  • How are things in Bangkok?
  • I hope you had a great time at the office.
Casual:
  • I hope everything's OK.
  • How's it going?
  • I hope you had a blast at the office.
SEE
Formal
Casual
Dear Karen,

How are things in Bangkok?

Our boss mentioned you have finally closed the contract. Congratulations! We're all excited because...

All the best,
Hi, Karen.

How's it going?

The boss said you got the deal in Bangkok. Great job! We're all excited because...

Cheers,
