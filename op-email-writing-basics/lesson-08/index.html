8
ORGANIZING THE DETAILS
BIZ TIP

Arrange the details of your message in bullet points to make them super clear. Here are some expressions to help you get started:

Formal:
  • The following is an overview of...
  • For your reference, here are the details:
  • The agenda is as follows:
Casual:
  • Here are the details:
  • Here's the data:
  • Here's the agenda:
SEE
Formal
Casual
Dear Ms. Tanaka,

I am writing to confirm our meeting on Monday. The agenda is as follows:
  • Project Objectives
  • Schedule
  • Assignment of tasks
Please let me know if you have any questions.
Hi, Yuka.

Just a note to confirm our meeting on Monday. Here's the agenda:
  • Project objective
  • Schedule
  • Assignment of tasks
Please let me know if this is OK.
