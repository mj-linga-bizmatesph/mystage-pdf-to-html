9
STATING THE CALL TO ACTION
BIZ TIP

Inform your reader what needs to be done at the end of your message. State the action plan clearly by using the phrases below:

Formal:
  • So our next course of action is...
  • What we need to do now is to...
  • It would be great if you could...
Casual:
  • Our next step now is...
  • So, I want us to...
  • Can you...?
SEE
Formal
Casual
Dear Scott,

I am writing to let you know that we've begun Phase 3 of the project.

So our next course of action is for you to coordinate with the designers about...

Best regards,

Hi, Scott.

Just wanted to let you know that we've already started Phase 3 of the project.

Our next step now is for you to discuss with the designers about...


Thanks,
