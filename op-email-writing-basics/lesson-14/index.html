SENDING A CONFIRMATION
14
BIZ TIP
Confirming a decision in writing is very important in business. Confirm the details with your reader by using these phrases:
Formal: Casual:
To confirm what we decided,…
To make it official,…
This is to confirm that…
Just to confirm,…
To make sure everyone understands,…
So we agreed to…
SEE

Formal

Casual
Dear Rodney,

Thank you for your time yesterday during my product presentation.

This is to confirm that I will be sending the samples to the Taipei office.

Best regards,
Hi, Rodney.

Thanks for letting me present our products yesterday.

Just to confirm, I’ll send the samples to the Taipei office.

Thanks,
▼▼▼
TRY

Practice confirming details for the following situations:

SITUATION FORMAL CASUAL
Your next meeting’s agenda
The date, time and venue of your appointment with a client
The design for your new product proposal
▼▼▼
ACT

Write a short e-mail and confirm the details for the following situations:

  • Confirm the goals you’ve set for your team next quarter.
  • If time permits, write a similar work-related e-mail.
