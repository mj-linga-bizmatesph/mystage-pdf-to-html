OTHER PROGRAMS E-MAIL WRITING BASICSILESSON 17
OFFERING FURTHER ASSISTANCE
17
BIZ TIP
Another way to establish connection in writing is to offer further assistance. Show your willingness to help by using the expressions below:
Formal: Casual:
Don’t hesitate to contact me if...
Feel free to get in touch with me if...
Let me know if I can assist you further.
Let me know if you need anything.
I'd be happy to assist you in...
I’d be happy to give you a hand in…
SEE

Formal

Casual
Dear Ms. Adams,

Thank you for choosing to upgrade your subscription to Business Plan 3.

You can now enjoy a much faster Internet connection. Please let me know if I can assist you further.

Best regards,
Hello, Nancy.

Thanks for upgrading your subscription to Business Plan 3.

You can now enjoy a much faster Internet connection. Let me know if you need anything.

Thanks,

▼▼▼
TRY

Practice offering further assistance for the following situations:

SITUATION FORMAL CASUAL
You helped a colleague proofread her report.
You helped your boss organize his files on his PC.
You helped a new contact who’s looking for a new job.
▼▼▼
ACT

Write a short e-mail and offer further assistance for the following situations:

  • Your new foreign supervisor asked you to set up your first team meeting.
  • If time permits, write a similar work-related e-mail.
