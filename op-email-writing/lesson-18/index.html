OTHER PROGRAMS E-MAIL WRITINGILESSON 18
Writing a report
18
BIZ TIP
The purpose of sending a report is to keep your recipient updated on a certain progress. Effectively write a report by:
  • Stating the purpose
  • Giving an overview
  • Going over the details
  • Attaching supporting documents, when necessary
SEE

Example:

  1. Stating the purpose of the report
    This report covers…
    This report outlines…
    I am writing this report to keep you informed on…

  2. Giving an overview
    The report has been divided into 3 parts.
    You’ll see that I’ve listed…, then came up with…, and finally suggested…
    I’ve broken the figures down into… and …

  3. Going over the details
    The following are projected figures for…
    The graph below illustrates a comparison between…
    The table below shows…

  4. Attaching supporting documents
    For your reference, I’ve attached…
    I’ve also included the data (see attached) that…
    I’ve attached some screenshots that show…
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  1. How often do you send e-mail reports to your boss or main office?
  2. What do you usually report about?
  3. What information do you include in your report?
  4. What sort of attachments do you include?
