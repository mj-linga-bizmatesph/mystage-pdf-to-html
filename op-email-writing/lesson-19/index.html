OTHER PROGRAMS E-MAIL WRITINGILESSON 19
Communicating good news and bad news
19
BIZ TIP
Be careful when communicating good news or bad news as they both have different structures. Take a look at the boxes below:
Good News:
  1. The good news
  2. Background information
  3. Appreciation
Bad News:
  1. Background information
  2. The bad news
  3. Reassurance
SEE

Example:

Good News Bad News
  1. Delivering the good news
    Congratulations on your promotion!
    Welcome to MMB!
    I’m happy to inform you that…
  1. Giving background information
    It’s been a very wonderful experience to…
    We truly appreciate your time in…
    I can only imagine what it’s like to…
  1. Giving background information
    Just to recap on the highlights of…
    This all started with…
    Among all candidates, you have…
  1. Going over the bad news
    We understand the effort you’ve put into…However,…
    We are sorry to hear that…
    Please accept our condolences…
  1. Showing appreciation
    Thank you for all your hard work.
    May you serve as an inspiration to…
    Again, welcome to the MMB family.
  1. Giving reassurance
    Rest assured that I will…
    I just want to let you know that…
    We hope that you continue to…

Answer these questions.

  1. What kind of news do you often send an e-mail about?
  2. How do you often start your message when delivering bad news?
  3. Which type of news is easier for you to communicate? Why?
  4. In what situations must you communicate good news and bad news right away?
