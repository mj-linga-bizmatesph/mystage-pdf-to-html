YOUR INDUSTRY: WHAT IS IT?
1
BIZ
TIP
Some people may not know your industry. So it’s important to describe your industry clearly. You can do this by following these key points:
Short background: In the past,…
Present situation: Nowadays,…
Future situation: In the future,…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., is explaining the watch industry.

Jun:

We manufacture timepieces such as watches and clocks. My company belongs to the watch industry. The first watch was made over 500 years ago. In the past, people wore watches to tell time.

However, nowadays, many people wear watches for different purposes. People use watches to tell time, for fashion, and for status. So, a watch is not just a tool to tell time, but it is jewelry and a status symbol. And there is a huge market in Japan. In the future, technology will become more and more advanced in our industry. Watches will become multi-functional, like the cell phone. So, we must become more high-tech.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer the following questions about your industry:

  • Which industry does your company belong to?
  • Has your company changed industries over the years?
  • What was your industry like in the past?
  • What is your industry like nowadays?
  • What will your industry be like in the future?
  • How will your industry change due to technology, globalization, foreign competition, etc.?
