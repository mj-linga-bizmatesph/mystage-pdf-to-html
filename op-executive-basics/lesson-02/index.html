BIZ TIP
Some people may be interested in your company’s history. When talking about your company’s history, tell them the following:
Current situation: We currently have…
Start of the company: Our founder, Mr. Kotaro Tanaka, worked…
Important highlights: So, in 1956, Mr. Tanaka…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., is explaining his company’s history.

Jun:

We currently have over 4000 employees working in over 200 sales offices and 8 factories in Japan, but we started as a really small company. Our founder, Mr. Kotaro Tanaka, worked in a watch repair shop. However, his customers often complained that foreign watches were difficult to use.

So, in 1956, Mr. Tanaka developed a simple watch that was much cheaper than foreign watches and founded Synchron. Then, in 1961, Synchron had its first success when Mr. Tanaka released the first all-steel watch. Everyone thought it had a modern design but still looked elegant. Then, in 1968,…

TRY

Answer the following questions about your company’s history:

  • How big is your company now?
  • When did your company start?
  • What do you know about the founder of the company?
  • How did your company become successful?
  • Can you give other highlights in your company’s history?
  • Are there any highlights in your company that you were a part of?
