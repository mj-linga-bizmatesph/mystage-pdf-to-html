YOUR CULTURE: WHAT MAKES IT UNIQUE?
3
BIZ TIP
Your corporate culture describes what you value as a company. Focus on these key points to explain your corporate culture.
Values: Our company has always valued…
Characteristics of your corporate culture: So our company encourages…
The good points or bad points of the culture: This gives employees the opportunity to…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., is explaining his company’s corporate culture.

Jun:

Our company has always valued innovation. We believe in making products that are special. So, our company encourages all employees to propose new ideas to top management. This gives employees the opportunity to communicate directly with the president.

Another area that we value is teamwork. We believe that a great product cannot be made alone. So, our company encourages employees from other departments to communicate freely. This makes it easy for employees to share ideas throughout the company.

TRY

Answer the following questions about your corporate culture:

  • How would you describe the culture in your company?
  • How is it different from other companies?
  • What does your company value?
  • What are some characteristics of your corporate culture?
  • What are the good points and bad points of these characteristics?
  • How do you feel about this kind of culture?
