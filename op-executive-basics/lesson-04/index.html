YOUR VISION: WHERE IS THE COMPANY HEADING?
4
BIZ
TIP
By knowing who your audience is, you can communicate your corporate vision clearly and effectively. Use the format below to communicate your message:
Problem: A big problem in our society is that…
Vision: Our goal is to…
How: We can do this by…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., is explaining his company’s corporate vision.

Jun:

A big problem in our society is that our lives are controlled by time. It is stressful to live in a time-controlled world if you cannot manage your time well. So, our goal is to give you back the control in your life.

We can do this by making our watches do more than just tell time. Our watches are multi-functional tools that act like your personal coach. They let you lead the life you want.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer the following questions about your corporate vision:

  • What is the problem that your company is trying to solve?
  • What is your company’s goal to solve this problem?
  • How can your company do this?
  • How do you communicate your vision to employees?
  • Does everyone in your company understand your vision clearly?
  • What’s your next step to bring you closer to your vision?
