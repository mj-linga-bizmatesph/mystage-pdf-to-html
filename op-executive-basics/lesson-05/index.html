YOUR PRODUCTS: WHAT MAKES THEM SPECIAL?
5
BIZ
TIP
When explaining your products and/or services, focus on how customers can benefit from them by discussing the areas below:
Feature: Our watches are made from…
Advantage: So, they are better because…
Benefit: This means, you don’t need to worry about…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., is explaining his company’s products and services.

Jun:

We manufacture wrist watches, wall clocks and digital timepieces. All our watches are made from stainless steel and strong metals. So, they are better because they are more durable than other watches. This means, you don’t need to worry about repairs.

In addition, all our watches are automatic. They are better because they don’t need batteries unlike other wrist watches. This means, you don’t need to worry about changing batteries every few years.

TRY

Answer the following questions about your products/services:

  • What are your products/services?
  • What is your most popular product/service?
  • Name some features of this product/service.
  • What are its advantages over other products/services?
  • What benefits would your customers get?
  • What future products/services will your company sell?
