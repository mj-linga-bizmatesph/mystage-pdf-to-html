YOUR CUSTOMERS: WHO ARE THEY?
6
BIZ
TIP
Sometimes, explaining a wide customer base can be difficult. Organize what you want to say by using the structure below:
Customer groups: We have two main customer groups:…
Explanation: Corporations consist of…
Examples: For example,…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., is describing his company’s customers.

Jun:

Our company has a wide customer base in Japan. We have two main customer groups. First is corporations. Corporations consist of offices, factories, and other business facilities. For example, PL Plastics uses our clocks in all of their factories and offices.

The second group is consumers. They are mostly businessmen in their 30s and 40s. They like quality products but cannot buy a luxury watch. For example, many young managers at my company are fans of our products.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer the following questions about your customers:

  • Do you have a wide customer base in your country? Overseas?
  • Describe the customer groups for your company’s products.
  • Can you give some examples for each customer group?
  • Which customer group brings the most profit?
  • Which customer group is giving your company difficulty?
  • Which customer is the most important? Explain.
