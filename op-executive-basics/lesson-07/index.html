THE COMPETITION: WHAT MAKES YOUR COMPANY BETTER?

BIZ
TIP
Explaining your company’s advantage can be challenging. Use the format below to clearly explain your company’s advantage over your competitors:
Overview of the market: Our industry can be divided into three main groups…
Direct competitors: There are 4 major…
Comparison: Synchron is priced slightly higher than the other manufacturers, but…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., is describing his company’s competitors.

Jun:

Our industry can be divided into three main groups: affordable casual watches, mid-range business watches, and high-end luxury watches. Although Synchron competes in all three markets, our strength is in the mid-range business watches. There are 4 major watch manufacturers in the mid-range market.

Synchron is priced slightly higher than the other manufacturers, but our watches offer additional features. All our watches are handmade so they are of very high quality. Although we have less brand awareness than the other three companies, we are viewed as a watch for serious collectors.

Answer the following questions about your competitors:

  • What is the competition like in your industry?
  • Which company is the leader in your market?
  • Which companies are your direct competitors? Indirect competitors?
  • How is your company better than its competitors?
  • How would you describe your brand image compared to your competitors?
  • What can your company do to beat the competition?
