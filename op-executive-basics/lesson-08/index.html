HIRING: WHAT KIND OF PEOPLE DO YOU LOOK FOR?

BIZ TIP
The people who join your company can make or break the business. Use the suggested flow below to explain the kind of people you’re looking for:
Preferred qualities: One of the key qualities we look for…
Hiring process: We have a very strict hiring process…
Why applicants choose your company: Our company is a stable company showing…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., is describing his company’s hiring process.

Jun:

One of the key qualities we look for in our applicants is passion for our business. We sell more than just a tool to tell time. We are experts in our industry. A quality that I personally look for in applicants is how detailed they are. We are a watch company, so our people need to be very organized and meticulous.

We have a very strict hiring process. The first step is our written test where we check for general knowledge. Then we have small group interviews to get an idea of each applicant’s personality type and communication skills. Next, we have individual interviews and finally, the president’s interview. Each year, we get over 2000 applicants. Synchron is a stable company showing good business growth, which makes us appealing to new grads.
