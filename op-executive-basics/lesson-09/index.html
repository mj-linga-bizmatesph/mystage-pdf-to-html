BIZ TIP
A company is a group of people together with a shared objective. The type of people you have makes your company unique. Explain your company’s workforce by highlighting the key areas below:
Size: We have a total of…
Demographics: Our workforce is made up of…
Comments on diversity: In order to become more global…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., is describing his company’s workforce.

Jun:

We have a total of 4000 employees. About half of them work in manufacturing plants while the other half work at our head office, sales offices, and research centers. We also have 200 based overseas in our foreign branch offices.

Our workforce is made up of about 75% men because we are a watch manufacturer for men. However, we have many women in marketing, design, and admin positions. Many of our sales staff and engineers are men in their mid 30s to late 40s. In order to become more global, we have started hiring foreigners, especially from China. So I hope to see a more diverse workforce in the next few years.
