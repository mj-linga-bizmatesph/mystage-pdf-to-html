STRENGTHS: WHAT MAKES YOUR COMPANY THE BEST?
11
BIZ
TIP
Your company may have a lot of strong points. So, organize your ideas clearly and confidently. You can do this by following the format below:
Strong point: One of our strengths is…
Supporting information: In fact,…
Results: This helped our brand become…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., explains his company’s strengths.

Jun:

I think Synchron Corp. has a lot of strong points. One of our strengths is innovation. We constantly come up with new and unique ideas. In fact, we have been awarded as the “Innovator of the year” 2 years ago. We were featured in many magazines. This helped our brand become more popular.

Another strong point is our pricing. Our products are very affordable. In fact, we’re priced 20% less than our competitors. This has led to our X1 model being named the most popular watch in our area.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer the following questions about your company’s strengths:

  • What are your company’s strengths?
  • What is your strongest point?
  • What information do you have to support this?
  • What were the results?
  • Do you think your strengths will continue to be strengths in the future?
