OTHER PROGRAMS EXECUTIVE BASICSILESSON 12
WEAKNESSES: WHAT AREAS DO YOU NEED TO IMPROVE?
12
BIZ
TIP
All companies have weaknesses. What’s important is how you plan to improve them. Discuss your weaknesses confidently by using the format below:
Weakness: We need to improve…
Action plan: Our plan is to…
Target result: We hope this will…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., explainshis company’s weaknesses.

Jun:

Our company also has areas for improvement. We need to improve our incentive system. Many of our sales reps leave the company because of this. Our plan is to place more funds in our incentive scheme. We hope this will reduce the number of sales reps leaving the company next year.

However, I think our greatest weakness is our limited market. We need to improve our presence in the global market. Our plan is to change our mindset. I think we can do this by reminding everyone about the corporate vision. We hope this will open more opportunities in the near future.

TRY

Answer the following questions about your company’s weaknesses:

  • What are your company’s weaknesses?
  • What do you think is your weakest point?
  • How does it compare to your competitors’?
  • How could you improve this?
  • What are your target results?
