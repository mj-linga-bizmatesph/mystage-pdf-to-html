TIP
Business opportunities come in different forms. This means they can be difficult to explain sometimes. Use the flow below to help organize your explanation:
Problem: The problem is, there are/aren’t…
Solution: So, to solve this,…
Target market: Our target market will be…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., discusses opportunities for his company.

Jun:

I think more and more people are getting into retro-style fashion. It’s easy to find women’s accessories. The problem is, there aren’t too many retro-style accessories for men, especially watches. You can find some online, but they can be expensive.

So, to solve this, we will bring back the old-fashioned pocket watch. It will have the same gentleman look, but built with modern technology. Our target market will be businessmen who want to look more mature. I think this will help us enter the global market.
