THREATS: WHAT CHALLENGES ARE YOU FACING?
14
BIZ
TIP
Your staff may not know the threats your company is facing. Communicate your ideas clearly so they can help find solutions. Use the format below:
The threat: One of the biggest challenges we are facing is…
Risks involved: This could greatly impact…
Action plan: So, my idea is to…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., explains his company’s challenges.

Jun:

One of the biggest challenges we are facing is mobile technology. Almost everyone uses a smart phone. And all these smart phones have built-in clocks. Who needs a watch when you have a phone? I think this idea appeals mostly to younger generations.

This could greatly impact our sales and the industry in the future. So, my idea is to partner with mobile phone manufacturers. We can develop smart watches that are compatible with any mobile phone. People who couldn’t access their phones at work or at school would find these watches useful.

TRY

Answer the following questions about your company’s challenges

  • What sort of challenges are you facing as a company?
  • What could be your biggest threat?
  • What are the risks involved?
  • How would this impact your company? The industry?
  • What are your company’s action plans?
