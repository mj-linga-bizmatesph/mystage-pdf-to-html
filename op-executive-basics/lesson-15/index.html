TIP
Training programs vary from company to company. You can clearly provide details of your training program by using the format below:
Background: Our customer service reps need to be…
Objective: So, we give them product knowledge training to…
Details: This 2-week training program covers…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., explains his company’s training programs.

Jun:

Our customer service reps need to be familiar with all our products. So, we give them product knowledge training to make sure that they can answer any question from customers. This 2-week training program covers not only the product concepts and features, but also maintenance of the products.

Another example is the management training program. We want our future leaders to be ready to take on a management position when necessary. So, we give them management training to make sure they have the tools and skills to manage people. The management training covers everything from basic management principles to communication skills.
