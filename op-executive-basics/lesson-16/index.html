MOTIVATION: HOW DO YOU KEEP YOUR STAFF HAPPY?
16
BIZ TIP
Every company has its own unique way to motivate its employees. Use the flow below to explain how you keep your staff happy:
Purpose: We believe in rewarding and recognizing…
Method: So, we give out incentives…
Impact: Our sales reps have become…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., explains how they motivate employees.

Jun:

We believe in rewarding and recognizing our employees. So, we give out incentives to sales reps who exceed their monthly targets and publish their photos in our company newsletter. Because of this, our sales reps have become much more determined to meet their goals.

We also want our employees to suggest new ideas to top management. So, we ask all employees to submit at least 1 new idea every week and give a cash prize to the employee with the best idea. This system has helped us implement many new programs.
