CUSTOMER SUPPORT: HOW DO YOU OFFER SUPPORT?
17
BIZ
TIP
Give specific examples when you discuss how you help your customers. By using the format below, you can clearly explain how you go the extra mile for your customers:
Situation: There was one time when…
Extra support: On top of that, we also…
Result: In the end, the customer was…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., discusses his company’s customer support.

Jun:

There was one time when a foreign customer went to our store to buy a men’s watch. A few days later we received an email saying he bought an item with a factory defect. That was the first time it happened after many years. We apologized and asked him to return the item so we can check it.

It really was a factory defect, so we replaced the item. On top of that, we also gave him a matching women’s watch as a gift for his wife. In the end, the customer was very thankful. We found out he has a YouTube channel doing product reviews. He gave our product and our customer support some really good feedback.
