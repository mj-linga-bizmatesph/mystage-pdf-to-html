CSR: HOW DO YOU GIVE BACK TO SOCIETY?
18
BIZ TIP
Corporate Social Responsibility is a very broad topic. Be specific by giving some examples. Follow the format below to make your explanation very clear:
Purpose: It’s very important for us to…
CSR activity: For instance, all our…
Impact: We have received many letters directly from customers…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., explains his company’s CSR activities.

Jun:

As a manufacturing company, it’s very important for us to be environmentally friendly. For instance, all our packaging is made from recyclable materials. We have received many letters directly from customers praising us for this.

We also feel it’s important for all our employees to give back to society. So, we hold tree-planting events for our employees and their families. We have been recognized as one of the most socially responsible companies in Japan.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer the following questions about CSR:

  • What sort of CSR activities does your company do?
  • Does your company sponsor any event or organization?
  • What impact does your CSR activities have on society?
  • How does it affect your brand image?
  • What other CSR activities would you like to do in the future?
