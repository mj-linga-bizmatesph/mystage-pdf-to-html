MANAGEMENT STYLE: HOW DO YOU RUN THE COMPANY?
19
BIZ
TIP
We all have our own management style. You can explain your management style clearly by following the format below:
Reasoning: I think it’s important for everyone to…
Approach: So, I try to…
Result: As a result,…
SEE

Jun, an executive at Synchron Corp., describes his management style.

Jun:

I have over 300 employees in my division in 3 different locations. So, it’s impossible for me to communicate one-on-one with all members. However, I think it’s important for everyone to know what I am thinking about all the time.

So, I try to send my message out every time to my staff. I have a daily online journal to share what I’m doing. I have weekly meetings with our managers. And I visit each office at least once a week and talk to some staff. As a result, everyone feels they can trust me.

TRY

Answer the following questions about your management style:

  • Why do you think it’s important to manage your staff?
  • What is your management approach?
  • What have been the results of your approach?
  • How do your employees feel about this approach?
  • Do other executives in your company take a similar approach?
  • Do you think you will need to change your approach in the future?
