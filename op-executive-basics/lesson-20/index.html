Explain the following topics as clearly as possible. Use the Biz Tips to guide you.

  1. Strengths: what makes your company the best?
    Strong point: One of our strengths is…
    Supporting information: In fact,…
    Results: This helped our brand become…
  2. Weaknesses: what areas do you need to improve?
    Weakness: We need to improve…
    Action plan: Our plan is to…
    Target result: We hope this will…
  3. Opportunities: what areas can you take advantage of?
    Problem: The problem is, there are/aren’t…
    Solution: So, to solve this,…
    Target market: Our target market will be…
  4. Threats: what challenges are you facing?
    The threat: One of the biggest challenges we are facing is…
    Risks involved: This could greatly impact…
    Action plan: So, my idea is to…
  5. Training: how do you keep your employees sharp?
    Background: Our customer service reps need to be…
    Objective: So, we give them product knowledge training to…
    Details: The 2-week training program covers…
  6. Motivation: how do you keep your staff happy?
    Purpose: We believe in rewarding and recognizing…
    Method: So, we give out incentives…
    Impact: Our sales reps have become…
  7. Customer Support: how do you offer support?
    Situation: There was one time when…
    Extra support: On top of that, we also…
    Result: In the end, the customer was…
  8. CSR: how do you give back to society?
    Purpose: It’s very important for us to…
    CSR activity: For instance, all our…
    Impact: We have received many letters directly from customers…
  9. Management style: how do you run the company?
    Reasoning: I think it’s important for everyone to…
    Approach: So, I try to…
    Result: As a result,…
