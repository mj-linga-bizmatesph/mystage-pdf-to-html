10
REVIEW

Imagine you are having a full interview with a reporter. Try to use some of the media interview techniques you’ve learned in this course.

  1. What is your role in your company?
  2. What is a typical day for you?
  3. What is your business?
  4. What makes your products special?
  5. How has globalization affected your company over the last few years?
  6. What barriers does your company have from becoming truly global?
  7. How important is entrepreneurial spirit in your company?
  8. What can your company do to encourage an entrepreneurial culture?
  9. What is your company’s global leadership program?
  10. What qualities do you look for in leaders?
  11. How have the standards of business ethics changed since your younger days?
  12. Why do you think they’ve changed so much? Are these changes good?
  13. How important is good health to you as an executive? What do you do to maintain it?
  14. How have your views on healthy living changed since your younger days?
  15. What are some tips that you can give in building a strong network?
  16. Would you consider your social network as a safety net?
  17. What personal traits do you look for in applicants? What are some red flags when hiring people?
  18. If you were to give some advice to people interested in applying to your company, what would you say?
