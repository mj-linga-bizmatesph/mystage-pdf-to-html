TECHNOLOGICAL CHANGES: ARE YOU READY?
BIZ TIP

One way to give impact to your answer is to make an analogy or a comparison of your idea with something more common. This will not only emphasize your answer but also make your answer easier to understand. You can do this by:

  • Explaining your idea
  • Making your analogy
  • Making sure your analogy is understood if needed
SEE

Ken, an executive director, is being interviewed by a reporter about technological changes.

Reporter:
What sort of impact have technological advances had on your company? And how has your company responded?
Ken:
Technology has had a huge impact on our industry. And the change has come so suddenly that it’s wiped out many of our competitors that could not adapt. Much like how the dinosaurs went extinct, technology is that giant meteor. Luckily for us, we got started very early to move towards digitization by going paperless and internalizing our IT functions. Moreover, our products have become very tech-oriented as well. However, we also need to be careful not to allow technology to overrun the company. We need to remember that our ideas come first, and technology supports them; not the other way around.
TRY

Discuss these questions with your trainer.

  1. What do you think of the meteor analogy in Ken’s response?
  2. How has technology affected your industry? Your company?
  3. Has technology made the lives of your employees better?
  4. Are you a supporter of advances in technology?
  5. How do you think technology will affect your business in the future?
ACT

Answer the question. Imagine you are having an interview with a reporter and you are being asked about how technology has impacted your business.
