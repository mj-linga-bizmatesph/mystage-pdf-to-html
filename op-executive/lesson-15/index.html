YOUR COMPETITION: DO YOU FEAR THEM?
BIZ TIP

Another way to support your views is to use quotes of famous people. If your opinion is supported by something a well-respected business leader has said, it gives your answer more credibility. You can do this by:

  • Expressing your views
  • Supporting it with a famous quote
  • Giving examples
SEE

Ken, an executive director, is being interviewed by a reporter about competitors.

Reporter:
Recently, we’ve seen several competitors enter your industry. They are priced lower and offer very similar products. Do you see them as a threat?
Ken:
Based on the products that these companies have released, we do not see them as a threat. They have not shown any originality at all. They have basically copied our products and priced themselves lower. As Disney once said, “We can innovate faster than our competitors can imitate.” Our products are technically far superior than our competitors’ products, and I believe the market can see the difference.
TRY

Discuss these questions with your trainer.

  1. Who are your main competitors?
  2. How are you different from your competitors?
  3. Are there any upcoming competitors that you see as a threat?
  4. Have your competitors copied anything that your company has done?
  5. Has your company copied anything that your competitors have done?
ACT

Answer the question. Imagine you are having an interview with a reporter and you are being asked about your company’s competitors.
