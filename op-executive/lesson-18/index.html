FUTURE PRODUCTS: WHAT CAN WE EXPECT?
BIZ TIP

Passion is a quality that is received well by reporters. Your passion will also be translated as your company’s passion for the business. You can express passion by:

  • Speaking with emotion
  • Getting the reporter to imagine your vision
  • Leaving the reporter wanting to know more
SEE

Ken, an executive director, is being interviewed by a reporter about future products.

Reporter:
I’m very interested to learn about Synchron’s upcoming products. Is there anything you can share with me about your new products?
Ken:
We are working on many new products that will revolutionize the industry. Just imagine a watch that gives you control of your life. So many of us are stressed because our lives are controlled by time. We want our customers to have the freedom to live their lives without the worry of time. I wish I could tell you more, but you will have to wait for the press release.
TRY

Discuss these questions with your trainer.

  1. How often does your company release new products?
  2. How open is your company to sharing information on upcoming products with the media?
  3. What new products is your company developing?
  4. How successful do you think your new products will be?
  5. How do you think your market needs will change over the next few years?
ACT

Answer the question. Imagine you are having an interview with a reporter and you are being asked about your company’s future products.
