REVIEW

Imagine you are having a full interview with a reporter. Try to use some of the media interview techniques you’ve learned in this course.

  1. How has technology affected your industry? Your company?
  2. How do you think technology will affect your business in the future?
  3. Do you think your company will continue to need more IT people in the future?
  4. What can you do to attract more IT people?
  5. How important is work-life balance in your company?
  6. What does your company do to support work-life balance for its employees?
  7. How diverse is your workforce?
  8. Do you see your workforce becoming more diverse in the future? If yes, how so?
  9. Who are your main competitors?
  10. How are you different from your competitors?
  11. Does your company believe that the customer is always right?
  12. How important is customer voice to your company?
  13. What’s a major change that you had to implement in your company?
  14. What other major changes do you foresee in your company?
  15. What new products is your company developing?
  16. How do you think your market needs will change over the next few years?
  17. What is your next step in your company?
  18. What opportunities are available to you inside and outside of your company?
