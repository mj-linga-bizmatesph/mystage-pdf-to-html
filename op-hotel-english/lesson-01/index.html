Giving directions
to the hotel
1
BIZ TIP
Getting lost can be very frustrating for the guest. Be sure to give clear directions by:
Telling them how long it'll take to get to the hotel
Using common landmarks
Reassuring the guest that you are here to help
SEE

Example:

Guest: Hi! Could you tell me how to get to your hotel?
Staff: Where are you now?
Guest: I’m at Narita International Airport.
Staff: Go to Ginza Station. From the station, walk three blocks and then turn right.
Guest: That’s easy. Thank you.

(However, the guest ends up getting lost.)

Bizmates style:

Guest: Hi! Could you tell me how to get to your hotel?
Staff: Certainly. May I know where you are now?
Guest: I’m at Narita International Airport.
Staff: I see. OK, take the Narita Express for one hour to Tokyo Station. Then, take the Marunouchi Line for three minutes to Ginza Station.
Guest: OK. So, Narita Express and Marunouchi Line to Ginza Station.
Staff: Yes, and we are just a 5-minute walk from Ginza Station. Go out Exit A1 and you’ll see a big Star Coffee sign. Walk towards Star Coffee for 3 blocks, and then turn right at the corner of Ginza Shopping Mall. We’re located next to the mall.
Guest: OK, got it. Thank you so much for your help.
Staff: You’re very welcome. Please feel free to call again and ask for Yuji if you lose your way.
▼▼▼
TRY

Practice giving directions to the following places.

  • From the airport to the nearest train station.
  • From the train station to your hotel.
  • From your hotel to the nearest shopping mall.

ACT

Role play

Give your guest directions from the airport to your hotel.
