Accommodating simple
requests
6
BIZ TIP
Your guests may ask for simple requests or have questions during their stay. Meet their needs and make them feel like VIPs by:
Letting them know you will take care of it right away
Exceeding their expectations
Showing willingness to help again
SEE

Example:

Guest: Excuse me. Could you please call me a cab?
Staff: OK.

(A few minutes later)

Staff: Your cab is here.
Guest: Thank you.

Bizmates style:

Guest: Excuse me. Could you please call me a cab?
Staff: Certainly, Mr. Young. I’ll call one for you right away.

(A few minutes later)

Staff: Mr. Young, your cab has arrived.
Guest: Thank you.
Staff: I’d be happy to tell the driver where to take you if you’d like.
Guest: That’s very thoughtful of you. Yes, I’d like to go shopping in Ginza.
Staff: Very well. Also, please feel free to call the hotel if you have any trouble. Have a good time, Mr. Young.
Guest: Thank you very much.
Staff: My pleasure, Mr. Young.

TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What sort of requests do you often get from guests?
  • What was the last request you received?
  • Were you able to exceed the guest’s expectations?
  • What can you say to offer further assistance?
