OTHER PROGRAMS HOTEL ENGLISHILESSON 9
Checking out
9
BIZ TIP
Your goal is to make your guests regular customers. Make them remember your hotel so they will come back. You can do this by:
Asking how their entire stay was
Offering some last-minute assistance
Parting with a warm and friendly farewell
SEE

Example:

Guest: Hello. I’d like to check out.
Staff: OK. One moment please.

(Clerk performs check-out procedures)

Staff: Thank you for waiting. We have now completed the check-out procedures. Thanks for staying at La Grandia.
Guest: OK. Thank you.

Bizmates style:

Guest: Hello. I’d like to check out.
Staff: Certainly, Mr. Smith. How did you find your stay at La Grandia?
Guest: Everyone was very helpful and so friendly.
Staff: Thank you for saying so. Did you have a chance to use some of our facilities?
Guest: Yes, the spa and gym were great. I also really liked the restaurant.
Staff: I’m glad to hear that. Would you like me to call a taxi for you?
Guest: Yes, I’m going to Tokyo Station.
Staff: Certainly. I’ll call one for you right away. Thank you for staying at La Grandia. Have a safe trip home, Mr. Smith.
Guest: Thanks again. I’ll surely visit you guys again next year.

TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What other questions could you ask your guest when they check out?
  • What last-minute assistance could you offer when they check out?
  • What warm and friendly farewell wishes can you say to them?
