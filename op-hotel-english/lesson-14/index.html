Explaining different
travel options
14
BIZ TIP
Your guests would likely want to visit other places and enjoy their vacation. Help them travel from place to place by:
Providing travel options along with travel times
Suggesting the best option
Supporting your recommendation
SEE

Example:

Guest: So how do I get to the Tsukiji fish market from here?
Staff: You can take a taxi.
Guest: A cab? Can’t I just walk there?
Staff: You can, but it’s almost a kilometer away. A taxi could get you there more quickly.
Guest: Well, I’m not in a hurry. Maybe I’ll walk.

Bizmates style:

Guest: So how do I get to the Tsukiji fish market from here?
Staff: It’s either a 3-minute taxi ride or a 20-minute walk, Mr. Hyun. You can go there by train too, but it’s very inconvenient.
Guest: I see.
Staff: If you have time, I would recommend going on foot. It’s a beautiful day for a walk, and you could do some sightseeing or visit some shops along the way. The directions are very simple too.
Guest: That’s a good idea. I think I’ll walk. Thank you for your suggestion. .
Staff: You’re most welcome, Mr. Hyun.

TRY

Practice explaining the best travel option to these places.

  • From your hotel to a nearby tourist spot
  • From your hotel to a nearby fine dining restaurant
  • From your hotel to a nearby shopping mall
