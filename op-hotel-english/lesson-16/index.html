Handling special
requests
16
BIZ TIP
There may be times when your guests make requests that are impossible to do. Make them feel like VIPs by:
Checking your understanding (rephrasing)
Offering an alternative solution
Giving details
SEE

Example:

(Guest is calling room service)

Guest: Hi. I’d like to order the sushi roll set. This is Mitch Foley. Room 304.
Staff: Certainly, Mr. Foley.
Guest: Is it possible not to have raw fish in my sushi? I just don’t like anything raw.
Staff: I see. So you don’t like anything raw?
Guest: Yes, that’s what I said.
Staff: I’m sorry. This is a set, so we cannot change the rolls.
Guest: OK, then I’ll just go get something to eat myself.

Bizmates style:

Guest: Hi. I’d like to order the sushi roll set. This is Mitch Foley. Room 304.
Staff: Certainly, Mr. Foley.
Guest: Is it possible not to have raw fish in my sushi? I just don’t like anything raw.
Staff: So, you’d like your fish cooked, Mr. Foley?
Guest: Yes, that’s right.
Staff: In that case, I suggest some of our other sushi rolls without raw fish.
