Explaining the hotel’s
facilities
4
BIZ TIP
The more your guests use your hotel facilities, the more they will appreciate their stay. Organize your explanation by:
Mentioning the location of key facilities
Giving important details on some facilities
Getting their interest in visiting these facilities
SEE

Example:

Staff: We have a gym, a spa, a swimming pool, and a gift shop…
Guest: I see. What floor is the gym on?
Staff: The 7th floor.
Guest: Thanks.

Bizmates style:

Staff: We hope you have a chance to use some of our facilities. In the basement, there are several small shops, including a gift shop, a flower shop, and a jewelry store. We also have a coffee shop and a restaurant on the first floor, near the lobby. On the 7th floor, you can find the gym and spa.
Guest: Great! Is the gym still open?
Staff: Yes. It’s open from 8AM to 9PM. It’s a very nice gym. The treadmills are right by the window, so you can work out while enjoying the view.
Guest: Wow. I can’t wait to see it!
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What are some of your most visited hotel facilities?
  • Where are they located?
  • What can you say to get your guest’s interest in checking them out?
▼▼▼
ACT

Role play.

Explain your hotel’s floor layout by explaining and giving some information about your facilities.
