Assisting guests with
disabilities
5
BIZ TIP
Some guests may need special attention due to physical disabilities. Offer proper care by:
Offering assistance
Considering the guest’s disability
Showing willingness to help again
SEE

Example:

(A guest in a wheelchair seems to be looking for something.)

Staff: Hello. Do you need some help?
Guest: Yes, where are the restrooms?
Staff: Just over there.

Bizmates style:

Staff: Hello Mr. Wong. Is there something I can help you with?
Guest: Yes, where are the restrooms?
Staff: There’s one on either side of the lobby. However, the one on this side is a little bigger. This way please.
Guest: Thank you.
Staff: And the first stall has wider doors for easy access. Please let me know if there’s anything you need.
Guest: I appreciate your help.
Staff: It’s my pleasure, Mr. Wong.

  • What could you do to offer assistance?
