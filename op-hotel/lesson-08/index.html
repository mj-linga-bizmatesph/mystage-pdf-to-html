Giving information on
corporate services
8
BIZ TIP
Some guests come to Japan on business, so be ready to give information on the hotel's corporate services. You can do this by:
Understanding their needs
Recommending a solution that meet their needs
Reassuring them that their needs will be met
SEE

Example:

Guest: How much is it to rent a meeting room for a couple of hours?
Staff: It starts from 8,000 yen/hour, Mr. Harrison.
Guest: OK, that’s fine. Can I rent a room this Friday from 5pm-7pm?
Staff: Oh there’s a premium charge after 5pm. So, it’ll cost 10,000 yen/hour.
Guest: I see. OK, I’ll try to have it earlier. Let me contact the other 5 people.
Staff: Oh, but this room is only for a max of four people. The next room up is 12,000 yen/hour.
Guest: It keeps getting more expensive.

Bizmates style:

Guest: How much is it to rent a meeting room for a couple of hours?
Staff: May I ask for how many people and the day and time?
Guest: Well, I have 5 people sitting in with me. I need the room for a videoconference this Friday from 5pm-7pm.
Staff: Let’s see… meeting room A can accommodate up to 10 people. It’s already equipped with LED widescreen and overhead projector. That’ll be 12,000 yen/hour, inclusive of high-end videoconferencing equipment.
Guest: OK, is it available this Friday?
Staff: Yes, I can have everything set up by Friday morning so that you can make sure you have everything before the meeting.

TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What types of corporate services does your hotel offer?
  • What are your meeting rooms like?
  • What features could you highlight when describing them?
