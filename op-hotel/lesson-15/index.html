Showing how to use
a device
15
BIZ TIP
Some guests may find it difficult to use devices or machines, especially when the instructions are in Japanese. Show them how to use devices like these by:
Organizing your instructions (First,… Next,.. And then,… etc.)
Using "We" and “this” when giving instructions
Offering further help if they're still having trouble
SEE

Example:

Guest: Could you help me with the ATM, please?
Staff: Sure, Ms. Chen. What’s the problem?
Guest: The instructions are all in Japanese. I’d like to withdraw some cash.
Staff: I see. You need to press that button.
Guest: This button?
Staff: No, the one below… OK, now you must enter your PIN and the amount you want to withdraw. Then, you press that button.
Guest: Sorry, which button?
Staff: Here, let me press it for you.

Bizmates style:

Guest: Could you help me with the ATM, please?
Staff: I’d be happy to, Ms. Chen. What exactly can I help you with?
Guest: The instructions are all in Japanese. I’d like to withdraw some cash.
Staff: I see. First, we press this button right here.
Guest: OK. And then?
Staff: Next, we enter the PIN and the amount you want to withdraw. Finally, we press this button here.
Guest: OK, got it. Thank you so much for your help.
Staff: My pleasure, Ms. Chen. Please feel free to call my attention if you need further assistance.

TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What kind of devices does your hotel have? (ATMs, ice machines, etc.)
  • Which device do most of your guests have trouble using?
  • Why do they have trouble with it?
▼▼▼
ACT

Role play.

Imagine your guest is having trouble using a device or equipment in your hotel. Give instructions and show him/her how to use it.
