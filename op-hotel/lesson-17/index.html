Handling complaints
effectively
17
BIZ TIP
Handling complaints is an opportunity to wow your guests. Effectively handle complaints by:
Showing that you understand their situation
Apologizing for what happened
Reassuring them that you will take action immediately
SEE

Example:

Guest: Hi. I can’t sleep because of the noise nearby.
Staff: We are sorry about that, Ms. Lane. Which room is it coming from?
Guest: No, it’s from outside. Sounds like a construction of some sort.
Staff: It must be the renovation in the gym.
Guest: Yes, but how am I supposed to sleep?
Staff: I’m terribly sorry, Ms. Lane.
Guest: Well, I can’t sleep.
Staff: I’m really sorry, Ms. Lane.

Bizmates style:

Guest: Hi. I can’t sleep because of the noise nearby.
Staff: We are sorry about that, Ms. Lane. What kind of noise do you hear?
Guest: Sounds like a construction of some sort.
Staff: I see. It must be the renovation in the gym. I understand it’s been a long day of travel for you and that you need to rest now. I sincerely apologize if you’ve been booked near the renovation site. Let me see if we can move you to a room on the other side of the hotel. May I call you in a few minutes?
Guest: OK. Thank you.
