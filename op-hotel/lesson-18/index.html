OTHER PROGRAMS HOTEL ENGLISHILESSON 18
Going the extra mile
18
BIZ TIP
Rebuilding trust with your guests after a complaint ensures customer satisfaction. Rebuild trust and go the extra mile by:
Offering an explanation about the issue
Telling them you've taken care of the situation
Adding something of value
SEE

Example:

Staff: Hello, Ms. Lane.
Guest: I hope you have some good news for me.
Staff: Yes. I’ve changed your room. Your new room is 1508.
Guest: OK, thank you.

Bizmates style:

Staff: Hello, Ms. Lane. Thank you so much for waiting. May I explain what’s going on?
Guest: Yes please.
Staff: To better serve our guests, we are currently renovating and expanding the hotel’s gym. We are terribly sorry. You shouldn’t have been booked on the 7th floor.

So, I’ve transferred you to another room on the 15th floor. I can assure you that you won’t hear the same noise from up there.
Guest: Thank you so much. So can I move there now?
Staff: Yes, Ms. Lane. I’ve sent a bellhop to assist you with your bags. He should arrive in a few minutes. And, as a small token, your new room has bigger windows to give you a much better view of Tokyo’s skyline. It also has…
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What was the last complaint you got from a guest?
  • What explanation did you offer about what happened?
  • What else did you do for the guest to go the extra mile?
