THANK YOU FOR COMING TODAY.
BIZ TIP

The first step in a job interview for both the applicant and the interviewer is to make a good first impression. The impression you make on them will be their image of your company. So, create a good image by:

  • Greeting your applicant
  • Introducing yourself
  • Showing appreciation
SEE

Examples

  • Greeting your applicant
  • Good morning. Mr. George Khoo? Welcome to Soft Solutions!
  • Hi, how are you doing? Sorry, how do you say your name?
  • Hi, George. Is it Mr. Kuu or Koo?
  • Introducing yourself
  • I’m Akira Watanabe, the HR Manager. It’s nice to meet you.
  • My name is Akira Watanabe. I’m the HR Manager here at Soft Solutions. Pleasure to meet you.
  • I’m Akira, the HR Manager here at Soft Solutions. Nice to meet you.
  • Showing appreciation
  • I’m sorry to keep you waiting. My meeting went a bit over.
  • Did you get lost while coming here?
  • Thanks for coming today.
