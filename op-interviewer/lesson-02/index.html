LET ME EXPLAIN THE FLOW OF THE INTERVIEW.
BIZ TIP

Job interviews make applicants nervous. When this happens, it becomes difficult to get their real answers. A relaxed applicant will be more willing to open up. Make your applicant feel comfortable by:

  • Making small talk
  • Prompting the start of the interview
  • Explaining the flow of the interview
SEE

Examples

  • Making small talk
  • Were you able to watch the game last night?
  • Did you have a hard time finding your way to our office?
  • How long did it take you to get here?
  • Prompting the start of the interview
  • Let’s begin our interview.
  • Let’s get started with the interview.
  • Shall we start the interview?
  • Explaining the flow of the interview
  • This interview has three parts… First,…
  • First, I will introduce our company… Then…
  • So in this interview,… Finally,…
