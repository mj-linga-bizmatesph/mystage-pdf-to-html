TELL ME ABOUT YOUR WORK EXPERIENCE.
BIZ TIP

By really getting the details of the applicant’s work experience, you’ll get a better understanding of the applicant’s skills and capabilities. Get to know him/her more by:

  • Asking about work experience
  • Asking WH questions
  • Following up with specific questions
SEE

Example

    Asking about past work experiences
  • Can you tell me about your last job?
  • Can you tell me about your experience in this industry?
  • Can you tell me about your relevant work experience?
    Asking WH questions
  • How long were you there?
  • What results did you achieve?
  • Why did you leave that company?
    Following up with specific questions
  • Did you have a good working relationship with your team?
  • Were you satisfied with the results?
  • Was it the right choice to change jobs?
TRY

Your trainer is your applicant. Come up with WH questions and specific questions for each situation.
