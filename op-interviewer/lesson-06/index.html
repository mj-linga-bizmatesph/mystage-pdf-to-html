WHY ARE YOU INTERESTED IN OUR COMPANY?
BIZ TIP

Applicants apply to many companies without really doing much research on the company. So it's a good idea to find out if the applicant really wants to join your company or if the applicant is just looking for any job. Here are some questions you can ask:

  • Why are you interested in our company?
  • What do you know about our company?
  • How do we compare to our competitors?
SEE

Examples

  • Why are you interested in our company?
  • What made you choose our company?
  • What is your reason for applying?
  • What made you interested in the position?
  • What do you know about our company?
  • Tell me what you know about our company.
  • How did you know about our company?
  • What do you think of our products?
  • How do we compare to our competitors?
  • How are we different from our competitors?
  • What do you think is our competitive advantage?
  • What makes us stand out from our competitors?
TRY

Your trainer is your applicant. Come up with follow-up questions for the applicant’s responses.

  INTERVIEWER’S QUESTION APPLICANT’S RESPONSE INTERVIEWER’S FOLLOW-UP QUESTIONS
TRY 1 What is your reason for applying? I’m looking for a company where I can really apply my skills.  
TRY 2 Tell me what you know about our company. Your company is one of the biggest in the industry.  
TRY 3 What do you think is our competitive advantage? Quality. I think your products are known for its quality.  
ACT

Imagine your trainer is your applicant. Find out why the applicant is interested in your company, what he/she knows about your company, and how your company compares to your competitors. Be sure to follow up on each of the applicant’s responses.
