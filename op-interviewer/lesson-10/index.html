MOCK INTERVIEW
PART 1

Imagine that you will be interviewing an applicant. Prepare for the interview by reviewing the steps you learned in Lessons 6-9.

TOPICS STEPS
LESSON 6:
WHY ARE YOU INTERESTED IN OUR COMPANY?

1.

Why are you interested in our company?

2.

What do you know about our company?

3.

How do we compare to our competitors?
LESSON 7:
WHY DID YOU LEAVE YOUR PREVIOUS COMPANY?

1.

Why do you want to leave your current company?

2.

What is your relationship with your boss?

3.

What would you change about your company?
LESSON 8:
TELL ME ABOUT WHEN YOU HAD TO…

1.

Tell me about a time you had to make a big decision in your job.

2.

What process did you follow?

3.

What was the result of your decision?
LESSON 9:
WHAT MOTIVATES YOU AT WORK?

1.

What motivates you in your work?

2.

How has your motivation changed over the years?

3.

What is the most important thing in your next job?
