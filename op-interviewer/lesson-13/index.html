WHAT DO YOU THINK OF…?
BIZ TIP

Opinion questions allow the interviewer to check the applicant’s way of thinking. For example, you can ask the applicant’s opinion on an industry trend or a news topic. You can do so by asking the following questions:

  • What do you think of..?
  • What do you mean?
  • Why do you think so?
SEE

Example

  • What do you think of our new website?
  • What’s your opinion on the spread of AI technology?
  • How do you feel about the recent news of the merger between Companies A and B?
  • How do you see the future of our industry changing in the next few years?
  • What do you mean?
  • Can you be a little more specific?
  • Can you give an example?
  • Can you explain that further?
  • Why do you think so?
  • What would you do differently?
  • How would it affect you?
  • Why is this important to you?
TRY

Your trainer will be your applicant. Come up with follow-up questions for the responses.

  INTERVIEWER’S QUESTION APPLICANT’S RESPONSE INTERVIEWER’S FOLLOW-UP QUESTIONS
TRY 1 What do you think of our new website? I think it’s simple and easy to view.  
TRY 2 Can you be a little more specific? The site doesn’t use many colors and there’s very little text.  
TRY 3 What would you change about it? Well, I’m not an expert. But I might add more graphics to make it stand out more.  
ACT

Imagine your trainer is your applicant. Learn more about the applicant’s way of thinking by asking for the applicant’s opinion. Be sure to follow up on each of the applicant’s responses.
