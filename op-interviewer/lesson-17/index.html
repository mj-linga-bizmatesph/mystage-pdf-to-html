WE HAVE SEVERAL OTHER PEOPLE TO INTERVIEW.
BIZ TIP

When you're unsure or undecided if your candidate is the right person for the job, you can buy some time before deciding by using the following expressions:

  • We have several other people to interview.
  • Do you have other interviews lined up?
  • When do you hope you can start?
SEE

Examples

  • We have several other people to interview.
  • We’ve just started recruiting for this position.
  • We need to carefully consider who will best fit this job.
  • We still have a few more people to go through.
  • Do you have other interviews lined up?
  • Are you scheduled to be interviewed elsewhere?
  • Do you have other interviews?
  • Have you applied to other companies?
  • When do you hope you can start?
  • How long can you wait?
  • Are you looking to start immediately?
  • Can you wait until the end of the month?
TRY

Your trainer will be your applicant. Come up with follow-up questions for the responses.

  INTERVIEWER’S QUESTION APPLICANT’S RESPONSE INTERVIEWER’S FOLLOW-UP QUESTIONS
TRY 1 We have several other people to interview. I understand. I can wait a while.  
TRY 2 Do you have other interviews lined up? Yes, I have two more scheduled this week.  
TRY 3 When do you hope you can start? I’d like to give my company one month’s notice.  
ACT

Imagine your trainer is your applicant. Find out how much time you have before you need to make a decision.
