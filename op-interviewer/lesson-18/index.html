THIS IS A GREAT OPPORTUNITY.
BIZ TIP

If you know that you have the right candidate for the job, you can make an effort to sell the position by showing how the job matches the applicant’s goals and interests.

  • This is a great opportunity.
  • You will have the chance to…
  • I believe this will be an invaluable experience for you.
SEE

Examples

  • This is a great opportunity.
  • This is not something that comes around very often.
  • It’s not every day that an opportunity like this comes up.
  • This is an exciting time to join our company.
  • You will have the chance to…
  • You’ll be able to…
  • This will be our chance to…
  • You’ll have the opportunity to…
  • I believe this will be an invaluable experience for you.
  • I know you will really like it here.
  • I can see you fitting right in with everyone.
  • You seem to be the type of person we’ve been looking for.
TRY

Your trainer will be your applicant. Come up with follow-up questions for the responses.
