LESSON 19
19
HERE ARE THE NEXT STEPS.
BIZ TIP

At the end of the interview, make sure the next steps are clear. End on a positive note by giving your applicant clear instructions and showing appreciation for their time.

  • Here are the next steps.
  • You should hear from us within a week.
  • Thank you for visiting us.
SEE

Examples

  • Here are the next steps.
  • The next step is for us to…
  • After this interview, we’ll…
  • From here, I’ll be sitting down with the HR Director to…
  • You should hear from us within a week.
  • We’ll contact you in 3 business days.
  • Please expect an email from our Recruiting Department by the end of the week.
  • I’ll be sure to get in touch with you no later than Friday.
  • Thank you for visiting us.
  • I appreciate your making the time to meet with me today.
  • Thank you for your time today.
  • I really enjoyed learning more about you.
TRY

Your trainer will be your applicant. Come up with follow-up questions for the responses.

  INTERVIEWER’S COMMENT APPLICANT’S RESPONSE INTERVIEWER’S FOLLOW-UP RESPONSE
TRY 1 The next step is for us to communicate with the field manager for possibly a second interview. I understand. Is there anything I should prepare while I wait?  
TRY 2 You should hear from us within a week. Great. Would you be contacting me by email or phone?  
TRY 3 Thank you for visiting us today. Not at all. I really enjoyed meeting you. I hope we meet again.  
ACT

Imagine your trainer is your applicant. End the interview on a positive note by clearly communicating the next steps and showing appreciation for their time.
