CONVEYING YOUR OPINIONS
BIZ TIP

It can be difficult to join a discussion with several speakers. You can get your point across clearly by:

  • Showing understanding
  • Giving your opinion
  • Providing reasons to support your opinion
      • We should implement a customizable system for each local region.
      • Yes, that way, the new system can meet each region’s needs.
      • Exactly. I don’t think it’s possible to find a standard enterprise resource planning F&A system that will meet all the regions’ needs.
      • Umm… excuse me. May I interrupt?
      • We should implement a customizable system for each local region.
      • I understand your point, but our mission is to have one system that will work for all regions. That’s why we need to standardize and simplify procedures so that we can have consistency among all regions. By doing so, we will improve efficiency and communication companywide.
      • But some of these differences are external and bound by legal regulations.
      • I completely understand. So, we will require some customization. However, in my opinion, that should be our last option, not our first. By streamlining our processes first, it will be much easier to select and implement a standard ERP system. And that will result in minimal customization.
      • That makes sense, Ken.
    • Respond to these comments by showing understanding, giving your opinion, and providing reasons to support your opinion.
      • I think our corporate language should be English so that we can be a global company.
      • I think all employees should start working from 5AM as it’s proven that people are more efficient in the mornings.
      • I think that all employees should wear a company uniform to build loyalty.
ACT

Role play.

Have a discussion with your trainer on a topic of interest. Show understanding, give your opinion, and give reasons to support your opinion.
