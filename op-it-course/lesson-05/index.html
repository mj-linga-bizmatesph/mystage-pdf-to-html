WRAPPING THINGS UP
BIZ TIP

It is the chairperson’s job to make sure that everything is clear at the end of the teleconference. So, the chairperson should:

  • Summarize key decisions and action plans
  • Mention the schedule of the next teleconference
  • Motivate the participants with some encouraging words
      • Thank you everyone. See you in our next teleconference.
      • Thanks, Ken. See you again soon.
      • Sure. OK, thanks.
      • OK, let’s summarize what we’ve discussed today. We’ve listed up the needs of the local regions and decided to standardize some processes. Is this OK?
        • (Eric and Joe agree.)
      • Joe, your team will do research on data centers. Eric, your team will gather input from the other regions about standardizing some F&A processes. My team will gather information on ERP systems from IT vendors. Do you have any questions so far?
      • All clear, Ken. Thanks.
      • Our next meeting will be on April 25th. This is a very challenging project. However, this is a great opportunity to really improve efficiency and communication within IMAYA Corp. Let’s do great work together. See you again on the 25th.
