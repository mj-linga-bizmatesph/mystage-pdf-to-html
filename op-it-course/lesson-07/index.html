7
GOING OVER THE PROJECT OBJECTIVES
BIZ TIP

It’s important to give background information to new partners. You can do this by:

  • Explaining your company and current situation
  • Clearly defining the project’s objectives and scope
  • Checking their understanding at the end
      • So we’re looking for an ERP system for Finance and Accounting. What system do you suggest?
      • Well, I would need to know a little more about your company, the current situation, and the objectives of implementing such a system.
      • We’re looking for an ERP system for Finance and Accounting. So, before we talk about your system, I’d like to give you some information on our company and current situation. Then, we can look at the objectives and scope of our project. And finally, we can discuss the details of your system.
      • That sounds perfect.
      • Great. I’ve prepared a 5-minute presentation to introduce IMAYA Corp. IMAYA Corp. is one of the largest providers of…
        • (5 minutes later)
      • Thank you, Ken. I now have a much better understanding of IMAYA Corp. and the situation you are facing. Can you tell me more about the project?
      • Sure. If you have any questions, please ask me anytime. From this month, I have been assigned to head a project to implement an ERP…
    • Answer these questions.
      • What information would you need to share about your company and current situation with a business partner for your project?
      • What details of the project would you need to explain?
      • How could you make sure that the other person understands you completely?
ACT

Role play.

Imagine you are meeting with a vendor or business partner. Explain your company and current situation. Then, explain the objectives and confirm.
