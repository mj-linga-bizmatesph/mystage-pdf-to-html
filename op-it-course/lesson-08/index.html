EVALUATING THE VENDOR
BIZ TIP

Selecting the right vendor is essential in successfully deploying an ERP system. Be sure to:

  • Learn as much as you can about their company
  • Know their area of expertise
  • Get references specific to your industry

      • Do you have clients in our industry?

      • I see. So, is that project is still in progress?
      • Yes, and it’s going very well.
      • That’s good to hear. Could you provide three references of satisfied clients from our industry?
      • I can provide dozens of references, but I would need to confirm if any of them are from your specific industry.
    • Answer these questions.
      • What information would you like to know about your vendor?
      • What would you like to know about your vendor’s clients?
      • How would you ask your vendor for references?
