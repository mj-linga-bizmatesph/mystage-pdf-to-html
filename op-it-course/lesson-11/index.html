11
PRESENTING YOUR PROPOSAL
BIZ TIP

Credibility is essential to have others believe in what you are saying. Build credibility by:

  • Highlighting your area of expertise
  • Sharing your experience
  • Showing confidence that you have the answer
      • (Ken is delivering a presentation to the global management team.)
      • Hello, my name is Ken, IT Manager at IMAYA Corp., Japan. Today I would like to propose an ERP system. Let’s begin with…
      • Good morning! Thank you for this opportunity to propose an ERP system for Finance and Accounting. My name is Ken, IT Manager at IMAYA Corp., Japan.
        Before joining IMAYA, I worked as a project manager for almost 10 years. In my previous company, I had a two-year project to implement an ERP system in the Logistics Department.
        Based on my experience, the most important part of the process is to select the right ERP system and vendor.
        I’m happy to say that after meeting with several vendors and testing many ERP systems, I’m confident that we have found the perfect one for IMAYA Corporation.
    • Answer these questions.
      • What could be your proposal the next time you present to your superiors?
      • How would you begin your presentation?
      • What experience or similar issues would you mention to build your credibility?
ACT

Role play.

Make a short introduction on a work-related proposal you’d like to present to your superiors.
