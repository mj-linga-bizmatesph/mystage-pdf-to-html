GETTING YOUR AUDIENCE'S ATTENTION
BIZ TIP

How you frame your proposal could have a big impact on the audience. Get your audience to listen to your presentation by:

  • Identifying the problem
  • Explaining the reasons for the problem
  • Explaining how your proposal could address those issues
      • I would like to introduce three ERP systems that we researched. And I will explain the merits and demerits of each one. Then, I would like to make my recommendation on which system to use.
      • Based on our research, only 3 out of the 10 global IMAYA offices are currently using the same Finance and Accounting system as Japan. This has led to miscommunication between global offices, unnecessary administrative work, and overall inefficiency.
        The reason that most regions are using a different system is that our current system is inflexible and difficult to use. Therefore, we need an ERP system that is flexible, easy to use, and affordable.
        So I’d like to present three ERP systems that meet our RFP requirements, explain their merits and demerits, and then make my recommendations.
    • Answer these questions.
      • What is a problem that your company is facing?
      • What are the reasons for this problem?
      • What could you propose to address this problem?
      • How would you make your proposal?
ACT

Role play.

Make a short presentation to your colleagues, boss, or executives. Frame your proposal accordingly based on who your selected audience is.
