USING DATA AND EXAMPLES
BIZ TIP

Using data and examples is a good way to support your opinion. Provide evidence to support your proposal by:

  • Citing relevant examples
  • Using charts, graphs, or tables
  • Quoting opinions from experts whenever possible
      • I think Option 3 can help us standardize processes in the Accounting Department. So I think it will increase our efficiency throughout the company.
      • Option 3 can help us standardize processes in the Accounting Department, especially with invoicing, credit management, and payroll.
