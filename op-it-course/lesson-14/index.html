CONNECTING WITH YOUR AUDIENCE
BIZ TIP

Facts and figures are important, but the truth is some people base their decisions on feelings. So, connect with your audience by:

  • Being open and honest
  • Showing passion in your proposal
  • Ending your presentation on a positive note
      • I’d like to close this presentation with a short summary of what we’ve covered today
        Out of the three ERP systems we researched, Option 3 is the best. It will be a challenge to ask all regions to streamline and standardize their processes, but we will do our best.
        Thank you. Now, I’d be happy to answer your questions.
      • Before I wrap up, I’d like to mention that deploying an ERP across all global regions is a major challenge. And Option 3 will be the biggest challenge as it will require all regions to streamline and standardize their processes instead of using a customized system.
        However, in the long term, I know Option 3 will give IMAYA the biggest return in terms of improved communication and efficiency.
        We have received huge support from all global regions as they are excited to see IMAYA truly become a global company. And I assure you that our project team will do everything we can to have a successful implementation. Now, I’d be happy to answer your questions.
    • Answer these questions.
      • How can you show your honesty in your next proposal?
      • What would you say to communicate your passion in your proposal?
      • How would you end your presentation on a positive note?

Role play.

Deliver the closing of a presentation and connect with your audience.
