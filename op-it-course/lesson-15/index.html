HANDLING THE Q&A SESSION
BIZ TIP

The most challenging part OF any presentation is the Q&A session. Handle questions professionally and confidently by:

  • Following the 4-step process (invite, check, answer, and confirm)*
  • Anticipating difficult questions
  • Not feeling pressured to have all the answers
      • Now I’d be happy to answer your questions… Yes?
      • When is the scheduled launch?
      • In 6 months.
      • 6 months? For all global regions?
      • Oh no. I’m sorry. I mean for the pilot launch.
      • Now I’d be happy to answer your questions*… Yes, the gentleman in the blue suit?
      • When is the scheduled launch?
      • Do you mean the pilot launch or the full launch companywide?
      • I mean companywide.
      • Well, the first step will be the pilot launch scheduled in May. If that is successful, we plan to go companywide by August. Does that answer your question?
      • Yes, thank you, Ken.
    • Answer these questions.
      • What difficult questions might you get after a presentation?
      • How would you handle them?
      • What would you say if you didn’t have the answers?
ACT

Role play.

Practice answering some typical and difficult questions you may get after a presentation from your trainer.
