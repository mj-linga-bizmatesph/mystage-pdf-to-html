OTHER PROGRAMS IT MANAGERILESSON 3
GIVING A TOUR
3
BIZ TIP
New foreign workers might not be familiar with even basic office rules or manners. So give a comprehensive office tour by:
Explaining each area in detail
Explaining any general rules or manners
Showing how to properly use the facilities
SEE

Yuki gives Sarah a short tour of the office.

Yuki: Let me show you around the office, Sarah. This entrance is for staff. The general rule is that staff use this entrance and the front entrance is only for clients.
Sarah: I see. OK, I’ll keep that in mind, Yuki-san. Thank you.
Yuki: To get into the office, touch your key card on the sensor and the door will open. Let’s try it with your key card. It’ll also record what time you arrived at the office. So, please do this even if the door is open. Please don’t lose your key card. If you do, please report it to me immediately.
Sarah: I understand.
Yuki: OK, let’s go to the elevator hall. The general rule is to let the other people off first before you get on. We’re on the second floor, so most people just take the stairs. The stairs are right over there. Let’s take the stairs to the 2nd floor.
Sarah: Got it, Yuki-san!
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What are some key areas in your office that you would need to introduce during an office tour?
  • What are these areas used for?
  • What are some general rules and manners for proper use of these areas?
  • Why is it important to tell your staff about this?
▼▼▼
ACT

Role play.

Imagine your trainer is a new team member. Give him/her a quick tour by explaining each area in detail, explaining any special rules or manners, and showing how to properly use the facilities.
