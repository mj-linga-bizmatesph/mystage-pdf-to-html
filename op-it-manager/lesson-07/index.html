EXPLAINING THE WORKFLOW
7
BIZ TIP
Just like house rules, foreign workers might have some difficulty understanding and adjusting to the workflow you have in your company because of cultural differences. You can clearly explain the workflow by:
Giving an overview of the process
Using an analogy as an example
Checking for understanding from time to time
SEE

Yuki:
The entire process is linear and if you look at it closely, it’s like a simple video game. You must complete one stage before moving on to the next. Does that make sense?
Sarah:
Yes, it does, Yuki-san. So, what happens if we can’t complete Phase 1 in 2 weeks?
Yuki:
Good question. Then it would delay the start of Phase 2. This could delay Phase 3 and so on, and eventually delay the launch. This is why we always have our daily meetings. Is that clear?
Sarah:
I see. Yes, Yuki-san. Thank you.

TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What specific workflow do you use in your department? What is it called?
  • How does it work?
  • What analogy would you use to explain it?
  • What are some questions you might get from your new member?

ACT

Role play.
