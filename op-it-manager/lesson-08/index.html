OTHER PROGRAMS IT MANAGERILESSON 8
WRAPPING UP THE ORIENTATION
8
BIZ TIP
It may be hard for foreign workers to understand everything you discussed during the orientation, So, as the manager, make sure that everything is clear by:
Summarizing key points
Checking for understanding
Ending on a friendly note
SEE

Yuki wraps up the orientation.

Yuki: Before we end the orientation, let’s go over what we’ve discussed, Sarah. So we talked about your main responsibility, the do’s and don’ts during our office tour, our house rules and company rules, and the workflow.
Sarah: Right.
Yuki: OK, so first, we talked about your main responsibility as a Software Developer. Can you tell me what that is?
Sarah: Sure, Yuki-san. My responsibility is to work with my team to meet the milestones set by our PM to ensure we have a successful launch.
Yuki: Good. And what else do you need to keep in mind?
Sarah: Yes, I need to consider the needs of the company and not just focus on the specs.
Yuki: Right. And what about some of our house rules and…?
. . .
Yuki: Well done, Sarah. I know that it’s tough working in a new environment, so please don’t be afraid to ask me anything.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What do you usually discuss when doing an orientation?
  • Why is it important to go over the key points at the end of a long discussion?
  • How would you know that your new member understands everything?
  • How can you end your orientation on a friendly note?
▼▼▼
ACT

Role play.

Imagine your trainer is a new team member and you’ve just ended the orientation. Summarize the key points, check for understanding, and end on a friendly note.
