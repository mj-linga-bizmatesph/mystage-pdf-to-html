OTHER PROGRAMS IT MANAGERILESSON 11
EMAILING YOUR STAFF
11
BIZ TIP
Your foreign staff might have difficulty understanding a long email message from you. Clarity is very important when communicating with foreigners. Write short, but clear, email messages by:
Explaining the purpose for writing
Giving specific details
Keeping it short
SEE
Yuki emails Sarah about having a meeting.
To:
sanand@softsolutions.com
Subject:
Meeting: New assignment
Hello, Sarah.
I’d like to talk to you about a new assignment. Here’s the agenda:
  1. Overview
  2. Objectives
  3. Deadline
Can we have a 1-hour meeting tomorrow, March 5, at 10AM here in my office?

Cheers,
Yuki
SEND
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • In what situation would you email your foreign staff?
  • What details would you give in your email?
  • How would you end the email?
▼▼▼
ACT

Role play.

Write an e-mail to set up a meeting with your staff. Keep the email short and clear by explaining the purpose for writing, giving specific details, and keeping it short.
