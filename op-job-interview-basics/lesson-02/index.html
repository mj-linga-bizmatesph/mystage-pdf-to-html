BIZ TIP
Respond to e-mail interview invitations quickly and professionally by using the following expressions below in your reply:
Thank you very much for considering me for the… position.
I’d be very honored to speak with you on… at…
Should you require any further information, please let me know.
I look forward to meeting with you.
