GREETING YOUR INTERVIEWER
3
BIZ TIP
When greeting your interviewer, show confidence, professionalism, and friendliness by using the following flow:
It’s a pleasure to meet you…
I really appreciate your taking the time to meet with me.
You have a wonderful office! Have you been in this building long?
SEE

Angela Ortiz, the Human Resources Director at Ark Co., welcomes Ken Sato into her office…

Angela: Hello, you must be Mr. Sato. Please come in.
Ken: Yes. Good afternoon. It’s a pleasure to meet you, Ms. Ortiz. Please, call me Ken.
Angela: Certainly, Ken. Thank you for coming today.
Ken: I really appreciate your taking the time to meet with me.
Angela: We are very impressed with your application and wanted to discuss your qualifications in person.
Ken: Thank you for the opportunity. You have a wonderful office! Have you been in this building long?
Angela: Thank you. Actually, we moved here last month…
