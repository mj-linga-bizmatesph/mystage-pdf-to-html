PART 1

Review the Biz tips with your trainer and make sure you are comfortable with the expressions and concepts.

When preparing your resume, include the most relevant skills, achievements, work history, and education/training. Customize your resume to fit the position you are applying for.

Respond to e-mail interview invitations quickly and professionally by using the following expressions below in your reply:

Thank you very much for considering me for the…position.
I’d be very honored to speak with you on… at...
Should you require any further information, please let me know.
I look forward to meeting with you.

When greeting your interviewer, display confidence, professionalism, and friendliness by using the following flow:

It’s a pleasure to meet you…
I really appreciate your taking the time to meet with me.
You have a wonderful office! Have you been in this building long?

When asked to introduce yourself, customize your response to meet the needs of the company by using the phrases below:

I’ve been in the … industry for … years as a…
I was responsible for…
I believe I have excellent…skills. As a result…
Now, I’m looking for new challenges and a chance to prove myself at your company.

PART 2

Prepare for your interview and make a positive first impression on your interviewer by completing the tasks below. Apply the expressions you learned in Lessons 1 to 4.

  1. Give your trainer a brief summary of your resume.
  2. Respond to an e-mail for an interview invitation.
  3. Greet your interviewer, make some small talk, and tell your interviewer about yourself.
