QUESTIONS ABOUT THEIR COMPANY
7
BIZ TIP
When answering questions about the company you are applying for, include how you can help them in their business. Use the expressions below:
Your company is very respected in the … industry.
It was established in…by…
Your company’s…really impresses me.
Although…is good, I believe I can help elevate it as a…
SEE

Angela Ortiz is asking what Ken knows about Ark Corporation…

Angela: So, what do you know about Ark?
Ken: Ark Co. is very respected in the pharmaceutical industry. It was established in 1982 by Tony Lowe and your company’s philosophy, “Make the world better,” really impresses me.
Angela: I’m very happy to hear that.
Ken: I understand your company is fairly new to the Japanese market and although sales performance is good, I believe I can help elevate it as a sales associate.
Angela: That’s good to hear.
