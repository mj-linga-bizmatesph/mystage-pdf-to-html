QUESTIONS ABOUT YOUR SKILLS
9
BIZ TIP
When talking about your skills, include your experience and results to sell yourself more effectively. Use the expressions below:
I have exceptional…skills.
In my previous job, I …
By using my…skills I…
SEE

Angela Ortiz, the HR Director, is asking Ken about his skills…

Angela: Can you tell me about your skills?
Ken: Yes, of course. I believe I have exceptional communication skills.
Angela: I see.
Ken: In my previous job, I managed many global projects. By using my communication skills, I completed many projects successfully with teams from different countries.
Angela: That’s very impressive.
Ken: Thank you very much.
Angela: Can you tell me more about…
