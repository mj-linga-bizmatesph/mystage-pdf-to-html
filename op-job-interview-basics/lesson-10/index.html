10
PART 1

Review the Biz tips with your trainer and make sure you are comfortable with the expressions and concepts.

When answering questions about your personality, be sure to include examples and goals to make your response more complete:

I am…
For example / because /
I’d like to…

When answering questions about the company you are applying for, include how you can help them in their business. Use the expressions below:

Your company is very respected in the … industry.
It was established in…by…
Your company’s…really impresses me.
Although…is good, I believe I can help elevate it as a…

When talking about your experience, highlight your strengths and the positive impact it has had on your company by using the SAR (Situation, Action, Result) approach:

I was expected to…
In order to…I…
In the end, I was able to…

When talking about your skills, include your experience and results to sell yourself more effectively. Use the expressions below:

I have exceptional…skills.
In my previous job, I …
By using my…skills I…
▼▼▼
PART 2

Have an interview and focus on answering questions about your personality, the company you are applying for, your experience, and skills.
