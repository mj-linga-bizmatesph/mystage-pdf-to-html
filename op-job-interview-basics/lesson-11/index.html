QUESTIONS ABOUT YOUR GOALS
11
BIZ TIP
When answering questions about your goals, include a plan to achieve your targets as well as a basic timeline:
My goal is to…
To achieve this, I must first…then…and finally…
I hope to accomplish this in…
SEE

Angela Ortiz is asking Ken about his future plans and goals…

Angela: What do you want to achieve in your first year at our company?
Ken: My goal is to improve the performance of the sales division and to become a valuable member of the company. To achieve this, I must first understand your company’s procedures and products, then evaluate the system, and finally create a plan to improve effectiveness and sales numbers. I hope to accomplish this in my first year.
Angela: Wonderful. Where do you see yourself in…
