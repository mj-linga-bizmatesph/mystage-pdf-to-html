QUESTIONS ABOUT YOUR OPINIONS
14
BIZ TIP
When giving your opinion in interviews, comment positively before making suggestions and talking about next steps. Use the flow below to get you started:
I feel…is/are doing well, especially…
That being said, I feel it would be a good idea to…
So, as a next step, I would…
SEE

Angela would like to hear about Ken’s opinions and how well he defends his position…

Angela: How do you feel about our products?
Ken: I feel your products are doing well, especially your Ark energy health drinks.
Angela: I’m glad you like them!
Ken: Yes, I tried them all! That being said, I feel it would be a good idea to change some of the other products to better match the Japanese market.
Angela: I see.
Ken: So, as a next step, I would reconsider the size of your products.
Angela: Thank you for your opinion.

Answer the following additional questions using the Biz Tip format:

  1. What’s your opinion on your current company’s situation?
  2. How do you feel about the current economy?
  3. What do you think about our company?
