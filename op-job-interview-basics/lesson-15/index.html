PART 1

Review the Biz tips with your trainer and make sure you are comfortable with the expressions and concepts.

When answering questions about your goals, include a plan to achieve your targets as well as a basic timeline:

My goal is to…
To achieve this, I must first… then… and finally…
I hope to accomplish this in…

When answering behavioral questions, use the PAR (Problem, Action, Result) format to add details to your answer and make the most impact.

Problem: I was/We were facing a problem with…
Action: To solve this issue, I…
Result: As a result…

When answering tricky questions, take some time to think about your answer and find a way to highlight your qualities or skills. Use the phrases below to help you:

That’s an interesting question.
Give me a moment to think about the answer.
I would say… because…
I am…

When giving your opinion in interviews, comment positively before making suggestions and talking about next steps. Use the flow below to get you started:

I feel… is/are doing well, especially…
That being said, I feel it would be a good idea to…
So, as a next step, I would…
