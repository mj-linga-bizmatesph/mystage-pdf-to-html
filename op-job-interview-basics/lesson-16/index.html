ASKING YOUR INTERVIEWER QUESTIONS
16
BIZ TIP
Asking questions about the company is important in deciding if the company is right for you and to show your excitement. Get the information you need by using the phrases below:
If you don’t mind, I’d like to ask about…
This is important for me because…
Thank you very much.
SEE

Ken is given the opportunity to ask some questions about Ms. Ortiz’s company…

Angela: Do you have any questions?
Ken: Yes. If you don’t mind, I’d like to ask about your company’s culture.
Angela: I see.
Ken: This is important for me because I feel I work best in an environment that has open communication and discussion.
Angela: I understand how you feel. I’m happy to tell you that our company is very open and we encourage discussion and communication among our employees.
Ken: Thank you very much.
Angela: Was there anything else?
Ken: Yes, I’d also like to ask about…
