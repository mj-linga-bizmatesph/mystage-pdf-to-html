ADDRESSING CONCERNS
17
BIZ TIP
Improve your chances of getting the job by asking your interviewer about their concerns and addressing them. Use the following flow and phrases to help you:
Is there anything that you have concerns about?
I understand your concerns. Allow me to address them.
Although you may feel I… I believe I…
SEE

Ken is asking Angela if she has any concerns about his qualifications…

Ken: I’d really like to work for Ark. Is there anything that you have concerns about?
Angela: To be honest, I do have some concerns about your international work experience. This job requires a lot of international travel and dealing with overseas customers.
Ken: I understand your concerns. Allow me to address them.
Angela: Please.
Ken: Although you may feel I don’t have enough international business experience, I believe I am very good at adapting to new situations, people, and cultures. I have many international friends and I travelled to over 15 countries.
Angela: I see. Would you feel confident in international business situations?
Ken: Absolutely.
