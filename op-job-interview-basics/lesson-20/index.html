PART 1

Review the Biz tips with your trainer and make sure you are comfortable with the expressions and concepts.

Asking questions about the company is important in deciding if the company is right for you and to show your excitement. Get the information you need by using the phrases below:

If you don’t mind, I’d like to ask about…
This is important for me because …
Thank you very much.

Improve your chances of getting the job by asking your interviewer about their concerns and addressing them. Use the following flow and phrases to help you:

Is there anything that you have concerns about?
I understand your concerns. Allow me to address them.
Although you may feel I… I believe I…

Before the interview finishes, thank your interviewer, ask about the next steps, and close with impact. Use the following expressions to help you:

Thank you very much for your time. I really enjoyed…
If you don’t mind, can I ask about the next steps?
I’m incredibly excited about the position and hope to have the chance to prove myself.

No matter the result of your interview, it’s important to respond politely and professionally. Use the following to help you write an effective reply:

Thank you very much for the wonderful news.
I am incredibly honored to accept the position of…
I will review the contract and…
Thank you very much for giving me this amazing opportunity.
▼▼▼
PART 2

Make a positive last impression on your interviewer by completing the tasks below. Apply the expressions you learned in Lessons 16 to 19.

  1. Ask your interviewer a few questions to get any important information.
  2. Ask them if they have any concerns about your qualifications and address them.
  3. Close your interview politely, ask and confirm the next steps, and finish with impact.
