KNOWING WHO YOU ARE
BIZ TIP

People who know their strengths, skills and experience, and ambitions, can comfortably answer the question, “Who are you?” However, this may be difficult for most of us because we seldom have a chance to reflect on who we are, what we’ve done, and what we want. One way to prepare is to talk about yourself in the 3rd person.

SEE

Read Ken’s answer in the 3rd person to the question, “Who are you?”

His motivation is getting results. His biggest achievement was at his previous company, where he led a 10-month project to launch a new website for the company.
