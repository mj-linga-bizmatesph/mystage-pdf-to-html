KNOWING THE COMPANY AND THE JOB
BIZ TIP

If you see a job interview as a “PASS or FAIL” test, it could be a very stressful experience. A job interview is really a matching game. The applicant has the same right to choose as the employer. So, the more you know about the company and the position, the easier it will be to know if and to show that you are a match.

SEE

Read Ken’s answer to the question, “What do you know about the company and the job?”

Ken MMB Corporation is a leading manufacturer of electric wires. It’s in the top 3 in the country and has had solid growth in sales and profit over the last 3 years. It has released dozens of new hi-tech products which make it unique. MMB has a strong brand image which it has built up for over 100 years in the wiring…
