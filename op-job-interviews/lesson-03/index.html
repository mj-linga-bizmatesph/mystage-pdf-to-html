CREATING AN EFFECTIVE RESUME
BIZ TIP

One important thing to note about a resume is that it is NOT a document that explains your work history. It is a document that has been carefully tailored for a specific company and job with the purpose of PASSING the screening process.

SEE

Bizmates style:

Your resume should have 2 main sections:

1.

Highlights of your skills, qualities, and achievements to show you are a perfect match (Objective, Selected Skills and Accomplishments)

2.

Evidence to show that the above is true (Professional Experience, Education, Awards, Professional Affiliations, and References)

Ken Sato

3378 West Drive, Kawagawa Prefecture, Japan
04657-555-2223 | sato.ken@bizmail.com

Objective

This is a description of why you are a perfect fit for this job.

Example:

Experienced online marketer with over 10 years management experience looking to apply expertise in SEO and online marketing as Marketing Manager at MMB Corp.

Selected Skills and Accomplishments

Only list skills and accomplishments that are relevant.

Example:

  • Designed corporate website which led to 33% increase in conversion ratio
  • Achieved conversion targets for 3 straight years through SEO
  • Launched e-mail marketing service which led to 12% increase in revenues in first 6 months

Professional Experience

List the companies you worked at starting with the most recent. Give a brief description of the position and highlight accomplishments.

Example:

Marketing Manager Apr 2013-Present
XCOM Tech Tokyo

Managed an 8-person team responsible for overlooking entire marketing campaign with a budget of 300MM yen/year.

Achievements:

  • Launched new corporate website
  • Coordinated global marketing event with 150 participants
  • Achieved consumer sales target for 3 consecutive years

Education

List formal education starting with the most recent.

Awards

List any awards relevant to the position.

Example:

  • Outstanding Achievement Award – April 2015
    (Awarded to top 5% performers)
  • President Select Award – March 2012
    (Awarded for leading a successful global project in Singapore)

Professional Affiliations

List any relevant organizations that you are a member of.

References

References are the people who would recommend you for this job, such as a former superior or colleague. It’s standard to just write, “Available upon Request”

Example:

Available upon request.

TRY

Brainstorm what you could include under each heading.
