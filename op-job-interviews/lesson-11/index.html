11
SHOWING YOU UNDERSTAND THEIR ISSUES
BIZ TIP

Many candidates can show that they are intelligent and highly skilled; however, very few are able to identify a company’s current issues and show that they are capable of resolving them. Being able to do this is a huge advantage.

SEE

A Typical Response:

Interviewer : What are your short-term goals?
Interviewee : I’d like to spend the first three months understanding your processes, people, and market. Then, I’d like to propose a strategy to help us maximize our resources. And by the end of the year, I hope to show some good results.

Bizmates Style:

Interviewer : What are your short-term goals?
Issues: growing competition from overseas
How to Answer: Show your plan to learn more about the company’s situation while sharing your understanding of current issues at the company and what you can do.
Interviewee :

My priority would be to learn your market situation and your decision-making process. However, just based on my knowledge, I think one issue is growing competition in this industry, especially from low-end suppliers overseas. So, marketing will be important. We encountered a very similar situation in my…

TRY

Study the issues and prepare your answers to these questions.

QUESTION ISSUES HOW TO ANSWER
What do you know about the company? Think of issues the company may be currently facing. Show you care about the company’s mission and understand the issues ahead and what you can do.
What is your greatest achievement? Explain your achievement and how this experience is related to current issues at the company.
Why do you want to work for our company? Show your understanding of the company and its issues and what you can do for them.

Role play
