12
TAILORING YOUR ANSWERS TO THE INTERVIEWER
BIZ TIP

Your interviewer might not always be the HR Manager. Your answers may be received differently depending on who the interviewer is. By tailoring your answers to the interviewer’s position or area of expertise, you’re more likely to make a good impression.

SEE

A Typical Response:

Interviewer What does it take to be a successful online marketer?
Interviewee You need to be able to read web metrics or analytics so that you can monitor the bounce rate, CTR, conversion, and so on. And with SEO, there is…

Bizmates Style:

Interviewer What does it take to be a successful online marketer?
Interviewer: VP of Finance
How to Answer: Answer the question without using technical terms. Show you understand the interviewer’s area of expertise and how your job relates to it.
Interviewee

The job of an online marketer is to get customers through the use of the Internet with tools, techniques, and marketing know-how. However, the real secret is to work with the people around you.

For example, as a marketer, I need to convince the Finance Department to approve my marketing budget. I know that the Finance Department needs to manage the company’s financial resources, so I need to show them clearly what our ROI will be. So the people in Finance are my best friends.

TRY

Take note of the interviewer and tailor your answers to them.

QUESTION INTERVIEWER YOUR ANSWER
What type of work environment do you prefer? HR Manager
What’s your greatest strength? President
How would your boss and colleagues describe you? Finance Manager
A question you might be asked The person likely to interview you
ACT
