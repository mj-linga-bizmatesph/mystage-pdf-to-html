13
GIVING EXAMPLES
BIZ TIP

One of the most powerful and simple tools that you can use to sound convincing is to back up your claims with real-life examples. Many applicants are able to say how great they are, but very few can support their claim with examples.

SEE

A Typical Response:

Interviewer So what are your strengths?
Interviewee Well, I’m organized, detailed, and goal oriented.

Bizmates Style:

Interviewer So what are your strengths?
Your claim: organized
Your example: I’m always given the task to organize company events
Interviewee

One of my biggest strengths is my organizational skills. I’ve always been very good at planning, scheduling, and keeping deadlines. So for example, at my current company, I’m always given the task of organizing events in our team.

Another strength is my…
TRY

Think of examples to support your claims when answering these questions.

QUESTION YOUR CLAIM YOUR EXAMPLE
How well do you handle stress?
What is your leadership style?
