14
BEING LIKEABLE
BIZ TIP

It takes more than having the right skills and experience to impress an interviewer. Likeability is a quality that many interviewers see as the determining factor when selecting between two qualified applicants.

SEE

A Typical Response:

Interviewer Do you have any concerns about working for a major corporation like MMB?
Interviewee No, I don’t. This is an exciting opportunity for me.

Bizmates Style:

Interviewer Do you have any concerns about working for a major corporation like MMB?
DOs DON’Ts
  • Smile and be energetic
  • Use positive body language
  • Be honest and genuine
  • Focus on the interviewer’s reaction
  • Treat it like a friendly, yet professional conversation
  • Have a poker face
  • Limit body language
  • Give scripted answers
  • Just focus on your answers
  • Treat it like a question-and-answer interview
Interviewee

Do you mean because my experience is mostly at startups? (yes) I see. That’s a very good point. Although I work at a small company now, I work with external business partners from big companies. So it doesn’t feel like a small company. Also, although MMB is a very big company, it’s very innovative, so I feel it has that startup spirit. Is that right?

TRY

Practice answering these questions and being likable at the same time.
