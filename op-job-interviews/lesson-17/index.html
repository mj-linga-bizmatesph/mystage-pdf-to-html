17
WRAPPING UP YOUR ANSWERS
BIZ TIP

Interviewers often have several interviews in a day and it’s very easy for them to miss some of the details you give them. So, make sure they remember what you’ve said by wrapping up your answers.

SEE

Bizmates Style:

Here are three good ways to wrap up your answers:

  1. Connect it to the job or the Company.
  2. Bring it back to the original question.
  3. Ask a question.
Interviewer : So what don’t you like about your current company?
Interviewee : I’m not unhappy with my current company. I’m being recognized for my work and I have a good team. However, the job is very domestic, so the opportunities to work globally are very limited. That’s why I’m attracted to this position at MMB.
(Connecting it to the job)
Interviewer : How confident are you in getting results overseas?
Interviewee : Although I’m not part of a global project now, last year I led our marketing project in Taiwan, Singapore, and Malaysia. I learned that the marketing tools are the same as in Japan. However, the key is having local contacts. I now have contacts across Asia. So going back to your question, I am very confident that I can get results overseas.
(Bringing it back to the original question)
Interviewer : That’s very good.
Interviewee : May I ask which countries MMB plans to expand to? (Asking a question)
TRY
