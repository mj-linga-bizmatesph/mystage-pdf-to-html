18
CLOSING AND ASKING QUESTIONS
BIZ TIP

It’s true that you should ask at least a few questions at the end of a job interview. However, the questions need to be meaningful as the quality of the question may affect their assessment of you.

SEE

Bizmates Style:

Here are three good ways to wrap up your answers:

  1. Find out if this is the job for you.
  2. Show you are interested in the job.
  3. Show you are the right person for the job.
Interviewee May I ask why the person currently holding this position is leaving? (Finding out if this is the job for him)
Interviewer Yes, the current manager handed in his resignation after he heard about our plans to expand overseas. He realized that his English was not good enough.
Interviewee How soon do you plan to expand overseas?(Showing he is interested)
Interviewer We’re hoping to start market research in March.
Interviewee That’s great. What skills and experience would make an ideal candidate?(Showing he is the right person)
