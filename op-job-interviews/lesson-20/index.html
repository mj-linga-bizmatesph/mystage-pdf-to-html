20
MOCK INTERVIEW
PART 1

Role play

Imagine that you are having a full job interview. Make a good first impression and then answer questions from your interviewer including some of the following questions.

QUESTION YOUR ANSWER

1.

Can you give me a quick summary of your resume?

2.

What don’t you like about your current company?

3.

How much overtime do you do in your current company?

4.

Can you describe a situation wherein you felt like you wanted to quit?

5.

Tell me about a project that you had to work on with someone you didn’t like.

6.

Give me an example of when you made a big mistake.

7.

How do you think we could improve our product or service?

8.

What are your salary requirements?

9.

Is there any area that you feel you need to improve to do well in this position?

10.

Do you have any questions?
PART 2

Self-assessment

Evaluate yourself by answering these questions and getting feedback from your trainer.

QUESTION COMMENTS

1.

Did I establish a good rapport with the interviewer?

2.

Did I answer the questions clearly?

3.

Did I sound confident and truthful in my answers?

4.

Did I use proper body language, facial reactions, tone?

5.

Did I answer the questions completely and honestly?

6.

Did I show the right level of enthusiasm?

7.

Did I make a lot of grammatical mistakes?

8.

Did I sound positive?

9.

Were there any questions that I had trouble answering?

10.

What could I do better in the next interview?
