Ken :

Our company was founded in 1979 by Hajime Suzuki. He was a great engineer. In 1998, our company merged with Gateway Digital and entered the global market. In 2003, the company was listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange. And since 2010, we have been known as one of the biggest data storage companies in Japan.

  • When was your company founded?

  • What is your company famous for?
  • What did your company achieve last year?
  • What was the most important part in your company’s history?
