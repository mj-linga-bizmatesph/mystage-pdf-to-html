OTHER PROGRAMS MANAGEMENT 1 I LESSON 1
TALKING ABOUT OUR INDUSTRY
1
SEE

Ken :

Our company belongs to the data storage industry. We manufacture computer data storage. The demand for bigger storage capacity is growing. So, the data storage industry is doing very well. However, this also means there is tough competition. Our company aims to become the leader in the industry in the next few years.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What industry does your company belong to?
  • What products/services does your company manufacture/provide?
  • Is the industry performing well? What about your company?
  • Does your company have a big market share?
  • Who are your competitors?
  • Who is the leader in your industry?
  • Do you think the industry will perform better next year?
  • What is your company’s goal in the next few years?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about your company’s industry. Use these phrases to help you.

  1. Our company belongs to…
  2. We manufacture…
  3. The industry is performing _____ because…
  1. Our competitors are _____
  2. The leader in the industry is…
  3. Our company aims to…
