Ken :

Our company manufactures data storage equipment. We develop products such as PC hard drives, SSDs, network servers, and so on. Our products are created with advanced technology. Therefore, they are very reliable and efficient. Our data storage products are used by consumers and data centers around the world.

TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What kind of products does your company have?
  • Does your company manufacture them?
  • How would you describe your products?
  • What is your best-selling product?
  • Who uses your products?
  • What is your company’s latest product?
  • Is your company developing a new product?
