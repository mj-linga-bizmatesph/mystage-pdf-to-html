Talking about my personality
11
SEE

Ken :

I am a shy person. But I like meeting new friends who share my interests. I’m goal-oriented and very passionate about my job. I give a lot of attention to detail. So, many of my friends think I’m a perfectionist. However, I’m not very good at speaking in front of many people. I hope I can build my confidence soon.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • How would you describe your personality?
  • What kind of people do you like to meet?
  • What are your interests?
  • What are you very passionate about?
  • What do your friends think of you?
  • What are your weaknesses?
  • What can you do to improve your weaknesses?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about your personality. Use these phrases to help you.

  • I am a _____ person.
  • I like meeting people who are…
  • I’m very passionate about…
  • My friends think I’m…
  • I’m not very good at…
  • I hope I can…
