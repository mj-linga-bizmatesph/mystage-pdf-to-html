OTHER PROGRAMS MANAGEMENT 1 LESSON 12
Talking about my team
12
SEE
Ken: I have 12 people on my team. They all have different personalities, but they all get along very well. We always share ideas and information. I’m proud of my team because everyone works independently and still meet our team goals. My team’s greatest strength is teamwork. My colleagues say I have a very strong team.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • How many people are there on your team?
  • What are their differences?
  • Do they get along with one another well?
  • What do you always do as a team?
  • What makes you proud of your team? Why?
  • What is your team’s greatest strength?
  • What do your colleagues say about your team?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about your team. Use these phrases to help you.

  • There are ____ on my team.
  • They all have different…
  • As a team, we always…
  • I’m proud of my team because…
  • My team’s greatest strength is…
  • My colleagues say…
