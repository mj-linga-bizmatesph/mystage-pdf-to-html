OTHER PROGRAMS MANAGEMENT 1|LESSON 13
Talking about my responsibilities
13
SEE
Ken: I work as a manager in the Shipping Department. My job is to make sure our products are delivered on time. Accuracy and efficiency are very important in my job. I also work closely with the Sales Department. This is important so we can deliver the items with the customer’s exact specifications. If I don’t do my job well, we will receive a lot of complaints.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • Which department do you work in?
  • What is your job there?
  • Do you have many responsibilities?
  • What’s the most important thing in your job?
  • Who do you work closely with?
  • What are your other responsibilities?
  • What happens if you don’t do your job well?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about your responsibilities. Use these phrases to help you.

  • I work as _____ in the _____ department.
  • My job is to…
  • _____ is/are very important in my job.
  • I work closely with…
  • This is important because…
  • If I don’t do my job well,…
