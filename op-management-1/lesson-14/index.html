OTHER PROGRAMS MANAGEMENT 1ILESSON 14
Talking about my targets
14
SEE
Ken: We try our best to ship orders as efficiently as possible. But, we still sometimes receive complaints from data centers. So my target is to reduce these complaints by 10% in the next 3 months. I can do this by shipping the correct orders and speeding up our delivery times. So, I need to coordinate with the Sales Department and our courier very closely.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What do you try your best to achieve?
  • What is your target as a manager?
  • How soon do you need to achieve this?
  • What can you do to hit this target?
  • Do you need to coordinate with other people to do this?
  • Is this target easy or difficult to achieve?
  • What else are you aiming to achieve this year?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about your targets. Use these phrases to help you.

  • I try my best to…
  • But I sometimes,…
  • My target is to…
  • I can achieve this by…
  • I need to coordinate with…
  • My other targets are…
