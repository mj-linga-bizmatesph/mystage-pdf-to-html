OTHER PROGRAMS MANAGEMENT 1 LESSON 15
Talking about my management style
15
SEE
Ken: I strongly believe in building good relationships. This makes the work environment fun and less stressful. My boss says I’m too friendly and lenient. However, I also know when to be strict. I motivate my team by recognizing everyone’s hard work. I encourage my staff to share their ideas. I try to involve them when making simple decisions sometimes.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What do you strongly believe in as a manager?
  • Why do you say so?
  • What does your boss say about your management style?
  • Are you a lenient or a strict manager?
  • Is motivating your team important?
  • How do you motivate your team?
  • What do you encourage your staff to do?
  • Do you sometimes involve them when making decisions?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about your management style. Use these phrases to help you.

  • I strongly believe in…
  • This is because…
  • My boss says I’m…
  • I motivate my team by…
  • I encourage my staff to…
  • I involve them when making decisions like…
