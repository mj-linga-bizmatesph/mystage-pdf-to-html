OTHER PROGRAMS MANAGEMENT 1 LESSON 16
Talking about training and
development
16
SEE
Ken: Our company provides many training programs. We need to be well-trained to meet our quality standards. Some training programs, like sales and marketing, are for 3 months. However, training for managers could last over a year. My management training program was long and difficult, but I’m glad I took it.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What kind of training programs does your company have?
  • Why are these training programs important?
  • How long do they usually last?
  • What do your staff learn in these training programs?
  • When did you take your management training?
  • What was your management training like?
  • What other training programs have you received?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about training and development. Use these phrases to help you.

  • Our company provides…
  • Our staff learn to…
  • We have training programs like…
  • These programs last…
  • My management training was...
  • I also received training in…
