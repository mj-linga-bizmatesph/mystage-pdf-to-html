SEE

Ken :

Our company provides many training programs. We need to be well-trained to meet our quality standards. Some training programs, like sales and marketing, are for 3 months. However, training for managers could last over a year. My management training program was long and difficult, but I’m glad I took it.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What kind of training programs does your company have?
  • Why are these training programs important?
  • How long do they usually last?
  • What do your staff learn in these training programs?
  • When did you take your management training?
  • What was your management training like?
  • What other training programs have you received?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about training and development. Use these phrases to help you.

  1. Our company provides…
  2. Our staff learn to…
  3. We have training programs like…
  1. These programs last…
  2. My management training was...
  3. I also received training in…
