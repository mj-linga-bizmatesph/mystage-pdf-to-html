Talking about managing costs

SEE

Ken :

Our typical expenses include office supplies, overtime work, team building, etc. Overtime work is our biggest cost. This is because it is the busy season now. I try to manage this by just having short meetings every week. When we order office supplies, I must approve it. Sometimes I pay when I have dinner with my team.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What are the typical expenses in your department?
  • What is the biggest cost in your department?
  • Why is this your biggest cost?
  • How are you managing this cost?
  • What other costs should you manage?
  • What can cause your costs to increase?
  • What do you do if your costs get too high?
▼▼▼
ACT

  • _____ is our biggest cost.
  • If our costs get too high, I need to…
  • I try to manage this cost by…
  • Sometimes I…
  • I also need to manage other costs such as…
