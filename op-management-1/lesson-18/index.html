OTHER PROGRAMS MANAGEMENT 1ILESSON 18
Talking about goal setting
18
SEE

Ken :

I meet with my staff twice a year to set their goals. I want their goals to be challenging but still achievable. I give them time to prepare because I want them to think about their goals carefully. At the end of each term, I meet with them individually to check their progress. Then, I give them feedback and advice on their performance.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • How often do you set goals with your staff?
  • What goals do they set?
  • What advice do you give them on their goals?
  • Do you give them time to think about their goals?
  • Do you always record their individual targets?
  • What advice and feedback do you give them?
  • What happens if your staff achieve their goals?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about goal setting. Use these phrases to help you.

  1. I meet with my staff _____ to set their goals.
  2. I want their goals to be…
  3. I give them time to prepare because…
  1. At the end of each term, I…
  2. I give them feedback on…
  3. I give them advice on…
