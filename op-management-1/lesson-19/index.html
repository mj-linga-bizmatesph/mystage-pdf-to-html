OTHER PROGRAMS MANAGEMENT 1ILESSON 19
Talking about my career path
19
SEE

Ken :

I joined the company more than 5 years ago. I started working in the Shipping Department. Then, I got promoted to Manager about 2 years ago. I would like to move up to Production and apply for the Senior Manager post. It won’t be easy, but I have my boss’ full support. I think I’m ready for the new position so I’ll take the promotion test next year.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • When did you join your company?
  • Which department did you start working in?
  • When did you get promoted to manager?
  • How has your job changed since you became a manager?
  • What position are you aiming for next?
  • What do you need to do before applying for a higher position?
  • Are you ready for a higher position?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about your career path. Use these phrases to help you.

  1. I joined the company…
  2. I started working in…
  3. I got promoted to manager about…
  1. I would like to apply for…
  2. It won’t be easy, but…
  3. I think I’m ready for a higher position because…
