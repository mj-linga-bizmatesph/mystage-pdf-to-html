9
FOLLOWING UP BY E-MAIL
BIZ TIP

By adding a personal touch, you can show the reader that you truly value them. To add a personal touch:

  • Use common everyday words
  • Use names
  • Write the way you speak
  • Express how you feel or felt
SEE

Example

Dear Mick,

I would like to express my appreciation for your time in our meeting. I would like to request the submission of your goals for the purpose of recordkeeping. The deadline for submission is fixed for end of next week.

Thank you,
Taka

Bizmates Style

Hi, Mick.

I’m so glad we had a chance to openly discuss your goals yesterday. Could I get a summary of your goals including:

  • Area of focus
  • Specific tasks
  • Measures and deadlines

Could you send them to me by the end of this week?

Looking forward to seeing you in our meeting next week.

Taka

TRY

Change these phrases from being impersonal to personal.

Impersonal Personal
It is with great regret that…
It is my pleasure to…
As per your request,…
This is to inform you that…
I was asked to…
ACT

Imagine that you are writing an e-mail to follow up on your staff after a meeting.
