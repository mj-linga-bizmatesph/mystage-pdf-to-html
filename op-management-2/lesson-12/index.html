OTHER PROGRAMS MANAGEMENT 2ILESSON 12
Getting everyone to participate
12
BIZ TIP
An effective meeting is when all attendees participate. Encourage everyone to speak up by:
Directing a question at one person rather than everyone
Mentioning the person’s area of expertise or experience
Keeping the question specific rather than too general
SEE
Example
Taka: So what are some issues we might face during implementation?
All: …
Taka: Anyone? Mick? What do you think?
Mick: Hmm, I’m not sure.
Bizmates style
Taka: So, I want to make sure that we have a back-up plan for any possible issues that we might face during implementation. Mick, you worked with the IT department on the last project. What technical problems did you encounter?
Mick: Yes, we had many bugs after the system was implemented. So it would be a good idea to have extra time for testing and retesting.
Taka: That’s a good point. Steve, you work closely with the operators. What is their biggest concern with the new system?
Steve: Well, many of them are concerned that the new system will be difficult to use.
Taka: I see.
