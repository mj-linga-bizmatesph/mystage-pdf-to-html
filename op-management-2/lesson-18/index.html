Lesson 18: Giving recognition
18
BIZ TIP
Recognizing your staff’s work is only effective when it’s sincere. Show sincerity when you recognize their work by:
Showing that you understand your staff’s efforts
Highlighting exactly what your staff did well
Connecting the staff’s work to the company’s goal
SEE
Example
Taka: Mick, thank you for your report.
Mick: No problem. It was a lot of fun.
Taka: OK.
Bizmates style
Taka: Mick, well done on completing the report. I know you put in many extra hours online and with your contacts to get all this information.
Mick: Yeah, I’m glad my contacts were able to help me.
Taka: The discount information in the report is very detailed and clear.
Mick: I’m glad to hear that.
Taka: So, I’m going to share this information with the marketing team, and this could help us develop a new strategy to promote our model X34.
Mick: That would be great. I’m glad to see my work might help our company.
Taka: Yes, well done, Mick.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  1. What are some important tasks that you delegate to your staff?
  2. What could you say to show that you understand your staff’s efforts?
  3. What could you say to highlight exactly what your staff did well?
  4. How are these tasks connected to the company’s goals?
▼▼▼
ACT

Imagine that your staff has completed an important task. Recognize your staff member’s performance.
