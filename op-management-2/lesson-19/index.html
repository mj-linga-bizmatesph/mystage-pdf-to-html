MAKING AN E-MAIL ANNOUNCEMENT
19
BIZ TIP
A short e-mail can sound rude while a long e-mail can be annoying. The 5-sentence rule is a simple structure you can use.
Sentence 1: a pleasantry
Sentence 2: the reason for writing
Sentence 3: a summary of the details or short bullet points
Sentence 4: a call to action
Sentence 5: a closing message
SEE
Example

Hi, everyone.

Please find the attached product comparison report of TechSoft’s new product and our model X34.

Regards,

Bizmates style

Hi, everyone.

I hope you all had a nice weekend. I’m happy to share a product comparison report of TechSoft’s new product and our model X34 that Mick put together for us.

The attached report highlights the following:

  • Features
  • Pricing and discounts
  • Customer feedback
  • Sales strategies

Please use this information to highlight the advantages of our model X34 on your next sales call. I’m sure this will be a useful sales tool for everyone.

Regards,

TRY

Answer these questions.

  1. What could you write as a pleasantry in your e-mail?
  2. What could you say to state the reason for writing this e-mail?
  3. What details should you include in the summary?
  4. What call to action would you include in the e-mail?
  5. What could you say in the closing of your e-mail?
