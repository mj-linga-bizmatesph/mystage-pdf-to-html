PART 1

Talk about the following topics.

  1. Lesson 11: GETTING YOUR MEETING STARTED

    Imagine that you are having your first meeting with your new team. Go over the ground rules, assign roles, and clarify the purpose and agenda.

  2. Lesson 12: GETTING EVERYONE TO PARTICIPATE

    Imagine that you are leading a meeting with your team. Get everyone to participate.

  3. Lesson 13: FACILITATING

    Imagine that your staff has brought up the concerns below to you. Use facilitation to lead the discussion to reach a solution.

    1. A colleague is always late with his work and it slows down my work.
    2. There are too many changes in the workplace.
    3. I have too much work.

  4. Lesson 14: QUESTIONING

    Have a discussion with your trainer using a combination of open-ended and closed questions.

  5. Lesson 15: SENDING THE MINUTES BY E-MAIL

    Using the above information, write an e-mail with the meeting minutes to your team.

  6. Lesson 16: DELEGATING WORK TO YOUR STAFF

    Imagine that you are assigning a task to your staff. State the objective clearly, explain any guidelines, and ask your staff what actions he or she will take.
