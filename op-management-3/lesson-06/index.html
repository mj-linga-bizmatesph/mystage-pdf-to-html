UNDERSTANDING WORKPLACE DIVERSITY

Read the article.

In modern business, the presence of diversity is almost always inevitable. Whether you’re part of a global company or a small start-up, you cannot avoid diversity as much as you cannot avoid change.

People think of diversity as just differences in culture, gender or age. However, diversity has a deeper meaning. It also covers values, skills, experiences, perspective and even behavior. Two things can happen when people with different personalities, ideas or backgrounds work together: First is synergy. When diverse individuals collaborate, the results can be mind-blowing. Second is conflict. Such diversity can lead to misunderstandings and tear the entire team. As a manager, your role is to harness these differences to work in your favor. The idea is to think as individuals but act as one unit towards a common vision.

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of workplace diversity:

  • Know yourself – understand your own culture, stereotypes, biases, etc.
  • Don’t discriminate – treat and respect everyone with equality and fairness
  • Know your team – understand their strengths and what motivates them individually and collectively
  • Encourage diversity – encourage your staff to accept one another’s skills, ideas, experiences through recognition
  • Manage conflict – handle conflict among your staff right away when it goes out of control
