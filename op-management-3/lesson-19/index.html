CHOOSING THE RIGHT PERSON FOR THE JOB

Read the article.

Your newfound management style has helped your staff become more efficient and more productive. You’re happy for having these people on your team. But sooner or later, one of them has to go on a different path.

Now you are tasked with choosing a strong candidate for promotion. The person you recommend for a certain position could end up being an asset to the organization or a liability. You might be staring at an excellent track record, but are these figures enough to select the right candidate? In some cases, yes. But you should also consider their core competencies to become a manager.

No matter what the position is, here are some competencies to help you choose the right person for the job:

  • Strategic – able to think and act strategically
  • Motivating – knows how to inspire and develop people to do more
  • Results-oriented – able to produce excellent results
  • Team player – knows how to collaborate and work with people
  • High ethical standards – upholds corporate values and acts as role model
  • Expertise – has the experience and understands how the business works
  • Change advocate – sees change as a way to improve things
  • Good communication – able to make others listen and take action
