OTHER PROGRAM MANAGEMENT 1 LESSON 1
TALKING ABOUT OUR INDUSTRY
1
SEE

Ken :

Our company belongs to the data storage industry. We manufacture computer data storage. The demand for bigger storage capacity is growing. So, the data storage industry is doing very well. However, this also means there is tough competition. Our company aims to become the leader in the industry in the next few years.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What is your company’s vision?
  • How would your company achieve this?
  • Is your company the industry leader now?
  • What is your company’s short-term goal?
  • Does everyone work hard to achieve your vision?
  • What do you do to help achieve this vision?
  • What does your company hope to become?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about your company’s industry. Use these phrases to help you.

  • Our vision is…
  • We can do this by…
  • We hope to become…
  • We’d like to develop…
  • Our short-term goal is…
  • Everyone helps achieve our vision by…
