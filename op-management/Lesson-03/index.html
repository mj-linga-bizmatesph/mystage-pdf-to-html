OTHER PROGRAM MANAGEMENT 1ILESSON 3
TALKING ABOUT OUR VISION
3
SEE

Ken :

Our vision is to improve data storage systems. We can do this by providing our clients with very efficient products. Our goal is to become the industry leader. We’d like to develop new data storage products for our clients. We hope to create high-tech products. We will work hard to achieve our goal.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What is your company’s vision?
  • How would your company achieve this?
  • Is your company the industry leader now?
  • What is your company’s short-term goal?
  • Does everyone work hard to achieve your vision?
  • What do you do to help achieve this vision?
  • What does your company hope to become?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about your company’s vision. Use these phrases to help you.

  • Our vision is…
  • We can do this by…
  • We hope to become…
  • We’d like to develop…
  • Our short-term goal is…
  • Everyone helps achieve our vision by…
