SEE

Ken :

Our company has many strong points. First, our products are priced competitively and very reliable. Second, we have one of the strongest brands in Asia. Third, we have a 24/7 customer support that assists our customers. And most of all, we are very proud of our workforce and our culture.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What are your company’s strengths?
  • What makes your company better than your competitors?
  • What’s the main reason your customers choose your company’s products?
  • Who are your customers?
  • What is your company’s strongest point?
  • What’s your company’s weakest point?
  • What does your company do to improve this area?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about your company’s strengths. Use these phrases to help you.

  • Our strong points are…
  • We are better than our competitors because…
  • Our customers are…
