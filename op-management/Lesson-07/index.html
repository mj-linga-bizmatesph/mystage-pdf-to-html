TALKING ABOUT OUR CHALLENGES
7
SEE

Ken :

Last month, we lost a big share in the European market. This is because of the complaints we received from our clients. We encountered a huge delay in shipping our products to Europe. Our company solved the issue last week. So, our customer support started e-mailing our clients about their orders. We are hoping to rebuild their trust soon.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What kind of challenges does your company face?
  • What was your company’s biggest challenge?
  • How did your company solve the problem?
  • What kind of challenges do you see in the future?
  • What can you do to prevent it?
  • What are some personal challenges you have at work?
  • What do you do to overcome them?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about your company’s challenges. Use these phrases to help you.

  • Our company is facing challenges like…
  • Our biggest challenge was…
  • We solved the issue by…
  • Some of our future challenges
    are…
  • We can prevent it by…
  • I deal with challenges at work by…
