OTHER PROGRAM MANAGEMENT 1ILESSON 8
TALKING ABOUT OUR CULTURE
8
SEE

Ken :

Our company has a very unique culture. Quality is very important in our company. So, we have strict rules for every process. However, we also have many ways to motivate our employees. We share our ideas openly. I really like our company culture because we treat one another as family.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What kind of culture does your company have?
  • What is very important in your company?
  • What is your company strict about?
  • How does your company motivate its employees?
  • How do your colleagues treat one another?
  • What do you like most about your company’s culture?
  • What don’t you like about your company’s culture?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about your company’s challenges. Use these phrases to help you.

  • Our company has a ____ culture.
  • ____ is very important…
  • We have strict rules for…
  • Our company motivates its employees by…
  • Everyone is encouraged to…
  • I like our culture because…
