OTHER PROGRAM MANAGEMENT 1ILESSON 9
TALKING ABOUT OUR GLOBALIZATION
9
SEE

Ken :

Our company entered the global market in 1998. Since then, we have opened over 5 factories overseas. Our largest customer base is in Asia. We will open more offices in the U.S., Europe and Australia in the future. For us, communication is important in globalization. So, our Japan office would like everyone to study English.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • When did your company enter the global market?
  • Do you have any offices/facilities overseas?
  • Which location has the most number of customers?
  • Where will you open offices/facilities in the future?
  • How does your company feel about globalization?
  • What steps are you taking towards globalization?
  • Is English important in your company? Why or why not?
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about globalization. Use these phrases to help you.

  • We entered the global market in _____.
  • We have overseas offices in…
  • Our largest customer base is in _____.
  • We will open more offices in…
  • Our company would like
    everyone to…
  • English communication is
    important because…
