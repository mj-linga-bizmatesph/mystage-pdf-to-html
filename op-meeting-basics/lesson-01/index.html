SCHEDULING THE MEETING
BIZ TIP

Telling your partner why you'd like to meet is the first step to scheduling your meeting. Do so effectively by using the following expressions:

  • Sorry to bother you. Do you have a minute?
  • I'd like to set up a meeting to discuss...
  • We need to think of some...
SEE

Ken is scheduling a meeting with his colleague, Cam.

Ken
Hi, Cam. Sorry to bother you. Do you have a minute?
Cam
Of course. How can I help you?
Ken
I'd like to set up a meeting to discuss the details of our new project.
Cam
That's a good idea.
Ken
We need to think of some concepts and ideas as soon as possible.
Cam
When would you like to meet?
Ken
Are you available...
TRY

Use the information below to schedule a meeting with your trainer. Include the topic and purpose in your invitation.

TOPIC PURPOSE
Current sales numbers Create new sales strategies.
Recent accident in the factory Set better safety regulations.
Market strategy Brainstorm ways to reach more people.
System update Come up with ways to speed up our system.
Ex. I'd like to set up a meeting to discuss our current sales numbers. We need to think of some new sales strategies.
ACT

You'd like to schedule an event or meeting. Approach your partner and ask them to participate.
