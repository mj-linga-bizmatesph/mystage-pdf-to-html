ASKING ABOUT AVAILABILITY
BIZ TIP

Matching your schedule with your counterpart can be challenging. Use the following
expressions to help you:

  • Would you be available...from...to...?
  • Are you free...at...?
  • Does...at...work for you?
SEE

Ken and Cam are deciding when to have a meeting to talk about their project.

Cam
When would be good for you to meet?
Ken
Would you be available on Wednesday from 3:00 to 5:30?
Cam
Unfortunately, I have another appointment at that time. Are you free tomorrow at 3:00?
Ken
Hmmm..., I have a meeting at 4:00 tomorrow. Does Thursday at 10:00 work for you?
Cam
Yes, that's fine.
Ken
Perfect, I'll check with the rest of the team to see if this works for everyone.
Cam
Sounds good.
