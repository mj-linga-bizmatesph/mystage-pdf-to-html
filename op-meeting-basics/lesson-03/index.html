CREATING AN AGENDA
BIZ TIP

Prepare for your meeting by creating the agenda. Use these expressions below:

  • Is there anything you'd like to add to the agenda?
  • How much time do you need?
  • Do you need anyone to prepare anything?
SEE

After fixing the time and date for the meeting. Ken asks Cam about the agenda.

Ken
Is there aanything you'd like to add to the agenda?
Cam
Yes, I'd like to go over the results of our market research studies.
Ken
That's a good idea. How much time do you need?
Cam
I think 15 minutes will be enough. I just want to present the results.
Ken
Okay, I'll put that on the agenda. Do you need anyone to prepare anything?
Cam
If possible, can you make sure there is a projector in the room? Last time we didn't have
one.
Ken
Yes, of course. Let me know if there's anything else. See you on Thursday at 10:00am.
