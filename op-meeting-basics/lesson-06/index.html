6
GREETING PARTICIPANTS & SMALL TALK
BIZ TIP

Greet your participants and set the right mood by using the following expressions:

  • Thank you for coming to today's meeting.
  • Let's take a few minutes and see how everyone's doing.
  • Let's get started with...
SEE

Ken is welcoming the participants at the start of his meeting...

Ken
Good morning, everyone. Thank you for coming to today's meeting.
All
Good morning.
Ken
Before we get started, let's take a few minutes and see how everyone's doing. Did you
enjoy your trip to Vietnam, Kim?
Kim
Yes, it was wonderful! I brought souvenirs for everyone!
Ken
That's very kind of you! Did you visit the floating market?
Kim
Yes! It was amazing! First, we...
---a few minutes later---
Ken
It's nice to hear everyone is doing well! Okay, let's get started with the meeting...
TRY

Think of some small talk questions and practice them with your trainer.

TOPIC QUESTIONS
Last Weekend How was your weekend? What did you do
last weekend?
Their work recently
Some news topic
Their family
ACT

Greet the participant(s) of your meeting and do some small talk with the topics above.
