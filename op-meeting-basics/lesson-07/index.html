7
RECAPPING THE PREVIOUS MEETING
BIZ TIP

Make sure all participants have all the necessary information by going over the main points
of the last meeting. Use the following expressions to help you:

  • In the last meeting, we discussed...
  • We decided on...
  • We agreed to...
SEE

Ken is going over the main points of their last meeting...

Ken
Let's recap what we decided last time we met. In the last meeting, we discussed the
results of our last project. We decided on a new concept for our next project and
everyone agreed to think of some ideas for today's meeting. Does anyone have any
questions?
All
[Nothing]
Ken
Okay, if there are no questions, let's go throught today's agenda.
