GOING THROUGH THE AGENDA
BIZ TIP

Make sure all the participants know the purpose and the flow of the meeting by going through the agenda. Use the following expressions to help you:

  • The purpose of this meeting is to...
  • First, ...will talk about ... for ... minutes. Then,... Finally,...
  • Does anyone have anything to add?
SEE

After recapping the main points of the previous meeting, Ken goes through today's agenda...

Ken
The main purpose of this meeting is to come up with a clear concept for our next
project. First, Cam will tell us about his market research results for 30 minutes. Then,
Ally will go through the problems we had with our last project for 30 minutes. Hopefully
everyone read the report she sent. Next, I'll have one hour to go through our project
timeline and hopefully decide on a concept. Finally, Shiori will give us a system update
for 20 minutes. Our meeting will take about two and a half hours. Does anyone have
anything to add?
All
[Nothing]
Ken
Okay, great.
