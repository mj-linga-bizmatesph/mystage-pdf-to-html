9
ASSIGNING ROLES
BIZ TIP

Assign key roles to your participants to ensure your meeting goes smoothly. Use the following expressions to help you:

  • ...., could I have you take the minutes for today's meeting?
  • ..., would you mind being the facilitator?
  • ..., can you keep track of the time for us?
SEE

Before starting with the first topic, Ken assigns roles to the participants...

Ken:
Okay, everyone. Before we start with our first topic, Rob, could I have you take the minutes for today's meeting?
Bob:
Of course.
Ken:
Thank you. Emiko, would you mind being the facilitator to keep us on topic?
Emiko:
No problem.
Ken:
Tony, can you keep track of the time for us?
Tony:
Sure.
Ken:
Thank you. Alright, let's start with the first topic. Cam, you...
TRY

Ask the following people to take on roles for the meeting.

PERSON ROLE
Landon Facilitator
Brian Minute taker
Mei Timekeeper
ACT

Tell your trainer about your colleagues and who would be the best facilitator, minute taker, and time keeper. Then practice assigning roles to them at the beginning of your meeting.
