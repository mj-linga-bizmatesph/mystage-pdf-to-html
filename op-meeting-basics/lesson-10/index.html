PART 1

Review the Biz tips with your trainer and make sure you are comfortable with the expressions and concepts.

Greet your participants and set the right mood by using the following expressions:

  • Thank you for coming to today's meeting.
  • Let's take a few minutes and see how everyone's doing.
  • Let's get started with today's meeting.

Make sure all participants have all the necessary information by going over the main points of last meeting. Use the following expressions to help you:

  • In the last meeting, we discussed...
  • We decided on...
  • We agreed to...

Make sure all the participants know the purpose and the flow of the meeting by going through the agenda. Use the following expressions to help you:

  • The purpose of today's meeting is to...
  • First, ... will talk about ... for ... minutes. Then,... Finally,...
  • Does anyone have anything to add?

Don't forget to assign key roles to your participants to ensure your meeting goes smoothly. Use the following expressions to help you:

  • ...., could I have you take the minutes for today's meeting?
  • ..., would you mind being the facilitator?
  • ..., can you keep track of the time for us?
