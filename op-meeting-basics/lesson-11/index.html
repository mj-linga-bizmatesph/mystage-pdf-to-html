11
GETTING PARTICIPATION & INPUT
BIZ TIP

Getting input from attendees is important in discussion and brainstorming. Use the following expressions to help you:

  • Does anyone have any ideas regarding…?
  • What do you think…?
  • …, do you have anything to add?
SEE

Ken is asking for everyone’s ideas during the meeting…

Ken
Alright, everyone. So does anyone have any ideas regarding our new project concept?
Tony
I think we need to create something that will link all our future home products together and can be controlled by smartphone.
Ken
That’s a great idea, Tony. What do you think, Cam?
Cam
I think there’s a lot of potential there. We don’t have anything like that now. We can use the information gathered for data and statistics as well.
Ken
Shiori, do you have anything to add?
Shiori
We’d have to create an entirely new system to handle the data, but I think it could be very useful for us and for our customers.
Ken
Thank you for your idea, Shiori. Does anyone else have any other ideas regarding…
TRY

Practice asking for input by making a statement and then asking your trainer for their input.

STATEMENT ASK FOR INPUT
People don’t know our brand. Does anyone have any ideas regarding this problem?
Casual clothing at the office is best. What do you think, [Trainer’s name]?
We should have a strict no-overtime policy.
We had better focus more on international markets.
ACT

Tell your trainer about some difficulties you are facing at work and ask for their input.
