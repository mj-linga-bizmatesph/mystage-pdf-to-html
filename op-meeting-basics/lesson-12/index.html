HANDLING DIFFICULT SITUATIONS
BIZ TIP

Difficult situations sometimes occur in meetings. Handle them effectively and make sure the meeting goes smoothly by using these expressions:

  • Let’s get back on topic.
  • Let’s keep things professional, everyone.
  • We’re starting to fall behind. Let’s come back to this at another time.
SEE

Ally is in the middle of talking about the problems from the last project…

Ally:
…so because of staffing problems, we had a lot of issues developing for multiple platforms as well as mobile.
Rob:
Speaking of smartphones, I just got a new phone! Check it out! There’re a lot of really cool features like…
[ a few moments later]
Ken:
Thank you, Rob. That’s very interesting, but let’s get back on topic. We have a lot to discuss today.
Rob:
Sorry, Ken. I was just so excited about this new phone.
Cam:
Yeah, come on, Rob. Stop wasting everyone’s time.
Ken:
Okay, okay, let’s keep things professional, everyone. We have a lot to cover in the agenda, so let’s move on. Does anyone have any other comments on Ally’s report?
Emiko:
Yes, I wanted to ask about…
[ 10 minutes later – time is running out ]
Ken:
Okay, thanks, everyone. We’re starting to fall behind, so let’s come back to this topic at another time and move on with the agenda.
TRY

Using the information below, handle the situation appropriately with the expressions you’ve learned.

SITUATION EXPRESSION
Conversation goes off topic. Let’s get back on topic
A small argument occurs between two members.

ACT
