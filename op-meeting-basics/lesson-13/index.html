EXPRESSING OPINIONS
BIZ TIP

Expressing and supporting your opinions logically are essential in meetings. Use the following expressions and flow to help you:

  • I believe that…
  • This is due to the fact that…
  • As a result, we had better…
SEE

The meeting participants are discussing which concept is best for their new project…

Ally:
We should focus on improving our current products instead of developing something new.
Ken:
That’s a good point; however, I believe that we need to take some risks and develop something completely different.
Cam:
Why do you say that, Ken?
Ken:
This is due to the fact that we’ve never been able to effectively gain any market position with our current products in the last 4 years. Although the quality is good, the market is just too competitive.
Shiori:
I agree. Although performance is stable, we’re not making any impact.
Ken:
That’s right. As a result, we had better try something new.
TRY

Practice expressing your opinions and providing support.

OPINION SUPPORT WITH REASONS / EVIDENCE ACTION
Our home entertainment business is at risk. The business has been losing money every year for the past 4 years. Sell the division to an interested party ASAP.
Brand awareness is weak. 8 out of 10 people don’t know our brand.
Company’s official language should be English.
ACT

Tell your trainer about your own opinions on a personal or business matter. Remember to provide support for your opinion as well as a suggested action.
