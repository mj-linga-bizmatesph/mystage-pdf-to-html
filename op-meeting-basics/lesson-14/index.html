MAKING DECISIONS
BIZ TIP

Once you have all the necessary information, it’s time to make a decision. Use the following expressions:

  • Thank you all for your input, suggestions, and opinions.
  • After careful consideration, I’ve decided to…
  • This is our best option because…
SEE

After a long discussion, Ken makes his final decision…

Ken:
Thank you all very much for your input, suggestions, and opinions. They were very valuable. After careful consideration, I’ve decided to proceed with Tony’s idea.
Emiko:
I think that’s a good decision.
Ken:
This is our best option because we need to find a way to integrate all of our home products together and, as Cam mentioned earlier, we can get a lot of valuable data from our customers. None of our competitors are doing this now, and I feel it will put us ahead of the competition.
Shiori:
Okay. So what are our next steps?
Ken:
First,…
