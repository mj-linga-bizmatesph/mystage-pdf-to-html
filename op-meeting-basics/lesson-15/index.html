PART 1

Review the Biz tips with your trainer and make sure you are comfortable with the expressions and concepts.

Getting input from attendees is important in discussion and brainstorming. Use the following expressions to help you:

  • Does anyone have any ideas regarding…?
  • What do you think…?
  • …, do you have anything to add?

Difficult situations sometimes occur in meetings. Handle them effectively and make sure the meeting goes smoothly by using these expressions:

  • Let’s get back on topic.
  • Let’s keep things professional, everyone.
  • We’re starting to fall behind. Let’s come back to this at another time.

Expressing and supporting your opinions logically are essential in meetings. Use the following expressions and flow to help you:

  • I believe that…
  • This is due to the fact…
  • As a result, we had better…if we hope to…

Once you have all the necessary information, it’s time to make a decision. Use the following expressions:

  • Thank you all for your input, suggestions, and opinions.
  • After careful consideration, I’ve decided to…
  • This is our best option because…
PART 2

Use everything you’ve learned to effectively handle a meeting.

You are having a meeting with your trainer about overseas expansion plans. You must decide where to set up your next branch office. Get your trainer’s participation, handle any difficult situations, express your opinions with support, and make a final decision.
