ASSIGNING TASKS
BIZ TIP

Once a decision has been made, you can assign specific tasks to your team. Here are some expressions to help you:

  • Could I have you…by…, if you don’t mind?
  • Would you mind…by…, please?
  • Please…by…, if you can.
SEE

Ken and his team have decided on the direction of their next project. To get things moving, Ken is assigning tasks to his team…

Ken:
Now that we’ve decided on the direction of our next project, let’s get things rolling. Tony, could I have you put together a proof of concept by next Friday, if you don’t mind?
Tony:
No problem.
Ken:
Thank you. Shiori, you mentioned that we need to develop a new system to handle the data. Would you mind telling me how much time and resources you need by Wednesday, please?
Shiori:
Of course, Ken.
Ken:
Fantastic. Cam, when can you prepare a market report on smartphone-controlled home automation?
Cam:
I think I can have one ready in two weeks.
Ken:
Alright, please prepare the market report by the 29th, if you can.
Cam:
No problem.
Ken:
Wonderful. Okay, to summarize today’s meeting, …
TRY

Assign the following tasks to your team members.
