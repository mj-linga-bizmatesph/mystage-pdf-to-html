18
SETTING UP THE NEXT MEETING
BIZ TIP

To ensure progress is being made, set up a follow-up meeting by using the expressions below:

  • Let’s meet again…
  • How about…? Does that work for everyone?
  • I’ll e-mail the agenda and details…
SEE

Ken is setting up the next meeting before wrapping up…

Ken:
Okay, let’s meet again next week to get an update on everyone’s progress. Is there a date that works best for everyone?
Tony:
Any day except Thursday is fine.
Ken:
Okay, how about Wednesday? Does that work for everyone?
Shiori:
Actually, I’ll be at a client’s office all day Wednesday. Sorry.
Ken:
I’ve got a business trip on Monday, so how about Tuesday or Friday?
All:
[Everyone agrees on Friday]
Ken:
I’ll email the agenda and details Thursday afternoon. If anyone wants to add anything to the agenda, let me know by then. Does anyone have any questions before we wrap up today?
