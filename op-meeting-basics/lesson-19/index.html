19
CLOSING THE MEETING
BIZ TIP

When wrapping up your meeting, make sure everyone is motivated to take on their assigned tasks. Use the following flow and expressions:

  • Thank you for your time, everyone. Could you remember to send the meeting minutes…
  • I think we made really good progress.
  • Have a great day, everyone. See you all at the next meeting.
SEE

The meeting has finished and Ken is thanking the participants…

Ken
Alright, thank you for your time, everyone. Rob, could you remember to send the meeting minutes?
Rob
Sure, I have another meeting after this so I’ll type up the minutes and send them out to everyone sometime tomorrow, if that’s alright.
Ken
Of course. If everyone is clear on their tasks and no one has anything else to add, that’s it for today.
All
[No questions]
Ken
I think we made really good progress and I’m excited about the new project. If anyone needs anything, please feel free to get in touch with me anytime. Have a great day, everyone. See you all at the next meeting.
