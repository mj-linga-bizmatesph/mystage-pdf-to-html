PART 1

Review the Biz tips with your trainer and make sure you are comfortable with the expressions and concepts.

Once a decision has been made, you can assign specific tasks to your team. Here are some expressions to help you:

  • Could I have you…by…, if you don’t mind?
  • Would you mind…by…, please?
  • Please…by…, if you can.

Make sure all members are on the same page by recapping the meeting’s key points and decisions. Use the expressions below:

  • Let’s summarize today’s meeting. We discussed…
  • We agreed to…
  • Does anyone have any questions before we wrap up?

To ensure progress is being made, set up a follow-up meeting by using the expressions below:

  • Let’s meet again…
  • How about…Does that work for everyone?
  • I’ll send an e-mail with the agenda and details…

When wrapping up your meeting, make sure everyone is motivated to take on their assigned tasks. Use the following flow and expressions:

  • Thank you for your time, everyone, … could you remember to send the meeting minutes to everyone…
  • I think we made really good progress today.
  • Have a great day, everyone. See you all next meeting.
PART 2

Use everything you’ve learned to effectively close your meeting.

Your meeting on how to improve employee performance is coming to a close. Assign tasks to your team, summarize the main topic and any decisions that were made, set up your next meeting, and wrap up.
