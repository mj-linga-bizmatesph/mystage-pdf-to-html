BIZ TIP
A successful meeting starts with a good agenda. Consider the following when creating an agenda:
  • Start with the important topics first
  • Include the meeting purpose, items, presenter, allotted time, and anything attendees need to prepare
  • Leave extra time for Q&A and Summary
