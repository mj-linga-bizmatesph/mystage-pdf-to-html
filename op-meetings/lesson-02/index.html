CALLING A MEETING
2
BIZ TIP
When scheduling a meeting, always give the other person necessary information, such as:
  • Purpose and agenda
  • Choice of dates and times
  • Deadline to reply
SEE

Example:

Subject: Project Meeting – April 16 or April 18

Dear Project Team,

To kick start our global project, I’d like to schedule a meeting with all team members at one of the following times:

Option 1: April 16, Wed – 2PM-3PM

Option 2: April 18, Fri – 10AM-11AM

Could you please let me know which day you can attend by April 7? Could you also take a look at the attached agenda below and let me know if you’d like to add anything?

Regards,
Makoto
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  1. How are meetings called at your company?
  2. What’s the objective of a typical meeting in your job?
  3. What topics are on the agenda of a typical meeting in your job?
