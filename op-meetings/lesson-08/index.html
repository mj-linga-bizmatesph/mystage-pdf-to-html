WRAPPING UP
8
BIZ TIP
Keep the following steps in mind when wrapping up:
  • announce that you will be wrapping up
  • go over the action points
  • mention the next meeting schedule
  • thank the attendees
SEE

Example:

Makoto:
All right, I think we covered everything on the agenda. Thank you for your time. Goodbye.

Bizmates style:

Makoto:
All right. If it’s OK with everyone, I’d like to wrap up this meeting. Mia, as the minute taker, could you please just go over the action points we decided on?
Mia:
Sure. Taro agreed to create a list of common requests from the sales reps by April 27. Yoshi will group the types of customizable features into simple, medium, and difficult by April 28. Finally, Liz will make amendments to the schedule by April 30. And I’ll e-mail the minutes to Makoto for review by the end of day.
Makoto:
Thank you, Mia. And I’ll be e-mailing everyone to set up our next meeting. Thank you for your time.

TRY

Answer these questions.

  1. How did Makoto signal that the meeting was coming to a close in the second dialogue?
  2. What did he ask Mia to do?
  3. What did Makoto promise to do at the end of the meeting?
  4. How do typical meetings close in your company?
