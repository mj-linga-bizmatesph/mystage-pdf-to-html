FOLLOWING UP
9
BIZ TIP
Consider the following when writing a follow up email:
  • Minutes of the meeting should be sent out to the attendees within one business day.
  • Include the action points in the e-mail as not everyone reads the minutes.
SEE

Example

Subject: Meeting minutes

Dear Project Team,

Here are the minutes from Mia.

Regards,
Makoto


Bizmates style

Subject: Minutes of project meeting – April 16

Dear Project Team,

Thank you for your time on April 16 for our first project meeting.

As a friendly reminder, here are the action points we decided on in our meeting:

  1. Create a list of common requests from the sales reps by April 27 – Taro
  2. Group features into simple, medium, difficult by April 28 – Yoshi
  3. Make amendments to the schedule by April 30 – Liz

Also, please review the attached minutes. Thank you, Mia, for the minutes. I’m truly excited to be working with all of you.

Regards,
Makoto

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  1. What did Makoto do in the first paragraph of the second e-mail?
  2. What did Makoto do in the second paragraph?
  3. What did Makoto do in the last paragraph?
