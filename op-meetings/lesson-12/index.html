BRAINSTORMING SESSIONS
12
BIZ TIP
The goal of brainstorming sessions is to keep the ideas free-flowing and to gather as many ideas as you can. To do this, make sure you:
  • Set the right mood
  • State the purpose and ground rules
  • Encourage the participants to share different ideas
  • Evaluate ideas ONLY at the end of the session
SEE

Example:

  • Setting the mood

    How’s everyone doing?

    I’m glad everyone can make it! Thank you all for coming.

  • Stating the purpose and ground rules

    The reason we’re here is to… | Before starting, let’s set some rules.

    The purpose of this session is to… | To maximize participation, I’d like…

  • Encouraging the group

    I’d like everyone to think out loud and…

    Let’s be creative and think outside the box.

  • Evaluating ideas

    Now that we’ve gathered enough input, let’s look at…

    OK, let’s analyze the ideas we’ve written down. First,…

TRY

Answer these questions.

  1. In what situations do you hold brainstorming sessions?
  2. What are brainstorming sessions like in your company?
  3. What was your last brainstorming session about?
