PROBLEM-SOLVING MEETINGS
13
BIZ TIP
The main goal of problem-solving meetings is to come up with a solution to address an issue permanently. This can be accomplished by:
  • Identifying the problem
  • Finding the root cause of the problem
  • Looking at the options
  • Playing devil’s advocate
SEE

Example

  • Identifying the problem

    Over the past few months, we’ve been struggling to…

    The issue we are faced with is that…

  • Finding the root cause

    The whole issue began when…

    The core of the problem we are facing is that…

  • Looking at the options

    There are several ways to address this. First,…

    One way to solve this is to…. Or, we can also…

  • Playing devil’s advocate

    I understand where you’re coming from. However,…

    That’s a good idea, but wouldn’t that…

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  1. How important is this kind of meeting to your company?
  2. Do you think it’s possible to solve a problem in just one meeting?
  3. What was your last problem-solving meeting about and how did it go?
