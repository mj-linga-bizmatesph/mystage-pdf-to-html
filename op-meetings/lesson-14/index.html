REGULAR STAFF MEETINGS
14
BIZ TIP
Staff meetings help people understand what’s going on in the organization and support one another’s work. Make it meaningful and interactive by:
  • Sharing information relevant to the participants
  • Getting updates from them
  • Raising issues for discussion
  • Calling on attendees to encourage participation
SEE

Example

  • Sharing information

    I have a couple of announcements to make.

    I’m pleased to say that…

  • Getting updates

    Jane, could you give us an update on…

    Grace, could you fill us in on…

  • Raising topics for discussion

    I’d like to get everyone’s input on…

    Let’s spend a few minutes to discuss the issue of...

  • Calling on participants

    Jim, we haven’t heard from you yet.

    Cindy, what’s your take on this?

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  1. What do you usually discuss in your regular staff meetings?
  2. Is everyone given a chance to report on something?
  3. What do you think can be done to make your regular staff meetings more meaningful?
