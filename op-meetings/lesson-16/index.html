BIZ TIP
Skill-building sometimes requires a lot of time and patience. Follow the steps below to get the most out of the training process:
  • Build the relationship
  • Set training goals
  • Giving instructions
  • Giving feedback
SEE

Example:

  • Building the relationship

    So, how’s your first week working here?

    So, are you enjoying working here?

  • Setting goals

    I’d like to know what you’d like to achieve by the end of…

    Do you have any experience in…

  • Giving instructions

    This first step is tricky, so I would take notes.

    The first thing we need to do is…

  • Giving feedback

    That’s good, but let’s try to…

    Yes, you’ve got it. But don’t forget to…
