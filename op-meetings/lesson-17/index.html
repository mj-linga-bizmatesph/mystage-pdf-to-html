EXTERNAL MEETINGS
17
BIZ TIP
In external meetings, your reputation and the company’s are at stake. So, professionalism plays a key role. Establish a good business relationship by:
  • Introducing yourself
  • Being courteous
  • Taking charge
  • Confirming
SEE

Example:

  • Introducing yourself

    It’s a pleasure to meet you, Mr. Page. I’m Taro Sato. I work in the…

    Thank you so much for coming here. It’s great to meet you in person.

  • Being courteous

    Let me show you to our meeting room. It’s on the 3rd floor. We can…

    Could I get you something to drink?

  • Taking charge

    Shall we get started? I was thinking we could start by…

    I’ve put together a draft agenda for our meeting. We’d like to know…

  • Confirming

    So, you mean to say that…

    So just to confirm what you said,…

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  1. Who do you usually have external meetings with?
  2. How often do you meet with these people?
  3. What is your special way of building a good relationship with them?

ACT
