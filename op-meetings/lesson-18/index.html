DISCIPLINARY MEETINGS
18
BIZ TIP
The goal of disciplinary meetings is not to reprimand, but to change someone’s behavior. Approach violating employees by:
  • Confirming their knowledge of the rule
  • Making them understand the reason behind the rule
  • Explaining how their actions affected the organization
  • Asking them for commitment
SEE

Example:

  • Confirming awareness

    Did you know that we’re not supposed to…

    Are you aware that we’re not allowed to…

  • Explaining the reason

    The reason we have this policy is because…

    It’s important that everyone does this because…

  • Explaining the consequences

    I’m afraid your actions have caused…

    If this happens again, I’ll have no choice but to…

  • Asking for commitment

    So, how can we prevent this from happening again?

    What will you do to avoid the same violation from here on out?
