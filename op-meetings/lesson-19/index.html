IMPROMPTU MEETINGS
19
BIZ TIP
Some meetings may come unexpectedly, so time is of the essence. Consider the following points when an impromptu meeting is necessary:
  • Request a meeting
  • Go straight to the point
  • Ask for other concerns
  • Show appreciation
SEE

Example

  • Requesting an impromptu meeting

    We have an issue that needs your immediate attention. Can we talk…

    I hate to bother you, but could I get 15 minutes of your time to…

  • Going straight to the point

    I’d like to ask for your feedback on…

    A client filed a complaint. I’d like to confirm if…

  • Asking for concerns

    Is there anything else that I should be aware of?

    Should I be aware of anything else before…?

  • Showing appreciation

    Thanks for your feedback, Jim. I really appreciate it.

    Thanks for your time. I owe you one.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  1. How often does your boss call for an impromptu meeting?
  2. When did you last have an impromptu meeting?
  3. How long did the meeting last and what was it about?

Role play.

  2. A concern brought up by a very important customer
  3. An update regarding a certain process
