GATHERING INFORMATION
1
BIZ TIP
The first step in the negotiation process is preparation. To make sure you are prepared, answer these 5 questions:
Who are they?
Where will we meet?
When is the deadline to decide?
Why are we negotiating?
How do we present?
SEE
Ken Hi Clyde. I need help preparing for my negotiation with a new Malaysian supplier.
Clyde OK, you can ask yourself five questions to prepare. First, who are they?
Ken Well, they’re a Malaysian manufacturer.
Clyde You need more information than that. Are they a big or small company? Do they have other Japanese customers? What is unique about their products?
Ken Oh, I see. I need more information.
Clyde Yes. The more information you have, the better. Let’s go to the next question. Where will you meet?
Ken Hmm… I’d like them to come to our office. I’d feel more comfortable.
Clyde Good. And when is the deadline to…?
