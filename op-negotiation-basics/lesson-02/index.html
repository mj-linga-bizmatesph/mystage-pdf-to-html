KNOWING YOUR GOALS
2
BIZ TIP
Gathering information is only the first step of the preparation process. Also, make sure you understand your situation by:
Knowing your objective
Knowing your bottom line
Knowing your options
SEE
Ken I did a lot of research on the new supplier. I think I’m ready for my negotiation.
Clyde Well, that’s good, but there are a few more things you should know to prepare for your negotiation.
Ken What do you mean?
Clyde Well, you should know your objective. What do you want to achieve with your supplier?
Ken I see. So, I need a clear goal.
Clyde That’s right. Also, what’s your bottom line? What is the worst situation you will accept?
Ken I was just thinking I will try to get the best deal.
Clyde That’s not good enough. You should have other options, too. So, if you can’t agree, you have the option of not making a deal.
