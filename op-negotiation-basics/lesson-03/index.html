E-MAILING YOUR COUNTERPART
3
BIZ TIP
The tone of your business e-mail should be professional and friendly. Use the following phrases to invite your counterpart to your office for a meeting:
I hope you had a wonderful weekend.
I’d like to set up a meeting to discuss…
Could we meet at our office...?
SEE

Business meeting – confirmation requested

Dear Ms. Khan,

I hope you had a wonderful weekend.

I’d like to set up a meeting to discuss a new business venture with your company. Are you free on July 3rd, Monday? Could we meet at our office at 2PM?

Please let me know if this schedule works for you.

Best regards,
Kenji Miyamoto
