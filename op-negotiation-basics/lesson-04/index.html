CALLING YOUR COUNTERPART
4
BIZ TIP
Just like your e-mail, your tone should sound professional and friendly on the phone. You may use the expressions below to confirm details and add extra information.
I just wanted to confirm our schedule for Monday.
I've booked a room at our office. Would you be able to come to our office?
I can e-mail you a map to our office.
SEE
Ken Hi, this is Kenji Miyamoto of EON Devices. May I speak with Ms. Mira Khan?
Mira Hello, Mr. Miyamoto. Yes, this is Mira Khan.
Ken Hi, Ms. Khan. I just wanted to confirm our schedule for Monday. That’s next week.
Mira Oh yes, Mr. Miyamoto. I’ve been looking forward to our meeting.
Ken Same here. Anyway, I've booked a room at our office. Would you be able to come to our office?
Mira Sure, but I’m not really familiar with the address you gave me.
Ken That’s OK. I can e-mail you a map to our office. See you on Monday, Ms. Khan.
Mira Thank you. See you then.
