BUILDING A RELATIONSHIP
6
BIZ TIP
The next step in the negotiation process is to build a relationship with your counterpart. Make small talk and connect on a personal level by saying:
Please call me Ken.
Did you have a hard time finding the building?
Okay, let me introduce myself first.
SEE
Mira Hello, Mr. Miyamoto. I’m Mira Khan. Please call me Mira. Nice to meet you.
Ken Hi, Mira. It’s a pleasure to meet you, too. Please call me Ken. Thank you for meeting with me today. Did you have a hard time finding the building?
Mira No. Thanks for the map you sent me.
Ken Glad it helped, Mira. Anyway, shall we start?
Mira Yes, please.
Ken Okay, let me introduce myself first. I used to work for Silicon, Inc. I lived in California before I joined Eon and…
---Ken continues to talk about his background---
Ken So, how long have you been with your company, Mira?
Mira I’ve been with the company for almost 7 years. I started out as…

TRY

Practice connecting on a personal level by thinking of good phrases or expressions.

PURPOSE PHRASE/EXPRESSION
To give a warm greeting It’s nice to finally meet you.
To show appreciation
To talk about your background
To ask questions about your counterpart
▼▼▼
ACT

Imagine you are meeting your counterpart for the first time. Make small talk and connect on a personal level.
