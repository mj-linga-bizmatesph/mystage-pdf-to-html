BIZ TIP
It’s always a good idea to check your understanding during the negotiation process. Use the following to clarify information:
So, you‘d like to... Is that correct?
Let me check my understanding.
So, just to confirm, ...
SEE
Mira Can you give me the specs?
Ken So you’d like the dimensions of the model. Is that correct?
Mira That’s right. We also need a prototype.
Ken Wait, let me check my understanding. So you also need a sample model to make sure the touch screens work, right?
Mira Exactly. However, the testing phase is usually the bottleneck. It may take several tests to make sure the screens work properly.
Ken I see. So just to confirm, the tests could slow down production, right?
Mira I’m afraid that’s right, Ken.
