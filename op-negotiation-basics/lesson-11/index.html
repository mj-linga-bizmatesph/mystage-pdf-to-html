BIZ TIP
When presenting your idea, you can follow a simple structure to make it easy to understand. You can use phrases like:
Introduce the topic and background: I’d like to talk about...
Make your proposal: So I propose that...
Highlight the merits: So, this would give your company... (merit)
SEE
Ken OK, thank you, Mira, for explaining the needs and concerns of your company with regard to this project. Now, allow me to share my ideas.
Mira You’re welcome, Ken. Yes, please.
Ken I've put together a draft proposal that could be a good starting point for us. I’d like to talk about my proposal to use a new technology for your products.
Mira Alright.
Ken As you know, the technology you are using now is over 10 years old. However, over the last three years, we have seen many changes in our industry. We have seen…
---Ken continues to explain---
Mira That’s right.
Ken So, I propose that you switch to this new technology. This would give your company a steady flow of production which could…
