BIZ TIP
Expressing agreement is easy. However, saying “yes” all the time doesn’t sound natural. Here are some expressions that show agreement:
Yes, I totally agree with you.
Yes, I'm fine with that.
That makes sense.
SEE
Mira Thank you, Ken. I like how you see this as a long-term project. This would certainly open business opportunities for both our companies.
Ken Yes, I totally agree with you.
Mira But for now, could you give us 6 months to develop our screens using your prototype?
Ken Yes, I’m fine with that.
Mira We haven’t tried making super AMOLEDS for mobile devices, so we don’t know what issues we’ll encounter along the way.
Ken I understand. Sure, that makes sense.
