SUMMARIZING
16
BIZ TIP
Make sure you go over what you agreed on before ending the meeting. Summarize by using the phrases below:
So we agreed to… (settled items)
However, you still need to… (unsettled items)
Could you get back to me by…? (action plans)
SEE
Ken OK, let me just go over what we agreed. So we agreed to have our special team run the tests and not have outsiders.
Mira Yes, that’s right.
Ken However, you still need to talk to your boss about the volume price, right?
Mira That’s right, Ken.
Ken Could you get back to me by next week so I can contact my friend about having your company featured in their magazine?
Mira Sure.
