17
BIZ TIP
Make sure you end the meeting on a positive note. Show your counterpart your appreciation, give some positive feedback, and mention the next steps by using the phrases below:
Thank you for your time today, [name].
I’m so glad that we had a chance to share our ideas today.
So, I’ll e-mail you the final version by next Monday.
SEE
Ken Thank you for your time today, Mira. I think we’re all set.
Mira Wonderful. Thank you too, Ken, for inviting me here.
Ken Sure. I’m so glad that we had a chance to share our ideas today.
Mira Yes, same here.
Ken Anyway, are you heading back to your office after this?
Mira Yes, that’s right.
Ken I see. Well, I’ll e-mail you the final version by next Monday.
Mira Sure, that sounds great.
