OTHER PROGRAMS NEGOTIATION BASICSILESSON 18
FOLLOWING UP BY E-MAIL
18
BIZ TIP
It’s always a good idea to summarize the terms of your agreement in writing. Send a follow-up e-mail by using the following expressions:
Thank you for visiting our office yesterday.
Just to confirm what we decided,...
Here are the next steps...
SEE

Business meeting summary – July 3rd

Hi, Mira.

Thank you for visiting our office yesterday. This is just to confirm what we decided. So, we agreed not to invite outsiders to test the prototypes and have a special team to do this instead.

Here are the next steps:

  1. You will talk to your boss about the volume price.
  2. I’ll talk to my contact who will feature your company in their magazine.
  3. Schedule our next meeting.

Looking forward to seeing you again soon.

Cheers,
Ken

▼▼▼
TRY

Use the situations below and write a short follow-up e-mail for each one:

SITUATION OPENING CONFIRMATION NEXT STEPS
Your last meeting in the office
A foreign colleague who visited you recently
A teleconference with your counterpart at HQ
