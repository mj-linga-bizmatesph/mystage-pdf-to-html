KEEPING TIES
19
BIZ TIP
You can continue to keep ties with your counterpart even after the negotiation by e-mail. Here are some expressions to keep in touch with your counterpart in an e-mail:
I hope you had a nice long weekend.
I just wanted to…
Please let me know if you have any questions or concerns.
SEE

Greetings from Ken

Hi, Mira.

I hope you had a nice long weekend.

It’s been a couple of months since we started our new arrangement, and I just wanted to make sure that everything was OK.

Please let me know if you have any questions or concerns. Talk to you soon.

Cheers,
Ken
