UNDERSTANDING NEGOTIATIONS
1
BIZ TIP
The word “negotiation” is often associated with strategies, deceit, and winning or losing. This is often why many fear negotiations. However, a better understanding of negotiations can remove this fear. You can do this by:
Understanding that negotiating is simply the process of reaching an agreement
Accepting that it’s a part of daily life
Knowing that a negotiation is not a win-or-lose game
SEE
Ken is speaking to his mentor, Ron, about an upcoming negotiation.
Ken Ron, my boss just asked me to renegotiate a contract with one of our business partners, but I don’t know anything about negotiations.
Ron Sure, you do. Negotiating is simply the process of reaching an agreement. You and your counterpart both have needs and wants. You just need to communicate yours, understand theirs, and come to an agreement.
Ken But I’ve never done that before.
Ron Sure, you have. It’s a part of daily life. Whether you’re deciding a deadline for a project with your boss, scheduling a meeting with your team, or deciding where to go for lunch with a colleague, you are in fact, negotiating.
Ken Yeah, but I know these people. So, it’s no big deal even if I lose.
Ron Lose? Negotiating isn’t a win-or-lose game. It’s about being creative and flexible with the purpose of finding the best solution so that everyone wins.
