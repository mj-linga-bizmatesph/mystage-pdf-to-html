REVIEW
10
Review the topics from Lessons 1-9 by answering the questions below.
  1. Explain why many people fear negotiations and what they could do to overcome this fear.
  2. Explain the five negotiation approaches to a junior colleague.
  3. Explain what it means to take a win-win approach to a junior colleague.
  4. Explain the importance of avoiding people problems in a negotiation and how to do it.
  5. Explain the importance of focusing on interests and how to do so.
  6. Explain Aristotle’s modes of persuasion and how they are important in negotiations.
  7. Explain how conflicts are a part of negotiations and what you can do to improve your chances of reaching an agreement.
  8. Explain what a BATNA is, why it’s important, and how to formulate it.
  9. Explain your inner self and how it will affect you as a negotiator.
