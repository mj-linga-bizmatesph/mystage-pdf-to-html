GIVING AND TAKING
17
BIZ TIP
Giving and taking are just part of negotiating. However, if you are not careful, you could be giving up more than you are taking. So, follow a process when giving and taking by:
Letting them know that you are giving something up for them
Asking the other side to reciprocate
Explaining exactly what you’d like in return
SEE
Ken is speaking to his mentor, Ron, about starting his negotiation.
Ken I hope my counterpart doesn’t make too many demands.
Ron Well, giving and taking are just part of negotiating. However, what’s important is that if you give something up, be sure to let them know what you are giving up for them.
Ken What do you mean?
Ron You need to get them to understand the value of what you are giving up. So tell them how meeting the demand will impact you. So you can say, “This is a very difficult request for us to meet, but if it’s that important to you, we can adjust our proposal.”
Ken I see. So, I let them know that I am sacrificing something for them.
Ron That’s right. Then, they will feel that they need to do something for you. So, you can say, “Now that we’ve made some adjustments, I hope that you can do the same for us.”
Ken That’s pretty direct.
Ron That’s OK. They should feel that you now have the right to ask for something in return. So, you could say, “We would like to have…”
Ken Yes, that would make it very easy for me to tell them what I want.
