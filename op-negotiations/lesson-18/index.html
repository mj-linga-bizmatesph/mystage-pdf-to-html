CLOSING THE DEAL
18
BIZ TIP
Now, we’ve come to the most exciting part of the negotiation process, which is closing the deal. However, we need to do more than just agree on the terms in this stage. Close the deal by:
Asking if all the issues have been resolved
Summarizing the terms and the benefits to them
Asking for their commitment
SEE
Ken is speaking to his mentor, Ron, about closing his negotiation.
Ken What should I say at the end of my meeting with my counterpart?
Ron If everything goes well, you can close the deal.
Ken Do you mean I should ask them to sign the contract?
Ron Before you do that, ask if all the issues have been resolved. So you can say, “I think we’ve covered all the terms of the contract. Is there anything else to discuss?”
Ken I see.
Ron Then, you can summarize the terms and the benefits to them. You can say, “Let me confirm what we’ve agreed to.” And as you summarize the points, you could add the benefits like this: “So this will save your company on delivery costs and…”
Ken Right. So he’ll clearly understand the benefits of the deal to him.
Ron Right. And finally, ask for commitment by saying something like, “Are you ready to form our new partnership?”
Ken What if he needs more time to decide?
Ron Give a deadline by saying, “Can you get back to me by the end of next week?” Sometimes a little pressure is needed, too.
