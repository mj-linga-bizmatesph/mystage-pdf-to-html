Talking about your personality
1
BIZ TIP
When someone is speaking too fast, you can say:
Can you speak more slowly please?
Please slow down.
You are speaking too fast for me.
SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: I’m shy, honest, and hardworking. I feel comfortable talking to my friends, but I’m quiet around people I don’t know. I’m very honest about my opinions. I always give 100% in everything I do.

Eri: I’m positive, goal-oriented, and detailed. I try to see the good points in everything. I always focus on getting results. And I always double-check my work.
▼▼▼
TRY

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Is Yuji shy, honest, and hardworking?
    • Is he positive, goal-oriented, and detailed?
    • What type of person is he?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about your personality. Think of at least three personality traits to describe yourself. Here are some examples.

positive loyal intelligent detailed
confident friendly persistent calm
hardworking independent honest reliable
