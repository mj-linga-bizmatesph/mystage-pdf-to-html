BIZ TIP
When you don’t understand a word or phrase, you can say:
What does that mean?
What do you mean?
Sorry, I’m not familiar with that expression.
SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: I’m very interested in technology. I read about the latest electronic products in tech magazines and online news. I also go to Tokyo to look for new electronic gadgets. I sometimes spend hours in Akihabara (Japan’s electronics shopping district).

Eri: I love playing sports. My favorite sport is basketball. I like it because it’s a fast-paced sport. I used to be on the high school basketball team. I played point guard for 3 years. Now, I watch basketball on TV.
▼▼▼
TRY

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Is Yuji interested in technology?
    • Is he interested in basketball?
    • What is he interested in?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
