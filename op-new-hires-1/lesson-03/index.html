SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: I majored in computer science in university. I took many courses on programming. My favorite course in university was gaming technology. Many advanced technologies are used in games.

Eri: I’m a business major. I got my Bachelor of Arts in commerce. The course on entrepreneurship was the most exciting. I learned how to start a business. I hope to get my MBA in the future.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
