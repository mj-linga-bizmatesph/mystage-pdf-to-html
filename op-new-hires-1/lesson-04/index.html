BIZ TIP
When you want them to spell a word, you can say:

SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: I only have limited work experience. When I was a university student, I worked part-time at a convenience store. I only worked a few hours a week. However, I learned about customer service and inventory management.

Eri: I have some non-work experience that is very valuable for my job. In my 2nd year of university, I traveled across Asia with my friends. We visited 5 different countries. I met many wonderful people.
▼▼▼
TRY

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Does Yuji only have limited work experience?
    • Does he have a lot of work experience?
    • How much work experience does Yuji have?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
