BIZ TIP
When you need time to think, you can say:
I never thought of that before. Let me see…
That’s a good question. Let me think…
Please give me a minute to think about that.
SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: I’m confident in my computer skills. I’m familiar with many programs. I’m also good at typing. I can type over 80 words per minute. I often help my friends with their computer problems.

Eri: I think I have strong leadership skills. I was captain of my basketball team in high school. I led my team to the city championships. I hope I can show my leadership skills in my work.
▼▼▼
TRY

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Is Yuji confident in his computer skills?
    • Is he confident in his leadership skills?
    • What skills is he confident in?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
