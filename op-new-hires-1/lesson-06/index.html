Talking about your weaknesses
6
BIZ TIP
When you want to express your opinion, you can say:
In my opinion,…
In my view,…
From my perspective,....
SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: My biggest weakness is English. I have studied English for many years, but this is my first time to study conversational English. I need to improve my speaking skills.

Eri: I think I’m too eager to please. I hate to say no. I try to give what the other person wants. Some people call me a yes-man. In my work, I will need to express my opinion more. It will be challenging for me.
▼▼▼
TRY

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Is Yuji’s biggest weakness English?
    • Is his biggest weakness Japanese?
    • What is his biggest weakness?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
▼▼▼
