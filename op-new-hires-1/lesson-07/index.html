OTHER PROGRAMS NEW HIRES 1ILESSON 7
Talking about your personal values
7
BIZ TIP
When you agree, you can say:
I completely agree with you.
I know what you mean.
I feel exactly the same way.
SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: My parents taught me to never give up. This has helped me do well in school. I was the top student in my class. I’m grateful to my parents for teaching me this.

Eri: I value stability. Stability is one of the reasons I joined my company. I believe that a stable job leads to less stress. If I have less stress, I can have a happier life.
▼▼▼
TRY

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Did Yuji’s parents teach him to never give up?
    • Did his parents teach him to play the piano?
    • What did his parents teach him to do?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about your three most important values and why they are important to you. Here are some examples.

family friendship loyalty nature the law
