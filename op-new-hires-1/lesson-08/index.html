BIZ TIP
When you partially agree, you can say:
I agree with you to a certain degree.
I partially agree with you on that.
That’s quite true, but…
SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: My biggest motivator is respect. I work hard because I want respect. I’m also motivated by recognition. I hope my company recognizes me for my work.

Eri: It’s motivating for me to help others. I joined my company because we help others with our products. I’m also motivated by interaction with people. I can meet many people in my job.
▼▼▼
TRY

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Is Yuji’s biggest motivator respect?
    • Is his biggest motivator money?
    • What is his biggest motivator?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
▼▼▼
ACT

Talk about what motivates you in your job and why. Here are some examples.
