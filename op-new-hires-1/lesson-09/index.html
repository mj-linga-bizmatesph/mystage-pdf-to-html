Lesson 9: Talking about
your goals
9
BIZ TIP
When you disagree, you can say:
I’m not sure I agree with you.
I have a different opinion on that.
I see things a little differently.
SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: My life goal is to retire at age 50. After I retire, I’d like to travel the world. I’ve always wanted to go to Antarctica. I’ll work hard for now and save money.

Eri: I hope to get my MBA someday. The main reason is for self-achievement. I envy people who have their MBA. I think it will give me more confidence in myself.
▼▼▼
TRY

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Is Yuji’s life goal to retire at age 50?
    • Is his life goal to retire at age 60?
    • What is his life goal?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
