Lesson 10: REVIEW
10
PART 1

Review the topics you learned.

  • Lesson 1: Talking about your personality
    Talk about your personality. Think of at least three personality traits to describe yourself.
  • Lesson 2: Talking about your interests
    Talk about your interests and what’s enjoyable about them.
  • Lesson 3: Talking about your education
    Talk about what you studied in school.
  • Lesson 4: Talking about your experience
    Talk about your work and non-work experiences.
  • Lesson 5: Talking about your skills
    Talk about the skills you have and the skills you need for your work.
  • Lesson 6: Talking about your weaknesses
    Talk about your weaknesses and what you can do to change them.
  • Lesson 7: Talking about your personal values
    Talk about your three most important values and why they are important to you.
  • Lesson 8: Talking about your motivation
    Talk about what motivates you in your job and why.
  • Lesson 9: Talking about your goals
    Talk about your life goals and how you plan to achieve them.
