Talking about why you joined your company
11
BIZ TIP
When you can’t think of a word, you can say:
What’s the word for…?
What do you call that thing that…?
I don’t know how to explain it. It has…
SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: I have 3 reasons for joining my company. The first reason is stability. My company has a long history for an IT company. The second reason is that it’s famous for high-technology. And finally it’s a global company, so I hope to work overseas.

Eri: I joined my company because I like the culture. The company has flextime for all employees. The company believes in work-life balance. I think it’s very good for employees.
▼▼▼
TRY

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Does Yuji have 3 reasons for joining his company?
    • Does Yuji have 10 reasons for joining his company?
    • How many reasons does he have for joining his company?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
