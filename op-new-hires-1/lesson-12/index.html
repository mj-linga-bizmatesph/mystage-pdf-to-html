BIZ TIP
When you want to ask for an opinion, you can say:
What do you think of…
What's your take on this?
How do you feel about this?
SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: I work for CrossNex. It’s a company that makes software. CrossNex’s head office is in Tokyo. It also has offices in Tokyo, Osaka, and Nagoya. It was founded in 1987 by Masahiro Nakao.

Eri: I work for ChroMag. ChroMag is a manufacturer of computer parts. It was established in 1982. It is one of the biggest producers of PC parts in Japan. We are starting to expand overseas.

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Does Yuji work for CrossNex?
    • Does he work for ChroMag?
    • What company does he work for?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
