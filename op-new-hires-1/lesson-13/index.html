SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: I’m going through the company’s employee training. However, I will be assigned to the Support Services Division. My job will be to provide support services to clients. My main responsibility will be answering the phone from clients.

Eri: I will work in the Sales Department in April. I will be a sales rep. This will be my first time to do sales. I will be trained to make sales presentations. I will also need to make proposals.
▼▼▼
TRY

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Is Yuji going through the company’s employee training?
    • Is he going through the company’s leadership training?
    • What training is he going through?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
