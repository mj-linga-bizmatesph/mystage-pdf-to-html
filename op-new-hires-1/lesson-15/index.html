SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: Most of our customers are in manufacturing. We provide IT solutions to many electronic companies. We also have contracts with many small companies.

Eri: Our main customers are computer manufacturers. Our biggest customers are Japanese computer companies. However, we also sell to American and Chinese PC companies too. We plan to expand our business to Europe next year.

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:

    • What industry are most of their customers in?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
