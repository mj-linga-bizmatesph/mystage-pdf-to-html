SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: We have a lot of competitors in our industry. There are many small IT companies that can create IT systems. Smaller IT companies offer lower prices. We offer better quality.

Eri: We are the biggest supplier of PC parts in Japan. However, there are many big foreign companies. Our biggest competitor is TES Computers. They are a Chinese company.

TRY

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Do they have a lot of competitors in their industry?
    • Do they have a few of competitors in their industry?
    • How many competitors do they have in their industry?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
