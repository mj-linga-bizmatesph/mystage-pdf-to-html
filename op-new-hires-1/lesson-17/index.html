Talking about your corporate culture
17
BIZ TIP
When you think something is funny, you can say:
That's hilarious.
That's very funny.
That cracks me up.
SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: Our corporate culture is very easygoing. Everyone is friendly and helpful. The average age of our company is very low. Many of my colleagues go out drinking after work. It’s a lot of fun.

Eri: We have a strict corporate culture. We have many rules. We are also very hierarchical. There are many levels of managers. I think it’s good for our company.
▼▼▼
TRY

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Is their corporate culture very easygoing?
    • Is their corporate culture very strict?
    • How is their corporate culture?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
