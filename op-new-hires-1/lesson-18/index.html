Talking about your company vision

BIZ TIP
When you feel bad for someone, you can say:

SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: Our vision is to become a total IT solutions company. We will help our customers with all IT requests. We want to help our customers improve their business through IT products.  

Eri: Our company vision is to be the number one PC parts supplier in the world. We want our parts to be used in every PC. We hope that our PC parts will help other PC companies create affordable PCs.
▼▼▼
TRY

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Is their vision to become a total IT solutions company?
    • Is their vision to be the number one PC supplier?
    • What is their vision?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
