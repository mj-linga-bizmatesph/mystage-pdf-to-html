BIZ TIP
When you want to show envy, you can say:
I'm green with envy.
I'm so jealous.

SEE

Read the two examples aloud.

Yuji: My goal is to become an expert programmer. We have some of the best programmers in Japan at our company. So, I hope to learn about programming from them.

Eri: My short-term goal is to be a good salesperson. I’d like to achieve my sales targets. In the future, I’d like to become a team leader. Then, I will be able to make changes in the company.
▼▼▼
TRY

Complete the tasks below.

  • Practice answering and asking questions.
    Example:
    • Is Yuji’s goal to become an expert programmer?
    • Is his goal to become a good salesperson?
    • What is his goal?

  • Summarize the two examples above.
