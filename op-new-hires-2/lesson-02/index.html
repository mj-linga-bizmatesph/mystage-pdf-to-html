GREETING YOUR GUEST
BIZ TIP

Make a good first impression on your guest by:


  • Introducing yourself professionally
  • Thanking your guest for visiting
  • Being courteous
SEE

Example

(Yuji goes to the lobby of his company to meet with Mr. Lawson.)

Yuji:
Are you Mr. Lawson?
Mike:
Oh hi. Yes, I am.
Yuji:
Nice to meet you. I am Suzuki. Please wait for Mr. Yasuda.
Mike:
Thank you.

Bizmates style

(Yuji goes to the lobby of his company to meet with Mr. Lawson.)

Yuji:
Hi, you must be Mr. Lawson. I’m Yuji Suzuki. We spoke over the phone.
Mike:
Ah yes, Mr. Suzuki. It’s nice to finally meet you.
Yuji:
Yes, it’s nice to meet you too. Thank you for coming all the way to our office. Was our office easy to find?
Mike:
Oh yes. I had no problems getting here.
Yuji:
I’m glad to hear that. Mr. Yasuda should be joining us in just a minute. Please have a seat while we wait for him.
Mike:
Thank you.
TRY

Discuss these questions.

  1. What could you say to call on a guest waiting in the lobby?
  2. What could you say to introduce yourself professionally?
  3. What could you say to greet a guest you’ve met before
  4. What could you say to thank your guest for coming?
  5. What could you say to ask your guest to wait?
ACT

Role play

Situation 1: Imagine that you are meeting a foreign guest in the lobby of your company. Call on your guest, introduce yourself, and ask him to wait.

Situation 2: Imagine that you’ve met the guest before
