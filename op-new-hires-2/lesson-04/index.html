4
GIVING A TOUR OF THE OFFICE
BIZ TIP

Build a closer connection with your guest by:


  • Talking about the building facilities
  • Introducing some departments
  • Introducing key people in your department or company
SEE

Example

(Yuji is taking Mr. Lawson from the company lobby to the meeting room.)

Yuji:
Please follow me.
Mike:
This is a really impressive building. Is it new?
Yuji:
Yes, it is. Please follow me.
Mike:
How many people work in this building?
Yuji:
About 300. This way please.

Bizmates style

(Yuji is taking Mr. Lawson from the company lobby to the meeting room.)

Yuji:
OK, shall we go to our meeting room?
Mike:
Sure. This is a really impressive building.
Yuji:
Thank you. It’s a new building. It has a nice view of downtown Tokyo from the top floor.
Mike:
Oh very nice.
Yuji:
OK, this way please. This is our Overseas Department. My desk is just over there.
Mike:
I see.
Yuji:
There’s Mr. Yasuda, our manager. Let me introduce you.
TRY

Discuss these questions.

  1. What interesting facts could you share about your office building?
  2. What landmarks can be viewed from inside the office building?
  3. Who are some key people you would introduce your guests to?
  4. What could you say when introducing someone to your guest?
ACT

Role play.

Imagine that you are taking a foreign guest from the lobby of your company to the meeting room. Give a short tour, introduce some departments and key people.
