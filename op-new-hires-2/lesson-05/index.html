5
MAKING A PRESENTATION
BIZ TIP

Start your presentation by:


  • Explaining the purpose of the presentation
  • Stating the length and topics
  • Explaining when the audience can ask questions
SEE

Example

(Yuji is making a short presentation about his company to Mr. Lawson.)

Yuji:
OK, I will present our company to you. Our company is CrossNex. We are an IT company. We make many kinds of software. We have three offices in Japan. One is in Nagoya. It is…

Bizmates style

(Yuji is making a short presentation about his company to Mr. Lawson.)

Yuji:
OK, shall we get started? I’d like to spend the next 10 minutes introducing CrossNex. I have 3 topics. First, I’d like to explain our company history. Second, I’ll discuss our products and services. And finally, I’d like to end the presentation with our next steps. I’d be happy to answer your questions after the presentation.
TRY

Discuss these questions.

  1. What can you say to explain the purpose of your presentation?
  2. What can you say to state the length of the presentation?
  3. What can you say to state the first topic, second topic, third topic, etc.?
  4. What can you say to explain when the audience can ask questions?
ACT

Role play.

Situation 1: Imagine that you are giving a presentation about your company. Give the opening of your presentation by following the Biz tips.

Situation 2: Imagine that you are giving a presentation about another topic of your choice. Give the opening of your presentation by following the Biz tips.
