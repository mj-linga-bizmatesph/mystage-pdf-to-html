7
READING NUMBERS
BIZ TIP

Know the different ways to read numbers.


  • Big numbers: 392,039,589,398,993
    • trillion billion million thousand hundred
  • Fractions: ½ = one half, ¼ = a quarter or one fourth
  • Decimals: 45.8 = forty-five POINT eight
SEE

Example

(Yuji is explaining some figures to Mr. Lawson)

Mike:
Can you tell me the sales figures for last quarter?
Yuji:
Ah, it is…three hundred million…I mean billion yen.
Mike:
Are you sure?
Yuji:
I think so.
Mike:
What about the annual growth rate?
Yuji:
Ah, it is 2 decimal 3 percent.
Mike:
You mean 2.3 percent?
Yuji:
Yes, you are right.
