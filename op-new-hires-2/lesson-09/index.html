LESSON 9
9
SENDING AN E-MAIL
BIZ TIP

Write an effective follow-up e-mail by:


  • Adding a personal touch
  • Summarizing what you discussed
  • Stating the action plans
SEE

Example

To:
Subject:
mlawson@cybertec.com
meeting
Dear Mr. Lawson,

This is Yuji Suzuki of CrossNex Corporation. Thank you for your support always.

Thank you for visiting our office. Please let me know if you have any questions about my presentation.

Suzuki

Bizmates style

To:
Subject:
mlawson@cybertec.com
Visit to CrossNex Corporation - Sep. 16, 2014
Dear Mike,

Thank you for visiting our office on Sep. 16 to meet with me and Mr. Yasuda. We really enjoyed meeting you and learning more about CyberTec and your products.

As we decided, I will be sending you our project plan by the end of September. I've also attached a copy of our product line with descriptions in English.

Please let me know if you have any questions. I hope to see you again soon.

Best regards,
Yuji
TRY

Discuss these questions.

  1. What can you write to add a personal touch to your e-mail?
  2. What can you write to restate the key points?
  3. What can you write to restate the action points?
ACT

Role play.

Write an e-mail to your guest after a visit to your office.
