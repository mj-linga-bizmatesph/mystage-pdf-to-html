REVIEW

Review the topics you learned.

  1. Lesson 1: Answering the phone

    Imagine that you are at your office and you receive a call from an English speaker who wants to speak to your boss. Answer the phone and transfer the call.

  2. Lesson 2: Greeting your guest

    Imagine that you are meeting a foreign guest in the lobby of your company. Call on your guest, introduce yourself, and ask him to wait.

  3. Lesson 3: Making small talk

    Imagine that you are with a foreign guest in the lobby. Make small talk, get to know your guest, and recommend a place to visit.

  4. Lesson 4: Giving a tour of the office

    Imagine that you are taking a foreign guest from the lobby of your company to the meeting room. Give a short tour, introduce some departments and key people.

  5. Lesson 5: Making a presentation

    Imagine that you are giving a presentation about your company. Give the opening of your presentation by following the Biz tips.

  6. Lesson 6: Handling questions

    Imagine that you are answering questions about your company after a presentation. Answer them by following the Biz tips.

  7. Lesson 7: Reading numbers

    Imagine that you are presenting the figures of three potential markets to your boss. Use the chart from lesson 7.

  8. Lesson 8: Explaining charts and graphs

    Imagine that you are describing the graph from lesson 8 to a business partner in English. Be sure to introduce the graph, describe changes in the figures, and try to come up with some reasons for the changes.

  9. Lesson 9: Sending an e-mail

    Write an e-mail to your guest after a visit to your office.
