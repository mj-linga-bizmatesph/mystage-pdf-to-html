SEE

Read the story aloud.

Effective communication in the workplace is important in order to work efficiently, solve problems, and maintain good relations with others. Good communication leads to better decision making, faster action, and less misunderstanding. Companies set up processes to encourage effective communication.
A communication tool, commonly used in Japanese business, is “hourensou”. Hourensou is made up of three parts which are updating, informing, and consulting. This means to inform and give frequent updates to all stakeholders, while actively consulting them for feedback. Hourensou is a continuous cycle of updating, informing, and consulting through both formal and informal channels to ensure that everyone has the same information and the same mindset.
