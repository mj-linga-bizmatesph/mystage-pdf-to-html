Time management is your ability to organize and plan the limited time resource that you have in order to complete the work you need to do. Having good time management skills is a big advantage in business. It can give you less stress, more opportunities and make you a better worker. Time management is a skill that can be developed. You can start by picking up some good time management habits.

Here are some tips to manage your time:

  • Keep a To-Do list
  • Set personal goals
  • Prioritize your work
  • Minimize distractions
  • Don’t procrastinate
  • Learn to say “no”
  • Multitask (low-level tasks only)
  • Take regular breaks
  • Know the time of day you are most efficient
