SEE

Read the story aloud.

Japan is the world’s 3rd largest economy. Until recently, the Japanese market was big enough for companies to survive without going overseas. However, with the aging population, slow economy, and strong foreign competition, there is more pressure on Japan to greatly increase overseas revenues.
Japanese companies have already started to expand their businesses overseas through mergers and acquisitions, teaming up with foreign partners, and establishing overseas offices. It will become more important for the young generation of Japanese workers to take the lead in developing these businesses overseas. The future of Japan is in your hands.
TRY

Discuss these questions.

  • How has the aging population affected business in Japan?
  • What foreign brands do you see in Japan?
  • Can you think of some examples of Japanese companies expanding their markets overseas?
  • How do you feel about the statement, “The future of Japan is in your hands”?
  • What has your company done to expand its business overseas?
  • What can you do to help your company with its expansion efforts?
  • In what situations will you need English in the future in your company?
ACT

Present your ideas.

Explain the impact of globalization on your country, your company, and you.
