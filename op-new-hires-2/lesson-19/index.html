SEE

Read the story aloud.

Career development is more than just workshops and training. It’s a lifelong process of gaining experience, expanding your knowledge, and developing your skills for business. You will spend a large part of your entire life working. So, it’s important that you are happy during this time. Career development will help you achieve happiness at work.

Career development starts with your professional goals. Ask yourself:

  • What would you like to achieve during your working life?

Next, you need to understand your current state. Ask yourself:

  • What skills, experience, and knowledge am I lacking?

Finally, you can plan your roadmap to achieving your goals. Ask yourself:

  • How can I gain these skills, experience, and knowledge?
TRY

Discuss these questions.

  • What are you most excited about at work?
  • What are you most worried about at work?
  • Would you like to be promoted in your company?
  • How would you like to contribute to your company?
  • Where would you like to work in the future?
  • What would make you happy at work?
  • Do you have a role model at work?
ACT

Present your ideas.

Explain your professional goals, your current state, and your plan to achieving your goals.
