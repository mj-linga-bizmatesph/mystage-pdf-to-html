4
GIVING REMINDERS
BIZ TIP

When giving reminders, keep it friendly by using the expressions below:

Start chat:
  • “Just a gentle reminder…”
  • “Please remember to…”
  • “By the way, did you…?”
Respond to chat:
  • “Thanks for reminding me.”
  • “I’m so sorry. It slipped my mind! I’ll…”
  • “Actually, I already…”
SEE

Eriko: Hey, Von. Just a gentle reminder about using the seminar room. Please lock it when you leave.

Von: Thanks for reminding me, Eriko!

Eriko: Cool. By the way, did you ask HR for your card key yet?

Von: Oh, I’m so sorry. It slipped my mind! I’ll email them now.

Eriko: No worries. You can drop by the HR office after your seminar.

Von: Sounds good. Thanks, Eriko!

TRY

Use the phrases from the Biz Tip for the following situations:

SITUATIONS
Remind your colleague about the PR project’s deadline
Remind your colleague to pick up a client at the airport at 10AM tomorrow
Remind your colleague to send the data from the last meeting
ACT

Your colleague is going on a business trip to visit your client. Remind your colleague to bring a souvenir. Be ready to answer some questions about what to bring.
