6
GIVING INSTRUCTIONS
BIZ TIP

When giving instructions, use steps to make them easy to understand. Use the expressions below:

Start chat:
  • “The first thing to do when… is…”
  • “First…then…next…lastly…”
  • “Be sure to…” / “Watch out for…”
Respond to chat:
  • “Got it! / I’m on it!”
  • “Let me confirm. You mean…?”
  • “What happens when / if… ?”
SEE

Nobu: The first thing to do when starting in the morning is login to the portal.

Steff: Got it! What’s next?

Nobu: Then, clock in by pushing the [START] button.

Steff: Let me confirm. You mean the big green button?

Nobu: Yes. Be sure to wait until the button turns [RED].

Steff: Sure, I’ll keep that in mind. Thanks, Nobu!

TRY

Use the phrases from the Biz Tip for the following situations:

SITUATIONS
Give instructions on how to exchange business cards
Give instructions on how to reserve a meeting room
Give instructions on how to reset your password
ACT

Give your colleague instructions on how to get to your office from Tokyo station. Be ready to answer questions about the route.
