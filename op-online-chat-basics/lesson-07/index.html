7
ASKING FOR UPDATES
BIZ TIP

Be clear and specific when asking for updates by using these expressions:

Start chat:
  • “How’s the … going?”
  • “I just wanted to check on the status of …”
  • “How soon can you…?”
Respond to chat:
  • “Sorry to keep you waiting.”
  • “So far, so good.”
  • “Unfortunately, I don’t think I can…”
SEE

Shiro: Hi, Fei. How’s the project going?

Fei: Hi, Shiro! So far, so good. I think it should be ready soon.

Shiro: Glad to hear that. How soon can you launch?

Fei: Well, we had some minor problems, but I think we should be able to launch next month. Is that okay?

Shiro: Sounds good! Please keep me posted.

TRY

Use the phrases from the Biz Tip for the following situations:

SITUATIONS
Ask your colleague when they can launch the project
Ask your colleague about the status of their research
Ask your colleague how their proposal is going
ACT

Ask your colleague for an update on the English translation of your company’s website. Be ready to answer some questions about the website.
