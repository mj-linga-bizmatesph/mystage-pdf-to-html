8
MAKING CORRECTIONS
BIZ TIP

Correct misunderstandings and other mistakes over chat using these expressions:

Start chat:
  • “Sorry, I accidentally…”
  • “I realized I...Can you…?”
  • “My mistake. …should be…,not…”
Respond to chat:
  • “No worries. Thanks for letting me know.”
  • “No problem. I’ll be sure to…”
  • “That’s okay. Don’t worry about it!”
SEE

Sayaka: Hey, Tim. Sorry, I accidentally gave you the wrong date for the conference.

Tim: No worries. Thanks for letting me know.

Sayaka: My mistake. The conference should be on the 25th, not the 26th.

Tim: No problem. I’ll be sure to update my calendar.

TRY

Use the phrases from the Biz Tip for the following situations:

SITUATIONS
You scheduled a teleconference for the wrong date. Correct your mistake.
You sent your colleague the wrong file. Correct your mistake.
You forgot to send your colleague the meeting agenda. Correct your mistake.
ACT

You sent your colleague the wrong customer information. Correct your mistake and be prepared to answer questions about the customer.
