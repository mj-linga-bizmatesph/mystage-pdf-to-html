11
GIVING UPDATES
BIZ TIP

Report important information quickly using the following expressions:

Start chat:
  • “Sorry, I’ll be late… due to...Thanks for your understanding.”
  • “I’ll be off...please feel free to…”
  • “I’ll be out of the office for...from…to…”
Respond to chat:
  • “Sorry / Glad to hear that.”
  • “Noted. Thanks for letting us know.”
  • “Copy that.”
SEE

Kei: Hi team. I’ll be out of the office for a seminar from 3 to 5 today.

Carrie: Hi, Kei. Noted. Thanks for letting us know.

Jam: Have fun!

Kei: Thanks! By the way, I’ll be off next Monday as well because of a personal matter. Please feel free to message me if you need anything.

Ed: Copy that. Hope everything’s okay.

TRY

Use the phrases from the Biz Tip for the following situations:

SITUATIONS
Inform the team that you are off today because you are sick
Inform the team that you will be late for the meeting
Inform the team that you will be out of the office to attend a conference
ACT

Tell your team that you will be travelling overseas. Be prepared to answer some questions about your trip.
