RESCHEDULING MEETINGS
BIZ TIP

Use the following expressions to help you reschedule meetings in a more professional way:

Start chat:
  • “Sorry, I need to/can we reschedule…because…”
  • “Will you be available at / on…?”
  • “I’m sorry for the trouble / inconvenience.”
Respond to chat:
  • “No worries.”
  • “That works for me.” / “Sorry, I can’t. How about…?”
  • “Noted. See you at…”
SEE

Kenta: Good morning, Jen. Sorry, I need to reschedule our meeting today because my son is sick.

Jen: Hey, Kenta. No worries. What time are you free?

Kenta: Thanks. Will you be available at 2PM tomorrow?

Jen: Sure! That works for me. I hope he is okay.

Kenta: That’s kind of you. I’m sorry for the trouble.

TRY

Use the phrases from the Biz Tip for the following situations:

SITUATIONS
Reschedule a meeting because of an emergency
Reschedule a meeting with your boss because you are stuck in a client meeting
Reschedule a meeting because you caught the flu
ACT

Reschedule a meeting with your boss because your train stopped due to an accident. Be ready to answer some questions about your availability.
