MAKING A TEAM ANNOUNCEMENT
BIZ TIP

Make your team announcements more professional using these expressions:

Start chat:
  • “Good morning, everyone. This is to inform you that…”
  • “Announcement: …”
  • “Hi, team. I regret / am pleased to announce that…”
Respond to chat:
  • “Way to go, team. Cheers!”
  • “That’s great news! I look forward to...”
  • “That’s terrible news. That being said, I’m sure…”
SEE

Taka: Good morning, team! I am pleased to announce that we launched our latest product today.
Here’s the link: COMPANY PRODUCT
Thank you for your hard work, as always!

TRY

Use the phrases from the Biz Tip for the following situations:

SITUATIONS
Announce that you are having a team-building activity
Announce that a new employee will join the team
Announce that the elevator in your building is not working
ACT

Announce to the team that you are transferring. Be prepared to answer some questions about your new post.
