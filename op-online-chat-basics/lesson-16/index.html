EXPRESSING OPINIONS
BIZ TIP

Express your opinion in the right manner by using these expressions:

Start chat:
  • “Honestly, I think…”
  • “Regarding…I feel we should / shouldn’t…”
  • “I think it would be best to/not to…”
Respond to chat:
  • “Thank you for your thoughts. I’ll consider…”
  • “I appreciate your insight. I’ll…”
  • “That’s a good point. Let’s…”
SEE

Masa: Hey, James. Thanks for working on the new site. Regarding the site, I feel we should add more languages.

James: Hi, Masa. I appreciate your insight. I’ll ask Marketing what they think.

Masa: Honestly, I think more people will visit the site if there are more languages.

James: That’s a good point. Let’s work on that as soon as possible.

TRY

Use the phrases from the Biz Tip for the following situations:

SITUATIONS
You think we should delay the launch of the new system. Express your opinion.
You don’t think it’s a good idea to lower the price of your product. Express your opinion.
You think it’s better to open the first overseas HQ in Singapore, not Taiwan. Express your opinion.
ACT

Your colleague wants to outsource customer service, but you disagree. Express your opinion and be ready to answer questions.
