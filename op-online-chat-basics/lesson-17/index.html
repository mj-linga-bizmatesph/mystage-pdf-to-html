17
RAISING CONCERNS
BIZ TIP

Speak your mind while maintaining a respectful tone when addressing concerns by using these expressions:

Start chat:
  • “Sorry to bother you, but I noticed…”
  • “There have been some concerns about you… Would you mind…?”
  • “...brought up an issue about… Do you know anything about this / is everything okay?”
Respond to chat:
  • “I didn’t realize…I will be sure to…”
  • “I see where…are/is coming from. I’ll try to…”
  • “Thank you for bringing this up… I will…”
SEE

Ken: Hi, Lana. HR brought up an issue about you not filling in your time sheet last week. Do you know anything about this?

Lana: I didn’t realize that. I will be sure to do it this week.

Ken: No worries. Just wanted to make sure everything is okay.

Lana: Thank you for bringing this up, Ken. I will contact HR about this today.

TRY

Use the phrases from the Biz Tip for the following situations:

SITUATIONS
Raise a concern about your subordinate always being late
Raise a concern about having so many meetings is decreasing work efficiency
Raise a concern about too much noise in the office
ACT

Raise a concern to your boss about always working until 11PM because of constant teleconferences with the US. Be ready to answer questions about your concern.
