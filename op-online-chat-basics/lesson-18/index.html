18
SHOWING APPRECIATION
BIZ TIP

Be specific when showing appreciation to spread positivity in the workplace. Use the expressions below:

Start chat:
  • “ I really appreciate you/ your…”
  • “I just wanted to take a moment to say thank you for…”
  • “Thank you so much again for… It really helped me out.”
Respond to chat:
  • “Not at all! / Don’t mention it! / My pleasure!”
  • “I’m always happy to help.”
  • “No, thank YOU for…”
SEE

Yuki: Hey, Torri. I just wanted to take a moment to say thank you for being such a great mentor.

Torri: No, thank YOU for always doing your best.

Yuki: I really learned a lot. Thank you so much again for your patience. It really helped me out

Torri: I’m glad to hear that! I’m always happy to help.

TRY

Use the phrases from the Biz Tip for the following situations:

SITUATIONS
Show your colleague appreciation for training you on a new procedure
Show your colleague appreciation for attending your meetings while you’re sick
Show your colleague appreciation for helping you practice your presentation
ACT

You just got promoted. Show appreciation to your boss for mentoring you and be ready to answer some questions about your new post.
