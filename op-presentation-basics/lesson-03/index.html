GIVING BACKGROUND INFORMATION
3
BIZ TIP
Give your audience some background information on the topic before starting. Talk about what was, what is, and what will be, using the following expressions to help you:
Formal:
The last…have been…
Currently…is…
In the future, we expect…to…
Casual:
…years ago…
Now…is…
In the future, …will…
SEE
Formal Casual

Today I’d like to take this opportunity to present our 5-year business plan.

The last two years have been quite difficult for us due to the weak economy. Currently, things are stabilizing because of our new product’s huge success. In the future, we expect this trend to continue due to our overseas expansion plans. This brings me to today’s outline…

Today I want to tell you about our 5-year business plan.

A couple of years ago, things were tough. The economy was bad and this impacted our performance. Now things are getting better, thanks to our new product’s huge success. We’re hoping that this trend will continue because of our overseas expansion plans. This brings me to today’s outline…
