EXPLAINING THE OUTLINE
4
BIZ TIP
Make sure your audience knows your presentation outline before you start. Use the following expressions to help you:
First, I’ll talk about [topic] for [total time]
Next, I’ll explain [topic] for [total time]
Finally, I’ll go through [topic] for [total time]
We’ll have [total time] for Q&A at the end
My presentation will take about [total time]
SEE

Example
First, I’ll talk about how to improve workplace safety for 30 minutes. . Next, I’ll explain the most common accidents that occur for 20 minutes. Finally, I’ll go through emergency procedures for 30 minutes. We’ll have 15 minutes for Q&A at the end. My presentation will take about 2 hours with some breaks in between. Does anyone have any questions before we start? No? Okay, let’s begin.
