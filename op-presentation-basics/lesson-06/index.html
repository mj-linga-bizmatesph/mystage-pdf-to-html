EXPRESSING AND SUPPORTING YOUR OPINION
6
BIZ TIP
Express and support your opinions in presentations by using the expressions and flow below:
Formal:
I strongly believe that…
This is due to the fact that…
Therefore, we had better…if we hope to…
Casual:
I think…
Because…
So we should…if we want to…
SEE
Formal Casual

I strongly believe that a sales campaign is our best option. This is due to the fact that last time we had a campaign, sales volume increased by 20%. Therefore, we had better lower prices if we hope to improve the situation.

I think a sales campaign is our best option because last time we had a campaign, sales volume increased by 20%. So we should lower prices if we want to improve the situation.
