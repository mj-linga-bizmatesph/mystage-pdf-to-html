7
BIZ TIP
Charts and graphs can be important tools to support your presentation. Use them effectively with the expressions below:
This chart shows [topic]
You’ll notice that [topic] [increased/decreased] from [#] to [#] in [year] because of [reason].
[topic] remained stable at [#]
SEE

XR-500 Sales Volume

This chart shows the XR-500’s sales results. You’ll notice that sales volume increased from 5,000 units to 8,000 in Q2 because of our sales campaign.


Next, you’ll notice that the numbers decreased slightly from 8,000 to 6,000 units in Q3. Sales volume remained stable at 6,000 units throughout Q4.
