ADDING DETAILS
8
BIZ TIP
Providing details to your comments will make your presentation more convincing. Use the following expressions to help you:
Moreover,…
However,…
Therefore,…
In addition,…
Unfortunately/Fortunately…
So,…
Furthermore,…
In contrast,…
As a result,…
SEE
Example Example

Our recent performance in Malaysia has been very good. Unfortunately, our performance domestically hasn’t been so positive. Therefore, it’s necessary for us to focus more on the Malaysian market. In addition, we should look into other potential Asian markets similar to Malaysia.

The last project was a big success. However, we did have a few problems. Our manufacturing line is fairly old. Moreover, we need access to more skilled labor. Therefore, I suggest we find a partner to help us with these challenges.
