MAKING AN IMPACT
9
BIZ TIP
The way something is presented is just as important as the content. To make your presentation impactful, consider these points:
Gestures: Use your hands and body to make certain points more impactful.
Voice: Vary your speed, tone, and stress to keep the audience interested.
Involving the audience: Ask the audience simple questions or have them give you a show of hands to get them engaged.
SEE
Gestures – What gestures would you use with the following words?
Big Small Now In the future In the past
Powerful Passion Think Imagine Expand
Voice – Read these sentences and make them more impactful.

The time to act is now. Not tomorrow, not next week, not next month. Now.

Success is determined by three things. Knowledge, strategy, and execution.

I never dreamed about success. I worked for it.
Involving the audience – Ask the audience these simple questions to keep them engaged.

Is everybody ready?

Do you want to…?

Who here has…?

Does anyone…?

TRY

Read the presentation excerpt below and use the tips above to be impactful.

Today, I will tell you what they are.


Is everybody ready? Do you want to know the secret to being happy? Do you want to wake up everyday and live your life the way you want? Well from today…from now…you are going to start your journey to true success.
