RECAPPING YOUR MAIN POINT
11
BIZ TIP
Make sure the attendees don’t forget what you presented by recapping the main points of your presentation. Use the expressions below:
Formal: Casual:
Today I presented [main topic].
I talked about [main topic].
I covered [#] main points in my presentation.
I went through [#] points.
First,…, Second,… Finally,…
1,… 2,… and 3,…
SEE
Formal Casual

Today, I presented our progress report. I covered three main points in my presentation. First, the problems we are having. Second, our action plan to solve them. Finally, our revised timeline.

I talked about our progress report. I went through 3 points. 1, the problems we are having. 2, our action plan to solve them, and 3, our revised timeline.
