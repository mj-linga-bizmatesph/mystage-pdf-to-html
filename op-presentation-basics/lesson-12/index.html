FINISHING WITH A BANG
12
BIZ TIP
Ending your presentation with a call to action is one way to finish with a bang. Use the following flow to help you:
If we/you really want to…
You/we must… and…
The time is now.
SEE

Example 1:

If we really want to improve our company’s condition, we must stop thinking and start doing. The time is now.

Example 2:

If you really want to be happy, you must find your passion and believe in yourself. The time is now.

Example 3:

If we really want to be a leader in the industry, we must start creating trends and stop following them. The time is now.

▼▼▼
TRY

Use the information below and finish the presentation with a bang.

Topic Action 1 Action 2
Innovate your business Start thinking outside the box Be willing to take risks
Be an effective leader Stop managing your staff Start motivating your staff
Balance work and private life Learn to let go of work
Become a global businessperson

Ex. If you really want to innovate your business, you must start thinking outside the box and be willing to take risks. The time is now.

▼▼▼
ACT

Make a short presentation about how to improve your lifestyle and motivate other people. Then, finish it with a bang.
