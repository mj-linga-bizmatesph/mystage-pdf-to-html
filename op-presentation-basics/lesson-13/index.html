BIZ TIP
Close your presentation professionally by using the flow and expressions below:
Formal:
Thank you so much for …
Should anyone have any further questions, please don’t hesitate to contact me.
Have a wonderful day.
Casual:
Thanks.
If there’s anything you need, let me know.
Have a good one.
SEE
Formal Casual

Thank you so much for attending my presentation today. It was a pleasure being here and I hope to have the chance to see everyone again soon. Should anyone have any further questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact me. Have a wonderful day.

Okay, that’s it for me. Thanks. It was great being here. It’s always fun seeing everyone. If there’s anything you need, let me know. Have a good one.
