PART 1

Review the Biz Tips with your trainer and make sure you are comfortable with the expressions and concepts.

Make sure the attendees don’t forget what you presented by recapping the main points of your presentation. Use the expressions below:

Formal:
Today I presented [main topic].
I covered [#] main points in my presentation.
First,…, Second,… Finally,…
Casual:
I talked about [main topic].
I went through [#] points.
1,… 2,… and 3,…

Ending your presentation with a call to action is one way to finish with a bang. Use the following flow to help you:

If we / you really want to…
You / we must…and…
The time is now.

Close your presentation professionally by using the flow and expressions below:

Formal:
Thank you so much for …
Should anyone have any further questions, please don’t hesitate to contact me.
Have a wonderful day.
Casual:
Thanks.
If there’s anything you need, let me know.
Have a good one.

Once your presentation is over, don’t be afraid to interact with the audience and make some connections. Use the following expressions:

Formal:
Thank you so much for attending. I really appreciate it. May I have your name?
Here’s my business card.
If you would like some additional information, I’d be happy to assist you.
Casual:
Thanks for coming. Sorry, I didn’t catch your name.
Here’s my card.
If you want some more information, let me know.

PART 2
