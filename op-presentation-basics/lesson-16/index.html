BIZ TIP
Asking the audience for questions is an essential part of the presentation process. Use the following expressions and flow to help you:
Formal:
I’d be happy to answer any questions.
Please don’t hesitate to speak up.
I’ve often been asked…
Casual:
Questions, anyone?
Don’t be shy.
People often ask me…
