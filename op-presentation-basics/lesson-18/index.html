ANSWERING QUESTIONS
18
BIZ TIP
Answering questions can be the most challenging part of the presentation. Use the flow below to help you answer simple questions:
Formal: Casual:
Thank you for your interest in….
Good question.
To answer your question,…
Let’s see…
Does that answer your question?
Is that okay?
SEE
Formal Casual
Jim:

When do you plan to launch the product?
Ken:

Thank you for your interest in our product. To answer your question, we hope by the end of this fiscal year. Does that answer your question?

Jim:

Yes, thank you.
Jim:

When do you plan to launch the product?
Ken:

Good question. Let’s see, by the end of this fiscal year.
Is that okay?

Jim:

Yes, thank you.
