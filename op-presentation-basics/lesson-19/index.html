HANDLING COMMON PROBLEMS
19
BIZ TIP
Use set phrases to handle difficult questions during Q&A:
The answer is confidential: I'm sorry, but that information is confidential. What I can tell you is…
You don’t know the answer: Unfortunately, I don’t have the answer, but, if you give me your contact information…
The person is being inappropriate or rude: Thank you for your comment/question. Does anyone else have any questions?
SEE

The answer is confidential:

Tim

Can you tell us about the hardware specifications for the new product?
Ken

Thank you for your question. I’m sorry, but that information is confidential. What I can tell you is that we’re sure everyone will be very pleased with the changes.

You don’t know the answer:

Lana

Can you give me more details about your development process?
Ken

Unfortunately, I don’t have the answer, but if you give me your contact information, I’ll put you in touch with someone who can give you the details.

The person is being inappropriate or rude:

Joe

It doesn’t seem like you have the proper experience to be here.
Ken

Thank you for your comment. Does anyone else have any questions?
