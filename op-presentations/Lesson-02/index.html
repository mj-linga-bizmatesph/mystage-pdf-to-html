OTHER PROGRAMS PRESENTATIONS I LESSON 2
OPENING YOUR PRESENTATION
2
BIZ
TIP
Remember the following when opening your presentation:
Get your audience’s attention in the first 30 seconds
Rather than the standard presentation opening, start by explaining the goal of your presentation
Memorize your opening
SEE
Example:
Greeting: Hello everyone.
Introduction: My name is Yasu Suzuki and I am an assistant manager in human resources at MMB Corp.
Topic: For the next 10 minutes, I’d like to talk about what it’s like to work for MMB Corp.
Subtopics: I have three points. First, some company information about MMB. Second, our compensation package. And finally, our hiring process.

Bizmates style:

Hello everyone.
When I was preparing for this presentation, I was thinking about how I felt when I was job hunting as a university student. I wondered which company had the highest salary, was the most stable, and was the most fun.
However, after working for 15 years at MMB, I now realize that the most important thing is whether the corporate culture matches your personality. So, that’s what I’d like to explain today.
My name is Yasu Suzuki and I am an assistant manager in human resources at MMB Corp. For the next 10 minutes, I’d like to …

▼▼▼
TRY
Answer these questions.
  • Who is the presenter?
  • What is the purpose of the presentation?
  • How long will the presentation be?
  • What are the subtopics in the presentation?
▼▼▼
ACT
Role play.
Deliver a short presentation opening using any of the following topics:
  • Your favorite restaurant
  • Using a new system at the office
  • Next year’s business strategy
