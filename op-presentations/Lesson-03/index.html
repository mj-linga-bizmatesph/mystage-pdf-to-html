SPEAKING NATURALLY
3
BIZ
TIP
To speak at a more natural pace, consider practicing the key points below:
Speak with a slow, steady pace
Know where to place pauses
Emphasize key words
SEE
Example:

Let me start by giving you some basic information about MMB Corp. MMB was founded in 1927 by Junpei Shinoda, who was an engineering genius. He had worked in the US and could speak English very well. He patented many new wiring designs and started his own company in Japan. In 1952, his son, Kotaro, took over the company. Now with over 2000 employees in Japan, China, Malaysia, and Vietnam, MMB Corp is one of the top electrical wiring companies in Japan.
