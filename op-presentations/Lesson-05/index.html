OTHER PROGRAMS PRESENTATIONS I LESSON 5
INVOLVING YOUR AUDIENCE
5
BIZ
TIP
Aside from using a question-answer approach, you can also involve your audience by:
Asking them to raise their hands
Asking them to imagine something
Having them read a section of your slide
SEE

Let me start by giving you some information about MMB Corp. MMB was founded in 1927 by Junpei Shinoda. How many people have heard of Junpei Shinoda? Please raise your hand. Not very many.

He was an engineering genius. He had worked in the US and could speak English very well. Imagine 80 years ago when very few people in Japan had ever been overseas, he was studying in the US.

In 1952, his son, Kotaro, took over the company. And how big is the company now? Now with over 2000 employees in Japan, China, Malaysia, and Vietnam, MMB Corp is one of the top electrical wiring companies in Japan.

TRY
Answer these questions.
  • What two approaches did the presenter use to involve the audience?
  • What two questions did the presenter ask the audience?
  • Why did the presenter ask those questions?
  • Why did the presenter ask the audience to imagine the situation 80 years ago?

ACT
Role play.

  • The most beautiful places you’ve been to
  • A brief history of your company
