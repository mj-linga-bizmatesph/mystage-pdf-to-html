USING IMPACT WORDS
6
BIZ
TIP
Below are some techniques that could have an enormous impact on your presentation:
Change your choice of words
Rephrase your sentences and vary your tone
Click the “B” key to make the screen go blank on PPT when you need to prove a point
SEE
Example

I’m happy to talk about the nice people we have at MMB. I think everyone in the company is very loyal and works together well. We also have many kinds of people including non-Japanese. So, I think we can do well in business.

Bizmates style

I speak with confidence when I talk about the outstanding people we have at MMB. I’m always amazed by the commitment and teamwork we have in our organization. Our diverse workplace consists of a rich mix of individuals from various cultural backgrounds and personalities. For these reasons, we are destined to succeed.
