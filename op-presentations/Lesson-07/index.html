OTHER PROGRAMS PRESENTATIONS I LESSON 7
CLOSING YOUR PRESENTATION
7
BIZ
TIP
Keep in mind that most people only remember the last part of your presentation. Make it memorable by:
Emphasizing the most important thing in your presentation
Answering the question to the problem
Having a call to action
SEE
Example

In summary, I talked about some basic information about MMB, our corporate culture, and our hiring process. Thank you.

Bizmates style

Let me wrap up my presentation. So, now you all know a little bit more about what MMB Corp is, what it would be like to work at MMB, and what you need to do to join our team. So, did I make the right decision to join MMB 15 years ago? Absolutely YES. Not because of the salary. Not because of its stability. It’s because the culture fits my personality. And I hope it fits yours, too.
