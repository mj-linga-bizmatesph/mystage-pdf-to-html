OTHER PROGRAMS PRESENTATIONS I LESSON 11
DIFFERENT TYPES OF PRESENTATIONS
11
BIZ
TIP
By having a clear goal of your presentation in mind, you can determine the type of presentation you need to prepare for and the approach you need to take.
SEE
Example
  • Jake, I need your help. Do you have a minute?
  • Sure, Sumi. What can I help you with?
  • I’ve been asked to prepare for a presentation, but I’m not sure where to start.
  • Depending on what the goal of your presentation is, you can determine the most appropriate type of presentation to make.
  • I see. What are the different types of presentation then?
  • Well, the most common that I know of are sales presentations, presentations for special events, making recommendations, making an announcement, delivering a status report, impromptu presentations, instructional presentations and motivational speeches.
  • I didn’t know there are so many.
  • By the way, what’s the goal of your presentation?
  • Well, I’ll be visiting a client’s office next week to talk about our products.
  • I see. In that case, a sales presentation would work best.
▼▼▼
TRY
Answer these questions.
  • What’s the usual goal of your presentations?
  • What type of presentation do you often give?
  • What type of presentation have you not delivered yet?
  • Which type do you think is the most difficult to make? The easiest?
▼▼▼
ACT
Role play
Discuss the last presentation you delivered and explain the following:
  • The goal of your presentation
  • The type of presentation you used
  • The reason you thought this was the most appropriate
