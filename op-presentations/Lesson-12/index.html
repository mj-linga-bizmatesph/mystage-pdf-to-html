OTHER PROGRAMS PRESENTATIONS I LESSON 12
SALES PRESENTATIONS
12
BIZ
TIP
A sales presentation doesn’t need to end up in a sale. The goal is to make the listeners understand what’s in it for them. You can do this by:
  • Showing you understand your listeners’ needs and interests
  • Explaining the features clearly
  • Emphasizing the benefits
SEE
Example
  • Showing you understand
    • One of the biggest problems with standard products sold in…
    • I know that you are looking for…
    • I’m well aware that you need…
  • Explaining the features clearly
    • This model comes with…
    • A great new feature on this model is…
    • The best thing about this model is that…
  • Emphasizing the benefits
    • So, this means that you won’t need to…
    • So, this will allow you to…
    • So, all you need to do is…
▼▼▼
TRY
Answer these questions.
  • What kinds of products or services does your company sell?
  • What are some of the features?
  • What are the benefits of each feature?
▼▼▼
ACT
Role play.
Make a sales presentation on one of the following topics:
  • A product you’re very familiar with
  • A service you used
  • Your company’s product/service
