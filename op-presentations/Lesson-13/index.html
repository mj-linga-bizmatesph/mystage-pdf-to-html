BIZ
TIP
A sales presentation doesn’t need to end up in a sale. The goal is to make the listeners understand what’s in it for them. You can do this by:
  • Showing appreciation to everyone
  • Monitoring time
  • Transitioning
SEE
Example
  • Showing appreciation to everyone (audience, speakers, sponsors,…)
    • First off, I’d like to thank all of you for coming…
    • This event wouldn’t have been possible without…
    • I can’t thank you enough for…
  • Monitoring the time
    • We’re making great time. Thank you for making my job so easy.
    • Just to speed things up, I’d like to ask everyone to…
    • We’re running a bit short on time, so we’ll just skip…
  • Transitioning
    • What a wonderful speech by our president. He always knows…
    • Thank you for your speech, Jim. I have to agree with you that…
    • I loved your last message. Maybe I can use it in my next speech.
