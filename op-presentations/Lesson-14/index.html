OTHER PROGRAMS PRESENTATIONS I LESSON 14
RECOMMENDATIONS
14
BIZ
TIP
When presenting a recommendation, keep the following key points in mind:
  • Frame your proposal based on what your audience wants to know
  • State the problem, present your idea and the possible results
  • Reinforce your proposal with facts or logic
SEE
Example
  • Framing the proposal
    • With the recent changes in our industry, we have faced…
    • Over the last few years, we have seen…
    • With the current market situation, we…
  • The main proposal
    • The biggest issue that we are dealing with is…
    • Our main concern is…
    • My recommendation is to…
    • My proposed solution is to…
    • If we take this course of action, we can…
    • With my recommendation, here’s what we can expect to see…
  • Supporting the proposal
    • The graph you see here shows that…
    • Based on the data we received, we found out that…
    • According to statistics,…
▼▼▼
TRY
Answer these questions.
  • What was your last recommendation about?
  • Who were your listeners?
  • What information did you build on to frame your proposal?
  • What factual information did you use to support your idea?
