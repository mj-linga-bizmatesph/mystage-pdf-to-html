OTHER PROGRAMS PRESENTATIONS I LESSON 15
ANNOUNCEMENTS
15
BIZ
TIP
When announcing a major change in the company, you can expect resistance from some people. So, clarify important information by mentioning:
  • The effects of the change
  • Reasons for the change
  • Key information, such as dates and the person in charge
SEE
Example
  • Mentioning the effects of the change
    • I’m pleased to announce that we have a new system that will give…
    • It is with great pleasure that I present our new program, which will…
    • I’m happy to say that we have redesigned our processes to save…
  • Mentioning the reasons for the change
    • The reason we are doing this is…
    • The purpose of this change is…
    • Why are we making this change? Well, we believe that…
  • Mentioning key information
    • This new policy will take effect on…
    • If you have any questions or concerns, please contact…
    • We’ve set up a hotline for troubleshooting. So please contact…
▼▼▼
TRY
Answer these questions.
  • When was the last important announcement made at your company?
  • What was it about?
  • Was the presenter able to put everyone at ease?
  • Was there any resistance to the change?
▼▼▼
ACT
Role play.
Come up with an announcement about the following topics:
  • A major negative change (closure of an office, restructuring, pay cut)
  • A major positive change (new reward system, big event, etc.)
  • A neutral change (new policy, new process, new system, etc.)
