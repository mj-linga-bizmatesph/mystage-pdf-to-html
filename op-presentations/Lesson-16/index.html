OTHER PROGRAMS PRESENTATIONS I LESSON 16
STATUS REPORTS
16
BIZ
TIP
When delivering a status report, make sure you outline the following key information:
  • The tasks: both accomplished and ongoing
  • The issues: problems encountered and corresponding action plans
  • The status: whether it’s ahead, behind or right on schedule
SEE
Example
  • Explaining the tasks
    • I’m happy to say that we’ve completed…| We’ve now started to…
    • I’m glad to report that all work on… | Currently we are focusing on…
  • Explaining the issues and action plans
    • We’ve encountered issues with… | To counter these problems, we’ve…
    • We’re faced with the issue of… | To avoid any delays, we’ve…
  • Explaining the status
    • In spite of these problems, we are still…
    • Overall, in terms of budget, we are…
    • At this point, we seem to be behind schedule. However,…
▼▼▼
TRY
Answer these questions about a project you’re currently working on.
  • Which tasks have been completed?
  • What about those that are still in progress?
  • What issues and challenges have you encountered?
  • What are your action plans to counter these problems?
▼▼▼
ACT
Role play.
Deliver a status report on one of the following topics:
  • A personal project you’re trying to finish
  • A project in your community
  • A project at the office
