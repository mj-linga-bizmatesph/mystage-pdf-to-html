OTHER PROGRAMS PRESENTATIONS I LESSON 17
IMPROMPTU PRESENTATIONS
17
BIZ
TIP
An impromptu speech is when you are given no time for preparation. Use the framework below as a guide:
  • Share a personal experience related to the situation
  • Have one clear message
  • End on a positive note
SEE
Example
  • Sharing a personal experience
    • The first time I met Bob was back in university when…
    • Being here today, brings back lots of memories of when…
    • Just a few minutes ago, I was speaking to Bob about…
  • Having one clear message
    • One thing I’d like everyone to think about is…
    • So I would just like all of you to know…
    • My only advice is…
  • Ending on a positive note
    • So please take this opportunity to…
    • So I hope you all have a great time today.
    • I’m looking forward to getting to know each of you today.
▼▼▼
TRY
Answer these questions.
  • When was the last time you presented something on the spot?
  • What was it about?
  • Have you seen anyone give impromptu speeches before?
  • In what situation might you have to give an impromptu speech?
▼▼▼
ACT
Role play
Make an impromptu presentation on one of the following subjects:
  • A few nice words about a colleague after winning an award
  • A short speech to promote your company’s products at an event
  • A word of advice to new employees in your company
