OTHER PROGRAMS PRESENTATIONS I LESSON 18
INSTRUCTIONAL PRESENTATIONS
18
BIZ
TIP
In this type of presentation, your audience should come away with new knowledge or a new skill. To do this, you should:
  • Explain the goal
  • Demonstrate the steps thoroughly
  • Incorporate hands-on experience when possible
SEE
Example
  • Explaining the goal
    • After this short session, you will be able to…
    • Our goals for today are…
    • By the end of our session, I’d like every one of you to be able to…
  • Demonstrating the steps
    • First, we need to…
    • Next, we click…
    • After that, we can…
  • Incorporating hands-on experience
    • Let’s have some practice.
    • Let’s go to our own screens and try…
    • Who wants to try this? Any volunteers?
▼▼▼
TRY
Answer these questions.
  • What kind of instructional presentation do you like participating in?
  • Has anyone delivered an instructional presentation at work recently?
  • What was it about?
  • Were you able to come away with new knowledge or a new skill?
▼▼▼
ACT
Role play.
Make an instructional presentation on one of the following topics.
  • How to cook a dish
  • How to do one of your tasks
  • How to use a piece of equipment (video camera, printer, photocopier, etc.)
