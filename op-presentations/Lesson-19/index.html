OTHER PROGRAMS PRESENTATIONS I LESSON 19
MOTIVATIONAL SPEECHES
19
BIZ
TIP
A dynamic motivational speech is only effective when the presenter can connect with the audience on an emotional level. To leave an impactful message, you should:
  • Get their attention
  • Use personal stories to prove your point
  • Develop a call to action
SEE
Example
  • Getting their attention
    • What if I told you that you could…?
    • Have you ever wondered why…?
    • There’s a famous quote that goes like this…
  • Using personal stories to prove your point
    • Here’s something my mother once told me.
    • Let me share a personal experience that changed my life.
    • Many people don’t know this about me, but I grew up…
  • Develop a call to action
    • We know WHAT to do. We know HOW to do it. Now let’s DO it.
    • We are at a crossroads. And the choice is ours. We can either…
    • The only thing that’s keeping us from winning is in our minds.
▼▼▼
TRY
Answer these questions.
  • Describe a time when you felt emotionally connected to movie, a song, or a book?
  • What was it that made you feel connected?
  • Did it motivate you to change or do something in any way?
  • Have you ever felt like this by someone’s speech?
▼▼▼
ACT
Role play
Use any of the following goals to deliver a motivational speech. You may also come up with your own.
  • To start using eco-friendly products
  • To pursue learning English
  • To strengthen teamwork among the members
