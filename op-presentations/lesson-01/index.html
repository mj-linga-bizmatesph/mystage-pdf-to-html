PREPARING YOUR PRESENTATION
1
BIZ
TIP
Consider the basics when preparing for a presentation:
Always keep the goal of your presentation in mind
Never go beyond your time limit
If you’re given 10 minutes to present, aim for 8 or 9 minutes only
SEE
The first thing you need to think about when preparing your presentation is what you’d like the audience to take away from your presentation. Do you want them to buy a product, to be more informed about something, or to take some sort of action? With a clear goal in mind, you can come up with a few subtopics to support your goal. Once you have your subtopics, you can begin to add in the details. The chart below can help you organize your thoughts.
