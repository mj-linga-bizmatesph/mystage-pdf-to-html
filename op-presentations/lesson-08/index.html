OTHER PROGRAMS PRESENTATIONS I LESSON 8
ANSWERING QUESTIONS
8
BIZ
TIP
The 4-step process below, can help you handle Q&A sessions with ease:
Inviting questions
Checking your understanding
Answering the question
Confirming
SEE
Example
Presenter: Are there any questions? Yes?
Attendee: What are the chances of going overseas?
Presenter: I think about 10%. OK, next question? Yes?
Bizmates style
Presenter: Now, I’d be happy to answer questions. Yes, the gentleman wearing the navy suit. Please go ahead.
Attendee: What are the chances of going overseas?
Presenter: Do you mean being transferred to an overseas office for a few years?
Attendee: Yes.
Presenter: Thank you for your question. Well, currently we have about 200 Japanese employees based in foreign countries. So that’s 10% of our workforce. These employees showed excellent performance in Japan, could speak English well, and requested to work overseas. So, if you can do those things, the chances may be quite high. Does that answer your question?
Attendee: Yes, thank you.
▼▼▼
TRY
Answer these questions.
  • What did the presenter say to invite questions?
  • What did the presenter say to check his understanding of the question?
  • What did the presenter say to answer the question?
  • What did the presenter say to confirm if the attendee understood the answer to the question?
▼▼▼
ACT
Role play.
Deliver a short presentation on a topic you’re very familiar with and answer some questions in a professional and effective way.
