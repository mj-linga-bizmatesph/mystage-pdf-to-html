OTHER PROGRAMS RESTAURANT ENGLISHILESSON 3
TAKING YOUR CUSTOMERS TO THEIR TABLE
3
BIZ TIP
Make them feel comfortable as you take your customer to the table. You can do this by:
Thanking them for waiting
Guiding them to the table and making conversation
Explaining when you will return
SEE
Example:

Staff:

This way please.

Patron:

OK.

Staff:

Here you are.

Patron:

Thank you.

Bizmates style:

Staff:

Thank you for waiting, Mr. Fuller. Your table is ready. Right this way, please.

Patron:

Great. Thank you.

Staff:

Please watch your step.

Patron:

Thanks.

Staff:

So, how are you this evening?

Patron:

Very well. Thank you.

Staff:

Glad to hear that. Here’s your table. You have a nice view of Tokyo Sky Tree.

Patron:

Beautiful.

Staff:

I’ll be back in just a few minutes to take your drink order.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What do you say when taking your customers to their table?
  • What could you say to make small talk with your customers?
  • How do you take your leave after seating your customers?
▼▼▼
ACT
Role play
Imagine you are taking your customer to his/her table. Make them feel comfortable.
