OTHER PROGRAMS RESTAURANT ENGLISHILESSON 7
SERVING THE MEAL
7
BIZ TIP
How the meal is served can greatly add to the customer experience. You can do this by:
Explaining what you are serving
Highlighting its key features
Explaining how to eat the food, if necessary
SEE
Example:

Staff:

Here’s your order.

Patron:

That looks delicious.

Staff:

…

Patron:

Do we dip it in soya sauce?

Staff:

Not soya sauce. It is ponzu.

Patron:

Oh OK. Thanks.

Bizmates style:

Staff:

Here’s the usuzukuri.

Patron:

That looks delicious!

Staff:

You see, it is sliced very thinly so you can see through the fish. White meat fish tastes much better when it is thinly sliced.

Patron:

It’s so beautiful. Do we dip it in soya sauce?

Staff:

This is not soya sauce. This sauce is called ponzu. You can add a bit of momiji-oroshi, which is Japanese radish with chilli peppers, to the ponzu sauce. And then lightly dip the fish in ponzu.

Patron:

Thank you for your kind explanation.

  • What instructions would you need to give on how to eat these dishes?
