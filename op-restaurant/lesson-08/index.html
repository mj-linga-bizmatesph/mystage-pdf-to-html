OTHER PROGRAMS RESTAURANT ENGLISHILESSON 8
CLEARING THE DISHES
8
BIZ TIP
Sometimes it’s difficult to determine whether customers are finished with their meal or not. Take extra care by:
Asking how they are enjoying their meal and continuing the conversation
Asking if they are finished with their meal
Offering assistance
SEE
Example:
(Staff attempts to clear the patron’s nearly finished meal.)

Patron:

I’m not finished.

Staff:

Oh, sorry.

Patron:

You can take this one.

Staff:

OK.

Patron:

Thank you.

Bizmates style:

Staff:

How are you enjoying your dinner?

Patron:

Everything is just wonderful.

Staff:

I’m glad to hear that. Did you like our original dressing in the salad?

Patron:

It was so good.

Staff:

Thank you for saying so. Are you finished with your meal?

Patron:

Not yet. I’m still enjoying this grilled fish.

Staff:

That’s fine. Please take your time. Is there anything else I can get for you?

Patron:

I think we are OK for now.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What could you ask to continue a conversation with your customer before asking to clear their dish?
  • What sort of responses could you expect from them?
  • How would you respond to their responses?
▼▼▼
ACT
Role play.
Ask your customer how their dinner is, continue the conversation, and then clear their dishes.
