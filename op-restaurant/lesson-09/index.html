OTHER PROGRAMS RESTAURANT ENGLISHILESSON 9
SERVING THE CHECK
9
BIZ TIP
At this point, your customers are ready to leave. This is your last opportunity to make them want to come back. You can do this by :
Asking about their whole dining experience
Explaining payment options
Giving a personal goodbye
SEE
Example:

Staff:

Here’s your check.

Patron:

Thanks… Do you take Diner’s Club?

Staff:

No, we don’t.

Patron:

How about American Express?

Staff:

OK, no problem.

(The staff charges the card.)

Staff:

Thank you. Good night.

Bizmates style:

Staff:

Here’s your check, Mr. Lee. Did you all enjoy your dinner?

Patron:

Yes. Everything was just perfect.

Staff:

Thanks for saying so.

Patron:

Do you take Diner’s Club?

Staff:

I’m afraid we don’t. We take VISA, MasterCard, and American Express.

Patron:

OK, here you go.

Staff:

Thank you. Just a moment please.

(The staff charges the card.)

Staff:

Thank you for dining with us, Mr. Lee. We hope to see you again soon. Have a great time in Ginza tomorrow.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What else could you say to get some feedback from customers?
  • How else would you say “goodbye” as they leave?
  • What else can you do to at this point to make your customers want to come back next time?
▼▼▼
ACT
Role play.
Imagine your customer is ready to leave. Provide the same level of service one last time and make him/her remember your restaurant.
