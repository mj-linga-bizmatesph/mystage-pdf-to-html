OTHER PROGRAMS RESTAURANT ENGLISHILESSON 11
GETTING TO KNOW YOUR CUSTOMERS
11
BIZ TIP
Getting to know your customers is a good way to raise the level of customer satisfaction. Connect with your customers by:
Asking if they are locals or tourists
Making small talk
Building on the information they give you
SEE
Example:

Staff:

Good evening. Here is our menu.

Patron:

Thank you.

Staff:

I will come back later. Please take your time.

Patron:

OK, thanks.

Bizmates style:

Staff:

Welcome to Shochikuen. Is this your first time to dine at our restaurant?

Patron:

Yes, it is. This is a very nice place.

Staff:

Thank you. Do you live here or are you traveling?

Patron:

We’re tourists.

Staff:

I see. Welcome to Tokyo! Have you visited any sightseeing spots?

Patron:

Yes, we went to Asakusa yesterday. It was so beautiful.

Staff:

Oh yes. Have you visited Kamakura?

Patron:

No, not yet. So, what’s in Kamakura?

Staff:

Well, Kamakura is…

▼▼▼
TRY

Practice building on the information and making appropriate suggestions. Use the sample responses below:

  • “It’s my first time to visit Japan.”
  • “We’re here on a business trip.”
  • “I might go shopping tomorrow.”
▼▼▼
ACT
Role play.
Get to know your customer and establish some sort of connection.
