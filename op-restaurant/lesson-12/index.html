OTHER PROGRAMS RESTAURANT ENGLISHILESSON 12
ASSISTING SMALL CHILDREN
12
BIZ TIP
Some customers may dine with their children. Show that your restaurant is a child-friendly place by:
Talking to the children
Offering assistance
Mentioning precautions
SEE
Example:

Staff:

Welcome to Shochikuen. May I take your drink order first?

Patron:

Yes, I’ll have a glass of iced tea.

Staff:

Okay. How about your child?

Patron:

She’ll have orange juice.

Bizmates style:

Staff:

Welcome to Shochikuen. May I take your drink order first?

Patron:

Yes, I’ll have a glass of iced tea.

(The staff turns to the child at the table.)

Staff:

Hi, that’s such a cute dress. What’s your name?

Child:

Tammy.

Staff:

OK Tammy. Would you like something to drink?

Child:

Orange juice.

Staff:

OK. Would you like a high chair or booster seat for her?

Patron:

Yes that would be great. Thank you.

(A few moments later)

Staff:

Here we go, Tammy. Ma’am, please be careful because it’s very unstable when the safety belt isn’t used.

Patron:

Thanks for the reminder.

Staff:

Not at all. I’ll be back with your drinks in a few minutes.
