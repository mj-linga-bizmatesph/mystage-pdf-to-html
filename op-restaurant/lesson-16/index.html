ACCOMMODATING SPECIAL REQUESTS
16
BIZ TIP
When accommodating special requests, it is important to show your willingness to help. Handle these requests by:
Showing willingness to help
Confirming details of the request
Explaining what you will do
SEE
Example:

Patron:

Hi! it’s our son’s birthday today. Could you do something for him?

Staff:

OK, OK.

(A few minutes later)

Patron:

Excuse me? So what are you going to do for our son?

Staff:

Oh, we will put a candle on his dessert.

Patron:

OK, thank you.

Bizmates style:

Patron:

Hi! it’s our son’s birthday today. Could you do something for him?

Staff:

Of course. So how old is he turning?

Patron:

He’s turning seven.

Staff:

And may I ask his name?

Patron:

Yes, it’s Jeffery.

Staff:

Could you please write it here? So, we can place seven candles and a chocolate sign with his name on his dessert.

Patron:

Great.

Staff:

And some of our staff can sing happy birthday and take his photo. Is this OK?

Patron:

That’s perfect.
