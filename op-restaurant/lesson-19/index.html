OTHER PROGRAMS RESTAURANT ENGLISHILESSON 19
SUGGESTING MERCHANDISE AND GIFT CARDS
19
BIZ TIP
Look for opportunities to offer your restaurant’s merchandise, such as packaged foods, branded products, and even gift cards. You can do this by:
Looking for opportunities to offer your restaurant’s merchandise
Offering the product
Making it easy to purchase
SEE
Example:

Staff:

Is everything OK?

Patron:

Yes, this dressing is delicious.

Staff:

Thank you. You can buy it at the counter.

Patron:

Oh, OK. Maybe I’ll take a look when we leave.

Bizmates style:

Staff:

How do you like the dressing?

Patron:

It’s so delicious.

Staff:

I’m so glad that you like it. If you’d like, you can take some back to the US. It’s our original dressing and it can only be purchased here.

Patron:

Oh really? How much is it?

Staff:

Let me show you our merchandise menu.

Patron:

Great. Thank you.

Staff:

We have two sizes. If you have lots of space in your suitcase, you can buy the big bottle. But if you don’t have so much space, I recommend the small bottle. This makes a great souvenir for friends and family too.

Patron:

I see. Maybe I’ll get a couple of the smaller bottles.

▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What merchandise does your restaurant offer?
  • What could you say or do to get your customers interested in your restaurant’s merchandise or gift cards?
  • How could you make it easier for your customers to purchase them?
▼▼▼
ACT
Role play.
Offer your restaurant’s merchandise or gift cards to your customers.
