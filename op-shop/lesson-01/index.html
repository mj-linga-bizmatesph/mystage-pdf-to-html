WELCOMING YOUR CUSTOMER
BIZ TIP

Connect with your customers right from the moment they enter the store by:

  • Welcoming them in a friendly way
  • Making small talk
  • Offering help
      • Irrashaimase!
      • Uh...Hello. Do you speak English?
      • A little. May I help you?
      • I’m interested in some bags....
      • Irrashaimase! Welcome to Matsushima!
      • Thank you!
      • How are you doing today? Is this your first time to visit Matsushima?
      • Oh yes, it’s also my first visit to Tokyo.
      • How do you like it so far ?
      • It’s really nice in Tokyo. I’m loving it so far!
      • That’s great. So how can I help you today?
      • Oh, I’m looking for…
    • Practice making small talk about the following:
      • The weather
      • Your customer’s visit to Tokyo
      • What your customer is wearing
ACT

Role play.

Imagine a foreign customer is visiting your store. Welcome them in a friendly way, make small talk, and offer your help.
