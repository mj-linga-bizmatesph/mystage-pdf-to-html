RECOMMENDING A PRODUCT
BIZ TIP

Don’t just recommend a product; let them experience it. You can do this by:

  • Presenting its features
  • Showing its advantages and benefits
  • Letting the customer hold, examine, or try out the product, if possible
    • (Still at the multimedia store)
      • Here are our headphones.
      • Which do you recommend?
      • This one has inner canal ear buds.
      • But it’s a bit expensive.
      • Yes, because it has a noise-cancelling feature, too.
    • (Still at the multimedia store)
      • Here are our headphones.
      • Which do you recommend?
      • This one has inner canal ear buds. So, it doesn’t let sound out. So, you don’t have to worry about noise leaking to other people around you.
      • But it’s a bit expensive.
      • Yes, because it has a noise-cancelling feature, too. You won’t be bothered by outside noise. So, you can concentrate on the meeting even when it’s noisy around you. Would you like to try it on?
      • Yes, please… OK, you’re right. The sound is very good.
    • Answer these questions.
      • What are three key features of your top-selling product?
      • What are the advantages of these features?
      • What are the benefits of these features to your customer?
ACT

Role play.

Recommend a product to your customer. State the features, advantages, and benefits of your product. Highlight the features that your customer wants.
