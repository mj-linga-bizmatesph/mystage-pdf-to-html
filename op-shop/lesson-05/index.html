SUGGESTING ANOTHER PRODUCT
BIZ TIP

Don’t give up on the sale when the first suggestion isn’t right. Offer the next option by:

  • Showing you understand how the customer feels
  • Showing that you understand what the customer really wants
  • Offering a more suitable product
    • (After the clerk explained the features of a rice cooker.)
      • This rice cooker is too complicated.
      • But it makes very good rice.
      • No, it’s not what I want.
    • (After the clerk explained the features of a rice cooker.)
      • This rice cooker is too complicated.
      • I understand how you feel. It has too many unnecessary features.
      • That’s right.
      • Maybe you just want a rice cooker that is simple to use and cooks great rice.
      • Exactly.
      • In that case, I’m sure you will like this model. It’s our most popular model among Chinese tourists.
    • Answer these questions.
      • What are some common concerns that customers have about your product?
      • What can you say to show that you understand how they feel?
      • What is it that your customers really want in these cases?
      • What options do you offer?
ACT

Role play.

Imagine your customer doesn’t like your first product offer. Show you understand how they feel, show that you understand what they want, and then offer a more suitable product.
