6
MENTIONING THE PRICE
BIZ TIP

Price has such a big impact on the customer’s decision. So, don’t just give them the price. Justify it by:

  • Mentioning the price in yen and in their local currency
  • Highlighting the benefits and long-term savings
  • Offering special discounts and payment options
    • (Still at the kitchen appliances store)
      • So, how much is this rice cooker?
      • It’s 120,000 yen.
      • Oh, that’s too expensive.
      • I see.
    • (Still at the kitchen appliances store)
      • So, how much is this rice cooker?
      • It’s 120,000 yen or about 30,000 Taiwan dollars.
      • Oh, that’s too expensive.
      • Yes, it’s about double or triple the price of most rice cookers. However, you can use this every day for over 10 years. Even inexpensive rice will taste like top quality rice.
      • Can you give me a discount?
      • If you create a free members’ card, you can get 12,000 points. So, you can get 12,000 yen off on another product at our store.
    • Answer these questions.
      • What’s the local currency and its exchange rate to the yen of your foreign customers?
      • What are some key benefits you can highlight on some of your products?
      • What discounts and payment options does your store offer?
ACT

Role play.

Mention the price of a product to your customer, highlight the benefits, and offer special discounts and payment options.
