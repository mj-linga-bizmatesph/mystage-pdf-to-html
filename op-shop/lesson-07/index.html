UP-SELLING AND CROSS-SELLING
BIZ TIP

Time your up-sell or cross-sell when customers are ready to buy. To up-sell or cross-sell effectively:

  • Ask more questions to see what else you can offer
  • Offer accessories, product bundles, or product upgrades
  • Assure them that they will enjoy their new item/s
    • (At a confectionery store)
      • I’d like 6 boxes of the matcha cookies.
      • Is that all?
      • Yes.
      • Thank you.
    • (At a confectionery store)
      • I’d like 6 boxes of the matcha cookies.
      • Are these souvenirs for family and friends?
      • Yes, for family and friends back home.
      • You can get 10% off if you buy 10 or more boxes.
      • Hmm… OK, I still have room in my suitcase. I’ll take 10 boxes.
      • Also, this matcha cream goes really well with the matcha cookies. Would you like to try it?
      • Great, thank you. This is delicious. OK, I’ll take 3 packs of the matcha cream, too.
      • Thank you. I’m sure everyone will enjoy them.
    • Answer these questions.
      • What are some products you can up-sell to your customers?
      • What are some product bundles you can cross-sell?
      • What can you say to assure your customer that they’ll enjoy their purchase/s?
ACT

Role play.

Imagine your customer is buying something. Ask questions, up-sell or cross-sell, and assure them they will enjoy their purchase/s.
