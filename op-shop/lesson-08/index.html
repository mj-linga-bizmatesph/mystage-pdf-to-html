SELLING WITH IMPACT
BIZ TIP

To close the sale, you can sell with impact by:

  • Sharing your own experience with the product
  • Sharing feedback from other customers
  • Describing the emotions associated with the product
    • (At a beauty appliances store)
      • I’m looking for a hair-styling gift for my wife.
      • How about this hair-styling iron?
      • Is it any good?
      • Yes.
    • (At a beauty appliances store)
      • I’m looking for a hair-styling gift for my wife.
      • I use this hair-styling iron at home, and it is great. It releases negative ions, so it naturally straightens your hair. My hair is naturally curly, but I can easily straighten it.
      • That’s interesting.
      • It’s our most popular styling iron. We’ve received excellent feedback from customers.
      • That’s great.
      • I’m sure your wife will be so happy to receive this as a gift.
    • Answer these questions.
      • What personal experiences do you have of your store’s products?
      • What positive customer feedback have you received about your products?
      • What emotions do you associate with your products?
ACT

Role play.

Your customer is looking for a product. Sell with impact by sharing your own product experience and feedback from satisfied buyers, and describing emotions.
