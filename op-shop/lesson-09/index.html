9
SAYING GOODBYE
BIZ TIP

Leave a lasting impression by:

  • Thanking customers for their purchase
  • Reassuring them that they made the right choice
  • Giving a personal goodbye
    • (At the beauty store checkout)
      • Arigatou gozaimasu! Thank you for buying.
      • Thanks.
      • You’re welcome.
    • (At the beauty store checkout)
      • Thank you very much for shopping at Matsushima.
      • Thanks for all your help.
      • Not at all. I really think your wife will enjoy this hair-styling iron.
      • I’m sure she will.
      • I hope you enjoy the rest of your stay in Japan. And have a great time in Roppongi tonight.
      • Will do. Thanks again.
    • Answer these questions.
      • What can you say to thank your customers for their purchase?
      • How would you reassure your customers for some items that they purchased?
      • What could you say to personalize your goodbye to your customers?
ACT

Role play.

Thank your customer, reassure them that they made the right purchase, and give them a personal goodbye.
