HELPING CUSTOMERS FIND THEIR WAY AROUND
BIZ TIP

When a customer asks where to find items, take the opportunity to connect with the customer by:

  • Confirming what they want to see
  • Chatting with them as you take them to the area
  • Highlighting the products
    • (At a clothing store)
      • Excuse me, do you know where I can find women‘s T-shirts?
      • Yes, over there.
    • (At a clothing store)
      • Excuse me, do you know where I can find women ‘s T-shirts?
      • Certainly. Are you looking for designer T-shirts or something more casual, like printed shirts?
      • Designer, please. Thanks.
      • This way please. So, is this your first time to visit our shop?
      • Yes, it is. My friend recommended your shop.
      • Oh, I’m so happy to hear that. So, are you looking for a gift or something for yourself?
      • Just for myself.
      • I see. All the T-shirts here are original designs, and the ones on the top are slim fit. These ones down here are more loose-fitting…
    • Answer these questions.
      • What could you ask your customers to confirm what they want to see?
      • What questions could you ask your customers as you take them to the area?
      • What can you say to highlight some of your products?
ACT

Role play.

Confirm with your customer what they want to see, then make small talk as you take them to the area, and finally highlight the products they are asking for.
