DEMONSTRATING THE PRODUCT
BIZ TIP

Make your product demo simple, logical, and attractive by:

  • Understanding what the customer wants to know
  • Using signposts, such as “3 simple steps, first, 2nd, and finally”
  • Encouraging the customer to try it
    • (At a household appliances store)
      • Our robotic vacuum cleaner is very easy to use. To set the timer, open the cover. And it says “SET TIME”. So press “NEW” and “DELETE” at the same time and prepare the clock timer. Pressing the plus buttons will change the day, hour, and minute. And press “SAVE” and it will set your robot’s schedule for cleaning. It’s that simple.
      • That’s confusing. I don’t think I’m going to use the timer anyway.
    • (At a household appliances store)
      • If you set the timer, it will automatically clean the room. Is this a useful feature for you?
      • Yes, that’s great.
      • You can set the timer in 3 simple steps. First, we open the cover. Can you see “SET TIME” on the display?
      • Yes, I see it.
      • Next, we press “NEW” and “DELETE” at the same time. Could you please press these buttons?
      • Like this?
      • That’s right. And finally, we use the plus buttons here to set the time and press “SAVE”. Please try it.
      • That’s simple.
    • Answer these questions.
      • What signpost expressions can you use to demonstrate your product?
      • How can you invite your customer to try out your product?
      • How can you encourage questions from your customer?
ACT

Role play.

Demonstrate to your customer how to operate a product, invite them to try it out as well, and encourage questions.
