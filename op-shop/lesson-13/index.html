HANDLING QUESTIONS
BIZ TIP

Some customers may ask you many questions about your product. Answer them effectively by:

  • Rephrasing the question
  • Giving the answer or finding out if you don’t know
  • Asking if they are satisfied with your answer
    • (Back at the household appliances store)
      • Hello. Do you know if this electric razor can be used in the US?
      • Oh, yes. It is a portable razor.
      • No, I mean is it the same voltage as in the US?
      • Sorry, I don’t know.
    • (Back at the household appliances store)
      • Hello. Do you know if this electric razor can be used in the US?
      • Do you mean if this electric razor uses the same voltage in the US?
      • Yes, that’s right.
      • Could you please wait a moment while I check for you?
      • Yes, sure.
        • (A few minutes later.)
      • Thank you for waiting. This razor has a range of 100-240 Volts and 50-60 Hertz. So it can be used in Japan, in Europe, and in the US. Does this answer your question?
      • Yes, it does. Thank you.
      • Not at all.
    • Answer these questions.
      • How do you confirm with customers if you’ve understood their questions correctly?
      • What can you say when you don’t know how to answer your customer?
      • If you know the answer to a challenging question, how do you confirm if your answer satisfies your customer?
