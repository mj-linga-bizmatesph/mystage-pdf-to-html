EXPLAINING DUTY-FREE SERVICE
BIZ TIP

Tax-free service varies from country to country, so it’s important to make sure your customers understand the system in your country. You can do this by:

  • Explaining the items that are tax free
  • Explaining how to pay
  • Explaining the packaging regulations
      • Hi. Are all items tax free?
      • Yes, they are.
      • Hi. Are all items tax free?
      • Yes, they are. For general goods, the total amount needs to be over 10,000 yen. And for consumable goods, the total needs to be over 5,000 yen.
      • I see.
      • We just need to see your passport and we will automatically deduct the tax from the amount when you pay.
      • Great! I’ll be getting these make-up sets.
      • Great. And for consumable goods, we’ll need to pack them according to government regulations. Please don’t open the make-up package while you’re in Japan. If you open it during your stay, you will be taxed at the airport.
      • Thanks for telling me.
    • Answer these questions.
      • What items are tax free in your store? What examples can you give?
      • What does the customer need to do to receive the tax-free service in your store?
      • How would you explain the packaging regulations?
ACT

Role play.

Explain your store’s tax-free shopping procedure and assist your customer when they’re ready to pay.
