WRAPPING GIFTS
BIZ TIP

Treat your customers to a unique Japanese gift wrapping service by:

  • Offering a choice of wrapping paper, ribbons, and gift cards
  • Asking the customer to wait
  • Presenting the gift-wrapped item
    • (At a souvenir shop)
      • Could I have these items gift-wrapped?
      • OK, it’ll take about10 minutes. Is it OK?
      • Yes, that’s fine.
    • (At a souvenir shop)
      • Could I have these items gift-wrapped?
      • Certainly. Could you please choose the wrapping paper, ribbon, and gift card?
      • Oh, these are lovely. I’ll choose this, this…and this.
      • Thank you. It should be ready in about 10 minutes. Here’s your waiting card. We’ll call your number when it’s ready.
      • Thank you.
        • (After a few minutes)
      • Thank you for waiting. We hope you like the way we have wrapped these gifts.
      • Oh, it’s beautiful. Thank you so much.
      • It’s my pleasure.
    • Answer these questions.
      • What options do your customers have for gift wrapping in your store?
      • What do you say to have your customers wait for their wrapped items?
      • What can you say to present the gift-wrapped item to the customer?
ACT

Role play.

Offer to have some purchased items gift-wrapped for your customers, have them wait, then present the wrapped items.
