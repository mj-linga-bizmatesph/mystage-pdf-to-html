18
DEALING WITH ANGRY CUSTOMERS
BIZ TIP

Angry customers want their problems solved quickly. You can do this by:

  • Apologizing sincerely and showing eagerness to solve the problem
  • Understanding their situation
  • Explaining your actions and getting confirmation from them
    • (At an electronics store)
      • I bought this digital camera yesterday, but it doesn’t work.
      • I’m very sorry.
      • I wanted to use it today, but now I can’t.
      • I’m very sorry.
    • (At an electronics store)
      • I bought this digital camera yesterday, but it doesn’t work.
      • I’m terribly sorry. Let me take care of this right away.
      • Thank you.
      • Can you tell me what you did after opening the box?
      • Yes, I opened it, put in the battery, and turned it on.
      • I’ll ask our camera specialist to check it out right away. Could you please wait a few minutes?
      • Sure.
        • (a few minutes later)
      • Thank you for waiting. I think I know the problem. Did you charge the battery before turning it on?
      • No, it’s a brand new battery.
      • Yes, but rechargeable batteries are empty when they are new. I’m sorry that this wasn’t made clear to you.
      • OK, great. Thanks for your help.
    • Answer these questions.
      • What are some reasons that a customer may complain in your store?
      • What are some questions you can ask to find out the customer’s situation?
      • What actions can you take to resolve your customers’ problems?
ACT

Role play.

Apologize sincerely to an angry customer, understand your customer’s situation, and resolve the issue.
