19
BUILDING CUSTOMER LOYALTY
BIZ TIP

Great customer service can lead to customer loyalty. Create fans of your store by:

  • Showing that you remember the customer
  • Making small talk
  • Offering appropriate items
    • (At the confectionery store)
      • Hello. May I help you?
      • Yes, you had a sale on matcha cookies last week. Is the sale still on?
      • I’m sorry. That sale is over.
      • I see. Thanks.
    • (At the confectionery store)
      • Welcome back. It’s great to see you again.
      • Hi, oh you remember me.
      • Yes, you bought matcha cookies and matcha cream.
      • Yes, that’s right.
      • So, have you visited any sites so far?
      • Yes, we visited Asakusa and Tokyo Sky Tree. We had a great time.
      • I’m glad to hear that. So what can I help you with today?
      • Well, I’d like more of those matcha cookies. Is the sale still on?
      • I’m afraid that sale is over. However, some of the other matcha items are on sale. Let me show you. So how much space do you have in your suitcase…?
    • Answer these questions.
      • What can you say to personalize your greetings and services to repeat customers?
      • What questions can you ask the customer to make small talk?
      • What can you say to lead the customer to their next purchase?
ACT

Role play.

Build your customer’s loyalty by personalizing your service, asking for their feedback, and thanking them for their loyalty.
