SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to travel?

  • Beware of tourist traps when sightseeing.
  • Some of the ruins of ancient civilizations are beautiful.
  • What’s your idea of a good summer getaway?
  • Do you collect keepsakes whenever you go traveling?
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about traveling. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What are the most popular travel spots in your country?
  • Which place have you travelled to many times?
  • Is traveling popular among Japanese?
  • Do you prefer staying at home or going out to travel?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What kind of place do you like going to?
  • Are there some dangerous places in your country for tourists?
  • How safe is it to travel in your country?
  • What do you think are the good points and bad points of traveling?
