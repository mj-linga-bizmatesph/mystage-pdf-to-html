SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to music?

  1. This is their only famous song. They were a one-hit wonder.
  2. I listen to ballads when I’m lonely, and rock when I need energy.
  3. Are you going to Jeff Ryan’s gig? His last performance was great!
  4. If something unexpected happens, we have to play it by ear.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about music. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. Do you know how to play any musical instruments?
  2. Do you enjoy listening to music while working out?
  3. What music genre is popular in your country?
  4. Do you see yourself having music as your profession?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. How does music influence your mood?
  2. Is music still being taught at schools in your country nowadays?
  3. Have you been to any concerts of your favorite artist?
  4. If you were to make a song, what would it be about?
▼▼▼
ACT

What are your thoughts about this quote?
