SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to dogs?

  1. I prefer having K9s as pets to cats.
  2. You should have a pooper scooper whenever you take your pet for a walk.
  3. My dog has become so calm since we sent it to obedience school.
  4. Let’s keep our spending on a tight leash as much as possible.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about dogs. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. What is the most popular breed of dog in your country?
  2. What’s a popular name for dogs in your country?
  3. Is it expensive to own a dog as pets in Japan?
  4. What tricks would you like to teach your dog?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. Would you enter your pet into any dog shows?
  2. What are the pros and cons of having a pet dog?
  3. How is the dog grooming business in your country?
  4. Would you let your dog sleep in your bedroom?

ACT

What are your thoughts about this quote?

“Dogs don't rationalize. They don't hold anything against a person. They don't see the outside of a human but the inside of a human..”

-- Cesar Millan (dog trainer)
