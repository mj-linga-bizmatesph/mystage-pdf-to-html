LESSON 5: HOBBIES
5
BIZ TIP
When you need time to think, you can say:
I never thought of that before. Let me see…
That’s a good question. Let me think…
Please give me a minute to think about that.
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to hobbies?

  • I started taking cycling for recreation during weekends.
  • Dishwashing is a tedious household chore.
  • Do you think stamp collecting is a lame pastime?
  • He usually spends his me-time reading books at the park.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about hobbies. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What is the most common hobby in your country?
  • What other hobbies do you think are worth pursuing?
  • What new hobby have you taken up lately?
  • What was your reason for taking up this hobby?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What is your own definition of the word hobby?
  • What do you think are some examples of dangerous hobbies?
  • What’s a popular hobby among men in your country? Among women?
  • What does a hobby say about a person’s character?
▼▼▼
ACT

What are your thoughts about this quote?
