BIZ TIP
When you want to express your opinion, you can say:
In my opinion,…
In my view,…
From my perspective,....
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to food?

  1. The food there is so palatable! I think you should try it.
  2. I might have eaten too much. I’m stuffed.
  3. I feel famished every time after working out at the gym.
  4. Let’s grab a bite at the new restaurant across the street.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about food. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. What food did you enjoy eating as a child?
  2. What food is your country most famous for?
  3. What sorts of food do you try to stay away from?
  4. What is the most staple food in your country?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. Do you prefer home-cooked meals over those served at restaurants?
  2. Do you often go for midnight snacks?
  3. How many times do you usually eat in a day?
  4. What do you usually eat for snacks?
