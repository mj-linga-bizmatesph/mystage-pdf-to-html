TOPIC 7: THE INTERNET
7
BIZ TIP
When you agree, you can say:
I completely agree with you.
I know what you mean.
I feel exactly the same way.
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to the Internet?

  1. Do your colleagues use netiquette?
  2. My PC is on the blink again.
  3. Tim is always on his computer. He is such a geek.
  4. I hate this new computer system. It has so many glitches.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about the Internet. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. What do you usually use the Internet for?
  2. What kind of Internet connection do you have?
  3. What company is the biggest Internet service provider in your country?
  4. Other than work, how do you make use of the Internet productively?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. Do you think you can get by without the Internet for a month?
  2. Do you think having an Internet connection at home is also a must?
  3. Is Internet service expensive in your country?
  4. How do you think life would be like right now without the Internet?
