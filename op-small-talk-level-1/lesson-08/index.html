SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to toys?

  • This collectible is so rare! I’m surprised you got one!
  • She loves plushies. She still keeps her stuffed bunny from childhood.
  • I would never go for knock offs. Fake goods are poor quality.
  • My mom threw away my old action figures. When I was a boy, they were my favorite.

TRY

Let’s talk about toys. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Are you still fond of toys even as a grown-up?
  • What sort of things do you consider now as toys?
  • What are some popular toys among children in your country?
  • What do you consider as "toys for big boys?"

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What is the most popular toy made in your country?
  • What fond memories do you have with your toys as a child?
  • Were there any toys you didn’t like to play with?
  • How do toys shape a child’s way of thinking?
