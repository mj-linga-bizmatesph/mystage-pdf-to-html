TOPIC 9: FASHION
9
BIZ TIP
When you disagree, you can say:
I’m not sure I agree with you.
I have a different opinion on that.
I see things a little differently.
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to fashion?

  • This style is just a fad. I still prefer the usual.
  • Don’t be too flamboyant if you’re going to an interview.
  • I haven’t decided what my getup will be for the party.
  • You look like someone from the 70’s. The style is so retro.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about fashion. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you think you can tell a lot about the person by their clothes?
  • What sort of fashion accessories do you usually wear?
  • What is the latest fashion in your country?
  • Are you always conscious of how others see you?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What is the biggest clothing company in your country?
  • Do you like wearing colorful, printed shirts or just plain ones?
  • Do you often go along with the latest trend?
  • Do you agree that being fashionable boosts a person’s confidence?
▼▼▼
ACT

What are your thoughts about this quote?

“Don’t be into trends. Don’t make fashion own you, but you decide what you are, what you want to express by the way you dress and the way you live.”

-- Gianni Versace (designer)
