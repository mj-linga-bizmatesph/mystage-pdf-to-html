BIZ TIP
When you can’t think of a word, you can say:
What’s the word for…?
What do you call that thing that…?
I don’t know how to explain it. It has…, it is…, it looks like…, it’s used for….
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to sports?

  • I always support the underdog. It’s more exciting.
  • Let's sit in the bleachers. The seats are already full.
  • Don’t make excuses. Nobody likes a sore loser.
  • Their roster this year is really strong. They’ll do well!
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about sports. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What are the most popular sports in Japan?
  • What sports do you watch?
  • Why do old people play golf?
  • What sport in your country is not popular in other parts of the world?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What other sports would you like to try?
  • What kind of sports do you think are too dangerous for you?
  • What are the pros and cons of team sports?
  • Is sports more of a pastime for you, or is it a way of life?
