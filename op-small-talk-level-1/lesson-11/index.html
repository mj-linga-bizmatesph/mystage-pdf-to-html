LESSON 11: MOVIES
11
BIZ TIP
When someone is speaking too fast, you can say:
What do you think of…
What's your take on this?
How do you feel about this?
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to movies?

  • I prefer suspense movies to chick flicks.
  • I like Steven Spielberg. Most of his movies are blockbusters.
  • Although it has a great story, the movie turned out to be a flop.
  • I gave the movie two thumbs up! It was fantastic!
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about movies. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What was the last Hollywood movie you saw?
  • What’s the next movie you’d like to see?
  • How big is the film industry in your country?
  • What kind of movies are popular among men/women in Japan?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you prefer going to cinemas or just watching DVDs?
  • What are the pros and cons of going to cinemas nowadays?
  • Are movie tickets expensive in your country?
  • How do you feel about watching 3D movies?
▼▼▼
