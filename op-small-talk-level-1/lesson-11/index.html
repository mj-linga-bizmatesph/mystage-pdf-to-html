TOPIC 11: MOVIES
11
BIZ TIP
When you want to ask for an opinion, you can say:
What do you think of…
What's your take on this?
How do you feel about this?
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to movies?

  1. I prefer suspense movies to chick flicks.
  2. I like Steven Spielberg. Most of his movies are blockbusters.
  3. Although it has a great story, the movie turned out to be a flop.
  4. I gave the movie two thumbs up! It was fantastic!
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about movies. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. What was the last Hollywood movie you saw?
  2. What’s the next movie you’d like to see?
  3. How big is the film industry in your country?
  4. What kind of movies are popular among men/women in Japan?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. Do you prefer going to cinemas or just watching DVDs?
  2. What are the pros and cons of going to cinemas nowadays?
  3. Are movie tickets expensive in your country?
  4. How do you feel about watching 3D movies?
▼▼▼

TOPIC 1: CARS
1
BIZ TIP
When someone is speaking too fast, you can say:
Can you speak more slowly please?
Please slow down.
You are speaking too fast for me.
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to cars?

  1. The used car that Tim bought is a lemon. It has so many problems.
  2. Can I ride shotgun? I hate sitting in the backseat.
  3. I’m going cruising in my new car. Do you want to come?
  4. The engine isn’t perfect yet. It still needs some fine-tuning.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about cars. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. What kind of car do you have or would you like to have?
  2. Is it expensive to have a car in your country?
  3. How dangerous is driving in your country?
  4. Are the driving laws really strict in your country?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. Do you have a car?
  2. What types of cars are popular in your country?
  3. How much is gas in your country?
  4. How have cars made the world a better place? A worse place?
