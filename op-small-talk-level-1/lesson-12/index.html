TOPIC 12: ELECTRONIC GADGETS

BIZ TIP
When you want to show that you’re following the conversation, you can say:
I see.
Right.
Ah huh.
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to electronic gadgets?

  • I’m afraid I’m not tech-savvy so I’m not that familiar with this phone.
  • This is too complicated. Don’t you want a more user-friendly app?
  • What do you think of my new gizmo? It’s the latest from Apple.
  • Latest models are always made with cutting-edge technology.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about electronic gadgets. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What is your newest electronic gadget?
  • What other electronic gadgets would you like to have?
  • What’s the most popular brand of electronic gadget in your country?
  • Are gadgets like these very expensive in your country?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What do you think is the coolest gadget out there in the market?
  • Are you very brand-specific when buying gadgets?
  • What are the pros and cons of using electronic gadgets?
  • Do you believe they can define someone’s social status?
