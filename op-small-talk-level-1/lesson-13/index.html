SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to celebrations?

  • I’m a little tired. I think I’ll skip the after-party.
  • The boss said the whole team will meet for a brief powwow after lunch.
  • My brother threw a pool party last week. My kids really had a ball!
  • We are painting the town red tomorrow night. It’s my birthday.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about celebrations. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • How does your family celebrate special occasions?
  • Do you celebrate after a promotion at work?
  • What are the most extravagant celebrations in your country?
  • How do you usually celebrate your birthday?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you prefer simple or extravagant celebrations?
  • What occasions do you often celebrate with your friends?
  • How do you celebrate weddings in your country?
  • Do you always stick to your budget when celebrating something?
