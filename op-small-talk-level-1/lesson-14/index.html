TOPIC 14: CHORES
14
BIZ TIP
When you want to show that you are shocked, you can say:
That's unbelievable.
Oh my goodness.
You've got to be kidding.
Are you serious?
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to chores?

  • This old vacuum cleaner is cumbersome. I think it’s time to buy a new one.
  • I find washing dishes too tedious, so I bought a dishwasher.
  • I always do the dirty work, so can you take out the trash tonight?
  • The hamper is full again, so I have to do the laundry.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about chores. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What household chores are commonly done by men in Japan? By women?
  • Who does the laundry at home?
  • What household chores did you do when you were a child?
  • What are some of the chores you do at work?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • How often do you clean your room?
  • What are some chores you do outside the house?
  • What are some chores that people do differently than in the old days?
  • What do you think of the idea of paying your children to do chores?
