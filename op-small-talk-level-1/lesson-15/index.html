SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to retirement?

  1. The number of retirees will likely increase over the next few years.
  2. Her grandma is still, surprisingly, spry at the age of 78.
  3. If you want good advice, ask the old-timers here.
  4. At the age of 63, he felt he wasn’t ready to be put out to pasture.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about retirement. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. What’s the retirement age in your country?
  2. Do you know anyone who has retired early?
  3. What will you do with your retirement money?
  4. How do elderly people spend their retirement in your country?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. How do you see yourself when you retire from work?
  2. What does your company do when an employee retires?
  3. Are you prepared to retire from work when the time comes?
  4. Do you view retirement as something positive or negative?
