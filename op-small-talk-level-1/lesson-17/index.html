SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to wealth?

  • She belongs to one of the most affluent families around here.
  • Some say he became wealthy because he’s a stingy person.
  • If I save more money, I’ll be more well-off.
  • Many celebrities, athletes and politicians live in silk-stocking districts.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about wealth. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Who are the wealthiest families in your country?
  • Do you think wealth is limited to money and material things?
  • What wealth would you willingly share with others?
  • What are some things money can’t buy?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you agree that wealth can sometimes change a man’s character?
  • How would you use your money if you were a very wealthy person?
  • How could you take good care of your wealth?
  • Who comes to your mind when you hear the word wealth?
▼▼▼
ACT

What are your thoughts about this quote?

“The greatness of a man is not in how much wealth he acquires, but in his integrity and his ability to affect those around him positively.”

-- Bob Marley (musician)
