SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to anime?

  • The Simpsons is a classic satire of the American family stereotype.
  • I find anime caricatures funny when they’re super-deformed with big heads.
  • My mom said I should act my age and stop watching cartoons.
  • I think anime has a big fandom among teens.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about cartoons. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What’s the most popular cartoon show in your country?
  • What’s your most favorite anime?
  • How popular is anime in Japan?
  • Do you still watch cartoons?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Why do you think anime became a big hit in many countries?
  • Is anime still popular in your country?
  • Do you know how to draw your favorite cartoon character?
  • What foreign cartoon shows are popular in your country?
