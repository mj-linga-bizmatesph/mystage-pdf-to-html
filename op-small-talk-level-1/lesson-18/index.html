SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to anime?

  1. The Simpsons is a classic satire of the American family stereotype.
  2. I find anime caricatures funny when they’re super-deformed with big heads.
  3. My mom said I should act my age and stop watching cartoons.
  4. I think anime has a big fandom among teens.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about cartoons. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. What’s the most popular cartoon show in your country?
  2. What’s your most favorite anime?
  3. How popular is anime in Japan?
  4. Do you still watch cartoons?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  1. Why do you think anime became a big hit in many countries?
  2. Is anime still popular in your country?
  3. Do you know how to draw your favorite cartoon character?
  4. Who comes to your mind when you hear the word wealth?
▼▼▼
