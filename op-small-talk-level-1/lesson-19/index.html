TOPIC 19: ANTIQUES

BIZ TIP
When you want to give encouragement, you can say:
Don't worry about it.
I'm sure it'll be fine.
I know you can do it.
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to antiques?

  • This wooden pipe is an heirloom that belonged to my great grandfather.
  • My grandfather has kept this vintage wine for many years.
  • The carvings on this old table bear the hallmarks of the Victorian era.
  • I’ve heard that joke so many times. It’s as old as the hills.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about antiques. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What is the oldest antique you have at home?
  • Are antiques very expensive in your country?
  • What kind of antiques do collectors usually look for in your country?
  • What kind of antique would you like to have?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Does anyone in your family like collecting antiques?
  • Are there many antique collectors in your country?
  • Do you prefer antique furniture or modern ones?
  • Do you have any family heirlooms?
