BIZ TIP
When you want to check your understanding, you can say:
Can I check my understanding?
Are you saying…?
Do you mean to say that…?
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to animals?

  • She’s looking for an exterminator to get rid of vermin problems at home.
  • I mostly sleep in the day, so I consider myself a nocturnal person.
  • He trained his feline pet to chase away mice.
  • You’re scared of creepy-crawlies, aren’t you? It’s just a small spider.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about animals. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What is your country’s national animal?
  • What kind of animals do you like?
  • What are some extinct animals in your country?
  • What’s a popular animal native to your country?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What kind of animal best describes your personality?
  • What’s the most majestic-looking animal to you?
  • What animals can be found in your local zoo?
  • Are you fond of watching animal shows?
