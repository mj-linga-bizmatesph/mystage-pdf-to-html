TOPIC 1: SOCIAL MEDIA
1
BIZ TIP
Answering in full or complete sentences makes it easier to add details to your answer and therefore continue the conversation.
Q: Do you use Facebook?
A: Yes.
A: Yes, I use Facebook to stay in touch with friends and family.
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to social media?

  • Their public announcement caused too much hype among their employees.
  • Did you know that your video has gone viral on Facebook?
  • To avoid lurkers, our online forum should be limited only to members.
  • We can now virtually rub elbows with strangers who share our interests.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about social media. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Is social media popular among business professionals in your country?
  • How many social media accounts do you have?
  • Do you consider yourself a die-hard user of social media?
  • Who uses social media more in your country, men or women?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • How does social media benefit you?
  • What’s the most popular social media network in your country?
  • Do you check your social media accounts even at work?
  • Does your company allow their employees to access social media sites?
▼▼▼
ACT

What are your thoughts about this quote?

“Social media has given us this idea that we should all have a posse of friends when in reality, if we have one or two really good friends, we are lucky.”

-- Brene Brown(author)
