Do you know these words and phrases related to fame?

  • You can see many people trying to impersonate Elvis Presley in the U.S.
  • It must be stressful for celebrities to always be in the limelight.
  • My boss is notorious for his short temper. He’s a good guy though.
  • She had a brush with fame when she was announced Sales Rep of the year.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about famous people. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Who’s famous in your country now?
  • Why is he/she famous in your country?
  • Who’s your favorite foreign personality?
  • Can you name someone from your country who reached international fame?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What are the pros and cons of being famous?
  • Do you like fame, or would you rather have a low profile?
  • Who’s a famous personality among men in your country? Among women?
  • Why do you think they are famous?
