BIZ TIP
Use contractions to connect words when you can to sound more natural during a conversation.
a. I will just read the paper tomorrow. I knew I should have watched the game.
b. I’ll just read the paper tomorrow. I knew I should’ve watched the game.
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to homes?

  • Jim now resides in Hawaii, but spends most of his time traveling.
  • The old manor was destroyed when the storm hit the countryside.
  • This is such a nice room. It’s very cozy.
  • My brother has always been a nomad moving from one city to another.

TRY

Let’s talk about homes. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What kind of homes are popular in your country?
  • Is it expensive to own a home in your country?
  • What do you look for when buying a house?
  • Do you prefer owning a home or renting an apartment?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What’s an ideal home like for bachelors in your country?
  • What’s a typical family home like in your country?
  • Is it easy to apply for housing loans in your country?
  • Do you prefer a home in the city or the countryside?
