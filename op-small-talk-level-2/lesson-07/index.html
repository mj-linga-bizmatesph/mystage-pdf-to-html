BIZ TIP
In conversation, some sounds are dropped to sound more natural.
Q: So, what do you think of the painting?
A: Generally, it has an ugly look, but what I find entertaining is...
A: Gen’rally, it’as an ugly look, bu’what I find en’ertaining is...
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to visual arts?

  • It’s a pity the murals in the old temple walls haven’t been preserved.
  • They’re selling small replicas of the sculpture found in the museum.
  • Be careful with your report. The boss has an eye for details.
  • I find her face very beautiful. To me, she’s eye candy.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about visual arts. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What painting or sculpture do you have at home?
  • Have you ever tried painting something?
  • What are some typical art forms in your country?
  • What’s the usual theme for art in your country?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Who’s a famous artist/painter in your country?
  • What can you see in your local art museum?
  • Is photography more popular in your country than painting?
  • Which do you admire most: paintings, sculptures or photographs?
