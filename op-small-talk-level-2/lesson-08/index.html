BIZ TIP
One way to expand your vocabulary is to use a thesaurus. Good = fantastic, wonderful, excellent, great, awesome, splendid, magnificent.
A: It’s good! I’m sure the kids will have a good time.
A: It’s fantastic! I’m sure the kids will have a wonderful time.
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to parenting?

  • I was born and reared here in Tokyo.
  • He continued to nurture the injured puppy until it was healthy again.
  • Sharp objects must be well-kept. Your home needs to be childproof.
  • His parents pampered him too much, so he ended up being spoiled.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about parenting. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What’s the average number of children in a typical family in your country?
  • What do you think are the joys of parenting? The frustrations?
  • What’s a successful parent like in your country?
  • What are some issues with parenting in your country?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Can you name 3 good qualities you’d like to teach your children?
  • What’s the most important value taught by parents to their kids?
  • What makes a good parent in your opinion?
  • How do you divide your time as a parent and as a worker/businessperson?
