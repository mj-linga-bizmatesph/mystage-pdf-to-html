TOPIC 11: TECHNOLOGY
11
BIZ TIP
Closed questions encourage Yes/No or short answers. Ask closed questions when you want to confirm or know a specific detail.
Is this your computer?
When did you buy it?
What brand is it?
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to technology?

  • The first light bulb was considered cutting edge technology back then.
  • The company launched its new system. It went live last week.
  • Her 70-year old grandma is a silver surfer. She even has her own blog.
  • You don’t need to be a rocket scientist to use this phone. It’s user-friendly.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about technology. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What technological devices do you use regularly?
  • What’s the latest technological advancement in your country?
  • What high-tech devices can be found in your office?
  • Do you think there’s an overreliance on technology these days?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What technological advancements would you like to have at home?
  • How does technology help you at work?
  • How has technology affected your life?
  • What are some of the things you do without the aid of technology?
