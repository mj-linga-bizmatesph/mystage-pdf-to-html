TOPIC 12: NATURE
12
BIZ TIP
Use open-ended questions when you want to get more detailed information.
What was your experience in the rain forest like?
What projects does the government have to preserve nature?
What impact has technology had on our natural environment?
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to nature?

  • I feel so free whenever I’m out in the wilderness.
  • Could you tell me where the men’s room is? Sorry, call of nature.
  • Our vision is to reach the pinnacle of quality in customer service.
  • It’s safer to continue on trekking. Let’s leave our mountain bikes here.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about nature. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Have you ever been camping?
  • How popular is camping in your country?
  • Do you think it’s useful to know survival skills in this day and age?
  • Does your country have huge national parks like Yellowstone National Park in the US?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What’s the most beautiful natural environment you’ve been to?
  • Who are you usually with when you go out to enjoy nature?
  • What survival tools do you bring?
  • Have you ever lost your way in the mountains/forest?
▼▼▼
ACT

-- Bear Grylls (survivalist)
