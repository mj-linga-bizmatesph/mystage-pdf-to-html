TOPIC 13: DESIGN
13
BIZ TIP
Avoid “one-liners” when responding to questions. Add some details too.
Q: What do you think of this design?
A: It’s good.
A: It’s good. It has a very sophisticated look and the color gives it a...
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to design?

  • Try not to put flashy animations in your presentation.
  • It’s strange how people focus on the aesthetics instead of performance.
  • The boss asked me to change the design, so I’m back to the drawing board.
  • Somehow I think they won more by accident than design.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about design. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What are you particular of when it comes to design?
  • What’s your idea of a good home design?
  • What kind of designs do you use in your presentations?
  • Is there a particular brand that you like because of the design?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What’s the worst color combination you’ve seen? Where did you see it?
  • Are you very particular with design?
  • What does a typical office design look like in your country?
  • What branch of design do you consider yourself good at?
