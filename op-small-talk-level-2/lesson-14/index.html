BIZ TIP
Active listening means you are making a conscious effort to hear not just the words in the message but to understand the meaning of the whole message.
Pay attention: Yes, OK, I see, uh huh, etc...
Provide feedback: So what you’re saying is..., I think that’s a good idea, etc...
Respond appropriately: Wow, that’s great! , I’m sorry to hear that, etc...
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to health?

  • I often lose the zest for work whenever I miss my yoga class.
  • I’m trying to abstain from fatty foods because of hypertension.
  • You feel like a million dollars, don’t you? You’ve just closed a big contract.
  • She was feeling under the weather this morning, but she’s OK now.

TRY

Let’s talk about health. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • How do you take care of your health?
  • What are the things you avoid to keep healthy?
  • How often do you have a medical check-up?
  • How do most people keep themselves healthy in your country?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you only visit a physician when you’re really sick?
  • Is it expensive to enroll in fitness/sports gyms in your country?
  • Are yoga classes popular in your country?
  • What activities do you engage yourself in to keep healthy?
