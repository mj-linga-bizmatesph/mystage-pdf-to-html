TRY

Let’s talk about study. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What are you currently studying for your work?
  • If you could go back in time, what would you study?
  • Do you think what you learned in school has helped you in your work?
  • Do you believe education is more important than experience?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What did you study in school?
  • Have you ever quit studying something because it was too difficult?
  • What subjects do you like studying the most?
  • How do you study English beyond lesson hours?
