TOPIC 18: TRADITIONS
18
BIZ TIP
When studying a second language, place importance on learning words and expressions that are more commonly used.
Compare the importance of these words and expressions:
a: These domiciles could withstand strong seismic activities.
b: These houses could survive strong earthquakes.
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to traditions?

  • Respect for elders is a part of our cultural heritage that I am proud of.
  • This tattoo is my way of carrying on our family’s tradition.
  • Sorry but I’m not really into following religious dogma.
  • Our company lives by a creed that customer satisfaction comes first.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about traditions. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Does your country have a lot of traditions?
  • What tradition do you find most strange or ridiculous?
  • Do you know the history of some of your country’s traditions?
  • What traditions are still highly valued in your country?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What are some of your traditions that have been forgotten?
  • What foreign tradition would you like to adapt?
  • Do you think traditions should be preserved or adapted to modern times?
  • What tradition do you think is unique to your country?
▼▼▼
ACT
