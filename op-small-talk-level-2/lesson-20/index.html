TOPIC 20: EMOTIONS
20
BIZ TIP
When you want to check your understanding, you can say:
a: The necklace I bought my girlfriend was very expensive.
b: The necklace I bought my girlfriend cost me an arm and a leg.
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to emotions?

  • I’m not crazy about having that painting. Maybe we should sell it.
  • His arrogance can really tick anyone off.
  • She’s heartbroken from the death of her pet.
  • The team is ecstatic about winning the competition.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about emotions. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What simple things can make you really happy?
  • Do you think it’s healthy to express our emotions?
  • What kind of attitude ticks you off easily?
  • How do you usually deal with a moody person?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Who are you comfortable showing your emotions to?
  • What do you love and hate about your job?
  • What situations often make you very nervous?
  • When was the last time you felt very upset?

ACT

What are your thoughts about this quote?
