BIZ TIP
In a situation where you're not sure how to answer, you can always throw the question back at them with these phrases.
Good question. How about you?
Hmmm, well what do you think?
Interesting question. What's your opinion on this?
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to global leaders?

  • The summit will have banking magnates as guest speakers.
  • I think his idea of cutting down costs in 2 years sounds ingenious!
  • Her prudence tells her to save up and invest wisely for her future.
  • Junpei Shinoda, the leading light of MMB Corp., had humble beginnings.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about global leaders. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What does it mean to be a global leader?
  • How can a person or organization achieve such a status?
  • In your opinion, what qualities make up a global leader?
  • Which of these qualities do you have or don’t have?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Who are the global leaders in your line of business?
  • Who do you admire most? Why?
  • What can your company do to become a global leader in the industry?
  • Would you rather innovate something old, or invent something new?
