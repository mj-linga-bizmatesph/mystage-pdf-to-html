SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to start-ups?

  • We’re still new in the industry but we’re getting off the ground steadily.
  • The huge project is still in its infancy stage, so expect a lot of issues.
  • Small companies need to be bankrolled by investors to acquire more capital.
  • Be patient, the company will grow. Rome wasn’t built in a day you know.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about start-ups. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Are there many start-ups in your country?
  • Which industry do they usually belong to?
  • On average, how many employees do these start-ups have?
  • Is it easy to establish a start-up company in your country?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What are the risks involved in running these kinds of businesses?
  • Have you thought of starting your own business?
  • Do you have any friends who started their own business?
  • Do you think start-ups will increase in the future?
▼▼▼
ACT

What are your thoughts about this quote?

"For me, the winning strategy in any start-up business is, 'Think big but start small."

-- Carmen Busquets (entrepreneur)
