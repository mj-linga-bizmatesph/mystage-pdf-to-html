BIZ TIP
Rather than cutting someone off to interrupt them, you can give a short response before stating your opinion.
That's really interesting, but have you thought about…
That’s a good point, but I think…
I see what you mean, but in my opinion…
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to branding?

  • Some companies use language tests as a benchmark when hiring people.
  • We need to be creative in order to achieve that level of brand association.
  • Our goal is to set the bar in product excellence.
  • Their brand has been a household name for decades around here.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about branding. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Could you name 5 of the most popular Japanese brands known globally?
  • Could you name top 5 foreign brands in your country?
  • Do you think a good brand image is always synonymous with high quality?
  • Do you think branding is more important in some fields than others?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you have a favorite brand?
  • How do you perceive companies with weak branding?
  • What brands do you associate these products with? Cameras, shoes, phones?
  • How does a company market a brand?
