TRY

Let’s talk about dress code. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What does typical Cool Biz attire look like? Warm Biz?
  • Is there a problem with younger employees dressing too casually at work?
  • What do you usually wear at work?
  • What happens when someone violates the dress code in your company?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What’s the typical dress code for dinner parties in your country?
  • What’s the typical dress code in factories/manufacturing plants?
  • Do you wish you could dress casually every day at work?
  • How important is it to dress appropriately for business?
