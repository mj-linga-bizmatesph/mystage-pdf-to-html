BIZ TIP
To explain a complex idea, an effective technique to use is to compare it with something the other person is familiar with.
It's a lot like…
If you can compare it to…
It's a bit like…
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to medicine?

  • The boss scolded me because I was late. It’s a bitter pill I had to swallow.
  • Do you know of any uppers I can take? I’ve been awake for almost 24 hours.
  • You can’t buy those over-the-counter. You’ll need a prescription for those.
  • Be kind to others or you might get a taste of your own medicine someday.

TRY

Let’s talk about medicine. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you think people rely too much on drugs?
  • What type of medicine can be bought over-the-counter in your country?
  • In general, do most people take a lot of medication in your country?
  • Do you take pain relievers for simple ailments like headaches?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you prefer taking herbal medicine to cure ailments?
  • Do you think generic medicine is as effective as branded ones?
  • What is the largest pharmaceutical company in your country?
  • Do you avoid taking medication unless absolutely necessary?
