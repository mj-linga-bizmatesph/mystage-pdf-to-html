BIZ TIP
One model to help you speak logically about a problem is to follow the Problem - Action - Results technique.
Problem: The overuse of water has become a serious problem in the world.
Action: We should simply raise the price of water.
Results: This would make people realize the value of water.
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to environment?

  • The new engine is designed to eliminate exhaust fumes from petrol.
  • I wasn’t sure why his tone changed from pleasant to a menacing one.
  • A quiet office like yours is very conducive to working.
  • The first step of rehabilitation is to remove the sludge from the riverbanks.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about environment. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What are some strict environmental laws in Japan?
  • How do you take care of the environment in your own way?
  • Have you participated in any environmental activities?
  • Are there any environmental rehabilitation projects going on in Japan?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What type of waste is recycled in your country?
  • What’s the environment like in major cities and the countryside?
  • How much impact does urbanization have in the environment?
  • How would you describe your work environment?
