TOPIC 8: YOUNG GENERATION

BIZ TIP
Sequencing allows you to state your opinion in an organized way.
There're 3 main reasons… First,… Second,… Third…
There are 3 simple steps. First,… Then,… Finally…
It’s a 3-step process. We start off by… Once that’s complete,… And at the end,…
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to young generation?

  • It’s been a while since I’ve seen my contemporaries back in high school.
  • Try as I may, but there’s a huge generation gap whenever I talk to my son.
  • Children are becoming more rebellious and less obedient nowadays.
  • My wayward daughter has finally learned the error of her ways.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about young generation. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • How would you describe the young generation?
  • What are some major influences among the young generation?
  • What are some of the positive traits among the young generation?
  • What are some of the negative traits among the young generation?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you feel a huge generation gap between you and the younger generation?
  • In general, how do parents discipline their children in your country?
  • Do you think school teachers have become less authoritative than before?
  • Do you feel you’ve changed a lot since you were 18 years old?
▼▼▼
ACT
