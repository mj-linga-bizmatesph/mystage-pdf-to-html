▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about the Olympics. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What preparations is Japan making for the upcoming Olympics?
  • Will Japan build a special stadium where the Olympic Games can be held?
  • Who are some of the top Olympic athletes in your country?
  • Do you have any idea how much a ticket would cost?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you know all the events for the 2020 Summer Olympics?
  • What’s your favorite event?
  • If you were to join the Olympics, which sport would you compete in?
  • What are the pros and cons of hosting the Summer Olympics?
