TRY

Let’s talk about astronomy. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • How much do you like astronomy?
  • How does it make you feel when you see all the stars at night?
  • Do you believe in the idea of wishing upon a falling star?
  • Do you know any folklore about stars?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you get excited when you hear news about having a solar eclipse?
  • How has astronomy shaped the culture in your country?
  • How interested are people in your country in astronomy?
  • What interests you in this particular branch of science?
