TRY

Let’s talk about certifications. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What are some common certifications in your country?
  • What kind of jobs require certifications?
  • How important are certifications in your job?
  • What are some certifications in English that you can get?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do most companies require English language certificates?
  • What are some important professional certifications?
  • How difficult is it to get such certifications?
  • Do you know anyone who has lots of certifications?
