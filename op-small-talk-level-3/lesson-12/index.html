SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to smoking?

  • He’s been short-tempered since he quit smoking. Withdrawal pangs maybe?
  • You’re having another cigarette? You are such a chain smoker.
  • The executives will discuss the issue in a smoke-filled room tomorrow.
  • My late uncle used to smoke like a chimney during coffee breaks.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about smoking. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Are there many smokers in your company?
  • Does your company have a designated area for smoking?
  • Are cigarette packs expensive in your country?
  • How does the government deal with smoking in public?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What’s a good way to quit smoking?
  • Do you find it difficult to understand why some people smoke?
  • Do you agree that second-hand smoke is dangerous?
  • Do you think people who can’t quit smoking are just weak-willed?
