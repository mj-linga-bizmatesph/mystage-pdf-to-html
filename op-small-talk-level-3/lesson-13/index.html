TOPIC 13: BODY LANGUAGE
13
BIZ TIP
If your question is a bit sensitive, it's a good idea to use one of these phrases to give the other person some warning.
If you don't mind my asking,…
Can I ask you a personal question?
I apologize if this makes you feel uncomfortable, but…
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to body language?

  • When the boss has her arms akimbo, that means there’s something wrong.
  • Well done! You deserve a pat on the back.
  • You work too much. You need to let your hair down sometimes.
  • The client gave me a furrowed brow when I explained the features.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about body language. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you think foreigners use more body language than people in your culture?
  • Are you always consciously aware of your body language?
  • What are some gestures that shouldn’t be used in your culture?
  • What does it mean when someone is “nail biting?”

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What types of body language are common in your culture?
  • Do you think too much body language is unnatural?
  • What are some typical hand gestures used in your culture?
  • Do you agree that a handshake can tell you a lot about that person?
▼▼▼
ACT

What are your thoughts about this quote?

"Body language is a very powerful tool… and apparently, 80% of what you understand in a conversation is read through the body, not the words."

-- Deborah Bull (writer)
