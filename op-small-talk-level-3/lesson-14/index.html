  • My old toy train billowed steam just like a real locomotive.

  • Divorce is often a third rail that only a few talk about.

TRY

Let’s talk about trains. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Why are trains so popular in your country?
  • How has this mode of transportation shaped your country?
  • How fast can the bullet train go?
  • How often do train accidents happen?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you prefer traveling by train or plane?
  • What was the most number of hours you’ve spent on a train?
  • What are the pros and cons of riding trains?
  • What’s the train system in your country like?
