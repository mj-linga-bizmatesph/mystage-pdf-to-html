BIZ TIP
In some cases, you may want to get out of a dying conversation. Some of these phrases can help you.
Excuse me. I need to go talk to my colleague before she leaves.
My throat is so dry. Excuse me while I go to get something to drink.
Pardon me. Do you know where the restrooms are?
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to crime?

  • Bill was caught red-handed forging some documents.
  • His accomplice stood nearby as he tried to break in through the window.
  • The boss said Kim will make the presentation. Finally, I’m off the hook!
  • Having a short-tempered boss has been very traumatic for her.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about crime. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What do you think about your criminal justice system?
  • What was the last controversial crime in the news?
  • Is it easy to get a lawyer in your country?
  • Are court cases increasing in your country?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Have you ever been to a crime scene?
  • What kind of crime do you often read in the paper every day?
  • What is considered a crime in your company?
  • What do you consider an inhumane punishment for criminals?
