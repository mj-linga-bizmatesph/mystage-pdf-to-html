BIZ TIP
When making a suggestion, it's important not to sound condescending. Here are some useful phrases.
Have you considered…?
What if…?
Just a thought. Wouldn't it be better to…
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to charity?

  • Being a philanthropist takes a lot of money and a lot more generosity.
  • Can’t you stay and help your sister first? Charity begins at home, you know.
  • We’d like to thank all our benefactors who helped us fund the project.
  • She open-handedly shared some advice to help me improve.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about charity. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Is giving to charity common practice in your country?
  • What type of fundraising activities are there?
  • What charitable institutions are there?
  • Is your company involved in any charity work?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What types of charities are common in your country?
  • Is there a time of year that people give?
  • Who’s a famous philanthropist?
  • If you were rich, what organization would you give to?
