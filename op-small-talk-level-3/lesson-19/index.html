BIZ TIP
In a situation where you don't agree with an opinion but want to be polite, you can use these phrases.
That's a really interesting point of view.
I find it fascinating that you feel that way.
Well, I've never thought of it like that before.
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to cloud technology?

  • This gateway filters out unauthorized access to our system.
  • Backing up your files may come in handy someday.
  • I may be in an out-of-the-way town, but I could still access my e-mail.
  • My PC just crashed. I should’ve prepared for contingencies like these.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about cloud technology. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What web-based programs do you often use?
  • Do most companies have cloud-based systems now?
  • In what situations do you find cloud technology very convenient?
  • Can you access your company system remotely?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you use cloud technology to create back-ups of your files?
  • How has cloud technology helped you?
  • What are the pros and cons of cloud technology?
  • Do you fear that your personal information may be illegally accessed?
