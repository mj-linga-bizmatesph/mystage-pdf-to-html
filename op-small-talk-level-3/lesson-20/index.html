TOPIC 20: COMEDY

BIZ TIP
When two people have completely differing opinions, it's sometimes best to end the topic of discussion.
Well, I think we can both agree to disagree.
It's always enlightening to hear the other side of the story.
Thank you for our fruitful discussion.
SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to comedy?

  • She doesn’t have a good sense of humor so she hates being pranked.
  • That joke was so corny, but I couldn’t stop laughing.
  • It's hard to be serious around her. She always cracks me up.
  • His simple remarks are like punch lines. He’s good at making people laugh.

TRY

Let’s talk about comedy. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What’s the sense of humor like in your culture?
  • Do you think humor in your country is different from the West?
  • What are some types of comedy that’s popular in your culture?
  • Can you tell me a funny joke?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Who’s your most favorite comedian?
  • Do you enjoy making people laugh?
  • What do you think is a cruel joke?
  • Do you think humor is necessary to maintain a healthy work environment?
