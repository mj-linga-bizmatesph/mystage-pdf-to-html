ENVIRONMENTAL CONCERNS
WORDS & PHRASES
  • proliferate – to expand and grow rapidly in number
  • short-sighted – lacking foresight or the ability to see negative consequences
  • crass – lacking sensitivity or propriety
  • apathy – a lack of interest, concern, or care
SEE

Read the following and take note of the highlighted phrases.

As the human race continues to proliferate, the Earth and its resources suffer the consequences. In a world of grey concrete, steel, and smog, there seems to be little room for green, unless it’s the green of commerce.

Grace:
People are short-sighted. This has always been our biggest weakness. It’s not so much an issue of global warming as it is an issue of people only living for today. One day we’ll realize our mistake and it’ll be too late. Problems like this can’t be solved. They can only be prevented. Wake up!
Yun:
I don’t mean to be crass, but it’s just not a priority. We have more immediate concerns. Issues like unemployment, health care, and security are frankly more important. I’ll be long gone before I have to worry about it anyway. Why worry about the trees when I struggle to put food on the table?

Global leaders, celebrities, and great minds have tried to shake humanity from its apathy towards environmental concerns (from deforestation and waste mismanagement to global warming) but have made little progress. Whatever the future holds, it will be the result of our action - or inaction.

TRY

Answer the following questions:

  1. Which side of the fence do you fall on?
  2. What do you think it would take for people to make more drastic changes to their lifestyles?
  3. What do you imagine a worst-case scenario to be?
  4. Do you agree with the notion that humans are generally short-sighted? Why or why not?
