TECHNOLOGY & SOCIETY
WORDS & PHRASES
  • breakneck speed – a very fast or dangerous pace
  • techie – someone very familiar with or good at technology
  • ironic – keenly opposite or contrary to expectations or intentions
  • crutch – something used for support, often excessively
SEE

Read the following and take note of the highlighted phrases.

Technology continues to progress at breakneck speeds, fundamentally changing the way we behave, think, and communicate as a species. With no limit in sight, one must wonder if the impact of technology has been entirely positive.

Yong:
I’m a huge techie. If it’s faster, smarter, and sleeker, I’m in. Our bodies are limited; there’s only so much we can do with it. I think we’ve evolved as much as we can biologically. The only real way for us to grow now is through the further integration of human life and technology.
Nathan:
Have you noticed that the more connected we are, the less connected we actually become? Ironic, right? I know how convenient and fast everything is, but it’s not about quantity, it’s about quality. When was the last time you actually called someone to say hi? Technology has somehow managed to isolate us from each other.

From the wheel to the smartphone, technology’s role in shaping the human race is irrefutable. As things continue to advance, it may be necessary to remember that even though technology as a tool is undeniably powerful - technology as a crutch may be inevitably disastrous.

TRY

Answer the following questions:

  1. How has technology changed your lifestyle from childhood to now?
  2. Can you think of any examples in which technology has resulted in a disastrous consequence?
  3. Do you see any dangers regarding the current trend of technological development?
  4. What do you see as the next big game changer in technology? How will it affect us as a species?
