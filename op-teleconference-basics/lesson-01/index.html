SCHEDULING BY E-MAIL
BIZ TIP

Scheduling your teleconference is the first step. Use the following expressions to help you:

  • I’m writing to schedule our next…
  • The details are as follows…
  • If anyone has anything to add, please…
SEE

Ken is e-mailing his team to set up their next teleconference…

Hi, everyone.

I hope everyone is doing well.

I’m writing to schedule our next teleconference to discuss the status of the current project.

The details are as follows:

  • Topic: Project update
  • Date and Time: Friday, July 8, 10:00am - 12:00pm JST
  • Agenda: See attached file


If anyone has anything to add, please let me know by Thursday morning. Have a great day.

Regards,
Ken
TRY

Write an e-mail to schedule a teleconference for each situation below.

SCHEDULING
  • Topic: New project planning
  • Date and Time: May 1, 2:00pm - 4:30pm
  • Topic: System update
  • Date and Time: November 9, 10:00am - 11:30am
ACT

Write an e-mail using the 5-sentence rule to the participants of your next meeting in your company.
