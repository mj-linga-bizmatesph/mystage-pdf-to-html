RESPONDING TO INVITATIONS
BIZ TIP

Responding to invitations is just as important as scheduling. Use the following expressions to help you:

  • I’d be delighted to…
  • Could I have 10 minutes to…?
  • I’m looking forward to speaking with everyone on…
SEE

Ken is responding to Edward’s teleconference invitation.

Hi, Edward.

Thank you for arranging the teleconference. I’d be delighted to join.

Could I have 10 minutes to talk about the issues we are having with manufacturing? I’m looking forward to speaking with everyone on July 8, at 10:00am JST.

Cheers,
Ken

TRY

Write a response to a meeting invitation and request the items below to be added to the agenda.

RESPONDING
  • Would like to ask for everyone's opinions on the new logo design for 15 minutes
  • Would like to talk about last project's performance for 20 minutes
ACT

Write an e-mail to respond to a meeting invitation you received from your overseas business partner. Include an item you’d like to add to the agenda.
