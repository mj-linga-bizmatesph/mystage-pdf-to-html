CREATING THE AGENDA
BIZ TIP

Prepare for your teleconference by creating an agenda. Use the following expressions to help you:

  • Is there anything you’d like to add?
  • How much time do you need?
  • Do you need anyone to prepare anything?
SEE

Ken is putting together the agenda for his upcoming teleconference. He is asking his colleague, Kang, for input.

Ken
Hi, Kang. I’m preparing the agenda for our upcoming teleconference with the global team. Is there anything you’d like to add?.
Kang
Actually, yes. I’d like a few minutes to talk about customer feedback.
Ken
Of course. How much time do you need?
Kang
Maybe 20 minutes?
Ken
Okay, I’ll add you to the agenda. Do you need anyone to prepare anything?
Kang
Nothing special.
Ken
If there’s anything else, let me know.
Kang
Will do. Thanks.
