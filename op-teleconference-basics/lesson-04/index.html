SENDING A CONFIRMATION E-MAIL
BIZ TIP

Ensure your e-mails are short, simple, and easy to understand by using the 5-sentence rule:

  • A pleasantry: Thank you for making the time to…
  • Reason for writing: Here are the details of…
  • Summary of main points or bullet points: Date, Time, Ext code, Topic, Agenda
  • Call To action: Please e-mail me by…
  • Closing: Have a great day…
SEE

After deciding the details of the teleconference, Ken sends an e-mail to the participants regarding the details.

Dear All,

Thank you for making the time to meet next week.

Here are the details of our next teleconference:

  • Date: Friday, July 8
  • Time: 10:00am - 12:30pm JST
  • Ext Code: 5374
  • Topic: Project update
  • Agenda: See attached file


If you have any changes, please e-mail me by Wednesday, July 6. Have a great day, everyone.

Cheers,
Ken
