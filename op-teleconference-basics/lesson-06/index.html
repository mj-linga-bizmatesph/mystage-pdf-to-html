6
HANDLING TECHNICAL DIFFICULTIES
BIZ TIP

Technical problems sometimes occur. Handle the situation effectively by using the following expressions:

  • Are you there, …?
  • There seems to be a problem with…
  • Could you try…?
SEE

A participant is having some difficulties with his connection as the teleconference begins...

Ken
Hello, everyone. Thank you for joining today’s teleconference.
All
[Hello]
Ken
Are you there, Pat?
Pat
…I…this….sound….…low…
Ken
I’m sorry, Pat. You’re cutting out. There seems to be a problem with the audio.
Pat
…hear…..back...
Ken
Could you try hanging up and calling us back, Pat?

---Pat calls back a few minutes later---

Pat
Sorry about that. Can you hear me now?
Ken
Welcome back, Pat. We hear you loud and clear.
TRY

Think of some solutions and expressions with your trainer for some of the common problems below and practice troubleshooting.

PROBLEM SOLUTIONS
Volume too low Speaking louder
Video not working Resetting your camera
Too much static
You hear an echo
ACT

Use the situations and expressions above and handle technical difficulties during your teleconference.
