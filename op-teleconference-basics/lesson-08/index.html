8
INTRODUCING OTHERS
BIZ TIP

When new participants join teleconferences, introduce them to the group by using the expressions below:

Formal:
  • Allow me to introduce…calling in from…
  • It’s an honor to have…
  • Please give a warm welcome to…
Casual:
  • This is…from…
  • …from…is joining us today.
  • Everyone, say hi to…from…
SEE
The teleconference is about to start and Ken is greeting the participants…

Casual
Ken
Good morning, everyone. Thankyou very much for being here today.
All
[Good morning]

We have a new member joining us. It’s an honor to have Rob Jetten, senior advisor, calling in from our Seattle branch today.

Hey guys. Good to be here. Thanks for the invite.
