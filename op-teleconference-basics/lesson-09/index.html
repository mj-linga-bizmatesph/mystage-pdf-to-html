GOING OVER THE AGENDA
BIZ TIP

Go over the agenda to make sure everyone understands the main topic and flow of today’s meeting. Use the following expressions to help you:

  • The purpose of today’s meeting is to…
  • First,…will talk about…for…minutes. Then,…will give us…for…
  • Does anyone have anything they’d like to add?
SEE

Ken is going through the agenda with the teleconference participants…

Ken
The purpose of today’s meeting is to discuss the status of our current project. Our meeting today will take about two and a half hours. Let’s go through today’s agenda.

First, I’ll talk about our current progress for 15 minutes. Then, Kang will give us an update on customer feedback for 10 minutes. Afterwards, Pat will talk about some problems we are facing for 15 minutes. Then, Len will give us a marketing update for 15 minutes. Finally, Jen will talk about our upcoming press release for 10 minutes.

Does anyone have anything they’d like to add? No? Alright. Let’s start with…
