PART 1

Review the Biz tips with your trainer and make sure you are comfortable with the expressions and concepts.

Technical problems sometimes occur. Handle the situation effectively by using the following expressions:

  • Are you there, … ?
  • There seems to be a problem with…
  • Could you try…?

Greet your participants and create a professional yet friendly atmosphere by using the following expressions:

  • Hello, everyone. Thank you for joining today’s teleconference.
  • Can everyone please check in to make sure we’re all here?
  • Does everyone have a copy of the agenda?

When new participants join teleconferences, introduce them to the group by using the expressions below:

Formal:
  • Allow me to introduce…calling in from…
  • It’s an honor to have…
  • Please give a warm welcome to…
Casual:
  • This is…from…
  • …from…is joining us today.
  • Everyone, say hi to…from…

Go over the agenda to make sure everyone understands the main topic and flow of today’s meeting. Use the following expressions to help you:

  • The purpose of today’s meeting is to…
  • First,…will talk about…for…minutes. Then,…will give us…for…
  • Does anyone have anything they’d like to add?
PART 2

Use everything you’ve learned to effectively start a teleconference.

As your teleconference starts, some technical difficulties occur. Once they have been fixed, greet your participants, introduce any new members, and go through today’s agenda.
