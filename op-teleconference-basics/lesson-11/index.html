11
BALANCING PARTICIPATION
BIZ TIP

It can be difficult for everyone to participate equally in teleconferences. Make sure there is a good balance by using the following expressions:

  • Everyone’s opinions are valuable so please don’t hesitate to speak up.
  • We haven’t heard from…What’s your take on this issue?
  • If anyone has anything they’d like to add, please speak up.
SEE

Ken and the participants are discussing the project’s progress; however, some members aren’t speaking up…

Ken
We’re behind schedule. How can we catch up?
All
[Silence]
Ken
Everyone’s opinions are valuable so please don’t hesitate to speak up.
Pat
Honestly, I don’t think we can meet the production deadline. The shipping delay has really set us back.
Ken
I see. We haven’t heard from Jen. What’s your take on this issue? You have a lot of experience handling these kinds of problems.
Jen
Well, we can consider looking for another supplier, but there’s no time.
Ken
I see. If anyone has anything they’d like to add, please speak up.
Kang

TRY

Come up with solutions and expressions to the following participation problems with your trainer.

PROBLEM SOLUTION EXPRESSION
One member speaks too much. Thank the member and ask other participants their thoughts by name. Thank you, Paul. What do you think, Alan?
A member is very shy. Mention their experience before asking their opinion.
Too many people try to speak at once.
ACT

You must decide whether to host your next face-to-face meeting in Japan or in the Philippines. Give your opinion and get your trainer’s participation.
