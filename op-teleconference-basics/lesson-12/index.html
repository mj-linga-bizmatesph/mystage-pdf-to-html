12
EXPRESSING OPINIONS
BIZ TIP

Expressing your opinion in a way that is simple, easy to understand, and logical is an important skill. Use the following flow to help you:

  • In my opinion,…
  • I feel this way because…
  • So I suggest we…
SEE

Ken and the participants are expressing their opinions on how to solve their project’s supply problems…

Ken
In my opinion, our supplier is unreliable.
Len
Really? We’ve used the same supplier for over 2 years.
Ken
I feel this way because the delivery has been late twice this month and there was a mistake with our invoice again last month.
Pat
So what do you suggest we do?
Ken
So I suggest that we make a shortlist of potential partners and switch over as soon as we find a suitable partner.
TRY

Provide support and make a suggestion for the opinions below.

OPINION SUPPORT SUGGESTION
Our factory is unsafe. Accidents at the factory have increased 15% the past two years. Have safety training twice a year.
ARX brand is losing market share. Sales have fallen 3 years in a row.
Tokyo is getting too expensive for our head office.
ACT

Express your opinion on how to improve your company. Provide support for your opinion as well as a suggestion.
