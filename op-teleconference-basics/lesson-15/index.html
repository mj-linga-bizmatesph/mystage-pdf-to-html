PART 1

Review the Biz tips with your trainer and make sure you are comfortable with the expressions and concepts.

It can be difficult for everyone to participate equally in teleconferences. Make sure there is a good balance by using the following expressions:

  • Everyone’s opinions are valuable so please don’t hesitate to share your thoughts.
  • We haven’t heard from…What’s your take on this issue?
  • If anyone has anything they’d like to add, please speak up.

Expressing your opinion in a way that is simple, easy to understand, and logical is an important skill. Use the following flow to help you:

  • In my opinion,…
  • I feel this way because…
  • So I suggest we…

Miscommunication can sometimes occur during teleconferences. Make sure no important information is missed by using the expressions below:

  • Sorry, I don’t follow. Allow me to clarify. You mean…
  • Sorry, I’m a bit lost. Let me confirm…
  • So you mean to say that…

Making sure everyone is looking at the same file or correct documents over teleconference can be challenging. Use the following expressions to make your directions clear and easy to understand:

  • Did everyone receive the… (that) I sent?
  • Could I have everyone open…?
  • Can everyone please…?
