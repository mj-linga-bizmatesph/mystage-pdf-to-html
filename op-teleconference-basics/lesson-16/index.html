DECIDING NEXT STEPS
BIZ TIP

Deciding the next steps is important in moving the process forward. Use the expressions below to make sure your team knows what to do next:

  • Since we agreed to…
  • Our first/next step should be to…
  • This will allow us to…
SEE

After deciding to go with a new supplier, Ken goes through the next steps with the team…

Ken
Thank you for everyone’s ideas and input. Since we agreed to go with a new supplier, we need to start looking as soon as possible.
Len
Does anyone have any ideas about who we should approach?
Ken
Well, our first step should be to compile a short list of potential suppliers and contact them for more information.
Jen
I can get started on that right away and present the top 3 options sometime next week.
Ken
Thank you, Jen. This will allow us to make a decision more easily. Once we decide on our new supplier, the next step will be to inform…
