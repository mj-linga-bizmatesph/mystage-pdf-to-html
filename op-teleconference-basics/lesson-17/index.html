SUMMARIZING KEY POINTS
BIZ TIP

Before wrapping up the teleconference, summarize the key points to make sure there is no misunderstanding. Use the expressions and flow below:

  • Today, we discussed…
  • We decided to…
  • …will…by…
SEE

As the teleconference agenda comes to a close, Ken summarizes today’s key points and decisions.

Ken
Okay, time is almost up. Let’s go through today’s main points. Today, we discussed the current status of our project. Although things are generally on the right track, we ran into some problems with our supplier, again. After some discussion, we decided to search for a new supplier. Jen will prepare a shortlist and send everyone the information by the end of next week. Does anyone have any further questions or concerns about this?
All
[nothing]

Good. Today, we also discussed our customer feedback results…
