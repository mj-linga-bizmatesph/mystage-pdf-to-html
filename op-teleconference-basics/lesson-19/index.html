19
WRAPPING UP
BIZ TIP

Finish your teleconference in a way that will motivate your team and leave a good impression by using the following expressions and flow below:

  • Thank you all for...
  • We’ve got a busy time ahead of us, but I’m sure everyone will do a great job.
  • Keep up the hard work. And I’m looking forward to…
SEE

The teleconference has finished and Ken is thanking the participants…

Ken
That’s all the time we have. Thank you all for participating today. I know you are all very busy.
All
[Thank you]
Ken
We’ve got a busy time ahead of us, but I’m sure everyone will do a great job. If anyone has any questions or if you need to contact me, please feel free to e-mail me. Keep up the hard work. And I’m looking forward to talking with all of you next time.
TRY

Think of some expressions you can use to close your teleconference.

THANK YOU MOTIVATIONAL EXPRESSION GOODBYE
Thanks for joining today’s teleconference, everyone. We made excellent progress today. See you all next time.
Thanks for your time, everyone.
ACT

Close your teleconference and end on a positive note.
