REVIEW
PART 1

Review the Biz tips with your trainer and make sure you are comfortable with the expressions and concepts.

Deciding the next steps is important in moving the process forward. Use the expressions below to make sure your team knows what to do next:

  • Since we agreed to…
  • Our first/next step should be to…
  • This will allow us to…

Before wrapping up the teleconference, summarize the key points to make sure there is no misunderstanding. Use the expressions and flow below:

  • Today, we discussed…
  • We decided to…
  • …will…by…

Before your teleconference closes, set up your next meeting to make sure everyone is on schedule and deadlines are being met:

  • Before we wrap up, I’d like to set up our next teleconference.
  • When is everyone available?
  • Let’s go with…

Finish your teleconference in a way that will motivate your team and leave a good impression by using the following expressions and flow below:

  • Thank you all for…
  • We’ve got a busy time ahead of us, but I’m sure everyone will do a great job.
  • Keep up the hard work. And I’m looking forward to…
PART 2

Use everything you’ve learned to effectively close a teleconference.

Your teleconference is coming to a close. Decide the next steps you’d like your team to take and summarize the key points of today’s teleconference. Before saying goodbye, remember to set up your next teleconference and finish with something positive and motivational.
