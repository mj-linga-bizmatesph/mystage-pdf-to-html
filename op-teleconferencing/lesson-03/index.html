STARTING THE TELECONFERENCING
BIZ TIP

At the beginning of a teleconference that we lead, you should:

  • introduce yourself and participants with their name, position, and location
  • conduct a roll call
  • make small talk with the participants to create a friendly atmosphere
SEE

Example:

Taro:
Welcome Project Team! I’m Taro from MMB Tokyo. I’m glad we can have this teleconference. OK, I think everyone’s already here. Shall we get down to business?

Bizmates Style:

Taro:
Welcome Project Team! I’m Taro, Head of Sales in the MMB Tokyo office. Before we start, could each of you give a brief introduction with your name, position, and location? Let’s start with Ken.
Ken:
Hi everyone! This is Ken, Head of Human Resources at the Yokohama office. Along with me is our Finance Manager, Yuki.
Taro:
Welcome, Ken and Yuki! What about our participants from Canada?
John:
Hi, this is John, Marketing Manager of MMB Canada, and with me is my marketing team. Nice to meet all of you.
Taro:
Pleased to meet you and your team, John. How about Sydney?
James:
Hello everyone! I’m James, Training and Operations Manager of MMB Australia. Sitting with me is our Training Supervisor, Chris.
Taro:
Great! Thank you James and Chris. And finally, are the participants from the New Delhi office here with us?
Sanjay:
Yes. Hi, my name is Sanjay, Head of IT in MMB India. Glad to be here.
Taro:
Thanks Sanjay. By the way, MMB has recently set up the IT office...
