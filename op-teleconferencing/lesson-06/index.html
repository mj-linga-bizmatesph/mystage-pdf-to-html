INVOLVING THE PARTICIPANTS
BIZ TIP

Being the chairperson does not mean you have to dominate the discussion. To encourage active participation:

  • direct questions at participants by stating their name and the department or location
  • mention their area of expertise before asking for an opinion
  • focus on the quiet participants to give everyone an equal chance to speak
SEE

Bizmates Style:

Taro:
Now, let’s brainstorm action plans for each department. First, let’s focus on the product. Sanjay, as a software developer, what would you suggest to make the new software marketable on a global scale?
Sanjay:
In my opinion, we need to develop this software with two basic features: one, it has to be user-friendly; and two, it has to be available in different languages.
Taro:
Yes, I think that makes sense. So John, in your years of marketing experience, what could be the best way to increase our brand and product awareness?
John:
That’s a good question, Taro. If we want the best results, a TV ad would definitely do the trick. However, a more cost-effective way is to market and advertise through the internet.
Taro:
Good point, John. So, Yuki, as the Finance Manager, how much do you think can be allocated to fund our global project?
Yuki:
I am in the process of making a proposal for…
