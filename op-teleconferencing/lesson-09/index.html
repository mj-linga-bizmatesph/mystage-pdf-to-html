9
ENDING THE TELECONFERENCE
BIZ TIP

Towards the end of the teleconference, the chairperson should:

  • summarize key points and reiterate action plans
  • mention the schedule of the next teleconference
  • impart a few words to motivate the participants to work as a team
SEE

Example:

Taro:
OK everyone, I think our meeting has been very productive. Let’s all do our share to make this project a huge success. Talk to you again soon!
Sanjay:
Goodbye!
Ken:
Sure.
John:
OK, bye now.
James:
See you!

Bizmates Style:

Taro:
OK everyone, let me summarize what we talked about. We’ve covered the details on our new software: marketing approach, budget, setting up a 24/7 CS center, and recruitment. Are we all clear with these?

(Everyone agrees)

Taro:
Sanjay, you and your team will look into making improvements to the interface. John and his team will research the best avenues to market our product. Yuki will keep us posted on the approval of her budget proposal. James will come up with training materials and set up the 24/7 CS hotline. Finally, Ken will take care of posting job ads and getting the right people. Does anyone have anything to clarify?

(Everyone is clear with the action plans)

Taro:
Our next conference will be on May 28th, after a month to check our progress. This is our most ambitious project yet, so let’s all work together to end this project on a high note. We can do this! Have a great week everyone!
TRY

Use the following topics below and close the meeting.

1. Summary: Speaking naturally and improving fluency in English
2. Action plan:
  • practice using new words/phrases learned
  • exposure to more English materials
  • practice connecting words
ACT

Role play.

End a teleconference by using a topic you previously discussed at work.
