12
DELIVERING YOUR PRESENTATION
BIZ TIP

When delivering a presentation during a teleconference, remember to:

  • customize your approach
  • involve the other participants
  • use visuals like charts/graphs
SEE

Example:

Jason:
All right, Hiro. Let’s begin.
Hiro:
Ah, yes. I’m here to present our company’s latest software. I have three points. First, our basic features. Next, our unique features. And finally, our price.
Our basic features include…
Next, our unique features include…
And here is our price…
Do you have any questions?

Bizmates Style:

Jason:
All right, Hiro. Let’s begin.
Hiro:
First of all, I’d like to thank you for giving me this opportunity to present our latest software. Before I start, did everyone go through the handouts?
Jason:
Yes, Luke and I both went through them last night.
Hiro:
OK, so I’ll skip the basic features and just focus on what makes our product special. Is that OK?
Jason:
Yes, that would be great.
Hiro:
OK, what are the common complaints from users that you get about the software that you are using now, Jason?
Jason:
It’s too difficult to use.
Hiro:
Exactly. And this is true in every industry. As you can see from this graph, the number one complaint…
TRY

Answer the following questions based on the second dialogue.

1. How did Hiro start his presentation?
2. How did he customize his approach?
3. How did he involve his audience?
4. What do you think his graph shows?
