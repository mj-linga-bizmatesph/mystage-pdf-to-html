16
COMMUNICATING YOUR OPINIONS
BIZ TIP

In a teleconference, your opinions matter and communicating them effectively can be achieved by:

  • stating your opinion
  • explaining your reasons
  • backing it up with evidence
SEE

Example:

Jason:
I’ve read somewhere in the reviews that the application’s keyboard shortcuts couldn’t be changed.
Hiro:
Ah yes. I’m very sorry about that.
Jason:
So, it’s true?
Hiro:
Ah yes, but many customers are very satisfied with our products.

Bizmates Style:

Jason:
I’ve read somewhere in the reviews that the application’s keyboard shortcuts couldn’t be changed.
Hiro:
I understand that it is something that can still be improved, but in general it’s still user-friendly. As far as the reviews are concerned, if we look at them closely, we will see that a lot of them say positive things about the software.
Jason:
Hmmm...
Hiro:
In fact, 95% of these reviews specifically mention that it’s very user-friendly. In addition, 76% of them have little computer experience.
Jason:
That’s a good point.
TRY

Communicate your opinion on the following topics.

1. Having daily meetings at work
2. Building relationship with a client
3. Involving employees in decision making processes
ACT

Role play.

Communicate your opinion about the importance of learning English in business.
