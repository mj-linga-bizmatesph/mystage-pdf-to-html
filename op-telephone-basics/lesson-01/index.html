MAKING A GOOD IMPRESSION
1
BIZ TIP
Speaking with the right tone over the telephone is important. Make a good impression by using the expressions below:
Thank you for calling _____. This is... How may I help you?
Good morning/afternoon, Mr./Ms. _____.
Sure, I'd be happy to help you with that.
SEE
Miki
Odenwa arigatou gozaimasu. BioG Corp. no Suzuki de gozaimasu.
Kyle
Hi. Do you speak English?
Miki
Thank you for calling BioG Corp. This is Miki speaking. How may I help you?
Kyle
Yes, this is Kyle Waterson. I have some questions about your products online.
Miki
Certainly, I’d be happy to help you with that, Mr. Waterson.
Kyle
Thanks. I was wondering if…
Miki
Are you available…
