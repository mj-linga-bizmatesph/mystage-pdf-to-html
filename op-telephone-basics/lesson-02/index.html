ASKING FOR DETAILS
2
BIZ TIP
Sometimes we may need to ask for details. Get the necessary information by using the expressions below:
  • May I have your full name, please?
  • May I also have your contact number?
  • May I ask what this is in reference to?
SEE
Miki
Thank you for calling BioG Corp. This is Miki. How may I help you?
Glen
Hi, I’d like to speak with Ms. Yamada.
Miki
May I have your full name, please?
Glen
Sure. This is Glen Dawson with Click Unlimited.
Miki
Thank you, Mr. Dawson. May I also have your contact number?
Glen
Yes, it’s 03-3792-5555.
Miki
And may I ask what this is in reference to?
Glen
It’s about our appointment on…
