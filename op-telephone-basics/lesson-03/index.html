OTHER PROGRAMS TELEPHONE BASICSILESSON 3
CONFIRMING DETAILS
3
BIZ TIP
Make sure you have accurate information. Confirm details with your caller by using the expressions below:
  • So that's A as in Adam. M as in Mary...
  • Is that correct?
  • Just to confirm,...
SEE

The caller wants to sign up for a subscription.

Don
Hi. I was wondering if I could sign up for a subscription over the phone.
Miki
Sure. May I have your full name and e-mail address, please?
Don
Yes, it’s Don Smith.
Miki
So that’s D as in David, O as in Oscar, N as in Nancy. Is that correct?
Don
Yes, that’s right. My e-mail address is funnydon@gmail.com.
Miki
Thank you, Mr. Smith. Just to confirm, your e-mail address is…
