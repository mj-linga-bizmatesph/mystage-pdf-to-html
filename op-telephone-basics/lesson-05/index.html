PART 1

Discuss the key points you learned in the last four lessons and give a few sample expressions.

Having a good tone over the telephone is important. Make a good impression by using the expressions below:
  • Thank you for calling _____. This is... How may I help you?
  • Good morning/afternoon, Mr./Ms. _____.
  • Sure, I'd be happy to help you with that.
Sometimes we may need to ask for details. Get the necessary information by using the expressions below:
  • May I have your full name, please?
  • May I also have your contact number?
  • May I ask what this is in reference to?
Make sure you have accurate information. Confirm details with your caller by using the expressions below:
  • So that's A as in Adam. M as in Mary...
  • Is that correct?
  • Just to confirm,...
Maintain professionalism throughout the call. End the phone call by using the expressions below:
  • Is there anything else I can help you with?
  • You're welcome. Glad I could help.
  • Have a great day. Goodbye.
