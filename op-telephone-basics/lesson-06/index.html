OTHER PROGRAMS TELEPHONE BASICSILESSON 6
TRANSFERRING A CALL
6
BIZ TIP
Maintain professionalism over the phone even when transferring calls. Use the following expressions to help you:
May I ask what this is regarding?
Please hold while I...
Thank you for waiting. I'll transfer your call now.
SEE
John
Hi, this is John Roberts. I’d like to speak with Mr. Sato, please?
Miki
Hello, Mr. Roberts. May I ask what this is regarding?
John
I’m with PCP Co. and I’m calling about our contract.
Miki
I see. Please hold while I get Mr. Sato for you.
---a few moments later---
Miki
Thank you for waiting. I’ll transfer your call now. Have a nice day, Mr. Roberts.
