OTHER PROGRAMS TELEPHONE BASICSILESSON 7
ANSWERING INQUIRIES
7
BIZ TIP
Answering inquiries is an opportunity to introduce your company. Answer questions with these expressions below:
Thank you for your interest in our...
Are you looking for something specific?
So…, is that correct?
SEE
Miki
Thank you for calling BioG Corp. This is Miki. How may I help you?
Rea
Hi, this is Rea Mills. I just have some questions about your magazine.
Miki
Thank you for your interest in our products, Ms. Mills.
Rea
I just wanted to know if you have any publication about cooking.
Miki
Yes, we have a lot, actually. Are you looking for something specific?
Rea
Yes. Something about Asian dishes. You see, I’d like to…
---the caller explains what she needs---
Miki
I see. So you’d like to read and learn more about Japanese dishes. Is that correct?
Rea
Yes, that’s right.
▼▼▼
TRY

Practice answering inquiries in the following situations:

Caller Situation
Tom Fernandez The caller is inquiring about your company’s services.
Sean Swanson The caller wants to know more about your company.
Ringo Starkey The caller is inquiring if you have any special products.
▼▼▼
ACT

Role play.

  • The caller wants to know more about your company and its products. Handle the call and answer the caller’s questions effectively.
  • If time permits, do another role play using a similar work-related situation.
