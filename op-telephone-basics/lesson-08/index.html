OTHER PROGRAMS TELEPHONE BASICSILESSON 8
ASSISTING YOUR CALLER
8
BIZ TIP
Wow your callers by helping them with each step. Assist them professionally by using the following expressions:
  • May I know exactly what the issue is?
  • Here's what we need to do: First,... Second,... Next,...
  • Please feel free to call us back if...
SEE

The caller is having trouble subscribing to BioG Corp.’s newsletter.

Miki
May I know exactly what the issue is?
Ross
Sure. I’d like to subscribe to your monthly newsletter but I don’t know how.
Miki
I see. Here’s what we need to do: First, login to your account, Mr. Lansing.
Ross
OK, and then?
Miki
Great. Second, go to “Preferences” and then check “Newsletter.”
Ross
I see. That was easy. So that’s it?
Miki
Yes. Then you will be receiving a welcome e-mail in a few minutes. Please feel free to call us back if you have further questions, Mr. Lansing.
Ross
Thanks for your help.
▼▼▼
TRY

Practice assisting your caller in the following situations:

Caller Situation Steps
Tanya Gray Forgot the password to her account
  1. Check “I forgot my password”
  2. Answer security questions
  3. Enter new password
Zack Grant Wants to cancel his online reservation
  1. Go to calendar
  2. Search for the reservation
  3. Click “Cancel” button
Jerry Lee Would like to upgrade his subscription
  1. Go to account profile
  2. Click “Available Plans”
  3. Click “Upgrade subscription”
▼▼▼
ACT

Role play

  1. Imagine someone is asking for directions to your office. Effectively assist your caller by giving clear instructions.
  2. If time permits, do another role play using a similar work-related situation.
