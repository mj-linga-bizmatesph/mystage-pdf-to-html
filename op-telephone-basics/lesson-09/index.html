TAKING A MESSAGE
9
BIZ TIP
Taking a message is also important with business calls. Give your caller some information using the expressions below:
I'm afraid he's in a meeting right now.
He should be back by...
Can I take a message?
SEE
Tony
Hi, this is Tony Fender with ABC Corp. I’d like to speak with Mr. Kawasaki.
Miki
Sure, Mr. Fender. Let me transfer you to Mr. Kawasaki.
---a few moments later---
Miki
I’m afraid he’s in a meeting right now, but he should be back by 3PM. Can I take a message?
Tony
That’s OK. I guess I’ll call back later. Thank you.
Miki
You’re welcome, Mr. Fender. Have a nice day.

ACT

Role play

  2. If time permits, do another role play using a similar work-related situation.
