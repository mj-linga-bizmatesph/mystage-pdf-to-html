PART 1

Discuss the key points you learned in the last four lessons and give a few sample expressions.

Maintain professionalism over the phone even when transferring calls. Use the following expressions to help you:
  • May I ask what this is regarding?
  • Please hold while I...
  • Thank you for waiting. I'll transfer your call now.
Answering inquiries is an opportunity to introduce your company. Answer questions with these expressions below:
  • Thank you for your interest in our...
  • Are you looking for something specific?
  • So…, is that correct?
Wow your callers by helping them with each step. Assist them professionally by using the following expressions:
  • May I know exactly what the issue is?
  • Here's what we need to do: First,... Second,... Next,...
  • Please feel free to call us back if...
Taking a message is also important when handling business calls. Use the expressions below to give your caller some information:
  • I'm afraid he's in a meeting right now.
  • He should be back by...
  • Can I take a message?
