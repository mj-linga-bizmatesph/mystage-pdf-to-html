CALLING ON BEHALF OF SOMEONE
11
BIZ TIP
When you’re calling on behalf of someone, say why you’re calling and provide the details. Here are some phrases:
  • This is _______ and I’m calling on behalf of…
  • I'm calling in reference to...
  • He/she would like to inform you that…
SEE
Jake
Hi, this is Jake Hanson.
Miki
Hello, Mr. Hanson. This is Miki Suzuki and I’m calling on behalf of Mr. Hyun.
Jake
Hi, Miki. What can I help you with?
Miki
I’m calling in reference to your appointment with him tomorrow.
Jake
I see.
Miki
He would like to inform you that he won't be able to make it tomorrow afternoon because of an emergency.
Jake
Oh, I hope everything’s OK. Thanks for letting me know, Miki.
