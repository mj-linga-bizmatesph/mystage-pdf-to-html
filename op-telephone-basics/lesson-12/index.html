SETTING UP AN APPOINTMENT
12
BIZ TIP
Setting up an appointment with a busy person can be difficult. Follow these steps to help you:
I'd be happy to visit your office on...
I can work around your schedule. What about on…?
Thank you. So just to confirm,...
SEE

Miki is trying to set up an appointment with Mr. Jones.

Miki
I’d be happy to visit your office on Wednesday, the 18th, Mr. Jones.
Paul
Sorry, Miki. I won’t be available then.
Miki
That’s OK. I can work around your schedule. What about on Friday, the 20th?
Paul
Friday, let’s see… Yes, 3PM should be OK.
Miki
Great. Thank you. So just to confirm, I’ll drop by on the 20th at 3PM.
Paul
OK, Miki. See you then.
Miki
Thanks for your time, Mr. Jones. Have a nice day.
