LEAVING A MESSAGE
13
BIZ TIP
Provide enough details when leaving a message. Make your request clear by using these sample phrases:
  • Could you please ask Mr./Ms.______ to call me back?
  • I’d like to talk to him/her about…
  • He/she can reach me…
SEE

Miki is trying to speak to Mr. Yamazaki.

Mary
Sorry, but Mr. Yamazaki is in a meeting right now.
Miki
I see. Could you please ask him to call me back?
Mary
Sure, Miki.
Miki
I’d like to talk to him about the new project we’re working on.
Mary
I will let Mr. Yamazaki know.
Miki
Thank you, Mary. He can reach me between 1PM and 5PM.

TRY

Practice leaving a message in the following situations:

Contact person Calling about… Your availability
