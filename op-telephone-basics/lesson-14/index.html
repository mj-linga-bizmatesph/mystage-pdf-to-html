OTHER PROGRAMS TELEPHONE BASICSILESSON 14
MAKING FOLLOW-UP INQUIRIES
14
BIZ TIP
Get enough information when following up on an urgent matter. Get the details you need by using these sample expressions:
I'd like to follow up on...
Would you know when...?
Could you give me a timeframe?
SEE

Miki is making a follow-up inquiry.

Miki
I’d like to follow up on the office chairs we ordered last week.
Roy
Sure, Miki. Let’s see here… I’m sorry but the order is still being processed.
Miki
I see. Would you know when it’ll be shipped?
Roy
Sorry, I’m really not sure.
Miki
Well, could you give me a timeframe?
Roy
It should be shipped in a couple of days. We’re sorry for the delay.
