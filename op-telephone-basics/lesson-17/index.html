FILING A COMPLAINT
17
BIZ TIP
Keep in mind that you still have to sound professional even when filing a complaint. Here are some phrases you can use:
I'd like to report an issue about...
Is there anything you can do to help me out?
It would be great if…
SEE

Miki is filing a complaint against the supplier.

Miki
I’d like to report an issue about the supplies we ordered.
Jean
Sorry to hear that, Miki. May I know what the problem is?
---Miki tells Jean what happened---
Miki
We can’t afford to let this delay happen again. Is there anything you can do to help me out?
Jean
Of course. Let me contact Shipping to sort it out ASAP.
Miki
Thanks, Jean. It would be great if you could get back to me as soon as…
