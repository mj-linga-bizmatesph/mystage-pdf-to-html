ASKING FOR CLARIFICATION
18
BIZ TIP
It’s difficult to handle a call when you can’t understand the other person. Use these expressions to understand them:
Sorry, could you rephrase that, please?
I'm really sorry, but could you slow down for me, please?
Sorry, I can barely hear you. Could you speak a bit louder?
SEE
Chuck
Hi, this is Chuck Helmsley and I’m trying to get a hold of Mr. Kawasaki.
Miki
Hi, Mr. Helmsley. Sorry, could you rephrase that, please?
Chuck
Sure. I’d like to speak to Mr. Kawasaki right away. It’s very important and I really need to talk to him now.
Miki
I’m really sorry, but could you slow down for me, please?
Chuck
No problem. I’d like to speak to Mr. Kawasaki about an urgent matter.
Miki
Sorry, Mr. Helmsley. I can barely hear you. Could you speak a bit louder?
Chuck
How’s this?
Miki
Thank you, Mr. Helmsley. Yes, that’s much better.
