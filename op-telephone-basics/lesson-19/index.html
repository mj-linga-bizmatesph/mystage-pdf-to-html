GOING BACK TO THE TOPIC
19
BIZ TIP
When a caller gets off topic, you may need to redirect them to the main topic. Use the phrases below to help you:
Anyway, you mentioned that…
Going back to the issue,…
So you said that…
SEE

Miki is dealing with a talkative caller.

Vic
So I was on the train when the line got cut. I’m a regular commuter but this has never happened before. Do you also ride the train every day?
Miki
I do, Mr. Sung. Anyway, you mentioned that you suddenly got disconnected.
Vic
Oh yeah. But before that, I noticed the line was a bit choppy. I mean, we shouldn’t be experiencing this with our technology nowadays. Don’t you think?
Miki
That’s true. Now, going back to the issue, Mr. Sung…
Vic
Right. Sorry I’m just a bit upset. I’m a big fan of modern technology and it’s hard to imagine experiencing a simple issue like this. Wouldn’t you feel the same way?
Miki
I definitely would. So you said that the line became choppy and then…

TRY

Practice going back to the topic in the following situations:

Ben Chou Wants to confirm the meeting schedule Asks questions about your boss
Karen Hall Asks you to double check a client’s address Talks about her first day in the field
Dave Singh Calls in to follow up on his online purchase Wants to have a friendly chitchat

ACT

Role play.

  • Imagine you got a call from your supervisor’s childhood friend. Your caller just wants to be transferred but started talking about their childhood memories. Handle the call.
  • If time permits, do another role play using a similar work-related situation.
