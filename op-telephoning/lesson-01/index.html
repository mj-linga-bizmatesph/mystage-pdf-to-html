Telephoning is much more difficult than face-to-face communication as we need to rely on our words and tone of voice. Consider the following whenever you make or answer a phone call:
Repeat the other person’s name to build the relationship
Use proper telephone English
Understand the 10-second rule

TRY

Let’s talk about social media. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Is social media popular among business professionals in your country?
  • How many social media accounts do you have?
  • Do you consider yourself a die-hard user of social media?
  • Who uses social media more in your country, men or women?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • How does social media benefit you?
  • What’s the most popular social media network in your country?
  • Do you check your social media accounts even at work?
  • Does your company allow their employees to access social media sites?
