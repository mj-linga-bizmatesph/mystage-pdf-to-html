OTHER PROGRAMS TELEPHONINGILESSON 3
TRANSFERRING A CALL
3
BIZ TIP
It is still important to sound professional even when transferring calls. Be sure
to follow the key points below:
Ask what the call is about
Ask the person to wait
Thank the caller for waiting
SEE

Example

Yuko:
Thank you for calling DataComm. Yuko speaking. May I help you?
Caller:
Hi, this is Carmon Logan with PL Plastics.
May I speak with Mr. Frank Connelly in the overseas department?
Yuko:
One moment please.

Bizmates style

Yuko:
Thank you for calling DataComm. Yuko speaking. May I help you?
Caller:
Hi, this is Carmon Logan with PL Plastics. May I speak with Mr. Frank
Connelly in the overseas department?
Yuko:
Certainly, Mr. Logan. May I ask what this is in reference to?
Caller:
Yes, this is about the order we placed last week.
Yuko:
Thank you. Could you please hold while I transfer your call?
Caller:
Yes, thank you.

(After a few seconds)

Yuko:
Thank you for waiting, Mr. Logan. I now have Mr. Frank Connelly on
the other line. Have a nice day.

TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What else can you say to ask what the call is about?
  • What else can you say to ask the caller to wait?
  • What else can you say to thank the caller for waiting?
  • In what situations do you have to transfer calls?
