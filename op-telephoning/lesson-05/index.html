OTHER PROGRAMS TELEPHONINGILESSON 5
HANDLING INQUIRIES
5
BIZ TIP
Every inquiry is an opportunity in business so make sure you show willingness
to help. Handle inquiries effectively by:
Looking for ways to help the caller
Answering questions in full
Making sure you have done everything for the caller
SEE

Example

Caller:
Hi, could I have a brochure sent to our office?
Yuko:
What are you looking for?
Caller:
I just want information about your products along with prices.
Yuko:
I see. You can check our website.
Caller:
OK, thanks.

Bizmates style

Caller:
Hi, could I have a brochure sent to our office?
Yuko:
Certainly. Is there something in particular you’d like to know?
Caller:
I just want information about your products along with prices.
Yuko:
I see. If you’d like, I’d be happy to send one of our representatives to
visit your office.
Caller:
No, that’s not necessary.
Yuko:
I understand. I’ll just send the brochure. OK, may I have your name
and address?
Caller:
Yes, my name is Grace Stewart with GI Holdings. And our address is…
Yuko:
Thank you, Ms. Stewart. Our brochure should arrive by tomorrow
afternoon. Is there anything else I can help you with?
Caller:
No, that’s all. Thank you.
