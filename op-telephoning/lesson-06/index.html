OTHER PROGRAMS TELEPHONINGILESSON 6
HANDLING DIFFICULT INQUIRIES
6
BIZ TIP
Some inquiries can be difficult to answer because they’re beyond our scope of knowledge or responsibility. Keep the following in mind when this happens:
Don’t waste the caller’s time
Offer to get back to the caller; or
Transfer the call to someone who can help the caller
SEE

Example

Caller:
Hi, I’d like to know if you can ship your products anywhere in Indonesia. I’m looking at your website right now.
Yuko:
Where exactly in Indonesia?
Caller:
Kalimantan.
Yuko:
I see. I’m not sure, but I think we do.
Caller:
You “think” you do?
Yuko:
Sorry, I don’t know.
Caller:
So who can find out for me?
Yuko:
Please contact our shipping department. Would you like their phone number?

Bizmates style

Caller:
Hi, I’d like to know if you can ship your products anywhere in Indonesia. I’m looking at your website right now.
Yuko:
Where exactly in Indonesia?
Caller:
Kalimantan.
Yuko:
I see. I’m sorry but I don’t have that information. May I transfer your call to our shipping department? They’d be happy to provide you with more accurate information.
Caller:
Actually, I’m about to get on the train.
Yuko:
In that case, I can find out for you and call you back. Would that be OK?
Caller:
That would be great. My name is Jason and my number is…
