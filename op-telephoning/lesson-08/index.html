OTHER PROGRAMS TELEPHONINGILESSON 8
Giving instructions
8
BIZ TIP
Giving instructions on the phone has to be very clear and needs to sound
polite. You can do this by:
Phrasing your instructions into a question
Organizing your instructions (example: First,… next,… finally,…)
Confirming that the caller is able to follow you
SEE

Example

Caller:
Hi, I’m having trouble with your website.
Yuko:
I see. What is your problem?
Caller:
I can’t seem to login.
Yuko:
Did you correctly type in your username and password?
Caller:
Yes, of course.
Yuko:
OK, I think you made a mistake with your password. Please click the
“I forgot my password” icon. And then answer the secret question.
Then, the system will send you…
Caller:
I’m sorry. Can you please slow down?

Bizmates style

Caller:
Hi, I’m having trouble with your website.
Yuko:
I see. May I ask what the problem is?
Caller:
I can’t seem to login.
Yuko:
OK, are you accessing our website now?
Caller:
Yes, I am.
Yuko:
I’m sorry to trouble you, but can you try logging in one more time?
Caller:
OK… No, it says the password is incorrect.
Yuko:
OK, do you see the “I forgot my password” icon?
Caller:
Yes.
Yuko:
OK, could you click that please?
Caller:
OK.
Yuko:
Now, can you see the blue box…
