SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to seasons?

  • The employees are seeing blue skies with the new management.
  • The frigid temperature is making my hands feel numb.
  • Everything seems right as rain since we moved here last month.
  • It was open season on the CEO after he announced his resignation.
▼▼▼
TRY

Let’s talk about seasons. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What’s the busiest season to visit your country?
  • How long does summer last in your country?
  • Which season does your birthday fall in?
  • What’s the usual cool biz and warm biz attire for men/women?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Do you do any winter sports?
  • How do you usually spend your summer?
  • What’s a popular activity during spring?
  • What’s the worst season you’ve experienced so far?
