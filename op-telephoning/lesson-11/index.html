OTHER PROGRAMS TELEPHONINGILESSON 11
Making a call
11
BIZ TIP
Be professional and identify yourself properly when making a phone call by
following the key points below:
State your name and company
Give some background information
Make your request
SEE

Example

Clerk:
Thank you for calling EXL Inc. May I help you?
Ken:
Hello, my name is Ken. Could I speak to Dave Smith in the
Marketing Department?
Clerk:
May I have your full name please?
Ken:
Yes. It’s Ken Sato.
Clerk:
And may I know what company you are with, Mr. Sato?
Ken:
DataComm Corp.
Clerk:
Thanks. May I ask what this is in reference to?
Ken:
I’m just returning a call.
Clerk:
Thank you, Mr. Sato. Please hold while I transfer your call.

Bizmates style

Clerk:
Thank you for calling EXL Inc. May I help you?
Ken:
Hi, this is Ken Sato with DataComm Corp. I’m returning a call
from Mr. Dave Smith in the Marketing Department. Could I speak to
him please?
Clerk:
Certainly, Mr. Sato. Please hold while I transfer your call.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • Who do you usually make calls to at work?
  • What information do you mention when you introduce yourself?
  • What’s the usual purpose of your call?
▼▼▼
ACT

Role play.

Make an outgoing call based on the following purposes:

  • To confirm an appointment with a client
  • To inquire about a certain product/service
  • To check the status of a recently placed order
  • Something related to your business
