OTHER PROGRAMS TELEPHONINGILESSON 17
Making an urgent call
17
BIZ TIP
When calling about an urgent matter, let the other person know the state of
urgency by:
Clearly stating the urgency of the call
Explaining why the call is urgent
Requesting action
SEE

Example

Clerk:
Thank you for calling PL Plastics. May I help you?
Ken:
Hi, this is Ken Sato with DataComm Corp. May I speak to Mr. Carmon
Logan?
Clerk:
I’m sorry, but he’s out of the office. May I take a message?
Ken:
Yes, could you please ask him to call me back when he gets in?
Clerk:
Certainly. May I ask what this is in reference to?
Ken:
Please tell him, it’s really urgent.

Bizmates style

Clerk:
Thank you for calling PL Plastics. May I help you?
Ken:
Hi, this is Ken Sato with DataComm Corp. May I speak to Mr. Carmon
Logan?
Clerk:
I’m sorry, but he’s out of the office. May I take a message?
Ken:
Yes, it’s really urgent. It’s been a week and we still haven’t received
our order. Could you tell me when he will return?
Clerk:
I’m sorry, Mr. Sato. I’m not sure when he’ll come back.
Ken:
Could you please call Mr. Logan and ask him to call me back
immediately?
Clerk:
Yes, I’ll try reaching him on his phone.
Ken:
Thank you. Could you do that now please? I’ll stay on hold.
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • In what situations do you make urgent calls?
  • How soon do you expect some sort of action from the other person?
  • Do you feel they consider your call as something really urgent?
▼▼▼
ACT

Role play.

Make an urgent call for the following reasons:

  • A sudden change of schedule in the appointment
  • Your order is being delivered to a different address
  • Something related to your business
