OTHER PROGRAMS TELEPHONINGILESSON 18
Asking for a subscription renewal
18
BIZ TIP
The goal of asking your clients to renew their subscription is to continue their
business with you. To do this, it’s always a good idea to:
Request for 5 minutes of their time
Show how easy it is to renew
Offer special promos if there are any
SEE

Example

Mike:
Hello?
Ken:
Good afternoon. This is Ken Sato with DataComm Corp. Is this Mr.
Mike O’Neil?
Mike:
Yes, speaking.
Ken:
Hello, Mr. O’Neil. I’m calling about your Pocket Wi-fi subscription.
Mike:
Sorry, I’m a little busy now.
Ken:
OK, sure.

Bizmates style

Mike:
Hello?
Ken:
Good afternoon. This is Ken Sato with DataComm Corp. Is this Mr.
Mike O’Neil?
Mike:
Yes, speaking.
Ken:
Hello, Mr. O’Neil. I’m calling to confirm if you’ve received a notice
about your Pocket Wi-Fi subscription?
Mike:
Yes, I have.
Ken:
Mr. O’Neil, I understand how busy it can get nowadays. Do you have
just 5 minutes?
Mike:
5 minutes? OK.
Ken:
To save you the trouble, I can help process your subscription renewal
over the phone. We have a promotion for our loyal customers like you.
We are offering a 10% discount upon renewing your subscription…
▼▼▼
TRY

Answer these questions.

  • What subscription might you ask for a renewal anytime soon?
  • What sort of background information would you give?
  • How would you renegotiate the terms in your favor?
▼▼▼
ACT

Role play.

Ask your customer to renew a contract or subscription with your company.
