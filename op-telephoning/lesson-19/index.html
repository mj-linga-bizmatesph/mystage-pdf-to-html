SEE

Do you know these words and phrases related to luck?

  • This amulet is said to bring good luck. Well, I hope it does.
  • Tough luck, but some people really play dirty.
  • She struck gold when she married him. They’re a perfect match.
  • I’ll be bringing sushi for our team’s potluck dinner. What about you?

TRY

Let’s talk about luck. Let the trainer take the lead in asking you questions. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • What item has brought you good luck many times?
  • Do people in your country believe in luck in general?
  • What good fortune has happened to you lately?
  • What’s a common lucky charm in your country?

Let’s switch roles. Now you take the lead in asking questions and giving your opinion. Here are some questions to get you started.

  • Does your culture have any superstitions to prevent or get rid of bad luck?
  • Does luck play a significant role in your work?
  • How often do you get this feeling it’s your lucky day?
  • Do you believe in superstitions about good or bad luck?
