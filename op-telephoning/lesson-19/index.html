OTHER PROGRAMS TELEPHONINGILESSON 19
Making a complaint
19
BIZ TIP
When making a complaint, understand that the other person is trying to help
you, so it’s a good idea to:
Be calm as much as possible
Be reasonable
Focus on solving the problem, not the problem itself
SEE

Example

Clerk:
Thank you for calling PL Plastics. May I help you?
Ken:
This is Ken Sato with DataComm Corp. again. I’d like to file a
complaint. We received the wrong model after waiting for a week!
Clerk:
I’m terribly sorry about that, Mr. Sato. This is a very rare case. Our
shipping department has been…
Ken:
I waited for a whole week and then you send the wrong order.
Clerk:
I understand your frustration. However, please understand that we’ll
try our best to…
Ken:
This is ridiculous. Do you realize how much trouble this is causing us?
We need…

Bizmates style

Clerk:
Thank you for calling PL Plastics. May I help you?
Ken:
This is Ken Sato with DataComm Corp. I’d like to report that we’ve
finally received our order, but we received the wrong one.
Clerk:
I’m terribly sorry about that, Mr. Sato. This is a very rare case. Our shipping department has been…
Ken:
OK, you don’t need to explain. I just want to know what can be done.
Clerk:
I can contact the shipping department to find out what happened.
Ken:
I need this order by tomorrow morning. Could you find out if we can
get 50 units of model XT100 sent to DataComm by tomorrow
morning? And please call me back by 1PM today.
Clerk:
Certainly. I will contact the shipping department right away.

TRY

Answer these questions.

  • When was the last time you filed a complaint?
  • What was it about?
  • What sort of resolution were you expecting about your complaint?

ACT

Role play.

Make a complaint about one of the following issues:

  • A mistake in your sales invoice
  • A defective product
  • Something related to your business
