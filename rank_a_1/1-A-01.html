Something =  n.

I am a student.

You are a trainer.

He is a colleague.

She is a client.

It is a problem.

We are engineers.

They are foreigners.

Something = adj.

I am fine.

You are kind.

He is funny.

She is French.

It is important.

We are happy.

They are creative.

Something = adj. +  n.

I’m a part-time worker.

You’re a good trainer.

He’s a 3rd year employee.

She’s a busy person.

It’s an interesting topic.

We’re best friends.

They’re new employees.

Questions

Am I correct?

How are you?

Is he 30 years old?

Is she a doctor?

Is it delicious?

Are we friends?

Are they busy?

Example
Bill:
Ken:
Bill:
Hi, I’m Bill. I’m a new employee.
I’m Ken. Nice to meet you, Bill. Welcome to the team. Are you nervous?
Yes, I am. But everybody is nice.
